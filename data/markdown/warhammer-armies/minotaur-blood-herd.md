# Minotaur Blood Herd

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Beastmen Brayherds army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Minotaur Blood Herd army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Doombull](../unit/doombull.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Bray-Shaman](../unit/bray-shaman.md) per 1,000 points
- [Gorebulls](../unit/gorebull.md), [Wargors](../unit/wargor.md) and [Centigor Chieftains](../unit/warhoof.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1 [Minotaur Herd](../unit/minotaur-herd.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Herdstone](../special-rules/herdstones.md)
- [Gor Herds](../unit/gor-herd.md), [Ungor Herds](../unit/ungor-herd.md) and [Chaos Warhounds](../unit/chaos-warhounds.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md) per 1,000 points
- [Harpies](../unit/harpies.md), [Minotaur Herds](../unit/minotaur-herd.md), [Centigor Herds](../unit/centigor-herd.md), [Razorgor Herds](../unit/razorgor-herd.md) and [Ghorgons](../unit/ghorgon.md)

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Dragon Ogre Shaggoth](../unit/dragon-ogre-shaggoth.md) per 1,000 points
- [Chaos Giants](../unit/chaos-giant.md)
- [Chaos Ogres](../unit/chaos-ogres.md) and [Chaos Trolls](../unit/chaos-trolls.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Gorebull or Wargor in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Banner with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Beastmen Brayherds magic items.
