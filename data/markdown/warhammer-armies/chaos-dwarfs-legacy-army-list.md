# Chaos Dwarfs Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Chaos Dwarfs. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Sorcerer-Prophet](../unit/sorcerer-prophet.md) or [Infernal Castellan](../unit/infernal-castellan.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Bull Centaur Taur’ruk](../unit/bull-centaur-taurruk.md) per unit of [Bull Centaur Renders](../unit/bull-centaur-renders.md) taken
- [Daemonsmith Sorcerers](../unit/daemonsmith-sorcerer.md), [Infernal Seneschals](../unit/infernal-seneschal.md) and [Hobgoblin Khans](../unit/hobgoblin-khan.md)
- 0-1 [Black Orc Bigboss](../unit/black-orc-bigboss.md) or [Black Orc Warboss](../unit/black-orc-warboss.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Infernal Guard](../unit/infernal-guard.md)
- [Hobgoblin Cutthroats](../unit/hobgoblin-cutthroats.md)
- 0-1 [Black Orc Mob](../unit/black-orc-mob.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Infernal Ironsworn](../unit/infernal-ironsworn.md), [Sneaky Gits](../unit/sneaky-gits.md), [K'daai Fireborn](../unit/kdaai-fireborn.md) and [Bull Centaur Renders](../unit/bull-centaur-renders.md)
- 0-1 [Iron Daemon](../unit/iron-daemon.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 war machine chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Deathshrieker Rocket Launcher](../unit/deathshrieker-rocket-launcher.md)
  - [Magma Cannon](../unit/magma-cannon.md)
- 0-2 [Hobgoblin Bolt Throwers](../unit/hobgoblin-bolt-thrower.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Hobgoblin Wolf Riders](../unit/hobgoblin-wolf-riders.md) per [Hobgoblin Khan](../unit/hobgoblin-khan.md) taken
- 0-1 war machine chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Dreadquake Mortar](../unit/dreadquake-mortar.md)
  - [Hellcannon](../unit/hellcannon.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Infernal Seneschal in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
