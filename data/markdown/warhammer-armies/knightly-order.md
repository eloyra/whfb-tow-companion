# Knightly Order

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Empire of Man army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Knightly Order army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Force of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md)
- 1+ [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md)
- 0-1 [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md) or [High Priest of Ulric](../unit/high-priest-of-ulric.md) per 1,000 points
- [Witch Hunters](../unit/witch-hunter.md), [Priests of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md) and [Priests of Ulric](../unit/priest-of-ulric.md)
- 0-1 [War Altar of Sigmar](../unit/war-altar-of-sigmar.md) (taken as a mount for a [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md))

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- 1+ unit of [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md) per 1,000 points
- [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) and [Demigryph Knights](../unit/demigryph-knights.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Empire State Troops](../unit/state-troops.md) or [State Missile Troops](../unit/state-missile-troops.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Pistoliers](../unit/pistoliers.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Chapter Master in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Empire of Man magic items lists.
