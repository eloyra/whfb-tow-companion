# Beastmen Brayherds Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Beastmen Brayherds. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Beastlord](../unit/beastlord.md), [Great Bray-Shaman](../unit/great-bray-shaman.md) or [Doombull](../unit/doombull.md) per 1,000 points
- [Wargors](../unit/wargor.md), [Bray-shamans](../unit/bray-shaman.md), [Gorebulls](../unit/gorebull.md) and [Centigor Chieftains](../unit/warhoof.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ [Gor Herd](../unit/gor-herd.md)
- [Ungor Herds](../unit/ungor-herd.md), [Chaos Warhounds](../unit/chaos-warhounds.md), [Razorgor Herds](../unit/razorgor-herd.md) and [Tuskgor Chariots](../unit/tuskgor-chariot.md)
- If your General is a [Beastlord](../unit/beastlord.md) or [Wargor](../unit/wargor.md), 0-1 [Bestigor Herd](../unit/bestigor-herd.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- If your General is a [Doombull](../unit/doombull.md) or [Gorebull](../unit/gorebull.md), 0-1 [Minotaur Herd](../unit/minotaur-herd.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Bestigor Herds](../unit/bestigor-herd.md), [Harpies](../unit/harpies.md), [Minotaur Herds](../unit/minotaur-herd.md), [Centigor Herds](../unit/centigor-herd.md), [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md), [Razorgor Chariots](../unit/razorgor-chariot.md) and [Cockatrice](../unit/cockatrice.md)
- [Chaos Ogres](../unit/chaos-ogres.md)
- If your General is a [Great Bray-Shaman](../unit/great-bray-shaman.md) or [Bray-Shaman](../unit/bray-shaman.md), 0-1 [Jabberslythe](../unit/jabberslythe.md) or [Cygor](../unit/cygor.md) may be taken as a Special choice
- If your General is a [Doombull](../unit/doombull.md) or [Gorebull](../unit/gorebull.md), 0-1 [Ghorgon](../unit/ghorgon.md) may be taken as a Special choice
- 0-1 [Preyton](../unit/preyton.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Herdstone](../special-rules/herdstones.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Dragon Ogre Shaggoths](../unit/dragon-ogre-shaggoth.md), [Jabberslythes](../unit/jabberslythe.md), [Cygors](../unit/cygor.md), [Ghorgons](../unit/ghorgon.md) and [Chaos Giants](../unit/chaos-giant.md)
- [Chaos Trolls](../unit/chaos-trolls.md) and [Chaos Spawn](../unit/chaos-spawn.md)
- 0-1 [Gigantic Spawn of Chaos](../unit/gigantic-spawn-of-chaos.md) per 1,000 point

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- 0-1 [Bonegrinder Giant](../unit/bonegrinder-giant.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any *Beastmen Brayherds* Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Orc & Goblin Tribes](../army/orc-and-goblin-tribes.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md)
  - [Tomb Kings of Khemri](../army/tomb-kings-of-khemri.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Wargor in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
