# Kingdom of Bretonnia Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Kingdom of Bretonnia. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Duke](../unit/duke.md)
- 0-1 [Baron](../unit/baron.md) or [Prophetess](../unit/prophetess.md) per 1,000 points
- [Paladins](../unit/paladin.md), [Damsels](../unit/damsel.md) and [Sergeants-at-Arms](../unit/sergeant-at-arms.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Knights of the Realm on Foot](../unit/knights-of-the-realm-on-foot.md) and/or [Mounted Knights of the Realm](../unit/mounted-knights-of-the-realm.md)
- 1+ unit of [Men-at-Arms](../unit/men-at-arms.md) and/or [Peasant Bowmen](../unit/peasant-bowmen.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Knights Errant](../unit/knights-errant.md) per unit of [Knights of the Realm on Foot](../unit/knights-of-the-realm-on-foot.md) or [Mounted Knights of the Realm](../unit/mounted-knights-of-the-realm.md) taken

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Squires](../unit/squires.md), [Questing Knights](../unit/questing-knights.md), [Pegasus Knights](../unit/pegasus-knights.md) and [Mounted Yeomen](../unit/mounted-yeomen.md)
- 0-2 units of [Battle Pilgrims](../unit/battle-pilgrims.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Grail Knights](../unit/grail-knights.md)
- 0-1 [Field Trebuchet](../unit/field-trebuchet.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Kingdom of Bretonnia Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Dwarfen Mountain Holds](../army/dwarfen-mountain-holds.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Empire of Man](../army/empire-of-man.md)
  - [Grand Cathay](grand-cathay-army-list.md)
  - [High Elf Realms](../army/high-elf-realms.md)
  - [Wood Elf Realms](../army/wood-elf-realms.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Paladin in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for no additional cost. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
