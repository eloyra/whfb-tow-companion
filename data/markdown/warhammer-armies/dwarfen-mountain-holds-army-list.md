# Dwarfen Mountain Holds Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Dwarfen Mountain Holds. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [King](../unit/king.md), [Anvil of Doom](../unit/anvil-of-doom.md) or [Runelord](../unit/runelord.md) per 1,000 points
- [Thanes](../unit/thane.md), [Runesmiths](../unit/runesmith.md), [Daemon Slayers](../unit/daemon-slayer.md), [Dragon Slayers](../unit/dragon-slayer.md) and [Dwarf Engineers](../unit/engineer.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Dwarf Warriors](../unit/dwarf-warriors.md), [Quarrellers](../unit/quarrellers.md) and [Thunderers](../unit/thunderers.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Rangers](../unit/rangers.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- If your General is a [King](../unit/king.md), 0-1 unit of [Longbeards](../unit/longbeards.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Longbeards](../unit/longbeards.md), [Ironbreakers](../unit/ironbreakers.md), [Miners](../unit/miners.md), [Slayers](../unit/slayers.md) and [Gyrocopters](../unit/gyrocopter.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Hammerers](../unit/hammerers.md) per [King](../unit/king.md) or [Thane](../unit/thane.md) taken
- 0-3 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Bolt Thrower](../unit/bolt-thrower.md)
  - [Grudge Thrower](../unit/grudge-thrower.md)
  - [Cannon](../unit/cannon.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Rangers](../unit/rangers.md), [Irondrakes](../unit/irondrakes.md) and [Gyrobombers](../unit/gyrobomber.md)
- 0-2 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Organ Gun](../unit/organ-gun.md)
  - [Flame Cannon](../unit/flame-cannon.md)
- 0-4 [Dwarf Carts](../unit/dwarf-cart.md)
- If army has one more Slayers of Legend, may include 0-1 [Goblin-hewer](../unit/goblin-hewer.md) per  
  1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- [Prince Ulther's Dragon Company](../unit/prince-ulthers-dragon-company.md)
- 0-3 [Doomseekers](../unit/doomseeker.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Imperial Dwarf Mercenaries](../unit/imperial-dwarf-mercenaries.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Imperial Ogres](../unit/imperial-ogres.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Dwarfen Mountain Holds Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Empire of Man](../army/empire-of-man.md)
  - [Grand Cathay](grand-cathay-army-list.md)
  - [Kingdom of Bretonnia](../army/kingdom-of-bretonnia.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [High Elf Realms](../army/high-elf-realms.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Thane in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on Weapon, Armour and Talismanic runes, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase Standard runes with no points limit.
