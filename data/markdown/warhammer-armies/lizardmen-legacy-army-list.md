# Lizardmen Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Lizardmen. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Slann Mage-Priest](../unit/slann-mage-priest.md)
- 0-1 [Saurus Oldblood](../unit/saurus-oldblood.md) or [Skink Priest](../unit/skink-priest.md) per 1,000 points
- [Saurus Scar-Veterans](../unit/saurus-scar-veteran.md) and [Skink Chiefs](../unit/skink-chief.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Saurus Warriors](../unit/saurus-warriors.md)
- [Skink Skirmishers](../unit/skink-skirmishers.md) and [Jungle Swarms](../unit/jungle-swarms.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Temple Guard](../unit/temple-guard.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Chameleon Skinks](../unit/chameleon-skinks.md), [Kroxigor](../unit/kroxigor.md) and [Cold One Riders](../unit/cold-one-riders.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Terradon Riders](../unit/terradon-riders.md) per Skink Hero taken
- 0-1 unit of [Ripperdactyl Riders](../unit/ripperdactyl-riders.md) per Skink Hero taken
- 0-2 [Bastiladons](../unit/bastiladon.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Salamander Pack](../unit/salamander-pack.md) or [Razordon Pack](../unit/razordon-pack.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Stegadon](../unit/stegadon.md) or [Troglodon](../unit/troglodon.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [High Elf Realms](../army/high-elf-realms.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Slann Mage-Priest, Saurus Scar-Veteran or Skink Chief in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit. A Slann Mage-Priest may be both your army’s Battle Standard Bearer and its General.
