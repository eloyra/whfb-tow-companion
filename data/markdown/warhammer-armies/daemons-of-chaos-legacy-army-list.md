# Daemons of Chaos Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Daemons of Chaos. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 Greater Daemon (of [Khorne](../unit/bloodthirster.md), [Nurgle](../unit/great-unclean-one.md), [Slaanesh](../unit/keeper-of-secrets.md) or [Tzeentch](../unit/lord-of-change.md))
- 0-1 [Daemonic Herald of Khorne](../unit/daemonic-herald-of-khorne.md), [Nurgle](../unit/daemonic-herald-of-nurgle.md), [Slaanesh](../unit/daemonic-herald-of-slaanesh.md) or [Tzeentch](../unit/daemonic-herald-of-tzeentch.md) per unit with the same [Daemonic Alignment](../special-rules/daemonic-alignment.md)
- 0-1 [Daemon Prince](../unit/daemon-prince-daemons-of-chaos.md) per 1,000 points

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- If your General is a:

  - [Daemon of Khorne](../special-rules/daemon-of-khorne.md), [Bloodletters](../unit/bloodletters-of-khorne.md) and [Flesh Hounds](../unit/flesh-hounds-of-khorne.md) may be taken as Core choices
  - [Daemon of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md), [Plaguebearers](../unit/plaguebearers-of-nurgle.md) and [Nurglings](../unit/nurglings.md) may be taken as Core choices
  - [Daemon of Slaanesh](../special-rules/daemon-of-slaanesh.md), [Daemonettes](../unit/daemonettes-of-slaanesh.md) and [Seekers](../unit/seekers-of-slaanesh.md) may be taken as Core choices
  - [Daemon of Tzeentch](../special-rules/daemon-of-tzeentch.md), [Pink Horrors](../unit/pink-horrors-of-tzeentch.md), [Blue Horrors](../unit/blue-horrors-of-tzeentch.md) and [Brimstone Horrors](../unit/brimstone-horrors-of-tzeentch.md) may be taken as Core choices
- [Chaos Furies](../unit/chaos-furies.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Bloodletters](../unit/bloodletters-of-khorne.md), [Flesh Hounds](../unit/flesh-hounds-of-khorne.md), [Plaguebearers](../unit/plaguebearers-of-nurgle.md), [Nurglings](../unit/nurglings.md), [Daemonettes](../unit/daemonettes-of-slaanesh.md), [Seekers](../unit/seekers-of-slaanesh.md), [Pink Horrors](../unit/pink-horrors-of-tzeentch.md), [Blue Horrors](../unit/blue-horrors-of-tzeentch.md) and [Brimstone Horrors](../unit/brimstone-horrors-of-tzeentch.md)
- If your General is a:

  - [Daemon of Khorne](../special-rules/daemon-of-khorne.md), [Bloodcrushers](../unit/bloodcrushers-of-khorne.md) may be taken as Special choices
  - [Daemon of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md), [Beasts of Nurgle](../unit/beasts-of-nurgle.md) and [Plague Drones](../unit/plague-drones-of-nurgle.md) may be taken as Special choices
  - [Daemon of Slaanesh](../special-rules/daemon-of-slaanesh.md), [Fiends](../unit/fiends-of-slaanesh.md), [Hellflayers](../unit/hellflayer-of-slaanesh.md) and [Seeker Chariots](../unit/seeker-chariot-of-slaanesh.md) may be taken as Special choices
  - [Daemon of Tzeentch](../special-rules/daemon-of-tzeentch.md), [Flamers](../unit/flamers-of-tzeentch.md) and [Screamers](../unit/screamers-of-tzeentch.md) may be taken as Special choices

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Bloodcrushers](../unit/bloodcrushers-of-khorne.md), [Beasts of Nurgle](../unit/beasts-of-nurgle.md), [Plague Drones](../unit/plague-drones-of-nurgle.md), [Fiends](../unit/fiends-of-slaanesh.md), [Hellflayers](../unit/hellflayer-of-slaanesh.md), [Seeker Chariots](../unit/seeker-chariot-of-slaanesh.md), [Flamers](../unit/flamers-of-tzeentch.md), [Screamers](../unit/screamers-of-tzeentch.md), [Burning Chariots](../unit/burning-chariot-of-tzeentch.md) and [Soul Grinders](../unit/soul-grinder.md)
- 0-1 [Skull Cannon of Khorne](../unit/skull-cannon-of-khorne.md) per 1,000 points

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Daemonic Herald in your army may be upgraded to be your [Daemonic Locus](../special-rules/daemonic-locus.md) for +25 points. A Daemonic Herald that has been upgraded to be your Daemonic Locus gains the Daemonic Locus special rule. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on Daemonic Gifts, a Daemonic Locus can purchase a single Daemonic Icon with no points limit.
