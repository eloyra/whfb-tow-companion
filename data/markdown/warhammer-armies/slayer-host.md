# Slayer Host

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Dwarfen Mountain Holds army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Slayer Host army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Force of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Ungrim Ironfist](../unit/ungrim-ironfist.md), Slayer King of Karak Kadrin
- 0-1 [Daemon Slayer](../unit/daemon-slayer.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Dragon Slayers](../unit/dragon-slayer.md) per unit of [Slayers](../unit/slayers.md) taken

### Core

At least 20% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Slayers](../unit/slayers.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Brotherhood of Grimnir](../unit/brotherhood-of-grimnir.md)
- 0-4 [Doomseekers](../unit/doomseeker.md) per [Daemon Slayer](../unit/daemon-slayer.md) or [Dragon Slayer](../unit/dragon-slayer.md) taken

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Goblin-hewer](../unit/goblin-hewer.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Dwarf Cart](../unit/dwarf-cart.md) per 1,000 points
- If your army includes [Ungrim Ironfist](../unit/ungrim-ironfist.md), 0-1 unit of [Hammerers](../unit/hammerers.md) may be included as a Rare choice

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- 0-1 unit of [Rangers](../unit/rangers.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Dragon Slayer in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on Weapon runes, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase Standard runes with no points limit.

### Runic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase runes may do as [described](../magic-items/runic-items.md).

## Runic Tattoos

Any models that have the option to purchase runic tattoos may do so as [described](../magic-items/runic-tattoos.md).
