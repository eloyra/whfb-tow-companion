# Minimum Three Units

An army must include a minimum of three non-character units, not including units whose troop type is [swarms](../troop-types-in-detail/swarms.md), [war beasts](../troop-types-in-detail/war-beasts.md) or [war machines](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md).
