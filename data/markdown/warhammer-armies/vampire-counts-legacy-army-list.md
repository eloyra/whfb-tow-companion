# Vampire Counts Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Vampire Counts. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ Wizard
- 0-1 [Vampire Count](../unit/vampire-count.md), [Master Necromancer](../unit/master-necromancer.md) or [Strigoi Ghoul King](../unit/strigoi-ghoul-king.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Wight King](../unit/wight-king.md) or [Tomb Banshee](../unit/tomb-banshee.md) per 1,000 points
- [Vampire Thralls](../unit/vampire-thrall.md), [Necromantic Acolytes](../unit/necromantic-acolyte.md), [Wight Lords](../unit/wight-lord.md) and [Cairn Wraiths](../unit/cairn-wraith.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Skeleton Warriors](../unit/skeleton-warriors-vampire-counts.md), [Zombies](../unit/zombies.md), [Crypt Ghouls](../unit/crypt-ghouls.md), [Bat Swarms](../unit/bat-swarms.md) and [Dire Wolves](../unit/dire-wolves.md)
- If your army includes one or more Wights, 0-1 unit of [Grave Guard](../unit/grave-guard.md) or [Black Knights](../unit/black-knights.md) may be taken as a Core choices

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Grave Guard](../unit/grave-guard.md) or [Black Knights](../unit/black-knights.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Crypt Horrors](../unit/crypt-horrors.md) or [Fell Bats](../unit/fell-bats.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-3 [Corpse Carts](../unit/corpse-cart.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Spirit Hosts](../unit/spirit-hosts.md) per [Cairn Wraith](../unit/cairn-wraith.md) or [Tomb Banshee](../unit/tomb-banshee.md) taken
- If your General is a [Strigoi Ghoul King](../unit/strigoi-ghoul-king.md), 0-1 unit of [Vargheists](../unit/vargheists.md) or 0-1 [Terrorgheist](../unit/terrorgheist.md) may be taken as a Special choice

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Vargheists](../unit/vargheists.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Black Coaches](../unit/black-coach.md)
- 0-1 [Terrorgheist](../unit/terrorgheist.md) or Varghulf per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Blood Knights](../unit/blood-knights.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Hexwraiths](../unit/hexwraiths.md) per [Cairn Wraith](../unit/cairn-wraith.md) or [Tomb Banshee](../unit/tomb-banshee.md) taken

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [Tomb Kings of Khemri](../army/tomb-kings-of-khemri.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Vampire Thrall or Wight Lord in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
