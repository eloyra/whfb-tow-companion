# Renegade Crowns

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook, alongside various profiles from the Empire of Man, the Kingdom of Bretonnia and other army lists found in *Forces of Fantasy* and the Arcane Journals.

If you wish to field a Renegade Crowns army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below. Over the following pages you will find new profiles and rules for certain models in your army, representing units unique to a Renegade Crowns army:

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Renegade Prince](../unit/renegade-prince.md) per 1,000 points
- [Renegade Captains](../unit/renegade-captain.md) and [Outcast Wizards](../unit/outcast-wizard.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Veteran Sellswords](../unit/veteran-sellswords.md) per 1,000
- [Sellsword Infantry](../unit/sellsword-infantry.md), [Freeblade Knights](../unit/freeblade-knights.md) and [Hireling Outriders](../unit/hireling-outriders.md)
- [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md) and [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md)

### Special

Up to 33% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- [Veteran Freeblades](../unit/veteran-freeblades.md)
- 0-2 [Border Princes Mortars](../unit/border-princes-mortar.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- [Border Princes Brigands](../unit/border-princes-brigands.md)
- 0-1 war machine chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Border Princes Organ Gun](../unit/border-princes-organ-gun.md)
  - [Border Princes Bombard](../unit/border-princes-bombard.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- Any [Dogs of War](dogs-of-war.md) from any Arcane Journal (i.e., any unit that has the 'Dogs of War' rule, allowing it to be taken as Mercenaries in one or more Grand Army composition lists):

  - [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
  - [Bonegrinder Giant](../unit/bonegrinder-giant.md)
  - [Doomseeker](../unit/doomseeker.md)
  - [Imperial Dwarf Mercenaries](../unit/imperial-dwarf-mercenaries.md)
  - [Imperial Ogres](../unit/imperial-ogres.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Renegade Captain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

## Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common magic items.
