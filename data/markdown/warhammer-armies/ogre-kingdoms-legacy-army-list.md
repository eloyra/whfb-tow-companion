# Ogre Kingdoms Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Ogre Kingdoms. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Tyrant](../unit/tyrant.md) or [Slaughtermaster](../unit/slaughtermaster.md) per 1,000 points
- [Bruisers](../unit/bruiser.md), [Butchers](../unit/butcher.md), [Hunters](../unit/hunter.md) and [Firebellies](../unit/firebelly.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ [Ogre Bulls](../unit/ogre-bulls.md)
- [Ironguts](../unit/ironguts.md), [Gnoblar Fighters](../unit/gnoblar-fighters.md), [Gnoblar Trappers](../unit/gnoblar-trappers.md) and [Sabretusk Packs](../unit/sabretusk-pack.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Leadbelchers](../unit/leadbelchers.md) and [Yhetees](../unit/yhetees.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Maneaters](../unit/maneaters.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Mournfang Cavalry](../unit/mournfang-cavalry.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 heavy chariot chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Ironblaster](../unit/ironblaster.md)
  - [Gnoblar Scraplauncher](../unit/gnoblar-scraplauncher.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Thundertusk Riders](../unit/thundertusk-riders.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Stonehorn Riders](../unit/stonehorn-riders.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-3 [Gorgers](../unit/gorger.md)
- [Giants](../unit/giant.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [Orc & Goblin Tribes](../army/orc-and-goblin-tribes.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Bruiser in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
