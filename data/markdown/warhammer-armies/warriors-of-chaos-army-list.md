# Warriors of Chaos Army List

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Chaos Lord](../unit/chaos-lord.md) or [Daemon Prince](../unit/daemon-prince-warriors-of-chaos.md)
- 0-1 [Exalted Champion](../unit/exalted-champion.md) or [Sorcerer Lord](../unit/sorcerer-lord.md) per 1,000 points
- [Aspiring Champions](../unit/aspiring-champion.md) and [Exalted Sorcerers](../unit/exalted-sorcerer.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Chaos Warriors](../unit/chaos-warriors.md), [Chaos Marauders](../unit/chaos-marauders.md), [Forsaken](../unit/forsaken.md), [Chaos Knights](../unit/chaos-knights.md), [Marauder Horsemen](../unit/marauder-horsemen.md) and [Chaos Warhounds](../unit/chaos-warhounds.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Chosen Chaos Warriors](../unit/chosen-chaos-warriors.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Chosen Chaos Knights](../unit/chosen-chaos-knights.md)
- [Chaos Ogres](../unit/chaos-ogres.md), [Chaos Trolls](../unit/chaos-trolls.md), [Chaos Spawn](../unit/chaos-spawn.md), [Chaos Chariots](../unit/chaos-chariot.md) and [Chimeras](../unit/chimera.md)
- [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Gorebeast Chariots](../unit/gorebeast-chariot.md)
- 0-1 [Hellcannon](../unit/hellcannon.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Dragon Ogre Shaggoth](../unit/dragon-ogre-shaggoth.md) per unit of [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md)
- 0-1 [Chaos Giant](../unit/chaos-giant.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Gigantic Spawn of Chaos](../unit/gigantic-spawn-of-chaos.md) per 1,000 point
- 0-1 [Warpfire Dragon](../unit/warpfire-dragon.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- 0-1 [Bonegrinder Giant](../unit/bonegrinder-giant.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Warriors of Chaos Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Orc & Goblin Tribes](../army/orc-and-goblin-tribes.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Beastmen Brayherds](../army/beastmen-brayherds.md)
  - [Tomb Kings of Khemri](../army/tomb-kings-of-khemri.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Exalted Champion or Aspiring Champion in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
