# Orion's Wild Hunt

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Wood Elf Realms army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field an Orion's Wild Hunt army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Forces of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Glade Lord](../unit/glade-lord.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Shadowdancer](../unit/shadowdancer.md), [Spellsinger](../unit/spellsinger.md) or [Waystalker](../unit/waystalker.md) per 1,000 points
- [Glade Captains](../unit/glade-captain.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Wild Riders](../unit/wild-riders.md) per 1,000 points
- [Glade Riders](../unit/glade-riders.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Sisters of the Thorn](../unit/sisters-of-the-thorn.md) per 1,000 points
- [Wardancers](../unit/wardancers.md), [Warhawk Riders](../unit/warhawk-riders.md), [Wild Riders](../unit/wild-riders.md) and [Wood Elf Beast Packs](../unit/wood-elf-beast-pack.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Waywatchers](../unit/waywatchers.md) per [Waystalker](../unit/waystalker.md) taken
- 0-3 [Great Eagles](../unit/great-eagle.md) per 1,000 points
- [Deepwood Scouts](../unit/deepwood-scouts.md) and [Wildwood Rangers](../unit/wildwood-rangers.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Glade Captain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Banner with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Wood Elf Realms magic items.
