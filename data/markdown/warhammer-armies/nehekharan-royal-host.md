# Nehekharan Royal Host

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Tomb Kings of Khemri army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Nehekharan Royal Host in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ [Tomb King](../unit/tomb-king.md) or [Tomb Prince](../unit/tomb-prince.md)
- [Mortuary Priests](../unit/mortuary-priest.md) and [Royal Heralds](../unit/royal-herald.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Skeleton Chariots](../unit/skeleton-chariots.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Tomb Guard](../unit/tomb-guard.md) or [Tomb Guard Chariots](../unit/tomb-guard-chariots.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- [Skeleton Archers](../unit/skeleton-archers.md), [Skeleton Infantry Cohorts](../unit/skeleton-infantry-cohort.md) and [Skeleton Cavalry Cohorts](../unit/skeleton-cavalry-cohort.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Tomb Guard](../unit/tomb-guard.md), [Skeleton Skirmishers](../unit/skeleton-skirmishers.md), [Skeleton Horse Archers](../unit/skeleton-horse-archers.md), [Ushabti](../unit/ushabti.md), [Necropolis Knights](../unit/necropolis-knights.md) and [Tomb Guard Chariots](../unit/tomb-guard-chariots.md)
- 0-2 [Tomb Scorpions](../unit/tomb-scorpion.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Khemrian Warsphinx](../unit/khemrian-warsphinx.md) (not counting character mounts) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-2 [Screaming Skull Catapults](../unit/screaming-skull-catapult.md) per 1,000 points

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Royal Herald in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Tomb Kings of Khemri magic items lists.
