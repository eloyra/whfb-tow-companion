# Wood Elf Realms Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Wood Elf Realms. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Glade Lord](../unit/glade-lord.md) or [Spellweaver](../unit/spellweaver.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Treeman Ancient](../unit/treemen-ancient.md) per 1,000 points
- [Glade Captains](../unit/glade-captain.md), [Spellsingers](../unit/spellsinger.md), [Shadowdancers](../unit/shadowdancer.md), [Waystalkers](../unit/waystalker.md) and [Branchwraiths](../unit/branchwraith.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Glade Guard](../unit/glade-guard.md)
- [Eternal Guard](../unit/eternal-guard.md), [Dryads](../unit/dryads.md) and [Glade Riders](../unit/glade-riders.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Deepwood Scouts](../unit/deepwood-scouts.md) may be taken as a Core choice per 1,000 points

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Deepwood Scouts](../unit/deepwood-scouts.md), [Wildwood Rangers](../unit/wildwood-rangers.md), [Wardancers](../unit/wardancers.md), [Sisters of the Thorn](../unit/sisters-of-the-thorn.md), [Wild Riders](../unit/wild-riders.md) and [Warhawk Riders](../unit/warhawk-riders.md)
- 0-2 units of [Tree Kin](../unit/tree-kin.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Treemen](../unit/treeman.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Waywatchers](../unit/waywatchers.md) per [Waystalker](../unit/waystalker.md) taken
- 0-3 [Great Eagles](../unit/great-eagle.md) per 1,000 points
- [Wood Elf Beast Packs](../unit/wood-elf-beast-pack.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Wood Elf Realms Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Empire of Man](../army/empire-of-man.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Grand Cathay](grand-cathay-army-list.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Kingdom of Bretonnia](../army/kingdom-of-bretonnia.md)
  - [High Elf Realms](../army/high-elf-realms.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Glade Captain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Banner with no points limit.
