# Jade Fleet

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Grand Cathay army list found in *Arcane Journal: Armies of Grand Cathay*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Miao Ying](../unit/miao-ying.md), the Storm Dragon
- 0-1 [Shugengan Lord](../unit/shugengan-lord.md) or [Lord Magistrate](../unit/lord-magistrate.md) per 1,000 points
- [Shugengan](../unit/shugengan-general.md), [Gate Masters](../unit/gate-master.md), [Gate Keepers](../unit/gate-keeper.md) and [Strategists](../unit/strategist.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Jade Warriors](../unit/jade-warriors.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md) per 1,000 points may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md)
- 0-3 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Fire Rain Rocket Batteries](../unit/fire-rain-rocket-battery.md)
  - [Cathayan Grand Cannon](../unit/cathayan-grand-cannon.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Sky Lanterns](../unit/sky-lantern.md) and [Cathayan Sentinels](../unit/cathayan-sentinel.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on mercenaries drawn from the [Empire of Man](empire-of-man-army-list.md) army list in *Forces of Fantasy*:

- 0-1 [Captain of the Empire](../unit/captain-of-the-empire.md), [Master Mage](../unit/master-mage.md), [Priest of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md) or [Priest of Ulric](../unit/priest-of-ulric.md) per 1,000 points
- [State Troops](../unit/state-troops.md), [State Missile Troops](../unit/state-missile-troops.md), [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md), and [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Pistoliers](../unit/pistoliers.md) or [Outriders](../unit/outriders.md) per 1,000 points

*Note that mercenary units within a Jade Fleet army are not subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Gate Keeper in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

## Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Empire of Grand Cathay magic items.
