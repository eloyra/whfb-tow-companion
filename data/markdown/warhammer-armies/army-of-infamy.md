# Army of Infamy

These are supplementary and characterful army composition lists. They differ from the Grand Army in a number of ways, often omitting some of the units available to the Grand Army and instead focussing on a particular type of unit or upon those units favoured by an army drawn from a certain region. Army of Infamy composition lists allow you to create more specialised armies at the expense of a degree of tactical flexibility.
