# Types of Alliance

The vast majority of alliances between an army and an allied contingent are made between trusted allies. As such, most allied contingents follow the rules already given. However, sometimes an allied contingent may be noted as being [Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md) or even [Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md). In such cases, the allied contingent represents an alliance between two forces that might not usually be on the best of terms.
