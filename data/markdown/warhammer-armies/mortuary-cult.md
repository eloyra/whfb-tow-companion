# Mortuary Cult

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Tomb Kings of Khemri army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field an army of the Mortuary Cult in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ [High Priest](../unit/high-priest.md) or [Mortuary Priest](../unit/mortuary-priest.md)
- 0-1 [Tomb Prince](../unit/tomb-prince.md) or [Arch Necrotect](../unit/arch-necrotect.md) per 1,000 points
- [Necrotects](../unit/necrotect.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Skeleton Warriors](../unit/skeleton-warriors-tomb-kings.md), [Skeleton Archers](../unit/skeleton-archers.md), [Skeleton Skirmishers](../unit/skeleton-skirmishers.md), [Skeleton Horsemen](../unit/skeleton-horsemen.md), [Skeleton Horse Archers](../unit/skeleton-horse-archers.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Tomb Swarms](../unit/tomb-swarms.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- 0-1 unit of [Ushabti](../unit/ushabti.md) may be taken as a Core choice
- 0-1 unit of [Necroserpents](../unit/necroserpents.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ [Tomb Scorpion](../unit/tomb-scorpion.md) per 1,000 points
- [Ushabti](../unit/ushabti.md), [Venerable Ushabti](../unit/venerable-ushabti.md), [Tomb Swarms](../unit/tomb-swarms.md), [Carrion](../unit/carrion.md), [Sepulchral Stalkers](../unit/sepulchral-stalkers.md), [Necroserpents](../unit/necroserpents.md) and [Skeleton Chariots](../unit/skeleton-chariots.md)
- 0-1 [Necrolith Colossus](../unit/necrolith-colossus.md) or [Necrosphinx](../unit/necrosphinx.md) may be taken as a Special choice

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Necrolith Colossus](../unit/necrolith-colossus.md) and [Necrosphinx](../unit/necrosphinx.md)
- 0-2 [Screaming Skull Catapults](../unit/screaming-skull-catapult.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Casket of Souls](../unit/casket-of-souls.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Winged Warsphinx](../unit/winged-warsphinx.md) per 1,000 points

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Mortuary Priest in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit. This model replaces the ["Hold your Ground"](../characters/hold-your-ground.md) rule given in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook with the version given in the [Banner of the King](../special-rules/banner-of-the-king.md) special rule.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Tomb Kings of Khemri magic items lists.
