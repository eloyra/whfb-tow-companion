# Host of Talsyn

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Wood Elf Realms army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Host of Talsyn army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Forces of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Glade Lord](../unit/glade-lord.md) or [Spellweaver](../unit/spellweaver.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Treeman Ancient](../unit/treemen-ancient.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Warden of Talsyn](../unit/warden-of-talsyn.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Shadowdancer](../unit/shadowdancer.md) per unit of [Wardancers](../unit/wardancers.md) taken
- [Glade Captains](../unit/glade-captain.md), [Spellsingers](../unit/spellsinger.md) and [Branchwraiths](../unit/branchwraith.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Eternal Guard](../unit/eternal-guard.md)
- [Dryads](../unit/dryads.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Glade Guard](../unit/glade-guard.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Deepwood Scouts](../unit/deepwood-scouts.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Deepwood Scouts](../unit/deepwood-scouts.md), [Guardians of Talsyn](../unit/guardians-of-talsyn.md), [Wildwood Rangers](../unit/wildwood-rangers.md) and [Sisters of the Thorn](../unit/sisters-of-the-thorn.md)
- 0-2 units of [Tree Kin](../unit/tree-kin.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Treemen](../unit/treeman.md) and [Wardancers](../unit/wardancers.md)
- 0-3 [Great Eagles](../unit/great-eagle.md) per 1,000 points

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Glade Captain or Warden of Talsyn in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Banner with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Wood Elf Realms magic items.
