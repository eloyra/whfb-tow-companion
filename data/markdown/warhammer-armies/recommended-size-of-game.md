# Recommended Size of Game

Quite what size a game should be depends largely upon how long the players want their game to last. Armies of 2,000 points will give players familiar with the rules a game that will last a couple of hours. Games with smaller armies can be played in less time, whereas a game with much larger armies might occupy most of a day. Players are encouraged to play games with different points values to find the size of game they most enjoy.
