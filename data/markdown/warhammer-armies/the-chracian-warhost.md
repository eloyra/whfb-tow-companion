# The Chracian Warhost

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the High Elf Realms army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field an army of the Chracian Warhost in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Forces of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [High Elf Prince](../unit/prince.md) or [Archmage](../unit/archmage.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Chracian Chieftain](../unit/chracian-chieftain.md) or [Storm Weaver](../unit/storm-weaver.md) per 1,000 points
- [High Elf Nobles](../unit/noble.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Elven Spearmen](../unit/elven-spearmen.md), [Elven Archers](../unit/elven-archers.md) and [Chracian Woodsmen](../unit/chracian-woodsmen.md)
- 0-1 unit of [White Lions of Chrace](../unit/white-lions-of-chrace.md) per 1,000 points

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [White Lions of Chrace](../unit/white-lions-of-chrace.md), [Lion Chariots of Chrace](../unit/lion-chariot-of-chrace.md)
- 0-1 unit of [War Lions](../unit/war-lions.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Lion Guard](../unit/lion-guard.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Shadow Warriors](../unit/shadow-warriors.md) or [Sisters of Avelorn](../unit/sisters-of-avelorn.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Great Eagles](../unit/great-eagle.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Eagle-Claw Bolt Thrower](../unit/eagle-claw-bolt-thrower.md) per 1,000 points

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single High Elf Noble or Chracian Chieftain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Standard with no points limit.
