# Orc & Goblin Tribes Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Orc & Goblin Tribes. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Black Orc Warboss](../unit/black-orc-warboss.md), [Orc Warboss](../unit/orc-warboss.md) or [Orc Weirdnob](../unit/orc-weirdnob.md) per 1,000 points
- [Black Orc Bigbosses](../unit/black-orc-bigboss.md), [Orc Bigbosses](../unit/orc-bigboss.md), [Orc Weirdboys](../unit/orc-weirdboy.md), [Goblin Warbosses](../unit/goblin-warboss.md), [Goblin Bigbosses](../unit/goblin-bigboss.md), [Goblin Oddnobs](../unit/goblin-oddnob.md), [Goblin Oddgits](../unit/goblin-oddgit.md), [Night Goblin Warbosses](../unit/night-goblin-warboss.md), [Night Goblin Bigbosses](../unit/night-goblin-bigboss.md), [Night Goblin Oddnobs](../unit/night-goblin-oddnob.md) and [Night Goblin Oddgits](../unit/night-goblin-oddgit.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Orc Mobs](../unit/orc-mob.md), [Goblin Mobs](../unit/goblin-mob.md), [Snotling Mobs](../unit/snotling-mob.md), [Goblin Spider Rider Mobs](../unit/goblin-spider-rider-mob.md) and [Goblin Wolf Rider Mobs](../unit/goblin-wolf-rider-mob.md)
- 0-1 [Night Goblin Mobs](../unit/night-goblin-mob.md) per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- 0-1 [Night Goblin Squig Herd](../unit/night-goblin-squig-herd.md) per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- If your General is a Black Orc Boss, 0-1 [Black Orc Mob](../unit/black-orc-mob.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Black Orc Mobs](../unit/black-orc-mob.md), [Common Troll Mobs](../unit/common-troll-mob.md), [River Troll Mobs](../unit/river-troll-mob.md), [Stone Troll Mobs](../unit/stone-troll-mob.md), [Orc Boar Boy Mobs](../unit/orc-boar-boy-mob.md), [Orc Boar Chariots](../unit/orc-boar-chariot.md), [Goblin Wolf Chariots](../unit/goblin-wolf-chariot.md) and [Snotling Pump Wagons](../unit/snotling-pump-wagon.md)
- 0-1 [Night Goblin Squig Hopper Mob](../unit/night-goblin-squig-hopper-mob.md) per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- 0-2 [Goblin Bolt Throwas](../unit/goblin-bolt-throwa.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Arachnarok Spiders](../unit/arachnarok-spider.md) and [Giants](../unit/giant.md)
- 0-1 [Mangler Squig](../unit/mangler-squigs.md) per Night Goblin Chief or Night Goblin Shaman taken
- 0-1 [Goblin Rock Lobber](../unit/goblin-rock-lobber.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Doom Diver Catapult](../unit/doom-diver-catapult.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Troll Hag](../unit/troll-hag.md) if army includes one or more Troll Mobs

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- 0-1 [Bonegrinder Giant](../unit/bonegrinder-giant.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Orc & Goblin Tribes Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Beastmen Brayherds](../army/beastmen-brayherds.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Tomb Kings of Khemri](../army/tomb-kings-of-khemri.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Bigboss (Orc, Black Orc, Goblin or Night Goblin) in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
