# Tomb Kings of Khemri Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Tomb Kings of Khemri. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ [Tomb King](../unit/tomb-king.md) or [Tomb Prince](../unit/tomb-prince.md)
- 1+ [High Priest](../unit/high-priest.md) or [Mortuary Priest](../unit/mortuary-priest.md)
- 0-1 [Tomb King](../unit/tomb-king.md) or [High Priest](../unit/high-priest.md) per 1,000 points
- [Royal Heralds](../unit/royal-herald.md) and [Necrotects](../unit/necrotect.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Skeleton Warriors](../unit/skeleton-warriors-tomb-kings.md) and/or [Skeleton Archers](../unit/skeleton-archers.md)
- [Skeleton Skirmishers](../unit/skeleton-skirmishers.md), [Tomb Swarms](../unit/tomb-swarms.md), [Skeleton Horsemen](../unit/skeleton-horsemen.md), [Skeleton Horse Archers](../unit/skeleton-horse-archers.md) and [Skeleton Chariots](../unit/skeleton-chariots.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Tomb Guard](../unit/tomb-guard.md) or [Sepulchral Stalkers](../unit/sepulchral-stalkers.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Tomb Guard](../unit/tomb-guard.md), [Ushabti](../unit/ushabti.md), [Sepulchral Stalkers](../unit/sepulchral-stalkers.md), [Carrion](../unit/carrion.md) and [Necropolis Knights](../unit/necropolis-knights.md)
- 0-3 [Tomb Scorpions](../unit/tomb-scorpion.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Khemrian Warsphinx](../unit/khemrian-warsphinx.md) (not counting character mounts) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Necrolith Colossus](../unit/necrolith-colossus.md), [Necrosphinx](../unit/necrosphinx.md)
- 0-2 [Screaming Skull Catapults](../unit/screaming-skull-catapult.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Casket of Souls](../unit/casket-of-souls.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Winged Warsphinx](../unit/winged-warsphinx.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Tomb Kings of Khemri Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Orc & Goblin Tribes](../army/orc-and-goblin-tribes.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Beastmen Brayherds](../army/beastmen-brayherds.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Royal Herald in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
