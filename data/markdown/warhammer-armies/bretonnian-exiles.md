# Bretonnian Exiles

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Kingdom of Bretonnia army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field an army of Bretonnian Exiles in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Force of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Baron](../unit/baron.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Damsel](../unit/damsel.md)
- [Paladins](../unit/paladin.md), [Outcast Wizards](../unit/outcast-wizard.md) and [Sergeants-at-Arms](../unit/sergeant-at-arms.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ unit of [Knights of the Realm on Foot](../unit/knights-of-the-realm-on-foot.md) and/or [Mounted Knights of the Realm](../unit/mounted-knights-of-the-realm.md)
- 1+ unit of [Yeoman Guard](../unit/yeomen-guard.md) and/or [Peasant Bowmen](../unit/peasant-bowmen.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Knights Errant](../unit/knights-errant.md) per 1,000 points
- [Men-at-Arms](../unit/men-at-arms.md) and [Mounted Yeomen](../unit/mounted-yeomen.md)

### Special

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Squires](../unit/squires.md) and [Pegasus Knights](../unit/pegasus-knights.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Battle Pilgrims](../unit/battle-pilgrims.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Questing Knights](../unit/questing-knights.md) and [Border Princes Brigands](../unit/border-princes-brigands.md)
- 0-1 [Field Trebuchet](../unit/field-trebuchet.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Border Princes Bombard](../unit/border-princes-bombard.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md) and [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Paladin in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for no additional cost. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Kingdom of Bretonnia magic items lists.
