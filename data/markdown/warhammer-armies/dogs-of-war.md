# Dogs of War

> *This rule is not an officially listed rule in the* Warhammer: The Old World *rulebook, but serves as a collection of units across various Arcane Journals that have the Dogs of War rule.*

The following units may be taken as [Mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md) in one or more Grand Army composition lists:
