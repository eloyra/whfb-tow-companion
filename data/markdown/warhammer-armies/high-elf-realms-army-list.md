# High Elf Realms Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the High Elf Realms. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [High Elf Prince](../unit/prince.md) or [Archmage](../unit/archmage.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Dragon Mage](../unit/dragon-mage.md) or [Handmaiden of the Everqueen](../unit/handmaiden-of-the-everqueen.md) per 1,000 points
- [High Elf Nobles](../unit/noble.md) and [Mages](../unit/mage.md)
- 0-1 [Storm Weaver](../unit/storm-weaver.md) per 1,000 points

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Elven Spearmen](../unit/elven-spearmen.md), [Elven Archers](../unit/elven-archers.md), [Lothern Sea Guard](../unit/lothern-sea-guard.md), [Ellyrian Reavers](../unit/ellyrian-reavers.md) and [Silver Helms](../unit/silver-helms.md)
- If your General is a [Handmaiden of the Everqueen](../unit/handmaiden-of-the-everqueen.md), 0-1 unit of [Sisters of Avelorn](../unit/sisters-of-avelorn.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [White Lions of Chrace](../unit/white-lions-of-chrace.md), [Swordmasters of Hoeth](../unit/swordmasters-of-hoeth.md), [Phoenix Guard](../unit/phoenix-guard.md), [Shadow Warriors](../unit/shadow-warriors.md) and [Tiranoc Chariots](../unit/tiranoc-chariot.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Dragon Princes of Caledor](../unit/dragon-princes.md) per 1,000 points
- If your General has the [Chracian Hunter Elven Honour](../magic-item/chracian-hunter.md), 0-1 [Lion Chariot of Chrace](../unit/lion-chariot-of-chrace.md) may be taken as a Special choice per 1,000 points
- If your General has the [Sea Guard Elven Honour](../magic-item/sea-guard.md), 0-1 [Lothern Skycutter](../unit/lothern-skycutter.md) may be taken as a Special choice

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Sisters of Avelorn](../unit/sisters-of-avelorn.md), [Lion Chariots of Chrace](../unit/lion-chariot-of-chrace.md) and [Lothern Skycutters](../unit/lothern-skycutter.md)
- 0-1 [Flamespyre](../unit/flamespyre-phoenix.md) or [Frostheart Phoenix](../unit/frostheart-phoenix.md) (not counting character mounts) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Great Eagles](../unit/great-eagle.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Eagle-Claw Bolt Throwers](../unit/eagle-claw-bolt-thrower.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [War Lions](../unit/war-lions.md) (if list includes one or more units of [White Lions of Chrace](../unit/white-lions-of-chrace.md))
- 0-1 [Merwyrm](../unit/merwyrm.md) (if list includes one or more units of [Lothern Sea Guard](../unit/lothern-sea-guard.md))

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any High Elf Realms Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Empire of Man](../army/empire-of-man.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Grand Cathay](grand-cathay-army-list.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Kingdom of Bretonnia](../army/kingdom-of-bretonnia.md)
  - [Wood Elf Realms](../army/wood-elf-realms.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single High Elf Noble in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Standard with no points limit.
