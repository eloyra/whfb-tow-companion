# Troll Horde

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Orc & Goblin Tribes army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Troll Horde army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Orc Warboss](../unit/orc-warboss.md) or [Orc Weirdnob](../unit/orc-weirdnob.md) per 1,000 points
- [Orc Bigbosses](../unit/orc-bigboss.md), [Orc Weirdboys](../unit/orc-weirdboy.md), [Goblin Warbosses](../unit/goblin-warboss.md), [Goblin Bigbosses](../unit/goblin-bigboss.md), [Goblin Oddnobs](../unit/goblin-oddnob.md), [Goblin Oddgits](../unit/goblin-oddgit.md) and [Troll Hags](../unit/troll-hag.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army’s points value must be spent on:

- 1 Troll Mob per 1,000 points
- 0-1 additional Troll Mob may be taken as a Core choice per [Troll Hag](../unit/troll-hag.md) taken
- [Orc Mobs](../unit/orc-mob.md), [Goblin Mobs](../unit/goblin-mob.md), [Goblin Spider Rider Mobs](../unit/goblin-spider-rider-mob.md) and [Goblin Wolf Rider Mobs](../unit/goblin-wolf-rider-mob.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Common Troll Mob](../unit/common-troll-mob.md), [River Troll Mob](../unit/river-troll-mob.md), [Stone Troll Mob](../unit/stone-troll-mob.md) and [Goblin Wolf Chariots](../unit/goblin-wolf-chariot.md)
- 0-1 [Orc Boar Boy Mobs](../unit/orc-boar-boy-mob.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Orc Boar Chariots](../unit/orc-boar-chariot.md) per 1,000 points

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Giants](../unit/giant.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- 0-1 [Bonegrinder Giant](../unit/bonegrinder-giant.md)

*Note that mercenary units are subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Bigboss (Orc or Goblin) in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Orc & Goblin Tribes magic items lists.
