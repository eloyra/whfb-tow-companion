# Expeditionary Force

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Dwarfen Mountain Holds army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Expeditionary Force in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Force of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 1+ [Engineer](../unit/engineer.md) or [Engineer Sapper](../unit/engineer-sapper.md)
- 0-1 [Daemon Slayer](../unit/daemon-slayer.md) or [Dragon Slayer](../unit/dragon-slayer.md)
- [Thanes](../unit/thane.md) and [Runesmiths](../unit/runesmith.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Dwarfen Warriors](../unit/dwarf-warriors.md), [Thunderers](../unit/thunderers.md) and [Dwarf Carts](../unit/dwarf-cart.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Rangers](../unit/rangers.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Scout Gyrocopters](../unit/scout-gyrocopter.md) may be taken as a Core choice per 1,000 points

### Special

Up to 50% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- [Miners](../unit/miners.md), [Scout Gyrocopters](../unit/scout-gyrocopter.md) and [Gyrocopters](../unit/gyrocopter.md)
- 0-3 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Bolt Thrower](../unit/bolt-thrower.md)
  - [Grudge Thrower](../unit/grudge-thrower.md)
  - [Cannon](../unit/cannon.md)

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army’s points value may be spent on:

- [Gyrobombers](../unit/gyrobomber.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Slayers](../unit/slayers.md)
- 0-2 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Organ Gun](../unit/organ-gun.md)
  - [Flame Cannon](../unit/flame-cannon.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Imperial Dwarf Mercenaries](../unit/imperial-dwarf-mercenaries.md)
- 0-2 [Doomseekers](../unit/doomseeker.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Goblin-hewer](../unit/goblin-hewer.md) per 1,000 points
- [Prince Ulther's Dragon Company](../unit/prince-ulthers-dragon-company.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Thane or Engineer in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on Weapon, Armour and Talismanic runes, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase Standard runes with no points limit.

### Runic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase runes may do as [described](../magic-items/runic-items.md).
