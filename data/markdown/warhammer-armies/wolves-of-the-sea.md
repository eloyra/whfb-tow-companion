# Wolves of the Sea

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Warriors of Chaos army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Wolves of the Sea army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Chaos Lord](../unit/chaos-lord.md), [Daemon Prince](../unit/daemon-prince-warriors-of-chaos.md) or [Sorcerer Lord](../unit/sorcerer-lord.md)
- 0-1 [Exalted Champion](../unit/exalted-champion.md) or [Exalted Sorcerer](../unit/exalted-sorcerer.md) per 1,000 points
- [Aspiring Champions](../unit/aspiring-champion.md) and [Marauder Tribe Chieftains](../unit/marauder-tribe-chieftain.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army’s points value must be spent on:

- [Chaos Marauders](../unit/chaos-marauders.md), [Marauder Horsemen](../unit/marauder-horsemen.md), [Marauder Tribe Berserkers](../unit/marauder-tribe-berserkers.md) and [Chaos Warhounds](../unit/chaos-warhounds.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Chaos Warriors](../unit/chaos-warriors.md), [Chaos Chariots](../unit/chaos-chariot.md), [Skin Wolves](../unit/skin-wolves.md), [Marauder Tribe Huscarls](../unit/marauder-tribe-huscarls.md) and [Chaos Spawn](../unit/chaos-spawn.md)

### Rare

Up to 33% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Chaos Ogres](../unit/chaos-ogres.md), [Chaos Trolls](../unit/chaos-trolls.md) and [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md)
- 0-1 [Gigantic Spawn of Chaos](../unit/gigantic-spawn-of-chaos.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Dragon Ogre Shaggoth](../unit/dragon-ogre-shaggoth.md) per unit of [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md)
- 0-1 [Chaos Giant](../unit/chaos-giant.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Gor Herds](../unit/gor-herd.md)
- [Ungor Herds](../unit/ungor-herd.md)

*Note that mercenary units are subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Aspiring Champion or Marauder Tribe Chieftain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Warriors of Chaos magic items.
