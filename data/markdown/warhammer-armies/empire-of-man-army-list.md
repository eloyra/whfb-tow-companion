# Empire of Man Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Empire of Man. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [General of the Empire](../unit/general-of-the-empire.md) or [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md) or [High Priest of Ulric](../unit/high-priest-of-ulric.md) per 1,000 points
- [Captains of the Empire](../unit/captain-of-the-empire.md), [Wizard Lords](../unit/wizard-lord.md), [Master Mages](../unit/master-mage.md), [Chapter Masters](../unit/chapter-master.md), [Witch Hunters](../unit/witch-hunter.md), [Priests of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md), [Priests of Ulric](../unit/priest-of-ulric.md) and [Empire Engineers](../unit/empire-engineer.md)
- 0-1 [War Altar of Sigmar](../unit/war-altar-of-sigmar.md) (taken as a mount for a [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md))
- 0-1 [Harbinger of Doom](../unit/harbinger-of-doom.md) includes one or more units of [Flagellants](../unit/flagellants.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Veteran State Troops](../unit/veteran-state-troops.md) per 1,000 points
- [State Troops](../unit/state-troops.md), [State Missile Troops](../unit/state-missile-troops.md), [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md), [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md) and [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) per 1,000 points
- [Empire Greatswords](../unit/empire-greatswords.md), [Pistoliers](../unit/pistoliers.md), [Outriders](../unit/outriders.md) and [Demigryph Knights](../unit/demigryph-knights.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Teutogen Guard](../unit/teutogen-guard.md) per [Warrior Priest of Ulric](../unit/priest-of-ulric.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Empire Road Wardens](../unit/empire-road-wardens.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-3 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Great Cannon](../unit/great-cannon.md)
  - [Mortar](../unit/mortar.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Flagellants](../unit/flagellants.md)
- 0-1 war machine chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Helblaster Volley Gun](../unit/helblaster-volley-gun.md)
  - [Helstorm Rocket Battery](../unit/helstorm-rocket-battery.md)
- 0-1 [Steam Tank](../unit/empire-steam-tank.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [War Wagon](../unit/empire-war-wagon.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)
- [Prince Ulther's Dragon Company](../unit/prince-ulthers-dragon-company.md)
- 0-3 [Doomseekers](../unit/doomseeker.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Imperial Dwarf Mercenaries](../unit/imperial-dwarf-mercenaries.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Imperial Ogres](../unit/imperial-ogres.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Empire of Man Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Dwarfen Mountain Holds](../army/dwarfen-mountain-holds.md)
  - [Grand Cathay](grand-cathay-army-list.md)
  - [Kingdom of Bretonnia](../army/kingdom-of-bretonnia.md)
  - [Wood Elf Realms](../army/wood-elf-realms.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Captain of the Empire in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
