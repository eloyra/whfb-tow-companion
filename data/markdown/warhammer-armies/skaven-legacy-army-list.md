# Skaven Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Skaven. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Skaven Warlord](../unit/skaven-warlord.md) or [Grey Seer](../unit/grey-seer.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Clan Skryre Warlock Engineer](../unit/warlock-engineer.md), [Clan Eshin Master Assassin](../unit/master-assassin.md) or [Clan Pestilens Plague Priest](../unit/plague-priest.md) per 1,000 points
- [Skaven Chieftains](../unit/skaven-chieftain.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Stormvermin](../unit/stormvermin.md) per 1,000 points
- 1+ unit of [Clanrats](../unit/clanrats.md) per 1,000 points
- [Night Runners](../unit/night-runners.md), [Giant Rats](../unit/giant-rats.md) and [Rat Swarms](../unit/rat-swarms.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Gutter Runners](../unit/gutter-runners.md)
- 0-2 units of [Rat Ogres](../unit/rat-ogres.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 unit of [Plague Monks](../unit/plague-monks.md) per [Clan Pestilens Plague Priest](../unit/plague-priest.md) taken
- 0-1 unit of [Warplock Jezzails](../unit/warplock-jezzails.md) per [Clan Skryre Warlock Engineer](../unit/warlock-engineer.md) taken
- 0-1 unit of [Poisoned Wind Globadiers](../unit/poisoned-wind-globadiers.md) per [Clan Skryre Warlock Engineer](../unit/warlock-engineer.md) taken

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Hell Pit Abomination](../unit/hell-pit-abomination.md) per 1,000 points
- If your army includes one or more [Clan Skryre Warlock Engineer](../unit/warlock-engineer.md), 0-1 of the following may be taken per 1,000 points:

  - [Doomwheel](../unit/doomwheel.md)
  - [Warp Lightning Cannon](../unit/warp-lightning-cannon.md)
- If your army includes one or more [Clan Pestilens Plague Priest](../unit/plague-priest.md), 0-1 of the following may be taken per 1,000 points:

  - [Plagueclaw Catapult](../unit/plagueclaw-catapult.md)
  - Unit of [Plague Censer Bearers](../unit/plague-censer-bearers.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Skaven Chieftain in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
