# Wild Herd

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Beastmen Brayherds army list found in *Ravening Hordes*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a Wild Herd army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Ravening Hordes*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Beastlord](../unit/beastlord.md) or [Great Bray-Shaman](../unit/great-bray-shaman.md) per 1,000 points
- [Wargors](../unit/wargor.md), [Bray-Shamans](../unit/bray-shaman.md) and [Centigor Chieftains](../unit/warhoof.md)

### Core

At least 33% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ [Primal Warherd](../unit/primal-warherd.md)
- 0-1 [Tuskgor Chariot](../unit/tuskgor-chariot.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Centigor Herd](../unit/centigor-herd.md) may be taken as a Core choice per [Centigor Chieftain](../unit/warhoof.md) taken
- [Gor Herds](../unit/gor-herd.md), [Ungor Herds](../unit/ungor-herd.md), [Chaos Warhounds](../unit/chaos-warhounds.md), [Primal Warhounds](../unit/primal-warhounds.md) and [Razorgor Herds](../unit/razorgor-herd.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 unit of [Dragon Ogres](../unit/dragon-ogres.md)
- [Warped Gors](../unit/warped-gors.md), [Bestigor Herds](../unit/bestigor-herd.md), [Harpies](../unit/harpies.md), [Centigor Herds](../unit/centigor-herd.md), [Tuskgor Chariots](../unit/tuskgor-chariot.md) and [Razorgor Chariots](../unit/razorgor-chariot.md)
- 0-1 [Cockatrice](../unit/cockatrice.md) or [Preyton](../unit/preyton.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Herdstone](../special-rules/herdstones.md)

### Rare

Up to 32% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Dragon Ogre Shaggoths](../unit/dragon-ogre-shaggoth.md), [Jabberslythes](../unit/jabberslythe.md), [Cygors](../unit/cygor.md), and [Chaos Giants](../unit/chaos-giant.md)
- [Chaos Spawn](../unit/chaos-spawn.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Wargor in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single Magic Banner with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase Common or Beastmen Brayherds magic items.
