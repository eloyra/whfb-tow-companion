# City-state of Nuln

This Army of Infamy composition list is designed to be used with the Empire of Man army list found in *Warhammer: the Old World* – *Forces of Fantasy*, and alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

If you wish to field a City-state of Nuln army in your games of *Warhammer: the Old World*, you may write your muster list using the Army of Infamy composition list below instead of the Grand Army composition list found in *Force of Fantasy*.

## Army of Infamy Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [General of the Empire](../unit/general-of-the-empire.md) or [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md) per 1,000 points
- [Captains of the Empire](../unit/captain-of-the-empire.md), [Master Mages](../unit/master-mage.md), [Witch Hunters](../unit/witch-hunter.md), [Priests of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md) and [Empire Engineers](../unit/empire-engineer.md)
- 0-1 [War Altar of Sigmar](../unit/war-altar-of-sigmar.md) (taken as a mount for a [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md))

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- 1+ regiment of [Nuln State Troops](../special-rules/nuln-state-troops.md) or [Nuln Veteran State Troops](../unit/nuln-veteran-state-troops.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Outriders](../unit/outriders.md) per 1,000 points
- [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md) and [Empire Archers](../unit/empire-archers.md)

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Empire Greatswords](../unit/empire-greatswords.md), [Pistoliers](../unit/pistoliers.md), [Outriders](../unit/outriders.md), [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md) and [War Wagons](../unit/empire-war-wagon.md)
- 0-3 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Great Cannon](../unit/great-cannon.md)
  - [Mortar](../unit/mortar.md)

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Empire Road Wardens](../unit/empire-road-wardens.md)
- 0-2 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Helblaster Volley Gun](../unit/helblaster-volley-gun.md)
  - [Helstorm Rocket Battery](../unit/helstorm-rocket-battery.md)
- 0-1 [Steam Tank](../unit/empire-steam-tank.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Prince Ulther's Dragon Company](../unit/prince-ulthers-dragon-company.md)
- [Imperial Ogres](../unit/imperial-ogres.md) and [Imperial Dwarf](../unit/imperial-dwarf-mercenaries.md)
- 0-2 [Doomseekers](../unit/doomseeker.md) per 1,000 points

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Captain of the Empire or Empire Engineer in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Magic Items

Any models within this army that have the option to purchase magic items may purchase magic items from the Common or Empire of Man magic items lists.
