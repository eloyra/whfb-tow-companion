# Grand Cathay Army List

This section updates the Grand Army composition list for Grand Cathay, found in *Arcane Journal - Armies of Grand Cathay*. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This updated Grand Army composition list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 Cathayan Dragon per 1,000 points
- 0-1 [Shugengan Lord](../unit/shugengan-lord.md), [Gate Master](../unit/gate-master.md), [Lord Magistrate](../unit/lord-magistrate.md) or [Supreme Astromancer](../unit/supreme-astromancer.md) per 1,000 points
- [Shugengan](../unit/shugengan-general.md), [Gate Keepers](../unit/gate-keeper.md), [Strategists](../unit/strategist.md) and [Astromancers](../unit/astromancer.md)

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Jade Warriors](../unit/jade-warriors.md) and [Peasant Levy](../unit/peasant-levy.md)
- If your General is a [Gate Master](../unit/gate-master.md) or [Gate Keeper](../unit/gate-keeper.md), 0-1 unit of [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md), [Crane Gunner Teams](../unit/crane-gunner-teams.md) and [Iron Hail Gunners](../unit/iron-hail-gunners.md)
- 0-2 war machines chosen from the following list per 1,000 points:

  - [Fire Rain Rocket Batteries](../unit/fire-rain-rocket-battery.md)
  - [Cathayan Grand Cannon](../unit/cathayan-grand-cannon.md)
- If your General is a [Shugengan Lord](../unit/shugengan-lord.md) or [Shugengan](../unit/shugengan-general.md), 0-1 [Cathayan Sentinel](../unit/cathayan-sentinel.md) may be taken as a Special choice
- If your General is a [Lord Magistrate](../unit/lord-magistrate.md) or [Strategist](../unit/strategist.md), 0-1 [Sky Lantern](../unit/sky-lantern.md) may be taken as a Special choice

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Sky Lanterns](../unit/sky-lantern.md) and [Cathayan Sentinels](../unit/cathayan-sentinel.md)

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from:

- Any Empire of Grand Cathay Army of Infamy composition list
- One of the following Grand Army composition lists:

  - [Dwarfen Mountain Holds](../army/dwarfen-mountain-holds.md)
  - [Empire of Man](empire-of-man-army-list.md)
  - [Kingdom of Bretonnia](../army/kingdom-of-bretonnia.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [Wood Elf Realms](../army/wood-elf-realms.md) ([Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md))
  - [High Elf Realms](high-elf-realms-army-list.md)

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Gate Keeper in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.

### Cathayan Allies

In addition to the Allies listed in Forces of Fantasy, armies made using the Dwarfen Mountain Holds, Empire of Man or Kingdom of Bretonnia Grand Army composition lists may include an Allied Contingent drawn from the Grand Cathay Grand Army composition list.

Armies made using the Wood Elf Realms or High Elf Realms Grand Army composition lists may include an Allied Contingent drawn from the Grand Cathay Grand Army composition list as [Suspicious](suspicious-allied-contingents.md) allies.
