# Dark Elves Legacy Army List

This section details the Grand Army composition list for the Dark Elves. This is the default army composition list for this faction. Using this army composition list to write your muster list will give you the widest variety of units to choose from and create a broad and balanced force.

This army list is intended for use alongside the [Forming Units](https://tow.whfb.app/forming-units) and [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies) sections of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Grand Army Composition List

### Characters

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- 0-1 [Dark Elf Dreadlord](../unit/dark-elf-dreadlord.md) or [Supreme Sorceress](../unit/supreme-sorceress.md) per 1,000 points
- [Dark Elf Masters](../unit/dark-elf-master.md), [Sorceresses](../unit/sorceress.md), [High Beastmasters](../unit/high-beastmaster.md), [Death Hags](../unit/death-hag.md) and [Khainite Assassins](../unit/khainite-assassin.md)
- 0-1 [Cauldron of Blood](../unit/cauldron-of-blood.md) per 1,000 points (taken as a mount for a [Death Hag](../unit/death-hag.md))

### Core

At least 25% of your army's points value must be spent on:

- [Dark Elf Warriors](../unit/dark-elf-warriors.md), [Repeater Crossbowmen](../unit/repeater-crossbowmen.md), [Black Ark Corsairs](../unit/black-ark-corsairs.md) and [Dark Riders](../unit/dark-riders.md)
- If your army includes one or more [Death Hags](../unit/death-hag.md), 0-1 unit of [Witch Elves](../unit/witch-elves.md) may be taken as a Core choice

### Special

Up to 50% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Har Ganeth Executioners](../unit/har-ganeth-executioners.md), [Witch Elves](../unit/witch-elves.md), [Dark Elf Shades](../unit/dark-elf-shades.md) and [Harpies](../unit/harpies.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Black Guard of Naggarond](../unit/black-guard-of-naggarond.md) per Dark Elf Noble taken
- 0-1 unit of [Cold One Knights](../unit/cold-one-knights.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Scourgerunner Chariots](../unit/scourgerunner-chariot.md) or [Cold One Chariots](../unit/cold-one-chariot.md) per 1,000 points
- If your General is a [High Beastmaster](../unit/high-beastmaster.md), 0-1 [War Hydra](../unit/war-hydra.md) or [Kharibdyss](../unit/kharibdyss.md) may be taken as a Special choice

### Rare

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on:

- [Sisters of Slaughter](../unit/sisters-of-slaughter.md), [Bloodwrack Shrines](../unit/bloodwrack-shrine.md), [War Hydras](../unit/war-hydra.md), [Kharibdyss](../unit/kharibdyss.md) and [Bloodwrack Medusas](../unit/bloodwrack-medusa.md)
- 0-1 unit of [Doomfire Warlocks](../unit/doomfire-warlocks.md) per 1,000 points
- 0-2 [Reaper Bolt Throwers](../unit/reaper-bolt-thrower.md) per 1,000 points

### Mercenaries

Up to 20% of your army's points value may be spent on [mercenaries](mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md), including:

- [Badlands Ogre Bulls](../unit/badlands-ogre-bulls.md)

*Note that mercenary units drawn from another army list gain the*  [Mercenaries](../special-rules/mercenaries.md)  *special rule and become subject to the*  [Misbehaving Mercenaries](misbehaving-mercenaries.md)  *rules.*

### Allies

Up to 25% of your army's points value may be spent on a single [allied contingent](creating-an-allied-contingent.md) drawn from one of the following Grand Army composition lists:

- [Warriors of Chaos](../army/warriors-of-chaos.md) ([Uneasy](uneasy-allied-contingents.md))

### Battle Standard Bearer

A single Dark Elf Master in your army may be upgraded to be your [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) for +25 points. In addition to their usual allowance of points to spend on magic items, a Battle Standard Bearer can purchase a single magic standard with no points limit.
