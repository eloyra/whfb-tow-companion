# Evil Sun Shinin'

Spell Level 4

*The sun is out and there's an enemy army over there – it's a great day for a battle! 'Ave at it Boyz!*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, friendly units that are within the caster's Command range may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1, and improve the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of their weapons by 1.
