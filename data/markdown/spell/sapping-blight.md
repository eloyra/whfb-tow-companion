# Sapping Blight

Spell Level 6

*As roots draw water from the earth, so too can the mages of Athel Loren sap the lifeforce from their foes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -D3 modifier to its Strength characteristic (to a minimum of 1). This spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat. If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Hex previously cast on the target unit immediately expire.
