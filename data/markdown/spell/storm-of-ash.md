# Storm of Ash

Signature Spell

*The sorcerer summons forth a hellish storm of choking hot ash, scalding and blinding anything and anyone unfortunate enough to be caught in its path.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to any rolls To Hit made whilst within 9" of the caster's model (rolls of a natural 6 are unaffected).
