# Raise Dead

Signature Spell

*One skilled in the dark arts of Necromancy can awaken mortal remains to reinforce the Undead legions under their control.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Place a unit of 2D3 Risen Zombies on the battlefield anywhere completely within 12" of this model, but not within 1" of any enemy models. This unit cannot declare a [charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) during the turn in which it was raised:

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Risen Zombie | 4 | 2 | 0 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 2 |

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)   
 **Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm  
 **Unit Size:** 2D3  
 **Equipment:** Hand weapons  
 **Special Rules:** [Necromantic Undead](../special-rules/necromantic-undead.md), [Regeneration (6+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md), [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md), [The Newly Dead](../special-rules/the-newly-dead.md)

*Note that Risen Zombies are not worth any*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) *.*
