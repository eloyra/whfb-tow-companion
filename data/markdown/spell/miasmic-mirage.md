# Miasmic Mirage

Spell Level 6

*Around the feet of the enemy, the earth itself shifts and blurs, causing warriors to trip and stumble upon half-imagined obstacles.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -2 modifier to its Movement characteristic (to a minimum of 1) and cannot march or charge. If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Hex previously cast on the target unit immediately expire.
