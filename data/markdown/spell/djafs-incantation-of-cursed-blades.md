# Djaf's Incantation of Cursed Blades

Signature Spell

*As the Liche Priest utters this ancient mantra, the weapons of the Nehekharan warriors gathered about them become imbued with the essence of Djaf, the jackal-headed god of the dead, who hungers for the souls of the living above all things.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, any friendly unit that has the [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md) special rule and that is within the caster's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1.
