# Vanhal's Danse Macabre

Signature Spell

*Filled with magical energy, the Undead jerk forwards on the attack with tireless and unnatural speed.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+/12+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

If this spell is cast with a casting result of 8 or more, the target friendly unit gains a +D3 modifier to one of the following characteristics (to a maximum of 10). If this spell is cast with a casting result of 12 or more, the target friendly unit gains a +D3 modifier to two of the following characteristics (to a maximum of 10). This spell lasts until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase.

- Movement
- Weapon Skill
- Initiative
