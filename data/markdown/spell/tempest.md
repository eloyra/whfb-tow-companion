# Tempest

Spell Level 3

*The High Mage summons forth a tempest of rain and sleet, the centre of which is all but impenetrable.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is within 12" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template does not move and is treated as [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). Whilst within 6" of the template, enemy units treat open ground as difficult terrain and difficult terrain as dangerous terrain.
