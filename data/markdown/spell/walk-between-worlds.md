# Walk Between Worlds

Spell Level 1

*The High Mage causes their allies to drift in and out of reality, enabling them to pass through terrain as if it was not there.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the caster and any unit they have joined gain the [Ethereal](../special-rules/ethereal.md) and [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rules.
