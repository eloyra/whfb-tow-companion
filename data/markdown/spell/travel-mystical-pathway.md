# Travel Mystical Pathway

Spell Level 6

*Tapping into the power of the Geomantic Web, the Elementalist opens a portal through which their allies can travel.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 9" |

If the target friendly unit is not fleeing and has not already moved during this [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase), you may immediately remove it from the battlefield and replace it anywhere completely within 12" of its original location, but not within 6" of any enemy models. The target friendly unit cannot move again during this Movement phase.
