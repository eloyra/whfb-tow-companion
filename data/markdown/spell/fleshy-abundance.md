# Fleshy Abundance

Signature Spell

*Great wobbling mounds of fat spill out from beneath the armour of the Sorcerer and their comrades.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

[Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) only. [Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this spell is in play, the caster and any unit they have joined gain a +1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic (to a maximum of 7).
