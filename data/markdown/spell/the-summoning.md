# The Summoning

Signature Spell

*From the Realm of Chaos, the Daemonologist summons forth daemonic beasts to strike at their foes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

The target enemy unit suffers 2D6 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.
