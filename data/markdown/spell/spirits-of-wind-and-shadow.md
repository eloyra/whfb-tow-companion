# Spirits of Wind & Shadow

Signature Spell

*Elemental spirits descend upon the foe, enveloping them in a whirlwind of flickering shapes and ghostly gales.*
*As they try to advance, those afflicted find themselves waylaid by the grasping talons of unseen spirits and buffeted by spectral winds.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit becomes subject to the [Random Movement (D6)](../special-rules/random-movement.md) special rule.
