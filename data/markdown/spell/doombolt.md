# Doombolt

Signature Spell

*At the Dark Mage's bidding, a bolt of black lightning tears from the heavens and stabs at the earth.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 24" |

Place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target enemy unit. Any enemy model whose base lies underneath the [template](../general-principles/templates.md) risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
