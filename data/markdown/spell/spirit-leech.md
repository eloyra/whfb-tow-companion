# Spirit Leech

Spell Level 6

*Chill winds gust and long shadows gather, sapping the enemy's will and sowing seeds of dread.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

Until the end of this turn, the target enemy unit suffers a -2 modifier to its Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2) and cannot use their General's [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) special rule. This spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat.
