# Shield of Saphery

Spell Level 6

*White light bursts forth from the High Mage's outstretched hands, clinging to armour and arcing overhead to form a protective barrier around their allies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

Until the end of this turn, the target friendly unit gains a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Enchantment previously cast on the target unit immediately expire.
