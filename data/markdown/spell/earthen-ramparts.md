# Earthen Ramparts

Spell Level 4

*At the Elementalist's summoning, mighty earthen ramparts rise up about their allies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target friendly unit gains a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and counts as being behind a defended [low linear obstacle](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md) if charged. However, whilst this spell is in play the target unit cannot [march](../movement-in-detail/marching.md) or [charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md).
