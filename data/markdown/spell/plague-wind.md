# Plague Wind

Signature Spell

*A debilitating cloud of noxious air forms, swirling around the battlefield and infecting all it touches.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

[Daemons of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md) only. [Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is within 12" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). The template moves D6" in a random direction during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any unit (friend or foe, but not including Daemons of Nurgle) the moving template touches or moves over suffers a -1 modifier to its Toughness characteristic for the remainder of the turn.
