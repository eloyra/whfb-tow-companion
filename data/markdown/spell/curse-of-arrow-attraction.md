# Curse of Arrow Attraction

Spell Level 2

*With a cry and a flourish, the Battle Mage curses an enemy, causing missiles to seek them out with almost sentient awareness.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | 21" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, you may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1 when shooting at the target enemy unit.
