# Fiery Convocation

Spell Level 2

*At the High Mage's bidding, a roiling storm of fire erupts within the midst of the enemy line.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

Place a large (5") blast template so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target enemy unit. Once placed, the [template](../general-principles/templates.md) will scatter D3+1". Any enemy model whose base lies underneath the template's final position risks being hit and suffering a Strength 4 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2. These hits have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
