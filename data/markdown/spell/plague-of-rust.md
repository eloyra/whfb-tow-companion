# Plague of Rust

Spell Level 2

*As the Elementalist utters a curse, the armour of the enemy loses its lustre, corroding and crumbling from their bodies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 21" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -2 modifier to its [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md). This spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat.
