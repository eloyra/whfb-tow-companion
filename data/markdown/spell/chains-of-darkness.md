# Chains of Darkness

Spell Level 6

*Shadowy ichor materialises about the foe, solidifying into blackened shackles that weigh heavy upon their limbs.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -1 modifier to its Movement (to a minimum of 1) and Leadership characteristics (to a minimum of 2).
