# Flames of Hashut

Signature Spell

*The fiery breath of Hashut erupts around the sorcerer, engulfing their body and scorching their foes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | Combat |

A single enemy unit the caster is engaged in combat with suffers D3+1 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1. These hits have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
