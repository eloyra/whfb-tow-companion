# Glittering Robe

Signature Spell

*Shimmering light radiates from the Illusionist's garb, dazzling and confounding the enemy.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to any rolls To Hit made against the caster and any unit they have joined. If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Enchantment previously cast on any of the affected models immediately expire.
