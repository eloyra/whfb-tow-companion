# Fury of Khaine

Spell Level 5

*The High Mage beseeches Khaine, God of Murder, to bestow his fury on the High Mage's allies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until the end of this turn, the target friendly unit gains the [Extra Attacks (+1)](../special-rules/extra-attacks.md) special rule. This spell may target a friendly unit engaged in combat.
