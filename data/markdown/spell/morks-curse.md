# Mork’s Curse

Signature Spell

*The Shaman points a bony finger at the enemy and utters a curse. Almost immediately, buckles begin to break and rivets pop, causing armour plates to slip.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this spell is in play, the target enemy unit must re-roll any [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll of a natural 6.
