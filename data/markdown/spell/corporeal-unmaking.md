# Corporeal Unmaking

Spell Level 4

*The physical forms of the High Mage's enemies begin to unravel, turning to dust on the wind.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | Combat |

A single enemy unit the caster is engaged in combat with suffers D3 Strength 5 hits, with no armour or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).
