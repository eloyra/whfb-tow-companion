# Great Bastion

Signature Spell

*With a word of power from the Wizard, the earth cracks and buckles as it is transformed into ramparts upon which gleaming battlements burn with celestial flame.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). The target friendly unit gains a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. In addition, every model within the front rank of an enemy unit that ends its charge move in base contact with the target unit whilst this spell is in play must make a [Dangerous Terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md) test. Any Wounds lost are counted as 'unsaved wounds inflicted' when calculating the combat result. However, whilst this spell is in play the target unit cannot [march](../movement-in-detail/marching.md) or [charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md). This spell may target a friendly unit engaged in combat.
