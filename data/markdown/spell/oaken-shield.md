# Oaken Shield

Spell Level 5

*The Battle Mage summons a mighty spectral oak around themselves and their allies, protecting them from the weapons of their enemies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the caster and any unit they have joined gain a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
