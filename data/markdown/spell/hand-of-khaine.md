# Hand of Khaine

Signature Spell

*With a simple gesture, white light bursts forth from the Mage's outstretched hand, banishing the life force of the foe.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | Combat |

A single enemy model the caster is engaged in combat with suffers a single Strength 4 hit with no [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) permitted ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).
