# Spiteful Torrent

Spell Level 3

*Swarms of Spites and forest insects erupt from the casters' staff, racing across the battlefield as a writhing mass that bites and stings all within reach.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is within 15" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as [difficult terrain](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md). The template scatters D6" during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any enemy unit that moves through the template, or that the template moves over, suffers 3D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.
