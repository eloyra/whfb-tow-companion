# Khsar's Incantation of the Desert Wind

Signature Spell

*Harnessing the power of Khsar, god of the desert winds, the Liche Priest summons forth a sandstorm that engulfs the undying warriors of Nehekhara and carries them across the battlefield.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 6+/10+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

If this spell is cast with a casting result of 6 or more, a single friendly unit that has the [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md) special rule and is within the caster's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) gains the [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rule. If this spell is cast with a casting result of 10 or more, every friendly unit that has the Nehekharan Undead special rule and is within the caster's Command range gains the Reserve Move special rule. This spell lasts until the end of this turn.
