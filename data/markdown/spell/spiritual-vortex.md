# Spiritual Vortex

Spell Level 3

*The Necromancer gestures and dark energy swirls, opening a vortex into the realm of Morr, god of death.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a large (5") blast template so that its central hole is within 12" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template does not move and is treated as [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). Whilst within 8" of one or more Spiritual Vortexes, enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2) and cannot use their General's [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) special rule.
