# 'Ere We Go!

Spell Level 5

*The Shaman begins a simple chant which is quickly and enthusiastically taken up by their friends.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Any friendly unit that is within the caster's Command range during the [Declare Charges & Charge Reactions](../the-movement-phase/declare-charges-and-charge-reactions.md) sub-phase of this turn increases its maximum possible charge range by 3" and, when it makes a Charge roll, may apply a +D3 modifier to the result.
