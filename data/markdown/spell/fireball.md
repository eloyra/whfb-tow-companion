# Fireball

Spell Level 1

*With a snap of the fingers, the Battle Mage summons forth a roiling ball of magical flame and hurls it at a nearby foe.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 24" |

The target enemy unit suffers 2D6 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -. This spell has the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
