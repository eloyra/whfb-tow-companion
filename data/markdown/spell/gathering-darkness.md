# Gathering Darkness

Spell Level 2

*At the Daemonologist's command, strange forms gather about the foe, their whispered taunts draining the enemy's courage.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -2 modifier to its Initiative characteristic (to a minimum of 1), a -2 modifier to its Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2) and cannot use their General's [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) special rule. This spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat.
