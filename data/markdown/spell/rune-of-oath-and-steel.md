# Rune of Oath & Steel

Bound Spell

*Dwarf mail hardens, each suit of armour remembering the hammer blows that forged it.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+/11+ |
| **Range** | 24" |

If this [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) is cast with a casting result of 8 or more, the target friendly unit may re-roll any failed [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) rolls. If this Bound spell is cast with a casting result of 11 or more, the target friendly unit may re-roll any failed Armour Save rolls and improves its armour value by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). This spell lasts until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase.
