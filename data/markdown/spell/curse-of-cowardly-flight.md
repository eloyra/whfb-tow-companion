# Curse of Cowardly Flight

Spell Level 6

*As the war drums beat, the Battle Mage reaches into the minds of the enemy, tapping into their fears and weakening their intestinal fortitude.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

The target enemy unit must immediately make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md). If the target unit automatically passes any Panic tests it is required to make for any reason, it must still make this test and, should it fail, it will [Give Ground](../movement-in-detail/give-ground.md).
