# Pillar of Fire

Spell Level 3

*The Battle Mage conjures a fireball which quickly grows to become a towering pillar. Loosed from their grasp, it advances towards the enemy.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is within 12" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as [difficult terrain](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md). The template scatters D6" during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any enemy unit that moves through the template, or that the template moves over, suffers D3+3 Strength 3 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2. These hits have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
