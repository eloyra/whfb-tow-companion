# Curse of Years

Spell Level 4

*As dead things draw near, they feed hungrily upon the vital energy of the living, ageing them rapidly.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -1 modifier to its Movement, Weapon Skill and Toughness characteristics (to a minimum of 1). If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Hex previously cast on the target unit immediately expire.
