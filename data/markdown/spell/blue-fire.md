# Blue Fire

Signature Spell

*As the Sorcerer's hands draw strange runes in the air, their enemies are consumed by blue flames.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

[Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) only. The target enemy unit suffers D6+3 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2 and with the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
