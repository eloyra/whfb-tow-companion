# Take-backs & Changing One's Mind

It is not uncommon for players of any game to occasionally second guess themselves, saying they are about to do something before immediately changing their mind. Players should be tolerant of this in their opponents, as they will likely do it themselves.

However, once dice have been rolled for any reason, or a move committed to and made, players must abide by their decision; they can no longer go back and change anything that came before the dice roll or the act of moving!
