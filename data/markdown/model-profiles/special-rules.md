# Special Rules

In addition to the special rules associated with their troop type, many models have one or more special rules. These fall into three broad categories:

- **Universal Special Rules:** These are rules that appear in all armies, a full list of these can be found in the [Special Rules](https://tow.whfb.app/special-rules) section. If a model has one or more universal special rules, their names will be listed as part of its rules.
- **Army Special Rules:** These are special rules unique to the army the model belongs to. If a model has one or more army special rules, their names will be listed as part of its rules.
- **Unique Special Rules:** Some models have special rules unique to them. If a model has one or more unique special rules, these will be listed as part of its rules.

*Note that army and unique special rules are included within each army list.*
