# Troop Type

All models have a troop type given as part of their rules. There are five broad categories of troop type: Infantry, Cavalry, Chariots, Monsters and War Machines, each of which is further divided into sub-categories.

Additionally, some models have the word 'Character' in brackets after their troop type. This denotes that the model is a character, one of the brave and powerful heroes that lead an army.

Troop types and their rules are explained in [brief](https://tow.whfb.app/troop-types-at-a-glance) and in [greater detail](https://tow.whfb.app/troop-types-in-detail) in the Advanced Rules. Characters are explained in greater detail in the [Characters](https://tow.whfb.app/characters) section.
