# Who Can Shoot?

Not all units are able to shoot. Only units equipped with missile weapons (including war machines) or that can cast certain types of spell (such as magic missiles) can shoot. A unit cannot shoot if:

- It charged or marched during the preceding Movement phase.
- It is engaged in combat.
- It is fleeing.
