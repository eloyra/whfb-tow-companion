# Target Behind Partial Cover

If up to half of the target model (if shooting at a lone model) is obscured, or up to half of the models within the target unit (if shooting at a unit) are obscured, by terrain or other models, they are in partial cover. Models shooting at a target in partial cover suffer a -1 To Hit modifier.
