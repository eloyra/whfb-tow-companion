# Standing and Shooting

Taking careful aim whilst the enemy charges ever closer can be difficult. Models that are making a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction suffer a -1 [To Hit modifier](to-hit-modifiers.md). Note that models making a Stand & Shoot charge reaction do not suffer any additional modifiers for [Firing at Long Range](firing-at-long-range.md).
