# Target Behind Full Cover

If more than half of the target model (if shooting at a lone model) is obscured, or more than half of the models within the target unit (if shooting at a unit) are obscured by terrain or other models, they are in full cover. Models shooting at a target in full cover suffer a -2 To Hit modifier.
