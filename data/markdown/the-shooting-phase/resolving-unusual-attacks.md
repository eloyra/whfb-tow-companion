# Resolving Unusual Attacks

Some unusual attacks or special rules may inflict hits during the [Strategy](https://tow.whfb.app/the-strategy-phase) or [Movement](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase) phase. Such hits are resolved using the steps outlined in [sub-phases 3](roll-to-wound-shooting.md) and [4](remove-casualties-shooting.md) of the Shooting phase.
