# League Campaigns

For many players, finding the time to devote to a long and involved campaign can prove a challenge, with the requirements of real life all too often making it difficult to commit to for fear of letting down friends. For others, the mere thought of running a campaign can itself be daunting – creating a map, keeping track of the battles fought and so on can seem like a tremendous undertaking.

In such cases, players may wish to partake in a simple league campaign. In such a campaign, each participant simply plays each other participant once (or twice, or thrice, if everyone is keen enough) and records the results. At the end of the campaign, the player with the most Campaign Points is declared the winner.
