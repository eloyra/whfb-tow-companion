# Path to Glory Campaign

*To the brute tribespeople of the frozen north, those that live, violent existence under the shadow of Chaos, the Path to Glory is the road they must travel to earn power and infamy. Yet the appeal of earning renown through decisive action is not limited to the followers of Chaos alone. Amongst the armies of the Empire, bold officers seek to elevate themselves through their courage, whilst in Bretonnia, chivalrous lords lead their knights against the foe in the pursuit of renown, and in the Badlands, brutal Orc Warbosses amass great Waaaghs! to prove their might.*
