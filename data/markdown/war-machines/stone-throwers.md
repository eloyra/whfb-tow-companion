# Stone Throwers

*Stone throwers lob large rocks high into the air to come crashing upon enemy ranks. The very largest can hurl a projectile big enough to flatten monsters and knock down city walls.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-60" | 4(8) | -1(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon does not use its crew’s Ballistic Skill. Instead, it shoots using the*  [Bombardment](bombardment.md)  *special rule. This weapon uses a 3" blast template and the*  [Stone Thrower Misfire table](stone-thrower-misfire-table.md)  *table. The*  [Multiple Wounds (D3+1)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*
