# Cannon

*Cannon are devastating, if sometimes unpredictable, weapons. When they work, cannon can shatter an enemy’s lines. However, should they misfire, the results can be spectacular.*

The following profiles represent the sizes of cannon most often seen:

## Cannon

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 48" | 8 | -2 | - |

## Great Cannon

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 60" | 10 | -3 | - |

***Notes:***  *Cannon (of any type) do not use their crew's Ballistic Skill. Instead, they shoot using the*  [Cannon Fire](cannon-fire.md)  *special rule. This weapon uses the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](black-powder-misfire-table.md) *.*
