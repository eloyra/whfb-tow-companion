# Mortars

*These short, heavy weapons lob a hollow, explosive shell high into the air to drop onto its target. This shell explodes with great force, scattering razor-sharp shrapnel capable of cutting easily through even the heaviest of armour over a wide area.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-48" | 2(6) | -2(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon does not use its crew’s Ballistic Skill. Instead, it shoots like a stone thrower, using the*  [Bombardment](bombardment.md)  *special rule. This weapon uses a 5" blast template and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](black-powder-misfire-table.md) *. The*  [Multiple Wounds (D3)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*
