# Common Causes of Panic

There are many special rules, spells and magic items that can cause an enemy to make a [Panic test](panic-tests.md). In such cases, the description of the special rule, spell or magic item itself will provide details. However, there are certain circumstances that arise regularly in games which always cause units affected to make a Panic test.
