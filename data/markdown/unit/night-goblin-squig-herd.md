# Night Goblin Squig Herd

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Squig Herder | 4 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Cave Squig | 4 | 4 | - | 5 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 3 |

*When writing your muster list, a Night Goblin Squig Herd must contain at least one Squig Herder for every five Cave Squigs. Cave Squigs always occupy the front rank(s) of the unit, pushing past any Squig Herders to get there if necessary (such as when the unit turns).*

**Cost:** 3 points (Squig Herder), 10 points (Cave Squig)

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 1+/5+

- **Squig Herders:** Assorted squig-herding implements (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))
- **Cave Squigs:** [Huge gobs](../weapons-of-war/huge-gob.md)

- Any Squig Herder in the unit may take a [thrusting spear](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
