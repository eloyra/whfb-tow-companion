[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) +1 point per model
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) +1 point per model
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to an Elder (champion) +6 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer +6 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a musician +6 points per unit
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 50 points
- An Elder may purchase:

  - [Weapon runes](../magic-items/weapon-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
  - [Talismanic runes](../magic-items/talismanic-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule +1 point per model
