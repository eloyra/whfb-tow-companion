# Infernal Guard

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Infernal Guard | 3 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 9 |
| Deathmask | 3 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 9 |

**Cost:** 11 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Fireglaives](../weapons-of-war/fireglaive.md) (+4 points per model)
  - [Hailshot blunderbusses](../weapons-of-war/hailshot-blunderbuss.md) (+5 points per model)
- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Deathmask (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Deathmask may take one of the following:

  - [Pistol](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md) (+3 points)
  - [Naptha bombs](../weapons-of-war/naptha-bombs.md) (+6 points)
- A Deathmask may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)\
  - Have the [Blackshard Armour](../special-rules/blackshard-armour.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
