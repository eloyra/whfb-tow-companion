**Cost:** 5 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [longbows](../weapons-of-war/longbow.md)

- The entire unit may take light armour (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Villein (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- Any unit may:

  - Be equipped with [defensive stakes](../weapons-of-war/defensive-stakes.md) (+10 points per unit)
  - Be equipped with [burning braziers](../weapons-of-war/burning-braziers.md) (+20 points per unit)

  - Replace the [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) special rule with Skirmishers (Free)
