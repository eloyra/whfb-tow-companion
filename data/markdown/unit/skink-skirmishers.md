| Skink | 6 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 5 |
| Patrol Leader | 6 | 2 | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 5 |

**Cost:** 5 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and calloused hides (counts as [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md))

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Blowpipes](../weapons-of-war/blowpipe.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Javelins](../weapons-of-war/javelin.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (Free)
- Any unit may upgrade one model to a Patrol Leader (champion) (+5 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may have one of the following:

  - The [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) special rule (+10 points per unit)
  - The [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule (+5 points per unit)
