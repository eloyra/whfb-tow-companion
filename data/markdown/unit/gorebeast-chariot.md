*A Gorebeast Chariot may be included in your army as a character's mount. If so, its points are added to that of its rider.*

**Cost:** 135 points

**Unit Category:** [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-chariots.md)

**Base Size:** 60 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

**Armour Value:** 3+

- **Chaos Charioteers:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md)
- **Gorebeast:** Goring horns (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any Chaos Chariot may replace the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](../special-rules/mark-of-chaos-undivided.md) with the:

  - [Mark of Khorne](../special-rules/mark-of-khorne.md) (+10 points per model)
  - [Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) (+10 points per model)
  - [Mark of Slaanesh](../special-rules/mark-of-slaanesh.md) (+10 points per model)
  - [Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) (+10 points per model)
