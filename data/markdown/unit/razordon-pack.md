# Razordon Pack

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |

| Skink Handler | 6 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 5 |
| Razordon | 6 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 2 | 4 |

*When writing your muster list, a Razordon Pack must contain at least three Skink handlers for every one Razordon.*

**Cost:** 5 points per Skink Handler, 60 points per Razordon

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [War Beast](../troop-types-in-detail/war-beasts.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm (Skink Handlers), 50 x 75 mm (Razordons)

**Unit Size:** 3+/1+

- **Skink Handlers:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and calloused hides (counts as [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md))
- **Razordons:** [Wicked claws](../weapons-of-war/wicked-claws.md), [razor barbs](../weapons-of-war/razor-barbs.md) and scaly skin (counts as [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md))
