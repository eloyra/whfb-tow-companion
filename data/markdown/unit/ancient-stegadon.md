# Ancient Stegadon

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ancient Stegadon | 6 | 4 | - | 6 | 6 | 5 | 1 | 3 | - |
| Skink Crew (x5) | - | 2 | 3 | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | 6 |

*An Ancient Stegadon may be included in your army as a character's mount. If so, its points are added to that of its rider. Note that, because it has the Howdah special rule, an Ancient Stegadon is considered to be a chariot mount, rather than a ridden monster.*

**Cost:** 230 points

**Unit Category:** [Monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md)

**Troop Type:** [Behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md)

**Base Size:** 60 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

**Armour Value:** 4+

- **Ancient Stegadon:** [Great horns](../weapons-of-war/great-horns.md) and [giant bow](../weapons-of-war/giant-bow.md)
- **Skink Crew:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [javelins](../weapons-of-war/javelin.md)

- An Ancient Stegadon may replace its [giant bow](../weapons-of-war/giant-bow.md) with:

  - [Giant blowpipes](../weapons-of-war/giant-blowpipes.md) (Free)
  - [Engine of the Gods](../special-rules/engine-of-the-gods.md) (+25 points)
