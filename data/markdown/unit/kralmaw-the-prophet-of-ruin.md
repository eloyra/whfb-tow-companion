# Kralmaw, the Prophet of Ruin

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |

*Kralmaw, the Prophet of Ruin is a Great Bray-Shaman. He may be included in any Beastmen Brayherds army made using any army composition list that includes this option. He must be fielded as presented here.*

**Cost:** 245 points

**Unit Category:** [Named Character](../warhammer-armies/named-characters-warhammer-armies.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [Named Character](../warhammer-armies/named-characters-warhammer-armies.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm (Kralmaw), 25 x 25 mm (Leering Spirit marker)

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [Grisly Totem](../weapons-of-war/grisly-totem.md) ([Braystaff](../weapons-of-war/braystaff.md))

**Magic:** Kralmaw, the Prophet of Ruin is a Level 4 Wizard who uses spells from [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), or [Lore of Primal Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-primal-magic.md).
