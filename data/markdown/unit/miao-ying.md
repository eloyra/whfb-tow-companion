# Miao Ying

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |

*Miao Ying is a Cathayan Dragon. She may be included in any Empire of Grand Cathay army made using any army composition list that includes this option. She must be fielded as presented here.*

*In her Human form, Miao Ying's troop type is heavy infantry (named character), in her Dragon form, it is behemoth (named character).*

**Cost:** 485 points

**Unit Category:** [Named Character](../warhammer-armies/named-characters-warhammer-armies.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) [Behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) [Named Character](../warhammer-armies/named-characters-warhammer-armies.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 50 mm (Human form), 100 x 150 mm (Dragon form)

**Unit Size:** 1

- **Human Form:** [Talons of the Storm](../magic-item/talons-of-the-storm.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Dragon Form:** [Talons of the Storm](../magic-item/talons-of-the-storm.md), [Dragon fire](../weapons-of-war/dragon-fire.md) and Draconic scales (counts as [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md))

In her Human form, Miao Ying is a Level 4 Wizard. In her Dragon form, she is a Level 2 Wizard. She may cast a number of spells equal to her current Level of Wizardry per turn. Miao Ying knows four spells, chosen in the usual way from one of the following Lores of Magic: [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [High Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/high-magic.md)
