# Cold One Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cold One Knight | - | 5 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 9 |
| Dread Knight | - | 5 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 9 |
| Cold One | 7 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 2 | 2 | - |

**Cost:** 31 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Cold One Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Cold Ones:** Claws and teeth (counts as a [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Dread Knight (champion) +7 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer +7 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a musician +7 points per unit
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
  - Replace [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) with [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) +4 points per model
- A Dread Knight may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
