# Troll Hag

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Troll Hag | 5 | 2 | 2 | 6 | 5 | 6 | 2 | 3 | 8 |

*Any army made using the Orc & Goblin Tribes Grand Army composition list that includes one or more Troll Mobs may include 0-1 Troll Hag as a Rare choice.*

**Cost:** 235 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 60 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

Gnarled stump (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)), [Troll vomit](../weapons-of-war/troll-vomit.md), [swamp breath](../weapons-of-war/swamp-breath.md) and scaly skin (counts as [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md))

**Magic:** Troll Hag is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), or [Lore of Troll Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-troll-magic.md).

- A Troll Hag may:

  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+35 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
