# Marauder Horsemen

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Marauder Horseman | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Marauder Horsemaster | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 7 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 12 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Marauder Horseman:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (Free)
  - [Throwing spears](../weapons-of-war/throwing-spear.md) (Free)
  - [Flails](../weapons-of-war/flail.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [throwing axes](../weapons-of-war/throwing-axe.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may belong to a single [Chaotic Cult](../special-rules/chaotic-cults.md):

  - The [Cult of the Bloodied Hound](../special-rules/cult-of-the-bloodied-hound.md) (+1 point per model)
  - The [Cult of the Carrion Crow](../special-rules/cult-of-the-carrion-crow.md) (+1 point per model)
  - The [Cult of the Slithering Serpent](../special-rules/cult-of-the-slithering-serpent.md) (+1 point per model)
  - The [Cult of the Fell Raptor](../special-rules/cult-of-the-fell-raptor.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Marauder Horsemaster (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Marauder Horsemaster may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- The entire unit may purchase a single [Chaotic Trait](../magic-items/chaotic-traits.md)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Replace the [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) special rule with [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) (Free)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
