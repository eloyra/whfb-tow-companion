# Rangers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ranger | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 9 |
| Ol' Deadeye | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 9 |

**Cost:** 11 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5-20

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may either:

  - Take [throwing axes](../weapons-of-war/throwing-axe.md) (+1 point per model)
  - Replace [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) with throwing axes (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to an Ol' Deadeye (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
- An Ol' Deadeye may replace their crossbow with:

  - [Pistol](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md) (Free)
  - [Brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (+5 points)
