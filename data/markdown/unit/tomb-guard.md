**Cost:** 10 points per model

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may take halberds (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Tomb Captain (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Tomb Captain may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Nehekharan Phalanx](../special-rules/nehekharan-phalanx.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
