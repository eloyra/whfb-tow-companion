| Sharpshooter | - | 3 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Empire Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 19 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Outriders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [pistols](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md), [repeater handguns](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Empire Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Sharpshooter (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- A Sharpshooter may replace their repeater handgun with:

  - [Brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (Free)
  - [Grenade launching blunderbuss](../weapons-of-war/grenade-launching-blunderbuss.md) (+10 points)
  - [Repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) (Free)
