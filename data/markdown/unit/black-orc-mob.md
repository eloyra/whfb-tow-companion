**Cost:** 12 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md)

- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any model in the unit may take a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Boss may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit in your army may:

  - Have the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rule (+1 point per mode)
