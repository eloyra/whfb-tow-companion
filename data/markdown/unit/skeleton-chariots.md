# Skeleton Chariots

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chariot | - | - | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | - | - | - |
| Skeletal Crew (x2) | - | 3 | 2 | 3 | - | - | 2 | 1 | 7 |
| Master Charioteer | - | 3 | 2 | 3 | - | - | 2 | 2 | 7 |
| Skeletal Steed (x2) | 8 | 2 | - | 3 | - | - | 2 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 41 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/light-chariots.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1+

**Armour Value:** 4+

- **Skeleton Crew:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [warbows](../weapons-of-war/warbow.md)
- **Skeletal Steed:** Skeletal hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to include a Master Charioteer (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to include a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to include a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Master Charioteer may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points

[Arrows of Asaph](../special-rules/arrows-of-asaph.md) [Dry as Dust](../special-rules/dry-as-dust.md) [Horde](../special-rules/horde.md) [Impact Hits (D3)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md) [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) [Regeneration (6+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md)

*Note that a Master Charioteer, a standard bearer or a musician each replace a single Skeleton Crewman, and that each must be placed aboard a different Chariot.*
