| Necroserpent | 7 | 3 | - | 5 | - | - | 3 | 3 | - |

**Cost:** 54 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 2+

- **Necropolis Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Necroserpent:** Lashing tails (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Necropolis Captain (champion) +7 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer +7 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a musician +7 points per unit
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Necropolis Captain may take:

  - A [great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) +3 points
  - Magic items up to a total of 25 points
