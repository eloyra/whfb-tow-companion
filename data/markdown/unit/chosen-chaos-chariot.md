**Character Mount:** In an army made using the [Heralds of Darkness](../warhammer-armies/heralds-of-darkness.md) Army of Infamy muster list, Chosen Chariots may be included as character mounts in addition to being taken as Special choices. If so, the chariot's points are added to that of its rider.

- A [Chaos Lord](chaos-lord.md), [Exalted Champion](exalted-champion.md) or [Aspiring Champion](aspiring-champion.md) may be mounted on a Chosen Chaos Chariot (+140 points)
