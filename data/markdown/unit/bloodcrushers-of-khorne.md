**Cost:** 65 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 75 mm

**Unit Size:** 3+

- **Bloodletters:** [Hellblades](../weapons-of-war/hellblade.md) and calloused hides (counts as [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md))
- **Juggernauts of Khorne:** [Brass bound hooves](../weapons-of-war/brass-bound-hooves.md) and brazen hide (counts as [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Bloodreaper (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - May purchase a [Daemonic Icon](../magic-items/daemonic-gifts-and-icons.md) worth up to 50 points
