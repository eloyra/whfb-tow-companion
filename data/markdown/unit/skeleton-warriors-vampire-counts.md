# Skeleton Warriors (Vampire Counts)

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skeleton Warrior | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 5 |
| Skeleton Champion | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 5 |

**Cost:** 5 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may take [thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Skeleton Champion (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
