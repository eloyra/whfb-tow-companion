# Dragon Princes

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dragon Prince | - | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 9 |
| Drakemaster | - | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 3 | 9 |
| Barded Elven Steed | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 37 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Dragon Princes:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md), [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Barded Elven Steeds:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Drakemaster (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic banner worth up to 50 points
- A Drakemaster may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
