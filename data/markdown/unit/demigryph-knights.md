# Demigryph Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Demigryph Knight | - | 4 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Demigryph Preceptor | - | 4 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 2 | 8 |
| Demigryph | 7 | 4 | - | 5 | - | - | 4 | 3 | - |

**Cost:** 58 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 75 mm

**Unit Size:** 2-12

- **Demigryph Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Demigryph:** [Wicked claws](../weapons-of-war/wicked-claws.md) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md) (Free)
  - [Lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Demigryph Preceptor (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
  - Replace [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) with [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) (+5 points per model)
- A Demigryph Preceptor may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
