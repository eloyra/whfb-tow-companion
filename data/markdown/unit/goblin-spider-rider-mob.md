# Goblin Spider Rider Mob

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spider Rider | - | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 5 |
| Boss | - | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 6 |
| Giant Spider | 7 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 12 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Spider Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Giants Spiders**: Poisonous fangs (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
