# Empire Road Wardens

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Road Warden | - | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Captain | - | 3 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 18 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Road Wardens:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may replace [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) with [braces of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Captain (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Have the [Feigned Flight](../special-rules/feigned-flight.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
- A Captain may replace their [crossbow](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) with:

  - [Brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (+2 points)
  - [Repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) (+2 points)
- Any unit may replace the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule with:

  - [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) (+1 point per model)
