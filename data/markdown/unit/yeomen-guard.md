**Cost:** 5 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md) (Free)
  - [Polearms](../weapons-of-war/polearm.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Warden (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a [Grail Monk](../special-rules/grail-monk.md) (see below) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 25 points
- A Grail Monk may be equipped with a [Blessed Triptych](../weapons-of-war/blessed-triptych.md) (+25 points)
