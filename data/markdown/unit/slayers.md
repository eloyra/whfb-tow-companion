# Slayers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Troll Slayer | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 10 |
| Giant Slayer | 3 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 10 |

**Cost:** 12 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

- Any number of models may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any number of models may be upgraded to Giant Slayers (+7 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 50 points

[Deathblow](../special-rules/deathblow.md) ["Fight Me!" (Giant Slayers only)](../special-rules/fight-me.md) [Hatred (Orcs & Goblins)](../special-rules/hatred.md) [Immune to Psychology](../special-rules/immune-to-psychology.md) [Loner](../special-rules/loner.md) [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) [Motley Crew](../special-rules/motley-crew.md) [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) [Resolute](../special-rules/resolute.md) [Slayer](../special-rules/slayer.md) [Unbreakable](../special-rules/unbreakable.md)

*Giant Slayers always occupy the front rank(s) of the unit, pushing past any Troll Slayers to get there if necessary (such as when the unit turns). If a unit that includes a standard bearer and musician includes enough Giant Slayers, both its standard bearer and musician will be Giant Slayers. If a unit that includes a standard bearer and musician includes only one Giant Slayer, that Giant Slayer will be its standard bearer.*
