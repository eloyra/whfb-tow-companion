# Marauder Tribe Huscarls

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Marauder Tribe Huscarl | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| First Sword | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 8 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 18 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Chaos Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (Free)
  - [Flails](../weapons-of-war/flail.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Throwing axes](../weapons-of-war/throwing-axe.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Javelins](../weapons-of-war/javelin.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may:

  - Replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with heavy armour (+1 point per model)
  - Take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may replace the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](../special-rules/mark-of-chaos-undivided.md) with the:

  - [Mark of Khorne](../special-rules/mark-of-khorne.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Slaanesh](../special-rules/mark-of-slaanesh.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a First Sword (champion) (+8 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+2 point per model)
- A First Sword may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- The entire unit may purchase up to two [Chaotic Traits](../magic-items/chaotic-traits.md)
