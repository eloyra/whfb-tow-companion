# Silver Helms

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Silver Helm | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 8 |
| High Helm | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 8 |
| Barded Elven Steed | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 23 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Silver Helms:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Elven Steeds:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a High Helm (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic banner worth up to 25 points
- A High Helm may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
