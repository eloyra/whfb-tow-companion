*A Chariot of Tzeentch may only be included in your army as a character’s mount. Its points are added to that of its rider.*

**Cost:** 190 points

**Unit Category:** [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-chariots.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

**Armour Value:** 4+

- **Exalted Flamer:** [Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [warpflame](../weapons-of-war/warpflame.md)
- **Blue Horror Crew:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [flames of Tzeentch](../weapons-of-war/flames-of-tzeentch.md)
- **Screamer:** [Lamprey's bite](../weapons-of-war/lampreys-bite.md)

An Exalted Flamer is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), or [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md).

- An Exalted Flamer may be a:

  - Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Level 3 Wizard (+60 points)
- An Exalted Flamer may purchase [Daemonic Gifts](../magic-items/daemonic-gifts-and-icons.md) up to a total of 50 points
