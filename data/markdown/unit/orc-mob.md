# Orc Mob

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Orc Boy | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Boss | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 7 |

**Cost:** 5 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Warbows](../weapons-of-war/warbow.md) (Free)
  - [Additional hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Throwing spears](../weapons-of-war/throwing-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Boss may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit in your army may:

  - Have the [Big 'Uns](../special-rules/big-uns.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Replace the [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) special rule with [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) (Free)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Frenzied unit may:

  - Have the [Warpaint](../special-rules/warpaint.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Take [Big Stabbas](../special-rules/big-stabbas.md) (+5 points per unit)
