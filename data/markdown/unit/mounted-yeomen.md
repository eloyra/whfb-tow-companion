# Mounted Yeomen

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Warden | - | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 6 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 13 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Mounted Yeomen:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md)
- **Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Warden (champion) (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
  - Have the [Feigned Flight](../special-rules/feigned-flight.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
