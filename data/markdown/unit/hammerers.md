**Cost:** 16 points per model

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [great hammers](../weapons-of-war/great-hammer.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Royal Champion (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 75 points
- A Royal Champion may purchase:

  - [Weapon runes](../magic-items/weapon-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
