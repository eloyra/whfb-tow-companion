# Plague Drones of Nurgle

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |

| Plagueridden | - | 3 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 3 | 2 | 2 | 7 |
| Rot Fly | 1 | 3 | - | 5 | - | - | 2 | 3 | - |

**Cost:** 63 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 75 mm

**Unit Size:** 3+

- **Plaguebearers:** [Plagueswords](../weapons-of-war/plaguesword.md) and [death's heads](../weapons-of-war/deaths-heads.md)
- **Rot Flies:** Filth-encrusted claws (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Plagueridden (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - May purchase a [Daemonic Icon](../magic-items/daemonic-gifts-and-icons.md) worth up to 50 points
- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Plague Proboscis](../special-rules/plague-proboscis.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Venom sting](../weapons-of-war/venom-sting.md) (+2 points per model)
