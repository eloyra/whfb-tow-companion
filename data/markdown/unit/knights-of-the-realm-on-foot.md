# Knights of the Realm on Foot

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Knight of the Realm | 4 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 8 |
| First Knight | 4 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 8 |

**Cost:** 11 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may:

  - Take [great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)  
    Or:
  - Replace [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with great weapons (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a First Knight (champion) (+6 points per unit)\
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 25 points
- A First Knight may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
