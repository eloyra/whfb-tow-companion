**Cost:** 12 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Hobgoblin Wolf Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Giant Wolves:** Claws and fangs (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (Free)
  - [Shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+8 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Feigned Flight](../special-rules/feigned-flight.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rule (+1 points per model)
