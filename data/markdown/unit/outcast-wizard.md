| Outcast Wizards | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 3 | 1 | 7 |

**Cost:** 45 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

**Magic:** Outcast Wizards is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md), or [Necromancy](../the-lores-of-magic/necromancy.md).

- May be mounted on a [Warhorse](warhorse-bretonnia.md) (+12 points)
- An Outcast Wizard may:

  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Be a Level 3 Wizard (+60 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 75 points
