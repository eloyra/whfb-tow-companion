# Chaos Warriors

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chaos Warrior | 4 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Champion | 4 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 8 |

**Cost:** 13 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may replace the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](../special-rules/mark-of-chaos-undivided.md) with the:

  - [Mark of Khorne](../special-rules/mark-of-khorne.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Slaanesh](../special-rules/mark-of-slaanesh.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a champion (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Champion may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- The entire unit may purchase a single [Chaotic Trait](../magic-items/chaotic-traits.md)
