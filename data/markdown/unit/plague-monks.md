**Cost:** 7 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

- The entire unit may take [additional hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) +2 points per model
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Plague Deacon (champion) +6 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer +6 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a musician +6 points per unit
- A Plague Deacon may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 point
