**Cost:** 85 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

**Magic:** Daemonsmith Sorcerer is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), or [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md).

- May take any of the following:

  - [Darkforged weapon](../weapons-of-war/darkforged-weapon.md) (+10 points)
  - [Naptha bombs](../weapons-of-war/naptha-bombs.md) (+15 points)
  - [Blood of Hashut](../weapons-of-war/blood-of-hashut.md) (+20 points per vial)
- A Daemonsmith Sorcerer may:

  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Be mounted on a [Great Taurus](great-taurus.md) (+120 points)
  - Be mounted on a [Lammasu](lammasu.md) (+95 points)
