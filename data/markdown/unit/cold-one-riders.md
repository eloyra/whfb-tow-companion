# Cold One Riders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cold One Rider | - | 4 | 0 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 8 |
| Pack Leader | - | 4 | 0 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 8 |
| Cold One | 7 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 2 | 2 | - |

**Cost:** 34 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Cold One Riders:**  [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), scaly skin (counts as [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Cold Ones:** Claws and teeth (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Pack Leader (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Pack Leader may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
