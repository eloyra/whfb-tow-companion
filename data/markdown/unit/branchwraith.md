**Cost:** 80 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and sapwood flesh (counts as [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md))

A Branchwraith is a Level 1 Wizard. Every Branchwraith knows spells from the following Lores of Magic: [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md)

- May take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points)
- May be a Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
- May take [Forest Spites](../magic-items/forest-spites.md) up to a total of 50 points
