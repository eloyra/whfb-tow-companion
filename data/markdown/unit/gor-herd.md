**Cost:** 7 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (Free)
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a True-horn (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A True-horn may:

  - Replace a hand weapon or its shield with a great weapon (Free)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
