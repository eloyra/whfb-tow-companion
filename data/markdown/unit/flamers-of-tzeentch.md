# Flamers of Tzeentch

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Flamer | 6 | 2 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 7 |
| Pyroclaster | 6 | 2 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 7 |
| Exalted Flamer | 6 | 2 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 2 | 7 |

**Cost:** 40 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm

**Unit Size:** 3-6

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [warpflame](../weapons-of-war/warpflame.md)

An Exalted Flamer is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), or [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md).

- Any unit may upgrade one model to a Pyroclaster (champion) (+7 points per unit)
- A Pyroclaster may be upgraded to an Exalted Flamer (champion) (+40 points)
- An Exalted Flamer may be a:

  - Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Level 3 Wizard (+60 points)
- An Exalted Flamer may purchase Daemonic Gifts up to a total of 50 points
