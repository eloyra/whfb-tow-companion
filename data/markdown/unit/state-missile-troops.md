# State Missile Troops

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| State Missile Trooper | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Sergeant | 4 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |

**Cost:** 7 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+ (5+ if a detachment)

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md)

- The entire unit may replace [crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) with [handguns](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Sergeant (champion) (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Sergeant may replace their crossbow or handgun with:

  - [Brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (+5 points)
  - [Hochland long rifle](../weapons-of-war/hochland-long-rifle.md) (+8 points)
  - [Repeater handgun](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md) (+5 points)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 25 points
