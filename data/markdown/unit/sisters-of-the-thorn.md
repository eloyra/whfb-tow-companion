# Sisters of the Thorn

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sister of the Thorn | - | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 9 |
| Handmaiden of the Thorn | - | 4 | 6 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 9 |
| Steed of Isha | 8 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 22 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Sister of the Thorn:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [blackbriar javelins](../weapons-of-war/blackbriar-javelin.md)
- **Steeds of Isha:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Handmaiden of the Thorn (champion) (+8 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Handmaiden of the Thorn may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
