# Glade Riders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Glade Rider | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Glade Knight | - | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Elven Steed | 9 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 17 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Glade Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [Asrai longbows](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md)
- **Elven Steeds:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may take one of the following types of enchanted arrows:

  - [Arcane bodkins](../weapons-of-war/arcane-bodkins.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Hagbane tips](../weapons-of-war/hagbane-tips.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Moonfire shot](../weapons-of-war/moonfire-shot.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Swiftshiver shards](../weapons-of-war/swiftshiver-shards.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Trueflight arrows](../weapons-of-war/trueflight-arrows.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Glade Knight (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - A Glade Knight may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
