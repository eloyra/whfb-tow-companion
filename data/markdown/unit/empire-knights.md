# Empire Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Empire Knight | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 8 |
| Preceptor | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 8 |
| Barded Warhorse | 7 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 21 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Barded Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (+1 point per model)  
    Or:
  - May replace [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with great weapons (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Preceptor (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 25 points
- A Preceptor may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
