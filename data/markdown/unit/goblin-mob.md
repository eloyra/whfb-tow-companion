# Goblin Mob

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Goblin | 4 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 5 |
| Boss | 4 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 6 |

**Cost:** 3 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may:

  - Take [thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)  
    Or:
  - Replace [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with [shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
  - Include 0-3 [Nasty Skulkers](nasty-skulker.md) (+10 points each)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Replace the [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) special rule with [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) (Free)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
