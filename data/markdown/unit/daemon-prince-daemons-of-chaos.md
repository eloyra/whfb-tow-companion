# Daemon Prince (Daemons of Chaos)

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Daemon Prince | 6 | 7 | 5 | 6 | 5 | 4 | 7 | 5 | 9 |

**Cost:** 210 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [ensorcelled weapon](../weapons-of-war/ensorcelled-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

**Magic:** Daemon Prince that is a Wizard uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), or [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md).

- May replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+6 points)
- May have the [Fly (9)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule (+30 points)
- May be one of the following:

  - [Daemon of Khorne](../special-rules/daemon-of-khorne.md) (+10 points)
  - [Daemon of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md) (+10 points)
  - [Daemons of Slaanesh](../special-rules/daemon-of-slaanesh.md) (+10 points)
  - [Daemons of Tzeentch](../special-rules/daemon-of-tzeentch.md) (+10 points)
- Unless it is a Daemon of Khorne, a Daemon Prince may be a Wizard:

  - Level 1 (+25 points)
  - Level 2 (+50 points)
  - Level 3 (+75 points)
  - Level 4 (+100 points)
- May purchase [Daemonic Gifts](../magic-items/daemonic-gifts-and-icons.md) up to a total of 100 points
