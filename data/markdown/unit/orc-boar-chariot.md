*An Orc Boar Chariot may be included in your army as a character's mount. If so, its points are added to that of its rider.*

**Cost:** 90 points

**Unit Category:** [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-chariots.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

**Armour Value:** 4+

- **Orc Crew:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md)
- **War Boars:** Tusks (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- May add a third Orc crew member +5 points
- 0-1 per 1,000 points may have the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) special rule +2 points per crew
