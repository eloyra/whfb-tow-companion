# Orc Boar Boy Mob

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Boar Boy | - | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Boss | - | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 7 |
| War Boar | 7 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 15 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Boar Boyz:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **War Boars:** Tusks (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) +1 point per model
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) +1 point per model
- The entire unit may replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) +1 point per model
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) +8 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer +6 points per unit
  - Upgrade one model to a musician +6 points per unit
- A Boss may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit in your army may have the [Big 'Uns](../special-rules/big-uns.md) special rule +2 points per model
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Replace light armour with the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) special rule +1 point per model
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Frenzied unit may have the [Warpaint](../special-rules/warpaint.md) special rule +1 point per model
