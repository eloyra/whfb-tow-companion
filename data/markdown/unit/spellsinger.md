[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

**Magic:** Spellsinger is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [High Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/high-magic.md), or [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md).

- May have the [Talismanic Tattoos](../special-rules/talismanic-tattoos.md) special rule (+15 points)
- May be mounted:

  - [Elven Steed](elven-steed.md) (+14 points)
  - [Unicorn](unicorn.md) (+35 points)
  - [Warhawk](warhawk.md) (+30 points)
  - [Great Eagle](great-eagle-mount.md) (+60 points)
- May take [Forest Spites](../magic-items/forest-spites.md) up to a total of 50 points
- A Spellsinger may:

  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
