**Cost:** 31 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 40 x 40 mm

**Unit Size:** 3+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points per model)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points per model)
  - [Ironfist](../weapons-of-war/ironfist.md) (+4 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Crusher (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a Bellower (musician) (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
  - Replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+4 points per model)
