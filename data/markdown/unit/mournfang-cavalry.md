# Mournfang Cavalry

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ogre | - | 3 | 2 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 2 | 3 | 7 |
| Crusher | - | 3 | 2 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 7 |
| Mournfang | 8 | 3 | - | 5 | - | - | 2 | 3 | - |

**Cost:** 64 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 2+

- **Ogre:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Mournfang:** [Monstrous tusks](../weapons-of-war/monstrous-tusks.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points per model)
  - [Ironfists](../weapons-of-war/ironfist.md) (+4 points per model)
- The entire unit may replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+3 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Crusher (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a Bellower (musician) (+7 points per unit)
- A Crusher may take:

  - A [brace of Ogre pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-ogre-pistols.md) (+12 points)
  - Magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
