# Veteran Freeblades

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Veteran Freeblade | - | 4 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 8 |
| Commander | - | 4 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 8 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |
| Barded Warhorse | 7 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 22 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Veteran Freeblades:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))
- **Barded Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Commander (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Replace Warhorses with Barded Warhorses (+3 points per model)
- Any unit of Veteran Sellsword Knights may:

  - Replace [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) with [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) (+4 points per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
