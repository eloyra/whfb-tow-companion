# Waywatchers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Waywatcher | 5 | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 8 |
| Sentinel | 5 | 4 | 6 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 8 |

**Cost:** 16 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [Asrai longbows](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md)

- Any unit may take one of the following types of enchanted arrows:

  - [Arcane bodkins](../weapons-of-war/arcane-bodkins.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Hagbane tips](../weapons-of-war/hagbane-tips.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Moonfire shot](../weapons-of-war/moonfire-shot.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Swiftshiver shards](../weapons-of-war/swiftshiver-shards.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Trueflight arrows](../weapons-of-war/trueflight-arrows.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may upgrade one model to a Sentinel (champion) (+6 points per unit)
- A Sentinel may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit in your army may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
