# Vampire Count

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vampire Count | 6 | 7 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 3 | 6 | 4 | 8 |

**Cost:** 160 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

**Magic:** Vampire Count that is a Wizard uses spells from [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md), or [Necromancy](../the-lores-of-magic/necromancy.md).

- May take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points)
  - [Lance](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (if appropriately mounted) (+4 points)
- May take a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+2 points)
- May take one of the following:

  - [Light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+3 points)
  - [Heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+6 points)
- May be mounted:

  - [Nightmare](nightmare.md) (+16 points)
  - [Coven Throne](coven-throne.md) (+210 points)
  - [Abyssal Terror](abyssal-terror.md) (+120 points)
  - [Zombie Dragon](zombie-dragon.md) (+215 points)
- A Vampire Count may:

  - Be a Level 1 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+60 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 100 points
  - Take [Vampiric Powers](../magic-items/vampiric-powers.md) up to a total of 100 points
