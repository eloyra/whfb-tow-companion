# Sellsword Infantry

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sellsword | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 5 |
| Officer | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 6 |

**Cost:** 4 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Crossbows](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Handguns](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md) (+3 points per model)
- The entire unit may:

  - Take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
  - Replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to an Officer (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
