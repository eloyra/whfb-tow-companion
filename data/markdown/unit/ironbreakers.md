# Ironbreakers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ironbreaker | 3 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 9 |
| Ironbeard | 3 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 9 |

**Cost:** 15 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to an Ironbeard (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
- An Ironbeard may replace their [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with one of the following:

  - [Brace of drakefire pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-drakefire-pistols.md) (+10 points)
  - [Drakegun](../weapons-of-war/drakegun.md) (see below) (+10 points)
- An Ironbeard may take [cinderblast bombs](../weapons-of-war/cinderblast-bombs.md) (see opposite) (+15 points)
- An Ironbeard may purchase [Weapon runes](../magic-items/weapon-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 50 points
