# Chosen Chaos Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chosen Chaos Knight | - | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 9 |
| Champion | - | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 3 | 9 |
| Chaos Steed | 7 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 36 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 4+

- **Chaos Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Chaos Steeds:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- The entire unit may take [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (+2 points per model)
- The entire unit may replace the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](../special-rules/mark-of-chaos-undivided.md) with the:

  - [Mark of Khorne](../special-rules/mark-of-khorne.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Slaanesh](../special-rules/mark-of-slaanesh.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a champion (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 100 points
  - Replace [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) with [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) (+3 points per model)
  - Have the Drilled special rule (+2 points per model)
- A Champion may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- The entire unit may purchase up to two [Chaotic Traits](../magic-items/chaotic-traits.md)
