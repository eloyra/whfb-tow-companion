# Dwarf Warriors

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dwarf Warrior | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 9 |
| Veteran | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 9 |

**Cost:** 8 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Veteran (champion) (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Veteran may purchase [Weapon runes](../magic-items/weapon-runes.md) up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 50 points
