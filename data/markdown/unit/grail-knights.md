# Grail Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Grail Knight | - | 6 | 2 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 9 |
| Grail Guardian | - | 6 | 2 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 5 | 3 | 9 |
| Bretonnian Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 38 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 3+

- **Grail Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Bretonnian Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Grail Guardian (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 100 points
- A Grail Guardian may take:

  - A single [Knightly Virtue](../magic-items/knightly-virtues.md)
  - Magic items up to a total of 50 points
