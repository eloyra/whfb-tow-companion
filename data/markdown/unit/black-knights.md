# Black Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Black Knight | - | 3 | 0 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Hell Knight | - | 3 | 0 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 6 |
| Skeletal Steed | 7 | 2 | - | 3 | - | - | 2 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 24 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Black Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Skeletal Steeds:** Skeletal hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take any of the following:

  - [Lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md) (+2 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Hell Knight (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Hell Knight may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
