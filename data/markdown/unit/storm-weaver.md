**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Magic:** Storm Weaver is a Level 1 Wizard who uses spells from [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), or [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md).

- May be mounted on a [Unicorn](unicorn.md) (+35 points)
- A Storm Weaver may:

  - Be a Level 2 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Be a Level 3 Wizard (+60 points)
- Purchase magic items up to a total of 75 points
