# Wild Riders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wild Rider | - | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 9 |
| Wild Hunter | - | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 2 | 9 |
| Steed of Kurnous | 9 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 26 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Wild Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [hunting spears](../weapons-of-war/hunting-spear.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Steeds of Kurnous:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Wild Hunter (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Wild Hunter may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
