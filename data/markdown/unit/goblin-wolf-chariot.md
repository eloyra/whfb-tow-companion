*A Goblin Wolf Chariot may be included in your army as a character's mount. If so, its points are added to that of its rider.*

**Cost:** 53 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/light-chariots.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 100 mm (two wolves), 60 x 100 mm (three wolves)

**Unit Size:** 1-5

**Armour Value:** 5+

- **Goblin Crew:**  [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md)
- **Giant Wolves:** Claws and fangs (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any Goblin Wolf Chariot may add a third Giant Wolf (+5 points per model)
- Any unit may upgrade one model to include a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
