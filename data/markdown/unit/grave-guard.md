**Cost:** 11 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may replace [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with [great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Seneschal (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Seneschal may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Implacable Defence](../special-rules/implacable-defence.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
