# Sorcerer Lord

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sorcerer Lord | 4 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 8 |

**Cost:** 195 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 30 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)

**Magic:** Sorcerer Lord is a Level 3 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Daemonology](../the-lores-of-magic/daemonology.md), [Dark Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/dark-magic.md), or [Lore of the Shadowlands](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-the-shadowlands.md).

- May be mounted:

  - [Chaos Steed](chaos-steed.md) (+16 points)
  - [Daemonic Mount](daemonic-mount.md) (+35 points)
  - [Chaos Chariot](chaos-chariot.md) (+110 points)
  - [Manticore](manticore-warriors-of-chaos.md) (+120 points)
  - [Chaos Dragon](chaos-dragon.md) (+285 points)
- May replace the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](../special-rules/mark-of-chaos-undivided.md) with the:

  - [Mark of Nurgle](../special-rules/mark-of-nurgle.md) (+10 points)
  - [Mark of Slaanesh](../special-rules/mark-of-slaanesh.md) (+10 points)
  - [Mark of Tzeentch](../special-rules/mark-of-tzeentch.md) (+10 points)
- May take [Gifts of Chaos](../magic-items/gifts-of-chaos.md) up to a total of 50 points
- May purchase up to two [Chaotic Traits](../magic-items/chaotic-traits.md)
- A Sorcerer Lord may:

  - Be a Level 4 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 100 points
