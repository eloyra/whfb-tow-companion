**Cost:** 15 points per model

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [drakeguns](../weapons-of-war/drakegun.md) and [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to an Ironwarden (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- An Ironwarden may replace their [drakegun](../weapons-of-war/drakegun.md) with one of the following:

  - [Brace of drakefire pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-drakefire-pistols.md) (+5 points)
  - [Trollhammer torpedo](../weapons-of-war/trollhammer-torpedo.md) (+15 points)
- An Ironwarden may take [cinderblast bombs](../weapons-of-war/cinderblast-bombs.md) (+15 points)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) up to a total of 50 points
