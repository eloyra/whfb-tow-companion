**Cost:** 70 points

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- May take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points)
  - [Halberd](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md) (+3 points)
  - [Lance](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (if appropriately mounted) (+4 points)
- May take one of the following:

  - [Repeater crossbow](../weapons-of-war/repeater-crossbow.md) (+6 points)
  - [Repeater handbow](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handbow.md) (+5 points)
  - [Brace of repeater handbows](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-repeater-handbows.md) (+10 points)
- May take a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+2 points)
- May replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with:

  - [Heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+3 points)
  - [Full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) (+6 points)
- May be mounted:

  - [Dark Steed](dark-steed.md) (+14 points)
  - [Cold One](cold-one-dark-elves.md) (+18 points)
  - [Cold One Chariot](cold-one-chariot.md) (+125 points)
- May have the [Sea Dragon Cloak](../special-rules/sea-dragon-cloak.md) special rule (+4 points)
- A Master may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points

[Eternal Hatred](../special-rules/eternal-hatred.md) [Hatred (High Elves)](../special-rules/hatred.md) [Murderous](../special-rules/murderous.md) [Strike First](../special-rules/strike-first.md)
