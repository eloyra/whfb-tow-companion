# Skeleton Warriors (Tomb Kings)

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skeleton Warrior | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 5 |
| Master of Arms | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 2 | 5 |

**Cost:** 4 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)

- The entire unit may take:

  - [Thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Master of Arms (champion) (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Master of Arms may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Nehekharan Phalanx](../special-rules/nehekharan-phalanx.md) special rule (+10 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
