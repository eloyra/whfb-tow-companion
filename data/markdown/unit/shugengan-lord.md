**Cost:** 220 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Creature](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-creatures.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 60 x 100 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

- **Shugengan:** [Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [iron talons](../weapons-of-war/iron-talons.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Great Spirit Longma:** [Crown of horns](../weapons-of-war/crown-of-horns-grand-cathay.md)

**Magic:** Shugengan Lord is a Level 2 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md), or [High Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/high-magic.md).

- May take one of the following:

  - [Celestial blade](../weapons-of-war/celestial-blade.md) (+6 points)
  - [Cathayan lance](../weapons-of-war/cathayan-lance.md) (+4 points)
- May take a [dragon fire pistol](../weapons-of-war/dragon-fire-pistol.md) (+4 points)
- May replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+4 points)
- Must choose one of the following special rules:

  - [Lore of Yang](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yang.md) (Free)
  - [Lore of Yin](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yin.md) (Free)
- A Shugengan Lord may:

  - Be a Level 3 Wizard (+30 points)
  - Be a Level 4 Wizard (+60 points)
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 100 points
