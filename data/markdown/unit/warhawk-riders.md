# Warhawk Riders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Wind Rider | - | 4 | 5 | 3 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 2 | 8 |
| Warhawk | 2 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 4 | 2 | - |

**Cost:** 44 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 50 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 3+

- **Warhawk Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [Asrai longbows](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md)
- **Warhawks:** [Wicked claws](../weapons-of-war/wicked-claws.md)

- Any unit may take one of the following types of enchanted arrows:

  - [Arcane bodkins](../weapons-of-war/arcane-bodkins.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Hagbane tips](../weapons-of-war/hagbane-tips.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Moonfire shot](../weapons-of-war/moonfire-shot.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Swiftshiver shards](../weapons-of-war/swiftshiver-shards.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Trueflight arrows](../weapons-of-war/trueflight-arrows.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may upgrade one model to a Wind Rider (champion) (+8 points per unit)
- A Wind Rider may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
