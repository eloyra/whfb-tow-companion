# Ship's Company

**Cost:** 9 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [warbows](../weapons-of-war/warbow.md)

- The entire unit may replace [warbows](../weapons-of-war/warbow.md) with [thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (Free)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Midshipman (champion) (+5 points per unit)
  - Or: Upgrade one model to a Bosun (champion) (+5 points per unit)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- The entire unit may take [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Replace the [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) special rule with [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) (Free)
  - Replace the [Valour of Ages](../special-rules/valour-of-ages.md) special rule with [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) (+1 point per model)
