[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md)

  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)\
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 100 points
- An Overseer may take one of the following:

  - [Pistol](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md) (+3 points)
