# Archmage

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Archmage | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 5 | 2 | 8 |

**Cost:** 155 points

**Unit Category:** [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) [Character](../troop-types-in-detail/characters-troop-types.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 1

[Hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)

**Magic:** Archmage is a Level 3 Wizard who uses spells from [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md), [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md), [High Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/high-magic.md), or [Illusion](../the-lores-of-magic/illusion.md).

- May be mounted:

  - [Elven Steed](elven-steed.md) (+14 points)
  - [Barded Elven Steed](barded-elven-steed.md) (+18 points)
  - [Moon Dragon](moon-dragon.md) (+235 points)
  - [Star Dragon](star-dragon.md) (+290 points)
  - [Great Eagle](great-eagle-mount.md) (+60 points)
- May take a single [Elven Honour](../magic-items/elven-honours.md)

  - A character with the [Chracian Hunter](../magic-item/chracian-hunter.md) Elven Honour may be mounted on a [Lion Chariot](lion-chariot-of-chrace.md) (+75 points)
  - A character with the [Sea Guard](../magic-item/sea-guard.md) Elven Honour may be mounted on a [Lothern Skycutter](lothern-skycutter.md) (+90 points)
  - A character with the [Blood of Caledor](../magic-item/blood-of-caledor.md) Elven Honour may be mounted on a [Sun Dragon](sun-dragon.md) (+180 points)
  - A character with the [Anointed of Asuryan](../magic-item/anointed-of-asuryan.md) Elven Honour may be mounted on a:

    - [Flamespyre Phoenix](flamespyre-phoenix-mount.md) (+170 points)
    - [Frostheart Phoenix](frostheart-phoenix-mount.md) (+205 points)
- An Archmage may:

  - Be a Level 4 Wizard +30 points
  - Purchase magic items up to a total of 100 points
