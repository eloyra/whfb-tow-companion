# Border Princes Brigands

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Brigand | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Desperado | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 7 |

**Cost:** 4 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 10+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Pistol](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Blunderbuss](../weapons-of-war/blunderbuss.md) (+4 points per model)
  - [Crossbow](../weapons-of-war/crossbow.md) (+3 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Desperado (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- A Desperado may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Replace the [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) special rule with [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) (Free)
  - Replace the Open Order special rule with [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) (Free)
- A unit of Skirmishers may take one of the following special rules:

  - [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) (+1 point per model)
