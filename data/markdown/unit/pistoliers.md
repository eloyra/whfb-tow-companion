# Pistoliers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pistolier | - | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 6 |
| Veteran | - | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Empire Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 16 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Pistoliers:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) and [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md)
- **Empire Warhorse:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Veteran (champion) (+8 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- A Veteran may replace their brace of pistols with:

  - [Grenade launching blunderbuss](../weapons-of-war/grenade-launching-blunderbuss.md) (+5 points)
  - [Repeater handgun](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md) (+5 points)
  - [Repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) (Free)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may have the Vanguard special rule (+2 points per model)
