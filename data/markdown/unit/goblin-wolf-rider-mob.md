# Goblin Wolf Rider Mob

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wolf Rider | - | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 5 |
| Boss | - | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 6 |
| Giant Wolf | 9 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 10 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 50 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Wolf Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Giant Wolves**: Claws and fangs (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may:

  - Take [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (Free)  
    Or:
  - Replace [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) with [shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (Free)
- The entire unit may take [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Boss (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+5 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+5 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the Feigned Flight special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the Reserve Move special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
