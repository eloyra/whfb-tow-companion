# Hireling Outriders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Captain | - | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 7 |
| Warhorse | 8 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 11 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Hireling Outriders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)
- **Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit must take one of the following:

  - [Cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Pistols](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md) (+3 points per model)
  - [Brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md) (+6 points per model)
- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+3 points per model)
- The entire unit may take [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Captain (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Feigned Flight](../special-rules/feigned-flight.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
