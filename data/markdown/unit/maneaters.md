# Maneaters

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maneater | 6 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 8 |
| Maneater Captain | 6 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 5 | 8 |

**Cost:** 54 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Monstrous Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 40 x 40 mm

**Unit Size:** 2+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)

- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points per model)
  - [Ironfist](../weapons-of-war/ironfist.md) (+4 points per model)
  - [Great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+4 points per model)
- Any model in the unit may take one of the following:

  - [Ogre pistol](../weapons-of-war/ogre-pistol.md) (+6 points per model)
  - [Brace of Ogre pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-ogre-pistols.md) (+12 points per model)
- Any model in the unit may replace [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) with [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) (+3 points per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Maneater Captain (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a Bellower (musician) (+7 points per unit)
- A Maneater Captain may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
- A standard bearer may have the [Look-out Gnoblar](../special-rules/look-out-gnoblar.md) special rule (+5 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- Any unit may have 0-2 of the following special rules:

  - [Immune to Psychology](../special-rules/immune-to-psychology.md) (Free)
  - [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) (Free)
  - [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) (Free)
  - [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) (Free)
