# Dark Riders

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dark Rider | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Herald | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 8 |
| Dark Steed | 9 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 16 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Dark Riders:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Dark Steeds** **:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may take:

  - [Repeater crossbows](../weapons-of-war/repeater-crossbow.md) (+2 points per model)
  - [Shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Herald (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Fire & Flee](../special-rules/fire-and-flee.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
