# Blood Knights

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Blood Knight | - | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 7 |
| Kastellan | - | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 4 | 3 | 7 |
| Nightmare | 7 | 3 | - | 4 | - | - | 2 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 39 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Blood Knights:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Nightmares:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as [hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Kastellan (champion) (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+7 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+7 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
  - Replace [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) with [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md) (+5 points per model)
  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+3 points per model)
- A Kastellan may take:

  - [Vampiric Powers](../magic-items/vampiric-powers.md) up to a total of 25 points
  - Magic items up to a total of 25 points
