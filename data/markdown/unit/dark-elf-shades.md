| Bloodshade | 5 | 5 | 6 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 5 | 1 | 8 |

**Cost:** 15 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)

**Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

[Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [repeater crossbows](../weapons-of-war/repeater-crossbow.md)

- The entire unit may take one of the following:

  - [Additional hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+1 point per model)
  - [Great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (+2 points per model)
- The entire unit may take [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may upgrade one model to a Bloodshade (champion) (+6 points per unit)
- A Bloodshade may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit in your army may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Chariot Runners](../special-rules/chariot-runners.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
