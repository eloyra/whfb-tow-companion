# Jade Lancers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jade Lancer | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 1 | 8 |
| Jade Lancer Officer | - | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 3 | 2 | 8 |
| Cathayan Warhorse | 7 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 3 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 20 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Heavy Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Jade Lancers:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [Cathayan lances](../weapons-of-war/cathayan-lance.md), [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) and [shields](../weapons-of-war/shield.md)
- **Cathayan Warhorses:** Iron-shod hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) and [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md)

- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Jade Lancer Officer (champion) (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 50 points
- A Jade Lancer Officer may purchase magic items up to a total of 50 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Drilled](../special-rules/drilled.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Have the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rule (+2 point per model)
