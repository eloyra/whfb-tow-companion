# Ellyrian Reavers

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ellyrian Reaver | - | 4 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 1 | 8 |
| Harbinger | - | 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 4 | 2 | 8 |
| Elven Steed | 9 | 3 | - | 3 | - | - | 4 | 1 | - |

**Cost:** 16 points per model

**Unit Category:** [Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)

**Troop Type:** [Light Cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md)

**Base Size:** 30 x 60 mm

**Unit Size:** 5+

- **Elyrian Reavers:** [Hand weapons](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md), [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) and [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md)
- **Elven Steeds:** Hooves (counts as a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md))

- The entire unit may:

  - Take [shortbows](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+2 points per model)  
    Or:
  - Replace [cavalry spears](../weapons-of-war/cavalry-spear.md) with shortbows (+1 point per model)
- Any unit may:

  - Upgrade one model to a Harbinger (champion) (+8 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a standard bearer (+6 points per unit)
  - Upgrade one model to a musician (+6 points per unit)
- A Harbinger may purchase magic items up to a total of 25 points
- 0-1 unit per 1,000 points may:

  - Have the [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) special rule (+2 points per model)
  - Have the [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) special rule (+1 point per model)
  - Purchase a magic standard worth up to 25 points
