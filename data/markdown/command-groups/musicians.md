# Musicians

When an army marches, it does so to the inspirational beat of drums and blare of horns (or the doot of a spectral flute). For trained troops, a prearranged sequence of notes can carry as much information as a bellowed order, and can do so more clearly and with greater speed amidst the tumult of battle.
