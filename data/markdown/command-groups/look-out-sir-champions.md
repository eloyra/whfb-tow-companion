# "Look Out, Sir!" (Champions)

If a champion is hit by a shooting attack, and if there are five or more rank and file models in the unit, the champion can make a "Look Out, Sir!" roll. To make a "Look Out, Sir!" roll, roll a D6. On a roll of 1, the champion is hit. On a roll of 2+, another member of the unit is hit in the champion's place.
