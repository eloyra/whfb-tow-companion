# Trophies of War (Warhammer Battles)

You win a bonus of 50 [Victory Points](victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) for every [enemy standard](../command-groups/trophies-of-war-command-groups.md) claimed as a trophy. Additionally, if the enemy Battle Standard Bearer is slain, has fled off the battlefield or is fleeing when the game ends, you win another bonus of 50 Victory Points.
