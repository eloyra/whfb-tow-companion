# Deployment

All scenarios include a map showing deployment zones and include instructions for the deployment of armies. These instructions might describe a unique method, but most often they will instruct players to follow the [alternating units](alternating-units.md) method. Some scenarios may also instruct players to keep a portion of their army in [reserve](reserves.md).
