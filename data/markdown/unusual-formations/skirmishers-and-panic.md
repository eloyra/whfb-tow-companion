# Skirmishers & Panic

Skirmishers cause [panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) in friendly units as normal should they be annihilated or Break and flee. Skirmishers cause panic in friendly Skirmishers if they flee through them as normal. However, Skirmishers do not cause panic in formed friendly units of any type (be they [Close Order](../forming-units/close-order-formation.md), [Open Order](open-order-formation.md) and so on) that they themselves flee through.
