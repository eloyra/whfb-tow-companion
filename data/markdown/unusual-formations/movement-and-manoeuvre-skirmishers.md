# Movement & Manoeuvre (Skirmishers)

Each model within a unit of Skirmishers moves as an individual. The unit does not turn, wheel or otherwise manoeuvre (although it may reform to adopt a different formation, if possible). Instead, each model is free to move in any direction without penalty, provided unit coherency is maintained.
