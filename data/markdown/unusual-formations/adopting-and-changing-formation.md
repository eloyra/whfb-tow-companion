# Adopting & Changing Formation

All units must adopt a formation. As mentioned previously, the type(s) of formation a unit can adopt are indicated by a special rule of the same name as the formation type. Models with more than one such special rule may choose their formation during [deployment](../warhammer-battles/deployment.md), and may change it and adopt a different formation by [reforming](../movement-in-detail/reform.md) during the game.
