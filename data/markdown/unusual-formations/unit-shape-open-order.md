# Unit Shape (Open Order)

As with units in [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) formation, the shape of a unit in Open Order is also important as such units gain bonuses in certain situations. Based upon this, such units can be said to be in [Combat Order](combat-order-open-order.md) or in [Marching Column](../forming-units/marching-column.md).
