# Skirmish Formation

A unit of models in Skirmish formation (often referred to as 'Skirmishers' in the rules that follow) never consists of rigid ranks and files. Instead, it moves as a single loose group or rough line. This enables Skirmishers to move quickly and take advantage of terrain to shelter from the enemy.
