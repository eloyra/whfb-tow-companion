# Marching Column (Unusual Formations)

See also: [Marching Column](../forming-units/marching-column.md)

A unit that is deeper than it is wide (i.e., that has more models per file than per rank) is said to be in [Marching Column](../forming-units/marching-column.md). A Marching Column cannot claim a [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) and cannot make a charge move, but may triple its Movement characteristic when [Marching](../movement-in-detail/marching.md).
