# Marching Column

A unit that is deeper than it is wide (i.e., that has more models per file than per rank, as shown in Fig 101.2) is said to be in Marching Column. A Marching Column cannot claim a [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) and cannot make a charge move, but may triple its Movement characteristic when [Marching](../movement-in-detail/marching.md).
