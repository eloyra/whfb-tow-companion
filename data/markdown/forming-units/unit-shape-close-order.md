# Unit Shape (Close Order)

The shape of a unit in [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) is also important as such units gain bonuses in certain situations. Based upon this, such units can be said to be in [Combat Order](combat-order-close-order.md) or in [Marching Column](marching-column.md).
