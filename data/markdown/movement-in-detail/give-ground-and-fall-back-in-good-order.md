# Give Ground & Fall Back in Good Order

The rules will often call for units to [Give Ground](give-ground.md) or [Fall Back in Good Order](fall-back-in-good-order.md). Most often, they will do this after losing a round of combat or after suffering heavy casualties from enemy shooting.
