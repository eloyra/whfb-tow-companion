# Charging Through Terrain

A charging unit must move by the shortest route possible to reach its charge target. Therefore, charging units will often be obliged to move through difficult or dangerous terrain, or across [low linear obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md). This is perfectly acceptable, but a charge made through any form of terrain is more difficult, and often much slower, than a charge made across open ground.

Before making a charging unit's Charge roll, check to see if any part of the unit will move through difficult or dangerous terrain, or across a low linear obstacle, when it makes its charge move. If so, that unit must discard the highest result when making its Charge roll, rather than the lowest, and will suffer a -1 modifier to its Movement characteristic (to a minimum of 1) when making its charge move.

Finally, if a unit ends its charge move with a quarter (25%) or more of its models within difficult terrain, or straddling a low linear obstacle, it becomes [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) and cannot claim a [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md).
