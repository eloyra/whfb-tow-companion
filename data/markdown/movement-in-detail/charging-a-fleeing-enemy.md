# Charging a Fleeing Enemy

When a charged unit turns tail and flees, the charging unit has two options: attempt to [run down](running-down-the-foe.md) an enemy that [flees](../the-movement-phase/flee-charge-reaction.md) from it, or attempt to [redirect](redirecting-a-charge.md) its charge.
