# Fleeing from an Enemy Unit

When the rules call for a unit to flee directly away from an enemy unit, pivot the fleeing unit about its centre so that it is facing directly away from the centre of the enemy unit it is fleeing from, as shown in Fig 132.1. After pivoting, the unit makes an immediate flee move, as shown in Fig 132.2.

![132-1-fleeing-from-enemy-unit](//images.ctfassets.net/0v8db9sevlqj/5AuWUO5tIhe7KikZsUugms/ae71626af59caf2a4c7b70d7fe9618f1/132-1-fleeing-from-enemy-unit.png?fm=jpg&fl=progressive) 

Fig 132.1 - 1. Unit A is fleeing from unit B. Therefore, unit A pivots around its centre point until facing directly away from unit B.

 ![132-2-fleeing-from-enemy-unit](//images.ctfassets.net/0v8db9sevlqj/3phB5PWyUxSTdI1z3MxTdA/11ccebebaa5d5c51f35dc1872784c75f/132-2-fleeing-from-enemy-unit.png?fm=jpg&fl=progressive) 

Fig 132.2 - 2. Unit A then moves forward a distance equal to its Flee roll.
