# Low Linear Obstacles (Movement)

See also: [Low Linear Obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md)

For the purposes of movement, low linear obstacles (i.e., less than 2" high), such as walls and hedges, are treated as [difficult terrain](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md).
