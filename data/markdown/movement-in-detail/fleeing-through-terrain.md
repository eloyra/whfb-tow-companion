# Fleeing Through Terrain

A fleeing unit moves through difficult and [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md) without suffering any negative modifiers to its Movement characteristic, though it must make any Dangerous Terrain tests required. Should a fleeing unit come into contact with impassable terrain, it must [pivot](pivoting.md) around its centre in order to move around it by the shortest possible route.
