# High Linear Obstacles (Movement)

See also: [High Linear Obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/high-linear-obstacles.md)

For the purposes of movement, high linear obstacles (i.e., 2" high or more), such as castle walls, are treated as [impassable terrain](impassable-terrain-movement.md).
