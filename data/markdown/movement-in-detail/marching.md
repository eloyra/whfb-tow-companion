# Marching

Most units are able to 'march', allowing them to move rapidly across the battlefield. A marching unit can double its Movement characteristic. Whilst marching a unit can wheel to change direction, but cannot perform any other manoeuvres. However, a unit that marched in the [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase) cannot shoot during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) (including casting [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) or [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) spells).

*Note that whilst in Marching Column, a*  [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md)  *formation may triple its Movement characteristic when Marching.*
