### [Can a Necrosphinx make more than one attack each turn with its Decapitating strike?](#can-a-necrosphinx-make-more-than-one-attack-each-turn-with-its-decapitating)

No. The decapitating strike profile must be used as noted – to make one additional attack.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Are the effects of the Casket of Souls' Bound spell 'Light of Protection' cumulative?](#are-the-effects-of-the-casket-of-souls-bound-spell-light-of-protection)

No.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a Casket of Souls move at the end of the Shooting phase of its turn using the Reserve Move special rule (should it have it)?](#can-a-casket-of-souls-move-at-the-end-of-the-shooting-phase-of-its-turn)

Yes. Whilst a Casket of Souls is an Immovable Object, this special rule clearly states that it prevents the model moving during the Remaining Moves sub-phase. It does not prevent movement made at other times/during other phases.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Is it possible to resurrect models in a unit of Skirmishers?](#is-it-possible-to-resurrect-models-in-a-unit-of-skirmishers)

Yes, simply place the resurrected models in coherency (i.e., within 1" of another model) with the rest of the unit.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
