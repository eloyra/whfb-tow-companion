### [If a unit is limited to, for example, 0-3 per specific type of character, or 0-3 per 1,000 points, how many such units can I field in a 2,000 points army at an event using Combined Arms army composition?](#if-a-unit-is-limited-to-for-example-0-3-per-specific-type-of-character-or-0)

You can field up to three per character in the first example, and up to six in total in the second example. Combined Arms composition rules only restrict units with no other restriction on them. If a unit has any form of restriction, that takes precedence.

Note that 1+, for example, is a restriction.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a unit control more than one object on the battlefield? For example, can a unit control a special feature, a battlefield objective, and a secondary objective such as a baggage train, all at the same time?](#can-a-unit-control-more-than-one-object-on-the-battlefield-for-example-can-a)

No. If a unit is in a position to control two or more such objects, you must choose one for it to control.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
