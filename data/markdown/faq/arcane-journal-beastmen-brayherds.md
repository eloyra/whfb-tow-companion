### [If a Beastmen Brayherds army includes an allied contingent of Beastmen, can the army contain two Herdstones, one in the main force and one in the allied contingent?](#if-a-beastmen-brayherds-army-includes-an-allied-contingent-of-beastmen-can)

No. You can only include 0-1 Herdstone per army and, whilst an allied contingent may be in effect a small army within a larger army, in this regard you are ultimately playing with but one army.

However, if two players partnered for a doubles game (as defined in the Matched Play Guide), they could each take a Herdstone.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [How does the Dark Coven army special rule from the Wild Herd Army of Infamy interact with the Plague Chalice?](#how-does-the-dark-coven-army-special-rule-from-the-wild-herd-army-of-infamy)

In cases such as this, both outcomes can apply. If a natural double is rolled, the spell is cast as a perfect invocation and the caster suffers a hit from the Plague Chalice. However, the caster and any other members of their coven will then also suffer the effects of a roll on the Miscast table, as per the Dark Coven special rule.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Does the Daemon Heart increase its user’s Dispel Range?](#does-the-daemon-heart-increase-its-users-dispel-range)

No.
