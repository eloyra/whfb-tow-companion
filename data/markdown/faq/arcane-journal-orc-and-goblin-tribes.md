# Arcane Journal: Orc & Goblin Tribes - Frequently Asked Questions

[View complete list](../faq.md) of all frequently asked questions.

Currently displaying all results.

### [Ogdruz Swampdigga has a Lore Familiar and knows spells from both the Lore of Elementalism and the Lore of Troll Magic. Can he choose his spells from both lores, or from just one?](#ogdruz-swampdigga-has-a-lore-familiar-and-knows-spells-from-both-the-lore-of)

He can choose from both.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If I take a Black Orc Warboss as the General of a Nomadic Waaagh!, can I take a unit of Boar Boys as a Core choice?](#if-i-take-a-black-orc-warboss-as-the-general-of-a-nomadic-waaagh-can-i-take)

No. A unit of Boar Boys can only be taken as a Core choice if your General is an Orc Warboss.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [In a Nomadic Waaagh!, I can take one Black Orc Boss per Black Orc Chariot. If I take a Black Orc Boss mounted on a chariot, does that count?](#in-a-nomadic-waaagh-i-can-take-one-black-orc-boss-per-black-orc-chariot-if-i)

No. You can take one Black Orc Boss per Black Orc Chariot taken as a Core or Rare choice. It does not matter what the Boss is mounted on.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a Black Orc Boss take a Black Orc Chariot as a mount in an army made using the Orc and Goblin Tribes Grand Army composition list?](#can-a-black-orc-boss-take-a-black-orc-chariot-as-a-mount-in-an-army-made)

They cannot. With heavy hearts, we must inform everyone who asked that that option is only available in armies made using the Nomadic Waaagh! Army of Infamy composition list.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a Troll Hag be the General of an Orc and Goblin Tribes army made using the Grand Army composition list?](#can-a-troll-hag-be-the-general-of-an-orc-and-goblin-tribes-army-made-using)

No. A Troll Hag is a Rare choice in the Orc and Goblin Tribes Grand Army composition list. She can, however, be the General of a Troll Horde.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a Troll Hag purchases a magic weapon, are any of its special rules conferred onto Motherly Love attacks?](#if-a-troll-hag-purchases-a-magic-weapon-are-any-of-its-special-rules)

No. If she attacks normally, she can use her magic weapon. If she makes a Motherly Love attack, she does not use her magic weapon.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can the size of a range 'self' spell's aura of effect be increased by the Idol of Gork?](#can-the-size-of-a-range-self-spells-aura-of-effect-be-increased-by-the-idol)

No. The aura of effect of a range 'self' spell is not its range.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
