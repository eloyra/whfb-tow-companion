### [Can the Hagtree Fetish be used to re-roll rolls To Wound caused by a Bound Spell?](#can-the-hagtree-fetish-be-used-to-re-roll-rolls-to-wound-caused-by-a-bound)

No. Bound Spells are cast by the item they are bound to, whilst Arcane Items such as the Hagtree Fetish affect spells cast by their bearer. Therefore, unless an item specifies it has an effect upon Bound Spells, it does not.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Does a unit with the Blood Greed special rule always roll only a single D6 when making a Pursuit roll, or only whilst Frenzied?](#does-a-unit-with-the-blood-greed-special-rule-always-roll-only-a-single-d6)

Only whilst Frenzied.
