### [Does the Martial Prowess special rule allow models to make supporting attacks with weapons that do not have the Fight in Extra Rank special rule?](#does-the-martial-prowess-special-rule-allow-models-to-make-supporting)

No. Martial Prowess grants the ability to make supporting attacks to the flank or rear, as well as to its front. It does not grant the ability to make supporting attacks to models not normally able to do so.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
