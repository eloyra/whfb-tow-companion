### [To defend a low linear obstacle, a unit must move its front rank into base contact with it. Are Skirmishers able to do this, or does their lack of ranks prevent it?](#to-defend-a-low-linear-obstacle-a-unit-must-move-its-front-rank-into-base)

Skirmishers can defend a low linear obstacle. To do so, simply move as many Skirmishers as possible into base contact with the feature.
