### [When fighting a Warrior's Duel challenge between two unit champions, are such things as magic standards carried by their units counted?](#when-fighting-a-warriors-duel-challenge-between-two-unit-champions-are-such)

No. A Warrior's Duel is fought between the two participants alone, without any support from their units.
