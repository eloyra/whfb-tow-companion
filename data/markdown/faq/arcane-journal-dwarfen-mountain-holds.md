### [Do runes inscribed upon a Doomseeker’s whirling blades of death apply to Impact Hits?](#do-runes-inscribed-upon-a-doomseekers-whirling-blades-of-death-apply-to)

Yes. As stated under Whirlwind of Death, Impact Hits caused by a Doomseeker are resolved using the profile of its whirling blades of death, which includes any runes the weapon is inscribed with.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a Doomseeker join a unit of Slayers?](#can-a-doomseeker-join-a-unit-of-slayers)

No, as per the errata, a Doomseeker is not a character. They are loners and outcasts even amongst Slayers and, as such, act as a unit of one model.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [When a Goblin Hewer shoots using the Whirling Death special rule, does it add a D3 modifier to the number of shots rolled for each rank or file, or just a single D3 modifier?](#when-a-goblin-hewer-shoots-using-the-whirling-death-special-rule-does-it-add)

Just a single D3 modifier. You do not get an extra D3 per rank of file.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
