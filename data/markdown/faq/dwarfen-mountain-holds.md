### [If I give armour runes to an Anvil of Doom, does the Anvil itself benefit, or just the crew?](#if-i-give-armour-runes-to-an-anvil-of-doom-does-the-anvil-itself-benefit-or)

Just the crew.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can the champion in a unit of Royal Clan Warriors or Hammerers put weapon runes on a gromril great axe or great hammer?](#can-a-royal-champion-in-a-unit-of-hammerers-put-weapon-runes-on-their-great)

No. Weapon runes can only be inscribed upon a hand weapon or great weapon, as defined in the Warhammer: the Old World rulebook. The Royal Clan Warriors' gromril great axes and the Hammerers' great hammers are unique types of weapon, each with their own profile and rules.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [When a model with the Slayer, Slayer of Daemons or Slayer of Dragons special rule hits an enemy unit using the Deathblow special rule, does that hit benefit from the Slayer special rule?](#when-a-model-with-the-slayer-slayer-of-daemons-or-slayer-of-dragons-special)

Yes.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If the musician and/or standard bearer in a unit of Slayers are models that have been upgraded to Giant Slayers, what happens if they are slain, is their instrument and/or standard picked up by another Giant Slayer or by a Troll Slayer?](#if-the-musician-and-or-standard-bearer-in-a-unit-of-slayers-are-models-that)

It rather depends on the models that remain in the unit. If any Giant Slayers remain, they will pick up the instrument or standard, replacing the slain models like for like. If no Giant Slayers remain, a Troll Slayer will take their place.

Note also that Giant Slayers are not unit champions. Thus in a unit of Slayers, the standard bearer would be the last model removed.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a unit bearing a standard inscribed with the Master Rune of Hesitation Falls Back in Good Order and is pursued and caught by an enemy unit, does the rune take effect?](#if-a-unit-bearing-a-standard-inscribed-with-the-master-rune-of-hesitation)

Yes. When a pursuing unit catches an enemy unit that Fell Back in Good Order, it counts as having charged that unit.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a unit of enemy infantry charges a unit with the Master Rune of Hesitation, can it make use of the Press of Battle rule to fight in two ranks?](#if-a-unit-of-enemy-infantry-charges-a-unit-with-the-master-rune-of)

No. Although the Rune prevents a charging enemy unit from using certain weapons or special rules, the unit has still charged, and thus cannot make use of the Press of Battle rule.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a unit of enemy infantry charges a unit with the Master Rune of Hesitation, does it gain the Initiative bonus for charging?](#if-a-unit-of-enemy-infantry-charges-a-unit-with-the-master-rune-of-initiative)

Yes. The Master Rune of Hesitation only prevents the use of certain weapons and special rules.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
