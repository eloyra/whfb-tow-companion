### [If Harald Gemunsen issues a second challenge using his Skilled Duellist special rule, he can fight again immediately. Can his opponent in that challenge fight again?](#if-harald-gemunsen-issues-a-second-challenge-using-his-skilled-duellist)

Yes. If Harald's second challenge is accepted it is fought immediately, meaning both Harald and his opponent can fight. However, Harald's mount may not fight, and nor may his opponent's.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [A unit of Empire Road Wardens may replace its crossbows with braces of pistols for +1 point per model. If it does so, must the Captain (champion) pay an additional +2 points to do the same?](#a-unit-of-empire-road-wardens-may-replace-its-crossbows-with-braces-of)

No. If the entire unit replaces its crossbows with braces of pistols, that includes the Captain. If the unit is armed with crossbows, the Captain has the option to replace theirs with a brace of pistols or a repeater pistol.

Source: Official Forces of Fantasy FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
