# Lores of Magic - Frequently Asked Questions

### [If the spell Arcane Urgency is used to move a unit with Random Movement, can the unit move into contact with an enemy unit?](#if-the-spell-arcane-urgency-is-used-to-move-a-unit-with-random-movement-can)

No. Whilst a unit with Random Movement can move into base contact with an enemy unit during the Compulsory Moves sub-phase, Arcane Urgency causes units to move when the spell is cast, during the Remaining Moves sub-phase.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a unit upon which Earthen Ramparts has been cast is obliged to declare a charge (if the unit is Frenzied or Impetuous, for example), must it do so? If so, can it make a charge move?](#if-a-unit-upon-which-earthen-ramparts-has-been-cast-is-obliged-to-declare-a)

Earthen Ramparts does not prevent a unit from declaring a charge, it prevents it from charging. In other words, yes the unit must declare a charge if obliged to do so. However, because the unit cannot charge, it does not move at all and the charge is failed.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can a unit upon which Earthen Ramparts has been cast make a Counter Charge charge reaction?](#can-a-unit-upon-which-earthen-ramparts-has-been-cast-make-a-counter-charge)

No.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [If a unit charges an enemy unit upon which Earthen Ramparts has been cast, must it follow all the rules for charging an enemy unit behind a defended low linear obstacle, even though there is no such physical terrain between the two units?](#if-a-unit-charges-an-enemy-unit-upon-which-earthen-ramparts-has-been-cast)

Yes. A unit upon which Earthen Ramparts has been cast counts as being behind a defended low linear obstacle when charged. Think of the spell’s effect as representing a magically manifested obstacle, rather than a physical terrain feature.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [Can the spell Spectral Doppelganger from the Lore of Illusion be used with a magic weapon that allows the wielder to make only a single attack?](#can-the-spell-spectral-doppelganger-from-the-lore-of-illusion-be-used-with-a)

No. Weapons limited to a single attack can only inflict a single hit.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [How does the Spectral Doppelganger spell interact with weapons that have either the Strike First or Strike Last special rule?](#how-does-the-spectral-doppelganger-spell-interact-with-weapons-that-have)

The spell is cast at the Wizard's Initiative, meaning attacks generated by the spell are made at the Wizard's Initiative. However, if the casting Wizard wishes to use a weapon with the Strike Last special rule, those attacks are made at Initiative 1.

Source: Official Warhammer: The Old World FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
