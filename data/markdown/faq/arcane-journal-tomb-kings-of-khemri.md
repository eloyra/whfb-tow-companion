### [When a Tomb Scorpion or unit of Necroserpents makes use of the Terrors Below special rule, is the number of Initiative tests the enemy unit takes equal to its own Unit Strength, or equal to the Unit Strength of the unit using the special rule?](#when-a-tomb-scorpion-or-unit-of-necroserpents-makes-use-of-the-terrors-below)

The latter. This special rule represents an emerging unit displacing earth and dragging infantry to their doom. How much earth the unit can displace is determined by its own Unit Strength, not by that of the enemy.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2

### [The crew of Tomb Guard chariots are equipped with shields. Does this improve the Armour Value of the model from 4+ to 3+?](#the-crew-of-tomb-guard-chariots-are-equipped-with-shields-does-this-improve)

No. A chariot's given Armour Value takes into account such equipment.

Source: Official Ravening Hordes FAQ & Errata - Version 1.5.2
