# Characters & Troop Type

Just like any other model, character models have a [troop type](https://tow.whfb.app/troop-types-in-detail). In most cases, this will be a sub-category of [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md), although some characters, such as Greater Daemons of Chaos, are so large that their troop type will be a sub-category of [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md). In addition, the majority of characters can be mounted in some way and, if they are, their troop type will change accordingly.
