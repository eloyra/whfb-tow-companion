# "Hold Your Ground"

To represent the Battle Standard's steadying presence, unless your Battle Standard Bearer is fleeing, friendly units within the Battle Standard Bearer's Command range may re-roll any failed Panic or Rally test. In addition, friendly units within the Battle Standard Bearer's Command range may re-roll the 2D6 when making a Break test. However, you must accept the result of the second roll, even if it is worse than the first.
