# Lone Characters

Whilst many characters ride to battle aboard a chariot or atop a monster, many more bold leaders and dangerous fighters prefer to move around the battlefield as individuals, fighting a solitary battle against the foe:

- Any character whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) and that has not joined a unit is considered to be a 'Lone character'.

During the course of a game, a Lone character may join a [friendly unit](characters-and-units.md), bringing their might and leadership to the unit to strengthen its ranks. Upon joining a unit, a character ceases to be a Lone character.

A Lone character benefits from the following rules:

- [Evade](evade.md)
- [Targeting Lone Characters](targeting-lone-characters.md)
