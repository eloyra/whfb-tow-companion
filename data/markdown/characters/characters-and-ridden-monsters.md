# Characters & Ridden Monsters

If a character is mounted upon a ridden monster (be it a [monstrous creature](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-creatures.md) or a [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md)), the whole model is treated as being of the mount's sub-category of troop type.
