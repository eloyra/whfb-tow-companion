# Selecting Your General

The General is the character in your army with the highest Leadership. If more than one character shares the highest Leadership value, you may choose which one is the General when writing your muster list. You must tell your opponent which model is your General before deploying your army.

*Note that a character with the*  [Loner](../special-rules/loner.md)  *special rule cannot be your General.*
