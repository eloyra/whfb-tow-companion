# Custom Scenarios

Some players may wish to create their own scenarios, either by utilising different elements of the pitched battles scenarios, or by creating their own special rules. Such scenarios can be assembled to tell a simple story, and you will find a table of [narrative motives](narrative-motives.md) intended to provide ideas for your own games.
