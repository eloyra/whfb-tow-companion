# Narrative Scenarios

There are many examples of narrative scenarios to be found in Warhammer: the Old World supplements. These provide unique deployment maps and maps of terrain to recreate, along with special rules to use. Narrative scenarios tend to be created along the lines of historical recreations, giving guidance as to the forces to use in order to represent the battle the scenario describes. But, as with the pitched battle scenarios, narrative scenarios can be played with any combination of armies.
