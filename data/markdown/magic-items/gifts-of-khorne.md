# Gifts of Khorne

The following Daemonic Gifts can only be purchased by Daemons of Khorne:

[Spell Eater](../magic-item/spell-eater.md) (Ability) 50 points

*Like a predator feasting upon the flesh of its prey, the Daemon hungrily consumes the magic of its enemies.*

This model may be nominated to attempt a [Wizardly Dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md), as if it were a Wizard. For the purposes of Wizardly Dispel attempts, this model counts as a Level 2 Wizard.

[Armour of Khorne](../magic-item/armour-of-khorne.md) (Ability) 40 points

*Clad in rune-laden armour, the Daemon's hide is all but immune to the blows of mere mortals.*

The Armour of Khorne is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.

[Axe of Khorne\*](../magic-item/axe-of-khorne.md) (Ability) 35 points

*Screaming with the power of a bound Daemon, this unholy blade thirsts for the blood of mortals.*

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Collar of Khorne\*](../magic-item/collar-of-khorne.md) (Ability) 25 points

*The dread sigils inscribed upon this brass necklace provide protection against the most powerful of magics.*

The wearer of a Collar of Khorne improves its [Magic Resistance](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) by 1. For example, a model with Magic Resistance (-2) wearing a Collar of Khorne would have Magic Resistance (-3).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Might of Khorne](../magic-item/might-of-khorne.md) (Ability) 25 points

*Blessed by its patron, the Daemon's massive limbs bulge with strength and power.*

This model has a +1 modifier to its Strength characteristic.
