# Skaven Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Skaven armies. These can be purchased by models within a Skaven army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Weapons

[The Fellblade](../magic-item/the-fellblade.md) (Magic Weapon) 100 points

*This most terrible of weapons was forged to slay Nagash himself. No foe can stand before it and even the wielder must eventually succumb to its baleful effects.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+3 | N/A | - |

***Notes:***  *Characters whose Troop Type is*  [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md)  *only. No*  [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) *,*  [Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md)  *or*  [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md)  *saves are permitted against wounds caused by this weapon. However, during the Command sub-phase of their turn, the wielder of the Fellblade must roll a D3. On a roll of 1, they lose a single Wound.*

[Weeping Blade](../magic-item/weeping-blade.md) (Magic Weapon) 50 points

*This weapon weeps a venom so corrosive it can melt through armour with ease to deeply pierce and poison the flesh beneath.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -2 | - |

[Blade of Nurglitch](../magic-item/blade-of-nurglitch.md) (Magic Weapon) 35 points

*Blessed by the Plaguelord of Clan Pestilens, even a scratch from this filth-encrusted weapon can turn into instant infection.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+1 | - | - |

***Notes:***  *Any enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Blade of Nurglitch must immediately make a Toughness test. If this test is failed, they suffer a -1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic (to a minimum of 1) for the remainder of the game.*

[Death Globe\*](../magic-item/death-globe.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*Warlock Engineers often carry much improved and far more deadly versions of the globes wielded by Poisoned Wind Globadiers.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 9" | N/A | N/A | - |

***Notes:***  *Single use. A model armed with a Death Globe must target a specific model within its target unit, such as a champion or a character. Make a roll To Hit as normal. If successful, centre a small (3") blast template over the target model. Any model whose base lies underneath the template must make an Initiative test. If this test is failed, they lose a single Wound.*

## Magic Armour

[Warpstone Armour\*](../magic-item/warpstone-armour.md) (Magic Armour) 25 points

*Cunningly wrought of warpstone-infused iron, this heavy suit of armour protects without stifling the Winds of Magic.*

The Warpstone Armour is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) which may be purchased and worn by a Wizard, even if they do not have the option to be equipped with armour, a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) or [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md), without penalty.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Cautious Shield](../magic-item/cautious-shield.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*This large, magical shield emphasises an already prominent trait of Skaven nature.*

The Cautious Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer may choose to wield it with two hands when their combat is chosen during Step 1.1 of any [Choose & Fight Combat](../the-combat-phase/choose-and-fight-combat.md) sub-phase. If they do, until the end of this [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) it improves its [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) by 3, but their Attacks characteristic is reduced to 0.

## Talismans

[Shadow Magnet](../magic-item/shadow-magnet.md) (Talisman) 40 points

*Warpstone-contaminated veins of ore can absorb light and cast an aura of deep, shadowy darkness.*

Any enemy model that targets the bearer of the Shadow Magnet or any unit they have joined during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Warpstone Amulet](../magic-item/warpstone-amulet.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*The Warpstone Amulet radiates disharmony and confusion, so that anyone who attacks its bearer becomes dazed and bewildered.*

The Warpstone Amulet gives its bearer a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.

## Magic Standards

[Storm Banner](../magic-item/storm-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 65 points

*This ancient banner possesses the power to wrack the sky with storms and tear the heavens apart with its fury.*

Single use. A unit carrying the Storm Banner may activate it during the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn. Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, enemy units cannot use the [Fly (X)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule and all enemy shooting suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Grand Banner of Superiority](../magic-item/grand-banner-of-superiority.md) (Magic Standard) 50 points

*This ragged shroud, emblazoned with clan signs, proclaims the superiority of the Warlord Clans.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Grand Banner of Superiority may claim an additional bonus of +D3 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) points.

[Dwarf Hide Banner](../magic-item/dwarf-hide-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*Made from the tanned hides and shorn beards of vanquished Dwarfs, this banner fills the Skaven with the courage to face their ancient enemies.*

A unit carrying the Dwarf Hide Banner gains the [Hatred (Dwarfs)](../special-rules/hatred.md) and [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rules.

[Banner of Verminous Scurrying](../magic-item/banner-of-verminous-scurrying.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*A great, skittering urgency fills those that scurry beneath this tattered banner.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Verminous Scurrying gains the [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rule.

## Enchanted Items

[Brass Orb](../magic-item/brass-orb.md) (Enchanted Item) 50 points

*This fist-sized orb of whirling cogs is capable of opening a crack into the twisting Realm of Chaos.*

Single use. Instead of attacking normally during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the bearer of the Brass Orb can toss it at a specific model within a unit they are engaged in combat with, such as a champion or a character. Make a single roll To Hit. If successful, the target model must make an Initiative test. If this test is failed, the target model is removed from play as a casualty.

[Skalm](../magic-item/skalm.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*This rare healing balm is smeared on wounds and is even rumoured to prolong a Skaven's life.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, the bearer of Skalm can use it. The model immediately recovers a single lost Wound and, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, has a +1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic.

[Skavenbrew\*](../magic-item/skavenbrew.md) (Enchanted Item) 20 points

*Brewed from warpstone and blood, Skavenbrew drives humble Skaven into a fighting frenzy.*

Single use. Skaven Warlords and Skaven Chieftains only. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, this character may attempt to distribute Skavenbrew to a unit they have joined by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next Start of Turn sub-phase this character and any unit they have joined gains the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) special rule.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

## Arcane Items

[Warp Condenser](../magic-item/warp-condenser.md) (Arcane Item) 50 points

*This rune-inscribed power pack is covered with copper coil wiring, flywheels, and enchanted mechanisms to draw extra energy from the fickle Winds of Magic.*

The bearer of the Warp Condenser increases their Dispel range by 3". Additionally, when attempting to cast a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) spell, the bearer of the Warp Condenser gains a +1 modifier to their [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md). Finally, should they miscast a spell, the bearer of the Warp Condenser can re-roll the result rolled on the [Miscast table](../magic/miscast-table.md).

[Storm Daemon](../magic-item/storm-daemon.md) (Arcane Item) 30 points

*Created by the Warlock Engineers of Clan Skryre, this warpstone-powered device crackles with barely contained power.*

This model can cast the following [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

The target enemy unit suffers D3+1 Strength 5 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[Warpstone Tokens\*](../magic-item/warpstone-tokens.md) (Arcane Item) 15 points

*Pieces of refined warpstone are consumed by power-hungry Skaven Wizards.*

A Wizard may purchase up to three Warpstone Tokens, each of which is single use. Before making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md), a Wizard may choose to consume a single Warpstone Token. For each Token consumed, they may roll an additional D3 and add its result to their Casting roll. However, for each natural 1 that is rolled, the Wizard loses a single Wound.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
