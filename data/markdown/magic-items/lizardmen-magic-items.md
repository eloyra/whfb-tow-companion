# Lizardmen Magic Items

## Magic Armour

[Shield of the Mirror Pool](../magic-item/shield-of-the-mirror-pool.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*Hostile enemy magic is absorbed by this ancient shield, and reflected back upon the caster.*

The Shield of the Mirror Pool is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, each time the bearer loses one or more Wounds to a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), the caster suffers a single Strength 5 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[Hide of the Cold Ones](../magic-item/hide-of-the-cold-ones.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*The hide of a Cold One exudes a toxic slime that dulls the wearer's mind.*

May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Hide of the Cold Ones improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). However, its wearer is also subject to the [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) special rule.

## Talismans

[Glyph Necklace](../magic-item/glyph-necklace.md) (Talisman) 45 points

*This ancient token of protection wards off attacks, causing enemies to fumble their blows.*

The bearer of the Glyph Necklace has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and gains the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.

[Aura of Quetzl](../magic-item/aura-of-quetzl.md) (Talisman) 40 points

*Blessed by Quetzl, this string of beaded shells, feathers and bones exudes a powerful protective aura.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against the bearer of the Aura of Quetzl during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.

## Magic Standards

[Sun Standard of Chotec](../magic-item/sun-standard-of-chotec.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*Beams of reflected sunlight blind all who look too long upon the Sun Standard of Chotec.*

Enemy units cannot declare a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction against a unit carrying the Sun Standard of Chotec. In addition, any enemy model that targets a unit carrying this banner during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Skavenpelt Banner](../magic-item/skavenpelt-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*The stench of this ragged Skaven hide fires the blood of those who venerate Sotek.*

A unit carrying the Skavenpelt Banner gains the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) and [Hatred (Skaven)](../special-rules/hatred.md) special rules.

[Totem of Prophecy](../magic-item/totem-of-prophecy.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*Those who look upon this totem witness the truth and learn of their insignificance in the scheme of the Old Ones.*

A unit carrying the Totem of Prophecy gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule.

[Jaguar Standard](../magic-item/jaguar-standard.md) (Magic Standard) 20 points

*Those in the shadow of this jungle beast hunt down their enemies with relentless ferocity.*

When a unit carrying the Jaguar Standard makes a [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, it may roll an extra D6 and discard the lowest result.

## Enchanted Items

[Cloak of Feathers](../magic-item/cloak-of-feathers.md) (Enchanted Item) 40 points

*When the wearer of this finely woven cloak extends their arms, the feathers rise up like powerful wings, lifting them into the air.*

Skink Heroes whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. The wearer of the Cloak of Feathers gains the [Fly (10)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules.

[Venom of the Firefly Frog](../magic-item/venom-of-the-firefly-frog.md) (Enchanted Item) 15 points

*Extracted from the rare and deadly firefly frog, even a tiny amount of this poison can prove fatal.*

All attacks made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) by the bearer of the Venom of the Firefly Frog have the [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) and [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rules.

*Note that this only applies to non-magical weapons and does not apply to a model's mount (should it have one).*

[Horned One\*](../magic-item/horned-one.md) (Enchanted Item) 10 points

*Horned Ones are a very rare, highly intelligent and preternaturally swift sub-species of Cold One.*

This magic item may only be taken by a Saurus Hero mounted on a Cold One. This character's mount loses the [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) special rule and has Movement characteristic of 8.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

## Arcane Items

[Cupped Hands of the Old Ones](../magic-item/cupped-hands-of-the-old-ones.md) (Arcane Item) 55 points

*A sphere of glowing light extends around the bearer as the divine protection of the Old Ones deflects the uncontrolled maelstrom of the Winds of Magic.*

Should the bearer of the Cupped Hands of the Old Ones [miscast](../magic/miscast.md) a spell, roll a D6. On a roll of 1, the bearer rolls on the [Miscast table](../magic/miscast-table.md) as normal. On a roll of 2+, the bearer instead nominates an enemy character. Centre a 3" blast template over this character. Every model whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 6 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[Cube of Darkness\*](../magic-item/cube-of-darkness.md) (Arcane Item) 50 points

*Through careful contemplation, this small black cube may be opened for a fraction of a second, drawing the Winds of Magic into its depths.*

Single use. A Wizard may use this item instead of making a dispel attempt. If they do so, the spell is automatically dispelled with no [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) required. In addition, all [remains in play](../magic/remains-in-play.md) spells currently in play are dispelled, including spells cast by friendly Wizards.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Itxi Grub\*](../magic-item/itxi-grub.md) (Arcane Item) 30 points

*The Wizard carries a gourd containing magically charged (and delicious) Itxi grubs.*

Single use. Before making a Casting roll, the bearer may attempt to consume a single Itxi Grub by making a Toughness test. If this test is passed, the bearer may apply a +3 modifier to the Casting roll. However, if this test is failed, the bearer immediately loses a single Wound.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
