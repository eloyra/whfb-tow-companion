# Icons of Slaanesh

The following Daemonic Icons can only be purchased by Daemons of Slaanesh:

[Banner of Acquiescence](../magic-item/banner-of-acquiescence.md) (Magic Standard) 55 points

*Beneath the shadow of this standard, enemies lower their weapons and bear their breasts to the blades of the foe.*

A single enemy unit engaged in combat with the bearer of the Banner of Acquiescence suffers a -D3 modifier to its Weapon Skill and Initiative characteristics (to a minimum of 1).

[Rapturous Standard](../magic-item/rapturous-standard.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*Gripped by a strange euphoria, enemy formations crumble into confusion in the presence of this standard.*

Any enemy unit that charges the front arc of a unit carrying the Rapturous Standard makes a [disordered charge](../the-combat-phase/disordered-charges.md).

[Siren Standard](../magic-item/siren-standard.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*A Daemonic Siren is imprisoned in this banner, its seductive song lulling the senses of all who hear it.*

If an enemy unit charged by a unit carrying the Siren Standard wishes to [Flee](../the-movement-phase/flee-charge-reaction.md) (or [Fire & Flee](../special-rules/fire-and-flee.md)) as a charge reaction, it must first make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, the unit may Flee (or Fire & Flee). However, if this test is failed, the unit must [Hold](../the-movement-phase/hold.md).
