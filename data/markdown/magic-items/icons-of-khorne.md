# Icons of Khorne

The following Daemonic Icons can only be purchased by Daemons of Khorne:

[Skull Totem](../magic-item/skull-totem.md) (Magic Standard) 50 points

*The pole of this banner is thick-set with the captured skulls of Khorne's victims, each marked with a brass-etched rune of slaughter.*

A unit carrying the Skull Totem (but not its mounts) gains the [Furious Charge](../special-rules/furious-charge.md) special rule.

[Great Standard of Sundering](../magic-item/great-standard-of-sundering.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*Runes of warding crowd close about this banner, their power focussed to deny enemy Wizards access to the Winds of Magic.*

Any enemy Wizard that can draw a line of sight to the model carrying the Great Standard of Sundering suffers a -1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) it makes.

[Icon of Endless War](../magic-item/icon-of-endless-war.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*This brass standard constantly drips blood, the smell of which drives all of Khorne's followers mad with battle-lust.*

When a unit carrying the Banner of Rage declares a [charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md), it may re-roll its Charge roll.
