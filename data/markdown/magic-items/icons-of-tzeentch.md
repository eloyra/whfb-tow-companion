# Icons of Tzeentch

The following Daemonic Icons can only be purchased by Daemons of Tzeentch:

[Banner of Discord](../magic-item/banner-of-discord.md) (Magic Standard) 60 points

*The air around this warped banner shimmers with the explosive unbinding of enemy spells.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Discord gains the [Magic Resistance (-3)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule. In addition, friendly units within 6" of the model carrying this standard gain the Magic Resistance (-1) special rule.

[Banner of Change](../magic-item/banner-of-change.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*So saturated is this banner with the power of change that it distorts and mutates everything around it.*

At the start of the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), a single enemy unit engaged in combat with this model suffers 3D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -.

[Icon of Sorcery\*](../magic-item/icon-of-sorcery.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*The runes upon this banner swirl and distort as they magnify the sorcerous might of the Horrors that caper beneath it.*

Pink Horrors only. When a unit carrying the Icon of Sorcery casts a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), it does so with a power level equal to twice its [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
