# Chaotic Icons

The following Daemonic Icons can be purchased by any Daemon, regardless of their Daemonic Alignment:

[Banner of Unholy Victory](../magic-item/banner-of-unholy-victory.md) (Magic Standard) 60 points

*Scenes from a million battles fill the minds of those that gaze upon this banner.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Banner of Unholy Victory may claim an additional bonus of +D3 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) points.

[Totem of Eternal War](../magic-item/totem-of-eternal-war.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*The power of the æther flows from this accursed icon and into the Daemons that march beneath it.*

[Daemonic Locus](../special-rules/daemonic-locus.md) only. Friendly [Daemonic](../special-rules/daemonic.md) units reduce the number of wounds suffered due to the [Daemonic Instability](../special-rules/daemonic-instability.md) special rule by an additional D3 whilst within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of this model.

[Standard of Chaotic Glory](../magic-item/standard-of-chaotic-glory.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*This banner flickers in and out of existence, creating a tear in reality through which the power of Chaos flows.*

[Daemonic Locus](../special-rules/daemonic-locus.md) only. Friendly [Daemonic](../special-rules/daemonic.md) units gain a +1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic whilst within this character's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) (to a maximum of 10).
