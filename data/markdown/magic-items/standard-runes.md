# Standard Runes

Standard runes can only be inscribed upon a standard carried by a [Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) or by a standard bearer within a unit that has the option to purchase Standard runes. A standard cannot have more than three Standard runes. A unit joined by a Battle Standard Bearer is considered to also be carrying their standard.

[Master Rune of Grungni](../magic-item/master-rune-of-grungni.md) (Magic Standard) 80 points

*This rune stirs the Winds of Magic until their swirling forces protect those that march beneath it.*

[Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) only. A unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Master Rune of Grungni has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. In addition, whilst within 6" of the model carrying this standard, friendly units have a 6+ Ward save against any wounds suffered during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase).

[Master Rune of Stromni Redbeard](../magic-item/master-rune-of-stromni-redbeard.md) (Magic Standard) 75 points

*This rune was first carved onto the Battle Standard of Durgin, son of Grindo, son of Grimnir.*

When calculating its combat result, any friendly unit within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of the model carrying the standard inscribed with the Master Rune of Stromni Redbeard may claim an additional bonus of +1 combat result point.

[Master Rune of Hesitation](../magic-item/master-rune-of-hesitation.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*The presence of this rune causes the enemy to hesitate before the Dwarfen throng and falter in their charge.*

An enemy unit that charges the front arc of a unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Hesitation does not count as having charged for the purposes of choosing which weapon to use or using any special rules it may have.

*Note that the unit carrying this rune still counts as having been charged by the enemy unit.*

[Rune of Confusion](../magic-item/rune-of-confusion.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*Under the shadow of this rune, the enthusiasm of charging enemies quickly wanes.*

Any enemy unit that charges the front arc of a unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Confusion makes a [disordered charge](../the-combat-phase/disordered-charges.md).

[Rune of Fear](../magic-item/rune-of-fear.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*Those that carry this banner to battle seem to tower over the enemy, looming like mythical giants.*

A unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Fear gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule.

[Rune of Battle](../magic-item/rune-of-battle.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*In ages past, even lesser strongholds and fortified mines had a standard bearing this humble rune.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying a standard inscribed with a Rune of Battle may claim an additional bonus of +1 combat result point.

[Strollaz' Rune](../magic-item/strollaz-rune.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*Tireless by nature, Dwarfs that march beneath Strollaz' Rune become relentless in their advance.*

A unit carrying a standard inscribed with Strollaz' Rune gains the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule.

[Rune of Courage](../magic-item/rune-of-courage.md) (Magic Standard) 15 points

*This inspirational rune further bolsters the already considerable courage of all Dwarfs near it.*

A unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Courage automatically passes any [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) or [Terror tests](../special-rules/terror.md) it is required to make.
