# Knightly Orders of the Empire

*It is common practice amongst the Empires young nobles, especially those younger sons and daughters not destined to inherit their family's estates, to join a Knightly Order. Some of these orders, such as the sinister Black Guard of Morr or the fanatical Knights of the Fiery Heart, are affiliated with one of the Empire's many cults and restrict their membership accordingly. Others are entirely secular organisations that accept any supplicant of noble birth.*

To represent this, you may chose one of the following Knightly Orders to include in your Empire of Man army: 0-1 [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md) or [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md), and 0-1 unit of [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md), [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) or [Demigryph Knights](../unit/demigryph-knights.md) in your army may be upgraded to belong to your chosen Knightly Order.

*Note that, if your army is made using the Knightly Order Army of Infamy composition list, this 0-1 limit does not apply. Instead, every unit that can belong to a Knightly Order must belong to the same Knightly Order as the army's General.*

*Note also that any special rules granted by these upgrades do not apply to the models' mounts.*

## Admittance to the Order

A Grand Master or Chapter Master and a unit of Empire Knights, Inner Circle Knights or Demigryph Knights may belong to one of the following Knightly Orders:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **Grand Master or Chapter Master** | **Unit of Empire Knights, Inner Circle Knights or Demigryph Knights** |
| **Order of the Knights Panther** | +10 points | +2 points per model |
| **Order of the White Wolf** | +15 points | +3 points per model |
| **Order of the Blazing Sun** | +10 points | +2 points per model |
| **Order of the Knights of Morr** | +15 points | +3 points per model |
| **Order of the Fiery Hearts** | +15 points | +3 points per model |

[Order of the Knights Panther](../magic-item/order-of-the-knights-panther.md) (Ability) +10 points per Character / +2 points per model

*Dedicated to the eradication of Chaos and its corrupt followers, these gallant warriors are amongst the most powerful of the Knightly Orders.*

Models belonging to the Order of the Knights Panther:

- Must be armed with [lances](../weapons-of-war/lance.md) (note that models able to purchase magic weapons may do so as normal).
- May re-roll a single dice that rolls a natural 1 when making a Charge roll, before discarding any dice that are required to be discarded.
- Gain the [Hatred](../special-rules/hatred.md) (Warriors of Chaos, Beastmen Brayherds & Daemonic models) special rule.

In addition, a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md), [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the Knights Panther:

- Gains the [Magic Resistance (-1)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.

[Order of the White Wolf](../magic-item/order-of-the-white-wolf.md) (Ability) +15 points per Character / +3 points per model

*The Knights of the White Wolf swing their infamous wolf hammers with primal savagery, shattering shields and skulls with each blow.*

Models belonging to the Order of the White Wolf:

- Must be equipped with either [great weapons](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) or [halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md), which they may replace with [wolf hammers](../weapons-of-war/wolf-hammer.md) for free (note that models able to purchase magic weapons may do so as normal).
- Gain the [Furious Charge](../special-rules/furious-charge.md) special rule.

In addition, a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md), [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the White Wolf:

- Has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

[Order of the Blazing Sun](../magic-item/order-of-the-blazing-sun.md) (Ability) +10 points per Character / +2 points per model

*The Knights of the Blazing Sun are vigilant students of war who strive for perfection on the battlefield.*

Models belonging to the Order of the Blazing Sun:

- Gain the [Evasive](../special-rules/evasive.md) and [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

In addition, a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md), [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the Blazing Sun:

- May claim an additional bonus of +1 combat result point when calculating its combat result during a turn in which it charges. This bonus is not cumulative.

[Order of the Knights of Morr](../magic-item/order-of-the-knights-of-morr.md) (Ability) +15 points per Character / +3 points per model

*Clad in jet-black armour, these dour warriors strive to protect the living from the ever-present threat of the undead.*

Models belonging to the Order of the Knights of Morr:

- Gain the [Hatred](../special-rules/hatred.md) (Undead models) special rule.
- Have a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.

In addition, a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md), [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the Knights of Morr:

- Gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) and [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rules.

[Order of the Fiery Heart](../magic-item/order-of-the-fiery-heart.md) (Ability) +15 points per Character / +3 points per model

*These fanatical knights work closely with the Cult of Sigmar, earning a reputation as the shield of the Sigmarite faithful.*

Models belonging to the Order of the Fiery Heart:

- Can only be joined by a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md) or [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) belonging to the Order of the Fiery Heart, a [Lector of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md) or a [Priest of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md).
- Gain the [Hatred](../special-rules/hatred.md) (enemy Wizards, Orcs & Goblins) special rule.
- Have +1 Initiative during the first round of any combat.

In addition, a Grand Master, Chapter Master or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the Fiery Heart:

- May re-roll any rolls To Hit or To Wound of a natural 1 made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
