# Empire of Grand Cathay Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Empire of Grand Cathay armies. These can be purchased by models within an Empire of Grand Cathay army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Armour

[The Armour of the Warbird](../magic-item/the-armour-of-the-warbird.md) (Magic Armour) 45 points

*The wearer of the Armour of the Warbird takes to the skies to strike at their enemies from above.*

Models whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) or [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) only. The Armour of the Warbird is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, the wearer gains the [Counter Charge](../special-rules/counter-charge.md), [Fly (9)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules. However, the bearer cannot join a unit.

[Jade Armour of Beichai](../magic-item/jade-armour-of-beichai.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*Crafted in secret in the city of Beichai, this jade armour glows from within with unwholesome power.*

The Jade Armour of Beichai is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, the wearer of the Jade Armour has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and gains the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.

[The Mantle of Heaven](../magic-item/the-mantle-of-heaven.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*Woven from silk harvested from the slopes of Kunlan, the wearer of these robes is filled with a measure of the Celestial Dragon's essence.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Mantle of Heaven improves their armour value by 2 (to a maximum of 2+) against non-magical shooting attacks.

[Shield of Nan-Gau](../magic-item/shield-of-nan-gau.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*Carved from a section of the Great Bastion, the Shield of Nan-Gau is a massive stone and iron shield.*

The Shield of Nan-Gau is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, during a turn in which its bearer was charged, the Shield of Nan-Gau improves its bearer's [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 2 (to a maximum of 2+).

## Talismans

[Crystal of Kunlan](../magic-item/crystal-of-kunlan.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*The crystals that grow in the peaks of Kunlan unleash sorcerous energy when struck.*

The Crystal of Kunlan gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. In addition, if a natural 6 is rolled when making the Ward save, every enemy model in base contact with the bearer suffers a Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -. These attacks have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

[Sigil of Smoke & Powder](../magic-item/sigil-of-smoke-and-powder.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*Clouds of gunpowder smoke hang about the bearer of this blackened sigil, obscuring them from the enemy's sight.*

Any enemy model that targets this character or any unit they have joined during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Vermilion Quills\*](../magic-item/vermilion-quills.md) (Talisman) 25 points

*Great heroes of Grand Cathay are awarded tokens of favour in the form of a Vermilion warbird's tail feather, tokens which are said to bless the bearer with unrivalled fortune.*

0-1 per model. Single Use. The bearer of a Vermilion Quill (but not their mount) can re-roll any failed rolls To Hit and/or To Wound made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Crown of Jade](../magic-item/crown-of-jade.md) (Talisman) 20 points

*Hostile magic recoils from the wearer of this gleaming crown of office.*

The Crown of Jade gives its wearer a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md), or an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell.

## Magic Standards

[Standard of Wei-Jin](../magic-item/standard-of-wei-jin.md) (Magic Standard) 60 points

*The banner of the Dragon Emperor inspires all Cathayan soldiers who gaze upon it, while spreading disorder and panic in their foes.*

A unit carrying the Standard of Wei-jin causes [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md). In addition, all enemy units within 12" of the model carrying the Standard of Wei-jin suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic when making a Fear, [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) test.

[Banner of the Dragon's Wrath](../magic-item/banner-of-the-dragons-wrath.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*Those that march to war beneath the Banner of the Dragon's Wrath descend upon the foe with the fury of Li Dao, the Fire Dragon.*

A unit carrying the Banner of the Dragon's Wrath gains the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) and [Impact Hits (1)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) special rules.

[Icon of Heavenly Fury](../magic-item/icon-of-heavenly-fury.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*The heavens are the realm of the Celestial Dragon, and those that would encroach upon his domain must face his wrath.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the bearer of this banner may attempt to unleash its power by making a Leadership test (using their own unmodified Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase enemy units cannot use the [Fly (X)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule.

[The Jade Banner](../magic-item/the-jade-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*Carved from purest jade by Cathay's finest artificers, this ancient banner fills the heart of a Jade Warrior with pride.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Jade Banner may claim an additional bonus of +2 combat result points.

[Dragon's Eye Banner](../magic-item/dragons-eye-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*Under the scrutiny of the Celestial Dragon, enemy Wizards falter.*

When an enemy Wizard chooses a unit carrying the Dragon's Eye Banner as the target of a spell, roll a D6. On a 4+, the Wizard's controlling player must choose another target. If there is no other viable target in range, the spell cannot be cast.

[Banner of the Bastion](../magic-item/banner-of-the-bastion.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*Those that would fight beneath this venerable banner must be as solid and unwavering as the Great Bastion itself.*

A unit carrying the Banner of the Bastion gains the [Shieldwall](../special-rules/shieldwall.md) special rules.

[The Shroud of Shiyama](../magic-item/the-shroud-of-shiyama.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*The spectral presence of Shiyama, the Spirit Dragon, lingers about those that fight beneath this silken standard crafted in her memory.*

A unit carrying the Shroud of Shiyama gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule. If they already have the Fear special rule, they instead gain the [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rule.

[The Banner of Xen Wun](../magic-item/the-banner-of-xen-wun.md) (Magic Standard) 15 points

*Enemies often falter before this ashen banner, touched by the spirits of Xen Wun.*

Enemy units that wish to declare a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) or [Fire & Flee](../special-rules/fire-and-flee.md) charge reaction against a unit carrying the Banner of Xen Wun must first make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, they may declare a Stand & Shoot or Fire & Flee charge reaction. If this test is failed, they must [Hold](../the-movement-phase/hold.md) instead.

## Enchanted Items

[Maw Shard](../magic-item/maw-shard.md) (Enchanted Item) 50 points

*Some have travelled to the broken lands around the Great Maw and brought back strange objects - among them the Maw Shard, an extra-worldly artefact.*

If an enemy Wizard rolls any natural double when making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) whilst within 18" of the bearer of the Maw Shard, the spell is [miscast](../magic/miscast.md), regardless of the casting result.

[Alchemist's Mask](../magic-item/alchemists-mask.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*Created in the House of Secrets of Shang Yang, this sorcerous mask gifts the wearer with the alchemical secrets of the Iron Dragon.*

The wearer of the Alchemist's Mask can cast the [Plague of Rust](../spell/plague-of-rust.md) spell from the Lore of [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md) (see page 327 of the Warhammer: the Old World rulebook) as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1.

[Ring of Jet](../magic-item/ring-of-jet.md) (Enchanted Item) 30 points

*Carved from purest jet quarried from the banks of the Dragon River, the Ring of Jet is infused with the power of Shiyama, allowing its wielder to summon ancestral spirits.*

The wielder of the Ring of Jet can cast the [Unquiet Spirits](../spell/unquiet-spirits.md) spell from the Lore of [Necromancy](../the-lores-of-magic/necromancy.md) as a [Bound Spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1.

[Golden Lion](../magic-item/golden-lion.md) (Enchanted Item) 25 points

*Jade Lions are revered in Cathay for their bravery, and golden amulets carved into their likeness inspire great courage.*

The bearer of the Golden Lion and their unit may re-roll any failed [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md), [Rally](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md) or [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) test.

[Spirit Lantern\*](../magic-item/spirit-lantern.md) (Enchanted Item) 25 points

*The ghostly light of a Spirit Lantern reveals the denizens of the underworld and lays bare their weaknesses.*

A model carrying a Spirit Lantern causes [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md). In addition, a model carrying a Spirit Lantern may re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 1 against enemy models that are 'Daemonic' or 'Undead'.

## Arcane Items

[Cloak of Po Mei](../magic-item/cloak-of-po-mei.md) (Arcane Item) 50 points

*This enchanted cloak grants its wearer exceptional magical strength and mastery over one of the Elemental Winds.*

In addition to their randomly generated spells, the wearer of the Cloak of Po Mei knows all three spells from the [Lore of Yin](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yin.md) or the [Lore of Yang](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yang.md) (chosen by their controlling player). However, they can only cast a number of spells equal to their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md) per turn.

[Clockwork Compass](../magic-item/clockwork-compass.md) (Arcane Item) 50 points

*This finely wrought clockwork mechanism emulates the properties of the Wu Xing compass in Wei-jin. When correctly used, it can temporarily still the Winds of Magic.*

Single use. The bearer may use this item instead of making a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md) attempt. If they do so, the spell is automatically dispelled with no Dispel roll required. In addition, all [Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md) spells currently in play are dispelled, including spells cast by friendly Wizards.

[Guardian Feng Shi Bo\*](../magic-item/guardian-feng-shi-bo.md) (Arcane Item) 35 points

*Feng Shi Bo are elemental familiars summoned by Cathayan Wizards. Most often, these earthbound spirits guard their masters, protecting them from harm.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against the owner of a Guardian Feng Shi Bo during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Seal of Xing Po\*](../magic-item/seal-of-xing-po.md) (Arcane Item) 35 points

*The bearer of a Seal of Xing Po has studied the elemental winds for decades, mastering the Winds of Yin or Yang.*

0-1 per model. If you wish, the bearer of a Seal of Xing Po may discard two of their randomly generated spells (rather than the usual one) and instead select two signature spells from either the [Lore of Yang](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yang.md) or the [Lore of Yin](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yin.md) (determined by which of those two special rules they have).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Learned Feng Shi Bo\*](../magic-item/learned-feng-shi-bo.md) (Arcane Item) 15 points

*Some Wizards create Feng Shi Bo to aid them with their scholarly studies, tasking the spirits with remembering obscure lore.*

The owner of a Learned Feng Shi Bo knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md).

*Note that this does not increase the Wizard's Level.*

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Scrolls of Wei-jin](../magic-item/scrolls-of-wei-jin.md) (Arcane Item) 10 points

*Those fortunate enough to study the Elemental Winds in the heart of the Celestial City learn many arcane secrets. Such scholars carry with them many scrolls, heavy with knowledge.*

The bearer of the Scrolls of Wei-jin knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md). However, they can only cast a number of spells equal to their Level of Wizardry per turn.
