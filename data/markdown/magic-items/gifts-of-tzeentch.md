# Gifts of Tzeentch

The following Daemonic Gifts can only be purchased by Daemons of Tzeentch:

[Staff of Change](../magic-item/staff-of-change.md) (Ability) 65 points

*The mere presence of this staff causes all natural things to cry out in pain as it reshapes the world according to the Daemon's whim.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *Any enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Staff of Change must immediately make a Toughness test. If this test is failed, the wounded model loses all of its remai*ning Wounds.

[Will of Tzeentch](../magic-item/will-of-tzeentch.md) (Ability) 55 points

*The Daemon perceives the intricate pathways of fate and can tug upon the strands of destiny should it so desire.*

Once per round, this model may re-roll a single D6. This may be a D6 rolled on its own, as part of a batch of dice, or as part of a multiple dice roll.

[Power Vortex](../magic-item/power-vortex.md) (Ability) 35 points

*The Winds of Magic billow about the Daemon, but remain beyond the reach of its enemies.*

Whilst within this Wizard's dispel range, enemy Wizards suffer a -1 modifier to their [Casting rolls](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md).

[Iridescent Corona\*](../magic-item/iridescent-corona.md) (Ability) 30 points

*Glittering multi-coloured fire encases the Daemon's form, confounding the aim of the enemy.*

Any enemy model that targets this model during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Twin Heads](../magic-item/twin-heads.md) (Ability) 20 points

*Possessed of two personalities and two intellects, this two-headed Daemon is a master of the Winds of Magic.*

This model knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md).

*Note that this does not increase the Wizard's Level.*
