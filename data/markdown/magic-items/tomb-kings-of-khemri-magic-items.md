# Tomb Kings of Khemri Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Tomb Kings of Khemri armies. These can be purchased by models within a Tomb Kings of Khemri army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Weapons

[Destroyer of Eternities](../magic-item/destroyer-of-eternities.md) (Magic Weapon) 75 points

*Imbued with powerful incantations, the great blade of this scything weapon is impossibly sharp and is said to destroy the souls of its victims, denying them any hope of reaching the afterlife.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *Rather than attacking normally, the wielder of the Destroyer of Eternities may choose to make a special 'Scything' attack. If they do, the enemy unit they are directing their attacks against suffers D6 automatic hits, each resolved using the Destroyer of Eternities' profile.*

[The Conqueror's Blade](../magic-item/the-conquerors-blade.md) (Magic Weapon) 55 points

*Many a king and queen, warlord and emperor foolish enough to resist the rule of the Lord of Nehekhara have found their heads parted from their bodies by this wicked weapon.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |

In addition, should the wielder slay the enemy*  [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md)  *in a challenge, you win a bonus of 100*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md)  *at the end of the game.*

## Magic Armour

[Armour of the Ages](../magic-item/armour-of-the-ages.md) (Magic Armour) 50 points

*Forged from bronze, cooled in blood and plated with gold, this ancient armour can turn aside even the strongest blow.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md), [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) or [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md) only. The Armour of the Ages is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, enemy models must re-roll successful rolls To Wound made against the wearer.

[Royal Mantle](../magic-item/royal-mantle.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*It is said that the wisdom of the kings and queens of Nehekhara is held within these armoured plates, giving counsel to the wearer as they command their warriors on the battlefield.*

The Royal Mantle may only be taken by models in a [Nehekharan Royal Host](../warhammer-armies/nehekharan-royal-host.md) Army of Infamy. May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Royal Mantle improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). In addition, the wearer's [My Will Be Done](../special-rules/my-will-be-done.md) special rule affects all friendly units with the [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md) special rule within 6" of them, not just a unit they have joined.

[Warding Splint](../magic-item/warding-splint.md) (Magic Armour) 35 points

*Protective strips of bronze and iron inscribed with regenerative hieroglyphics are placed across the Liche Priest's shrivelled body, for without their power the armies of the Tomb Kings will crumble.*

The Warding Splint is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) which may be purchased and worn by a Liche Priest, even if they do not have the option to be equipped with armour, a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) or [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md), without penalty. In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.

[Shield of Ptra](../magic-item/shield-of-ptra.md) (Magic Armour) 25 points

*Bursting from the face of this ornate shield, the radiance of the Sun God Ptra blinds the enemy.*

The Shield of Ptra is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, any enemy model that directs their attacks against the bearer during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic.

## Talismans

[Amulet of the Serpent](../magic-item/amulet-of-the-serpent.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*Cast in antique gold with glittering emeralds for eyes, this magical snake amulet laces the weapons of nearby Nehekharan warriors with a fast-acting and deadly venom.*

The bearer of the Amulet of the Serpent and any unit they have joined gains the [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) special rule.

[Crown of Kings\*](../magic-item/crown-of-kings.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*Ancient and ornate crowns are a symbol that servitude extends beyond the bounds of death.*

Monarch of Nehekhara only. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the wearer of a Crown of Kings may attempt to [resurrect the fallen](../special-rules/resurrecting-the-fallen.md) by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, a single friendly unit of Skeleton Warriors, Skeleton Archers, Skeleton Horsemen or Skeleton Horse Archers that is within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) recovers D3+1 Wounds.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Collar of Shapesh](../magic-item/collar-of-shapesh.md) (Talisman) 25 points

*This ornate collar is a powerful charm that trades the life of one for that of another.*

Single use. When the wearer of the Collar of Shapesh loses their last Wound, roll a D6. On a roll of 4+, the Wound is not lost. Instead, a single friendly model within the wearer's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) is removed from play as a casualty.

[Relic of the Desert Sun](../magic-item/relic-of-the-desert-sun.md) (Talisman) 25 points

*With their withered, mummified forms vulnerable to open flame, some Tomb Kings placed their faith in the sun god Ptra to protect them.*

The bearer of the Relic of the Desert Sun is not subject to the [Dry as Dust](../special-rules/dry-as-dust.md) or [Flammable](../special-rules/flammable.md) special rules.

## Magic Standards

[Standard of the Cursing Word](../magic-item/standard-of-the-cursing-word.md) (Magic Standard) 80 points

*This potent icon contains a powerful curse, similar to that which protects the mortal remains of the ancient monarchs of Nehekhara.*

[Battle Standard Bearer](../characters/the-battle-standard.md) only. At the end of any phase in which one or more models in a unit joined by the bearer of the Standard of the Cursing Word lost its last Wound to an enemy attack, the unit that made the attack must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the enemy unit suffers D3 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -, for each model that lost its last Wound.

[Icon of the Sacred Eye](../magic-item/icon-of-the-sacred-eye.md) (Magic Standard) 50 points

*The arcane power contained within this unblinking eye guides the arms of those that march beneath it.*

A unit carrying the Icon of the Sacred Eye has a +1 modifier to its Weapon Skill characteristic (to a maximum of 10).

[Royal Standard of Settra](../magic-item/royal-standard-of-settra.md) (Magic Standard) 50 points

*When Settra's personal banner marches to war, it is an omen of doom for those who stand against him. For the True King of Nehekhara kneels to no one; all shall bow to him or face annihilation.*

The Royal Standard of Settra may only be taken in a muster list that includes Settra the Imperishable and/or Nekaph, Emissary of Settra. A unit carrying the Royal Standard of Settra gains the [Hatred (enemy characters)](../special-rules/hatred.md) and [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rules.

[Sigil of Centuries](../magic-item/sigil-of-centuries.md) (Magic Standard) 45 points

*Time behaves strangely around this odd icon, the weight of years slowing even the most agile warriors to a sluggish crawl.*

All enemy units within 6" of the model carrying the Sigil of Centuries suffer a -1 modifier to their Initiative characteristic (to a minimum of 1).

[Icon of Rakaph](../magic-item/icon-of-rakaph.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*Those that march beneath the banner of King Rakaph II are instilled with unrivalled discipline.*

Unless making a charge move, a unit carrying the Icon of Rakaph may perform a single free [reform](../movement-in-detail/reform.md) at any point during its movement.

[Tapestry of Conquered Lands](../magic-item/tapestry-of-conquered-lands.md) (Magic Standard) 35 points

*Woven from the standards of vanquished enemies, this patchwork banner serves as a warning to those who stand against the rulers of Nehekhara.*

Any enemy standard captured by a unit carrying the Tapestry of Conquered Lands is worth 100 [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) as a trophy of war.

[Banner of the Desert Winds](../magic-item/banner-of-the-desert-winds.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*This tattered banner snaps and waves under the command of a spectral breeze, whose gusts drive forward those who march under it.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. A unit carrying the Banner of the Desert Winds gains the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) and [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rules.

[Mirage Banner](../magic-item/mirage-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 20 points

*Shimmering with the heat of the desert, this banner shifts constantly, confusing the eye of the enemy.*

Any enemy model that targets a unit carrying the Mirage Banner during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

## Enchanted Items

[Cloak of the Dunes](../magic-item/cloak-of-the-dunes.md) (Enchanted Item) 50 points

*This tattered cloak is infused with the magic of the deserts, enabling its wearer to transform into an abrasive storm of sand that cuts through the foe.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. The wearer of the Cloak of the Dunes gains the [Fly (9)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule. In addition, any enemy unit the wearer moves over during the [Remaining Moves](../the-movement-phase/remaining-moves.md) sub-phase suffers D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.

[Staff of Awakening](../magic-item/staff-of-awakening.md) (Enchanted Item) 50 points

*Carried only by the most revered Liche Priests, this powerful artefact amplifies the wielder's already formidable necromantic talents.*

High Priest only. When the wielder of the Staff of Awakening uses the [Arise!](../special-rules/arise.md) special rule on a friendly unit whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md), that unit recovers an additional D3 Wounds.

[Orb of Ptra](../magic-item/orb-of-ptra.md) (Enchanted Item) 40 points

*Upon uttering a prayer to the mighty Ptra, this orb becomes as bright as the midday sun, confounding enemy archers.*

Any enemy model that targets this character or any unit they have joined during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Icon of Rulership\*](../magic-item/icon-of-rulership.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*The hero's chariot bears a totemic standard that pulses with greatness and proclaims their many victories over their enemies.*

Models whose troop type is [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md) only. This model doubles its Unit Strength, from 3 to 6. In addition, any [Impact Hits](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) caused by this model have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2 and the [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rule.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Death Mask of Kharnutt](../magic-item/death-mask-of-kharnutt.md) (Enchanted Item) 20 points

*The expressionless Death Mask of Kharnutt has borne witness to the demise of untold thousands over its long millennia, causing an aura of dread and despair to hang heavily about it.*

The wearer of the Death Mask of Kharnutt gains the [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rule.

## Arcane Items

[Phâzerakt's Kanopi](../magic-item/phazerakts-kanopi.md) (Arcane Item) 40 points

*It is said that Phâzerakt's spirit can be unleashed from within the ornate vessel in which it is trapped to aid in a mass summoning.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the bearer of Phâzerakt's Kanopi may attempt to unleash its contents by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own, unmodified Leadership). If this test is passed, place a unit of 2D6+3 Summoned Skeleton Warriors on the battlefield anywhere completely within 12" of this model, but not within 1" of any enemy models:

|  | M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | A | Ld |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skeleton Warrior | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 5 |

**Troop Type:** [Regular Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md)   
 **Base Size:** 25 x 25 mm  
 **Unit Size:** 2D6+3  
 **Equipment:** Hand weapons and shields  
 **Special Rules:** [Horde](../special-rules/horde.md), [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md), [Regeneration (6+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md), [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md)

*Note that Summoned Skeleton Warriors are not worth any*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) *.*

[Enkhil's Kanopi](../magic-item/enkhils-kanopi.md) (Arcane Item) 30 points

*Within this dusty clay vessel resides the wicked heart of the Liche Priest Enkhil. Should it ever be opened, the spirit of the jealous priest consumes the magic of his rivals.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the bearer of Enkhil's Kanopi may attempt to unleash its contents by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own, unmodified Leadership). If this test is passed, all [Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md) spells currently in play are dispelled, including spells cast by friendly Wizards.

[Hieratic Jar\*](../magic-item/hieratic-jar.md) (Arcane Item) 25 points

*This earthenware jar, sealed with wax and bound with glyphs, contains the magically charged remains of a once-mighty Liche Priest.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the bearer of a Hieratic Jar can release the power held within it. The bearer may attempt to [resurrect the fallen](../special-rules/resurrecting-the-fallen.md) by using the [Arise!](../special-rules/arise.md) special rule twice during this Command sub-phase (rather than the usual once).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Curse-Weaver Wand](../magic-item/curse-weaver-wand.md) (Arcane Item) 20 points

*The power within this accursed wand seeks only to enfeeble the enemies of its wielder.*

The bearer of the Curse-weaver Wand gains a +1 modifier to their [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) when attempting to cast a [Hex](../magic/hex.md) spell.

[Scarab Brooch](../magic-item/scarab-brooch.md) (Arcane Item) 20 points

*From far beneath the desert sands, scuttling dead things are drawn towards this blackened brooch.*

When using the [From Beneath the Sands](../special-rules/from-beneath-the-sands.md) special rule, a summoned unit can be placed on the battlefield anywhere completely within 18" of a Liche Priest wearing the Scarab Brooch, rather than the usual 12".

[Tablets of Tahoth\*](../magic-item/tablets-of-tahoth.md) (Arcane Item) 20 points

*These powerful relics of Tahoth's wisdom crumble to dust once their power is spent.*

A Wizard may purchase up to three Tablets of Tahoth, each of which is single use. Before making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md), a Wizard may choose to use any number of their Tablets. For each Tablet used, you may apply a +1 modifier to the result of the Casting roll.

*Note that this is a modifier to the result of a roll – it does not negate a roll of a natural double 1.*
