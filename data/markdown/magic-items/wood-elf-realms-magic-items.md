# Wood Elf Realms Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Wood Elf Realms armies. These can be purchased by models within a Wood Elf Realms army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Armour

[Helm of the Hunt](../magic-item/helm-of-the-hunt.md) (Magic Armour) 50 points

*This enchanted great helm imbues its wearer with the furious spirit of Orion himself.*

May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Helm of the Hunt improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). In addition, the wearer has a +1 modifier to both their Weapon Skill and Attacks characteristics during a turn in which they charge.

[Mantle of Rebirth](../magic-item/mantle-of-rebirth.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*This enchanted armour fills the wearer with the vitality of spring, instilling them with power and quickly healing their wound.*

The Mantle of Rebirth is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.

[Railarian's Mantle](../magic-item/railarians-mantle.md) (Magic Armour) 35 points

*Woven by the eldest Branchwraiths, this armour is only gifted to one chosen by Athel Loren itself.*

Railarian's Mantle is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, whilst its wearer is within 6" of a woodland terrain feature, they have a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.

[Cloak of Tumbling Leaves](../magic-item/cloak-of-tumbling-leaves.md) (Magic Armour) 30 points

*The wearer draws upon the power of the wind to carry them across the battlefield with great haste.*

Models whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) or [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) only. The Cloak of Tumbling Leaves is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has the [Fly (10)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rule. However, the wearer cannot join a unit.

## Talismans

[Merciw's Locus](../magic-item/merciws-locus.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*The eccentric Merciw held the concepts of personal honour and fairness in unusually high esteem.*

The Strength characteristic of the bearer of Merciw's Locus cannot be modified by any weapon. However, the Strength characteristic of any model that directs its attacks against the bearer during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) cannot be modified by any weapon either.

[Ariel's Favour](../magic-item/ariels-favour.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*A token of the Mage Queen's goodwill, those who bear her gifts are shielded from the baleful magic of their enemies!*

The bearer of Ariel's Favour has the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.

[Glamourweave](../magic-item/glamourweave.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*Enemies that view this brooch become entranced by its hypnotic colours and patterns.*

Enemy models must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) before making any rolls To Hit against the wearer of the Glamourweave during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). If this test is failed, only rolls of a natural 6 will hit.

[Orion's Favour](../magic-item/orions-favour.md) (Talisman) 25 points

*Those who bear the trinkets gifted by Orion know that their spear will strike true and their arrows will soar unhindered.*

Single Use. The bearer of Orion's Favour can re-roll any failed rolls To Hit and/or To Wound made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).

## Magic Standards

[Tapestry of Talsyn](../magic-item/tapestry-of-talsyn.md) (Magic Standard) 80 points

*Known as the Groves of Eternity, Talsyn is the largest and most prosperous region of Athel Loren, its heraldry a rallying point for all the Asrai.*

Battle Standard Bearer in a [Host of Talsyn](../warhammer-armies/host-of-talsyn.md) Army of Infamy only. A model carrying the Tapestry of Talsyn increases their [Command Range](../characters/command-range.md) to 18". In addition, friendly units within the bearer's Command Range improve their Leadership characteristic by 1 (to a maximum of 10).

[Banner of the Wildwood](../magic-item/banner-of-the-wildwood.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*The malevolence of the spirits that reside within the Wildwood clings to this tattered banner, chilling the souls of those that cast their gaze upon it.*

A unit carrying the Banner of the Wildwood gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule. If the unit already has the Fear special rule, it instead gains the [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rule.

[Banner of the Hunter King](../magic-item/banner-of-the-hunter-king.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*The tattered threads of this ancient standard resound with Orion's eagerness for battle.*

A unit carrying the Banner of the Hunter King gains the [Vanguard](../special-rules/vanguard.md) special rule.

[Banner of the Wild Hunt](../magic-item/banner-of-the-wild-hunt.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*When the Horn of the Wild Hunt sounds, this great banner is unfurled and Orion's warriors feel the call of the hunt echo through their veins.*

The Banner of the Wild Hunt may only be taken by a model in an [Orion's Wild Hunt](../warhammer-armies/orions-wild-hunt.md) of Infamy. When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Banner of the Wild Hunt may claim an additional bonus of +1 combat result point. In addition, the unit may re-roll [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) rolls.

[Standard of Morning's Chill](../magic-item/standard-of-mornings-chill.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*As the mists cloud the forest before dawn, so too do they now shield those faithful to Athel Loren from harm.*

The bearer of the Standard of Morning's Chill can cast the [Swirling Mists](../spell/swirling-mists.md) spell from the [Lore of the Wilds](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-the-wilds.md) as a [Bound Spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 2.

[Banner of Springtide](../magic-item/banner-of-springtide.md) (Magic Standard) 20 points

*Adorned with symbols of spring, this standard fills the Asrai with uncanny speed.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Springtide gains the [Quick Shot](../special-rules/quick-shot.md) special rule.

[Banner of the Eternal Queen](../magic-item/banner-of-the-eternal-queen.md) (Magic Standard) 20 points

*This banner was woven by no lesser hand than that of Ariel, Mage Queen of Athel Loren.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Banner of the Eternal Queen may claim an additional bonus of +1 combat result point if it is within 6" of a woodland terrain feature.

[Banner of Midsummer's Eve](../magic-item/banner-of-midsummers-eve.md) (Magic Standard) 15 points

*Glimmering with the light of the midsummer sun, this shining pennant reveals the enemies of Athel Loren no matter where they may try to hide.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Midsummer's Eve gains the [Ignores Cover](../special-rules/ignores-cover.md) special rule.

## Enchanted Items

[Wraithstone](../magic-item/wraithstone.md) (Enchanted Item) 50 points

*The screams of all who have met death within the bounds of Athel Loren emit from within this crystal.*

Unless the bearer of the Wraithstone is fleeing, enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2) whilst within 6" of the bearer.

[Crown of Antlers](../magic-item/crown-of-antlers.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*A glittering circlet of gold adorned with a resplendent set of antlers, this exquisite headpiece is as deadly as it is beautiful.*

The Crown of Antlers gives its wearer the [Armour Bane (1)](../special-rules/armour-bane.md) and [Impact Hits (D3)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) special rule. This Impact Hit has an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2.

[Hail of Doom Arrow\*](../magic-item/hail-of-doom-arrow.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*When shot from a longbow, a Hail of Doom arrow splits into dozens of deadly shards.*

Single use. Models with [Asrai longbow](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md) only. A Hail of Doom Arrow can be fired as normal from the bearer's Asrai longbow. If the roll To Hit is successful, place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target unit. Any model whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 4 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1 and the [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rule.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Moonstone of the Hidden Ways](../magic-item/moonstone-of-the-hidden-ways.md) (Enchanted Item) 30 points

*Some of the most mysterious hidden pathways within the depths of Athel Loren can only be unlocked with the power of ancient moonstones.*

Single Use. The wearer of the Moonstone of Hidden Ways may cast the [Forest Walker](../spell/forest-walker.md) spell from the [Lore of Athel Loren](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-athel-loren.md) as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) with a Power Level of 3.

[Wailing Arrow\*](../magic-item/wailing-arrow.md) (Enchanted Item) 20 points

*These arrows wail when they draw blood, chilling the soul of even the bravest warrior.*

Single use. Models with [Asrai longbow](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md) only. A Wailing Arrow can be fired as normal from the bearer's Asrai longbow. Any unit that suffers an unsaved wound from this shot must make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) as if it had taken heavy casualties.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Blight-Tipped Arrows\*](../magic-item/blight-tipped-arrows.md) (Enchanted Item) 15 points

*Coated in virulent toxins harvested from the depths of the Wild Wood, blight-tipped arrows need only nick the flesh to bring about a slow and painful death.*

Single use. Models with [Asrai longbow](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md) only. A Blight-Tipped Arrow can be fired as normal from the bearer's Asrai longbow. If an enemy model suffers an unsaved wound from a Blight-Tipped Arrow, at the beginning of each subsequent [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase that model must make a Toughness test. If the test is failed, the model immediately suffers a single wound with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) may be attempted as normal).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

## Arcane Items

[Deepwood Sphere](../magic-item/deepwood-sphere.md) (Arcane Item) 45 points

*This twisted and thorny orb of ebony wood speaks to the trees in the bitterest words of vengeance and spite, exhorting them to strike down all who intrude upon them.*

Unless the bearer of the Deepwood Sphere is fleeing or engaged in combat, they may use it when an enemy Wizard that is within 6" of a woodland terrain feature successfully casts a spell. If they do so, no dispel attempt can be made and the spell is cast as normal. Once the spell has been resolved, the enemy Wizard immediately suffers D3 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.

[Oaken Stave](../magic-item/oaken-stave.md) (Arcane Item) 40 points

*This crooked staff awakens the power of Athel Loren itself against those enemies that would use their magic to harm it.*

Whilst within 3" of a woodland terrain feature, the bearer of the Oaken Stave increases their Dispel range by 3". Additionally, whilst within 3" of a woodland terrain feature, the bearer may roll an extra D6 when making the [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) and discard the lowest result.

[Orb of Midsummer](../magic-item/orb-of-midsummer.md) (Arcane Item) 35 points

*At the height of summer, when the great forest is filled with life, the magic of the Wood Elves reaches the peak of its power.*

Once per turn, the bearer of the Orb of Midsummer may re-roll a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md).

[Wand of Wych Elm](../magic-item/wand-of-wych-elm.md) (Arcane Item) 30 points

*Fashioned from the innately magical Wych Elm tree, this humble wand allows its bearer to easily bend the Winds of Magic to their will.*

Once per turn, if they are within 3" of a woodland terrain feature, the bearer of the Wand of Wych Elm may re-roll a failed [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md).

[Sigil of the Mage Queen](../magic-item/sigil-of-the-mage-queen.md) (Arcane Item) 25 points

*Upon the eve of battle, Ariel, beloved Queen of Athel Loren, infuses arcane vessels with a measure of her power. These tokens she gifts to her most favoured mages.*

Single Use. The bearer of the Sigil of the Mage Queen may use it before making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md). If they do, they may apply a +2 modifier to that Casting roll.

[Heartwood Pendant\*](../magic-item/heartwood-pendant.md) (Arcane Item) 15 points

*Crafted from the heartwood of an ancient oak felled deep within the darkest depths of the great forest, this pendant allows its bearer to tap into the power of Athel Loren itself.*

In addition to the [Lores of Magic](https://tow.whfb.app/the-lores-of-magic) they may normally know spells from, the bearer of a Heartwood Pendant may know spells from the [Lore of the Wilds](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-the-wilds.md).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
