# Magic Armour

Only a model that is equipped with (or that has the option to be equipped with) armour, a shield or barding may purchase a magical equivalent. A model can only wear a single suit of armour and can only carry one shield, and a mount can only wear a single suit of barding. Therefore, magic armour replaces any mundane equivalent the model is, or may be, equipped with.

Magic armour follows the same rules as its mundane equivalent (if given). For example, a suit of magical heavy armour will grant its wearer an armour value of 5+.

[Armour of Destiny](../magic-item/armour-of-destiny.md) (Magic Armour) 70 points

*The potent Armour of Destiny protects its wearer from all but the most grievous of harm.*

The Armour of Destiny is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.

[Bedazzling Helm](../magic-item/bedazzling-helm.md) (Magic Armour) 60 points

*A gleaming helm that confuses and confounds enemies, causing their blows to fall wide of their mark.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Bedazzling Helm improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). In addition, any enemy model that directs its attacks against the wearer during the Combat phase suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.

[Armour of Silvered Steel](../magic-item/armour-of-silvered-steel.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*Polished to a mirror shine, the Armour of Silvered Steel encases its wearer from head to toe.*

The Armour of Silvered Steel is a suit of armour that gives its wearer an [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of 3+ which cannot be improved in any way.

[Glittering Scales](../magic-item/glittering-scales.md) (Magic Armour) 35 points

*Each and every scale of this armoured surcoat is crafted from shards of precious metal.*

The Glittering Scales is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, once per turn, you may make your opponent re-roll a single roll To Hit made against the wearer.

[Shield of the Warrior True](../magic-item/shield-of-the-warrior-true.md) (Magic Armour) 30 points

*A heavy shield of oak and iron that turns aside all but the most deadly of missiles.*

The Shield of the Warrior True is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase).

[Levitating Shield](../magic-item/levitating-shield.md) (Magic Armour) 25 points

*Born aloft by a cunning enchantment, this shield seems to place itself between its owner and the weapons of the enemy.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. The Levitating Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, the bearer may use it alongside a weapon with the [Requires Two Hands](../special-rules/requires-two-hands.md) special rule during combat.

[Spellshield\*](../magic-item/spellshield.md) (Magic Armour) 25 points

*The Winds of Magic lose their power when in the presence of the brass-bound Spellshield.*

The Spellshield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md), or an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Armour of Meteoric Iron](../magic-item/armour-of-meteoric-iron.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*This heavy armour was forged of ore mined from a meteor that fell from the heavens.*

The Armour of Meteoric Iron gives its wearer an [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of 5+, which cannot be improved in any way. However, nor can this armour value be reduced in any way either.

[Trailblazer's Hauberk](../magic-item/trailblazers-hauberk.md) (Magic Armour) 20 points

*The local flora seems to shift and bend to allow the wearer to pass unimpeded through even the thickest briar.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. The Trailblazer's Hauberk is a suit of heavy armour. In addition, its wearer gains the [Move Through Cover](../special-rules/move-through-cover.md) and [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) special rules.

[Enchanted Shield\*](../magic-item/enchanted-shield.md) (Magic Armour) 10 points

*A finely crafted and sturdy shield that protects its bearer from harm time and time again.*

The Enchanted Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Charmed Shield\*](../magic-item/charmed-shield.md) (Magic Armour) 5 points

*A simple shield hung with charms and fetishes to bring the bearer good fortune. In the heat of battle, such ornamentation rarely survives intact for long.*

The Charmed Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, once per game the Charmed Shield gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against a single wound. Once this Ward save has been used, the Charmed Shield is considered to be an ordinary, non magical shield.
