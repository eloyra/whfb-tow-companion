# Chaos Dwarfs Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Chaos Dwarf armies. These can be purchased by models within a Chaos Dwarf army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Weapons

*This burning, black-hafted hammer bears the horned rune of Hashut, Father of Darkness, and has been carried into battle for centuries by the champions of Zharr-Naggrund.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |

***Notes:***  *Against models with the*  [Flammable](../special-rules/flammable.md)  *special rule, this weapon also has the*  [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md)  *special rule.*

[Dark Maul](../magic-item/dark-maul.md) (Magic Weapon) 50 points

*This mysterious weapon of unknown origin smoulders with a dark power that seems to eat the light around it and devours the warmth of its victims, leaving them withered husks.*

***Notes:***  *Any enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Dark Maul must immediately make a Strength test. If this test is failed, the enemy model loses an additional Wound.*

## Magic Armour

[Armour of Bazherak the Cruel](../magic-item/armour-of-bazherak-the-cruel.md) (Magic Armour) 50 points

*Once worn by the Castellan of the Tower of Gorgoth, this armour was pulled from the Bitter Sea along with its owner's drowned corpse to stand as a warning to the arrogant of the price of hubris.*

The Armour of Bazherak the Cruel is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has the [Immune to Psychology](../special-rules/immune-to-psychology.md) and [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rules.

[The Mask of the Furnace](../magic-item/the-mask-of-the-furnace.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*This iron mask contains the essence and agonies of all who have perished before it in the fires of sacrifice.*

May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Mask of the Furnace improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). In addition, they have a 3+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

## Talismans

[Mantle of Stone\*](../magic-item/mantle-of-stone.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*Stitched from the flayed flesh of failed sorcerer-apprentices, a Stone Mantle preserves the wearer and reminds them of failure's price.*

The wearer of a Mantle of Stone has a +1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic, but a -1 modifier to their Initiative characteristic.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Hellshard](../magic-item/hellshard.md) (Talisman) 20 points

*The malice of its wearer resides within the Hellshard's black depths, waiting to be unleashed on any who would dare spill their blood.*

If the bearer of the Hellshard loses their last Wound to an enemy attack during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the unit that made the attack suffers D6 Strength 5 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.

## Magic Standards

[The Lammasu's Beard](../magic-item/the-lammasus-beard.md) (Magic Standard) 65 points

*This totem depicts the leering face of an old man, beneath which bristles a blood-stained beard torn from the chin of an ancient Lammasu.*

A unit carrying the Lammasu's Beard has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and gains the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule. In addition, whilst within 6" of the model carrying this standard, friendly units have a 5+ Ward save against any wounds suffered during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) and gain the Magic Resistance (-1) special rule.

[Ashen Banner](../magic-item/ashen-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 30 points

*Clouds of soot and ash billow from this smouldering pennant, concealing its bearers from their enemies.*

Any enemy model that targets a unit carrying the Ashen Banner during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.

[Overseer's Sigil](../magic-item/overseers-sigil.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*Beneath this hateful banner the workers of Zharr-Naggrund must swear devotion to their dark masters.*

Whilst within 9" of the model carrying the Overseer's Sigil, friendly units lose the [Levies](../special-rules/levies.md) special rule.

[Shroud of the Ancestor](../magic-item/shroud-of-the-ancestor.md) (Magic Standard) 10 points

*Those that march beneath this black pennant, supposedly torn from the corpse of an unnamed Ancestor in ages past, seethe with rage towards their hated western kin.*

A unit carrying the Shroud of the Ancestor gains the [Hatred (Dwarfs)](../special-rules/hatred.md) special rule.

## Enchanted Items

[Daemonic Familiar](../magic-item/daemonic-familiar.md) (Enchanted Item) 30 points

*The Chaos Dwarfs bind many daemonic creatures to their will. In battle, these entities cluster about their master's enemies, feeding hungrily upon the wards that protect them.*

Enemy models engaged in combat with the owner of a Daemonic Familiar must reduce the [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of any [Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) save they have by 1, to a minimum of 6+.

*Through the blackest of sorceries, the breath of Hashut can be sealed within an earthen jar and unleashed upon his foes.*

Single use. The Breath of Hashut is a breath weapon with the following profile:

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| N/A | 4 | -2 | - |

[Obsidian Vambraces](../magic-item/obsidian-vambraces.md) (Enchanted Item) 15 points

*Carved of cold, hard obsidian, these powerful yet fragile artefacts ward against magic.*

The wearer of the Obsidian Vambraces has a +1 modifier to their Strength characteristic and the [Magic Resistance (-1)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule. However, if they roll a natural 1 when making a roll To Hit during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the gauntlets shatter into pieces and can no longer be used.

## Arcane Items

[Daemon Flask](../magic-item/daemon-flask.md) (Arcane Item) 50 points

*This rune-inscribed vessel holds a raging daemonic entity of phenomenal power. When unleashed its screams can shatter stone and cause the ground to split asunder as it flees into the æther.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, the bearer of the Daemon Flask may open it. When they do so, all enemy units that are within this character's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) must make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md).

[Vial of Lammasu Blood](../magic-item/vial-of-lammasu-blood.md) (Arcane Item) 40 points

*The blood of a Lammasu is a powerful ward against magic. Consuming it still warm allows a sorcerer to dispel the petty conjurations of their rivals with ease.*

Single use. A Wizard may use this item instead of making a dispel attempt. If they do so, the spell is automatically dispelled with no [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) required.

*Note that a*  [perfect invocation](../magic/perfect-invocations.md)  *cannot be dispelled.*

[Scroll of Binding\*](../magic-item/scroll-of-binding.md) (Arcane Item) 30 points

*Chaos Dwarf Sorcerers make many foul bargains with Daemons, hoping to increase their powers. These infernal contracts are etched in blood upon scrolls of binding.*

Single use. A Wizard may use this scroll when attempting to cast a spell. If they do so, the Wizard rolls 3D6 when making their [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) (rather than the usual 2D6). However, if a double 1 is rolled on any two of the dice rolled, the spell is [miscast](../magic/miscast.md).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
