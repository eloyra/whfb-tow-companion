# Talismans

Talismans are tokens of magical protection, worn by warriors to protect them from harm both physical and magical.

[Dawnstone](../magic-item/dawnstone.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*The magic trapped within this semi-precious stone binds together shattered armour plates.*

The bearer of the Dawnstone may re-roll any [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll of a natural 1.

[Icon of Fortitude](../magic-item/icon-of-fortitude.md) (Talisman) 35 points

*Carefully woven with protective enchantments, this exquisite brooch is said to shield its bearer from the deadliest blows.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The bearer is immune to the [Multiple Wounds (X)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule. If the bearer is wounded by an attack with this special rule, they suffer a single wound.

[Ironhide Talisman](../magic-item/ironhide-talisman.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*It is said that the one who bears this powerful heirloom can endure even the most telling blows.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Any enemy model that makes a successful roll To Hit against the bearer of the Ironhide Talisman during the [Shooting](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) phase or [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) must re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 6.

*Note that this magic item does not work against attacks that hit automatically.*

[Talisman of Protection](../magic-item/talisman-of-protection.md) (Talisman) 30 points

*A powerful magical aura surrounds this humble talisman, protecting its wearer from harm.*

The Talisman of Protection gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.

[Paymaster's Coin\*](../magic-item/paymasters-coin.md) (Talisman) 25 points

*This heavy golden coin may not be enchanted, but its weight in its owner's pocket certainly inspires heroism.*

Single use. The bearer of a Paymaster's Coin can re-roll any failed rolls To Hit made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Obsidian Lodestone\*](../magic-item/obsidian-lodestone.md) (Talisman) 20 points

*An air of chill hangs about obsidian, stilling the Winds of Magic in its presence.*

A model may purchase up to three Obsidian Lodestones. A model that bears a single Obsidian Lodestone has [Magic Resistance (-1)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md), a model that bears two has Magic Resistance (-2), and a model that bears three has Magic Resistance (-3).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Luckstone\*](../magic-item/luckstone.md) (Talisman) 15 points

*Can luck be trapped within a dull stone pendant? Possibly.*

Single use. The bearer of a Luckstone can re-roll a single failed [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll.
