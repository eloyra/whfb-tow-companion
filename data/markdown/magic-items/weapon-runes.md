# Weapon Runes

Weapon runes can be inscribed upon a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) or [great weapon](../weapons-of-war/great-weapon.md) (whatever form it takes). A model cannot have more than three Weapon runes, no matter how many weapons it carries. A weapon with one or more runes inscribed upon it is a magic weapon (as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook) and automatically gains the [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rule.

[Master Rune of Smiting](../magic-item/master-rune-of-smiting.md) (Magic Weapon) 75 points

*The secret of this rune was preserved in the Rundrokikron, an ancient tome kept in the treasure troves of Karak Kadrin.*

A weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Smiting gains the [Multiple Wounds (D6)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule.

[Master Rune of Skalf Blackhammer](../magic-item/master-rune-of-skalf-blackhammer.md) (Magic Weapon) 65 points

*Runesmith Skalf forged many hammers of great might, and some maintain that even the hammer of Sigmar, Ghal Maraz itself, was his work.*

When making a roll To Wound with a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Skalf Blackhammer, a roll of 2+ is always a success, regardless of the target's Toughness.

[Master Rune of Alaric The Mad](../magic-item/master-rune-of-alaric-the-mad.md) (Magic Weapon) 45 points

*Alaric the Mad forged the famous Runefangs for the Elector Counts of the Empire before meeting an unknown fate.*

No [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) is permitted against wounds caused by a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Alaric the Mad ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).

[Master Rune of Dragon Slaying](../magic-item/master-rune-of-dragon-slaying.md) (Magic Weapon) 35 points

*With this mighty rune the Dwarfs have slain many great wyrms and foul creatures of Chaos.*

When making a roll To Wound against an enemy whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) with a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Dragon Slaying, a roll of 2+ is always a success, regardless of the target's Toughness.

[Rune of Parrying](../magic-item/rune-of-parrying.md) (Magic Weapon) 35 points

*A weapon with this rune moves like quicksilver to block the attacks of the enemy.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against a model wielding a weapon inscribed with a Rune of Parrying during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.

[Master Rune of Breaking](../magic-item/master-rune-of-breaking.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*Runesmiths take pride in seeing the inferior works of other races break beneath a hammerblow.*

Any magic weapon carried by an enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Breaking is destroyed and cannot be used for the remainder of the game.

*Note that even if their magic weapon is destroyed, the model is still considered to be armed with a hand weapon.*

[Master Rune of Flight](../magic-item/master-rune-of-flight.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*This cunning rune has been used to surprise the Dwarfs' foes from afar since the world was young.*

Hand weapon only. Once per turn, during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase), a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Flight can be thrown with the following profile (which may be modified by additional runes):

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12" | S | - | - |

[Master Rune of Swiftness](../magic-item/master-rune-of-swiftness.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*This rune was first struck by Thurgrom the Hermit, the last runesmith to study Elven crafts.*

The wielder of a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Swiftness gains the [Strike First](../special-rules/strike-first.md) special rule.

[Rune of Banishment](../magic-item/rune-of-banishment.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*First crafted in the days of the Ancestor Gods, the touch of this potent rune is anathema to the creatures of Chaos.*

Enemy models with the [Warp-spawned](../special-rules/warp-spawned.md) special rule cannot make any [Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against hits caused by a weapon inscribed with the Rune of Banishment.

[Rune of Fury\*](../magic-item/rune-of-fury.md) (Magic Weapon) 25 points

*A Rune of Fury is an emblem of the anger all Dwarfs feel towards the enemies of their ancestors.*

For each Rune of Fury inscribed upon a weapon, its wielder has a +1 modifier to their Attacks characteristic.

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)

[Grudge Rune\*](../magic-item/grudge-rune.md) (Magic Weapon) 20 points

*Grudge Runes seek out the enemies of the Dwarfs with unerring accuracy.*

For each Grudge Rune inscribed upon a weapon, its wielder may re-roll a single roll To Hit of a natural 1 made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)

[Rune of Might\*](../magic-item/rune-of-might.md) (Magic Weapon) 20 points

*A weapon inscribed with Runes of Might can fell a Giant in a flurry of blows.*

For each Rune of Might inscribed upon a weapon, its wielder has a +1 modifier to their Strength characteristic.

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)

[Rune of Cleaving\*](../magic-item/rune-of-cleaving.md) (Magic Weapon) 15 points

*This rune was first made for mining tools, to allow them to break through rock with ease.*

Hand weapon only. For each Rune of Cleaving inscribed upon a weapon, its [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic is improved by 1.

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)

[Rune of Striking\*](../magic-item/rune-of-striking.md) (Magic Weapon) 15 points

*A weapon with this rune moves to strike the foe's most vulnerable area with an uncanny precision.*

For each Rune of Striking inscribed upon a weapon, its wielder has a +1 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic.

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)

[Rune of Fire](../magic-item/rune-of-fire.md) (Magic Weapon) 10 points

*Struck whilst the weapon glows white-hot, a Rune of Fire forever binds the heat of the forge.*

The wielder of a weapon inscribed with a Rune of Fire gains the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

[Rune of Speed\*](../magic-item/rune-of-speed.md) (Magic Weapon) 5 points

*This rune enhances its bearer's awareness and quickness, catching unwary enemies by surprise.*

Hand weapon only. For each Rune of Speed inscribed upon a weapon, its wielder has a +1 modifier to their Initiative characteristic.

[\*Rule of Duplication](rules-of-the-runes.md)
