# Chaotic Gifts

The following Daemonic Gifts can be purchased by any Daemon, regardless of their Daemonic Alignment:

[Many Arms\*](../magic-item/many-arms.md) (Ability) 55 points

*The Daemon has been blessed with a surfeit of grasping and clawing appendages.*

This model has a +1 modifier to its Attacks characteristic.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Æther Blade](../magic-item/aether-blade.md) (Ability) 55 points

*The Daemon's weapon flickers in and out of reality, slipping through armour and into the soft flesh beneath.*

No [armour saves](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) are permitted against wounds caused by this Daemon's [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).

[Winged Horror](../magic-item/winged-horror.md) (Ability) 45 points

*Gigantic wings sprout from the Daemon's back, allowing it to dive upon the unprepared foe.*

Daemonic Heralds whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) only. This model gains the [Fly (8)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules.

[Daemonic Robes](../magic-item/daemonic-robes.md) (Ability) 35 points

*The Daemon is clad in shimmering robes that deflect the fury of incoming blows.*

This model cannot be wounded by a roll To Wound of 2, regardless of the Strength of the attack.
