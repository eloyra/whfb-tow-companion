# Runic Items

Being extremely resistant to magic and its influence, Dwarfs have learned to use its power by crafting it into magic items such as weapons and armour. The Dwarfs are the greatest of all races when it comes to making magic items. Indeed, many of the most powerful magic weapons used by Elves and Men were, in fact, forged by the Dwarfs.
