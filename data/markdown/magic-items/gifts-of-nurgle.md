# Gifts of Nurgle

The following Daemonic Gifts can only be purchased by Daemons of Nurgle:

[Nurgling Infestation](../magic-item/nurgling-infestation.md) (Ability) 45 points

*Multitudinous Nurglings swarm about the Great Unclean One and nestle within the folds of its skin.*

Great Unclean One only. Whilst engaged in combat, this model may make an additional D6 attacks. These attacks are resolved at Initiative 2, with a Weapon Skill and Strength of 2, and with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -. Additionally, when this model loses its last Wound, place a Nurgling Swarm of D3 models so that each model is within 3" of this model before removing this model from play.

*Note that these Nurgling Swarms are not worth any*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) *.*

[Sloppity Bilepiper](../magic-item/sloppity-bilepiper.md) (Ability) 35 points

*The infectious tunes of a Bilepiper fill their companions with joyous murrain.*

Daemonic Heralds whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Friendly units with the [Daemons of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md) special rule that begin their movement within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) have a +1 modifier to their Movement characteristic.

[Spoilpox Scrivener](../magic-item/spoilpox-scrivener.md) (Ability) 30 points

*A Spoilpox Scrivener scrutinises the work of Nurgle's Daemons as they spread and record their master's blessings.*

Daemonic Heralds whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Whilst within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), friendly units with the [Daemons of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md) special rule may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1.

[Trappings of Nurgle](../magic-item/trappings-of-nurgle.md) (Ability) 30 points

*The Daemon is bedecked in rusted armour and rotted robes.*

The Trappings of Nurgle is a suit of armour that gives its wearer an [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of 4+ which cannot be improved in any way.

[Stream of Contagion\*](../magic-item/stream-of-contagion.md) (Ability) 25 points

*Opening its gullet wide, the Daemon unleashes a stream of noxious bile upon its foes.*
