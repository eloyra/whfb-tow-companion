# Incantations Scrolls

[Scroll of Withering](../magic-item/scroll-of-withering.md) (Unique) 50 points

*When read aloud, the incantation contained within a Scroll of Withering slowly and painfully drains the life force from the reader's enemies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8 |
| **Range** | 21" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -1 modifier to its Strength and Initiative characteristics and cannot make any [Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves. This Bound spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat.

[Scroll of Summoning](../magic-item/scroll-of-summoning.md) (Unique) 45 points

*The words inscribed upon a Scroll of Summoning enable the reader to reach into the underworld and call forth the souls of the dead.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8 |
| **Range** | 21" |

The target friendly unit regains D3 lost Wounds and, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, improves the [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of its [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) save by 1.

[Scroll of Awakening](../magic-item/scroll-of-awakening.md) (Unique) 40 points

*When the spirits of the dead are awakened by the words of this incantation, they cluster about the living like moths about a flame.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9 |
| **Range** | 27" |

Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target enemy unit. Any enemy model whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 4 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[Scroll of the Outcast Dead](../magic-item/scroll-of-the-outcast-dead.md) (Unique) 35 points

*Upon reading aloud from a Scroll of the Outcast Dead, the souls of the damned are dragged screaming from the coldness of the void to torment the living.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8 |
| **Range** | 18" |

[Remains in play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is within 18" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as difficult terrain. The template scatters D6" during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any unit (friend or foe) that moves through the template, or that the template moves over, suffers a single Strength 4 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2. This hit has the [Multiple Wounds (D3)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule. Any unit that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from this weapon must make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) as if it had taken heavy casualties.

[Scroll of Urgency](../magic-item/scroll-of-urgency.md) (Unique) 30 points

*Scrolls of Urgency contain a simple but effective incantation which, when read aloud, imbues the undead servants of the Mortuary Cult with morbid vitality.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7 |
| **Range** | 21" |

If the target friendly unit has already moved during this [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase) (not including failed charge moves), it can immediately move again.

[Scroll of Righteous Smiting](../magic-item/scroll-of-righteous-smiting.md) (Unique) 10 points

*In response to the words upon a Scroll of Righteous Smiting, the warriors of ancient Nehekhara redouble their efforts to strike down their enemies.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7 |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

[Remains in play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this spell is in play, friendly units whose troop type is [war machine](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md) that are within the caster's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) can re-roll a single Artillery dice or Scatter dice per turn.
