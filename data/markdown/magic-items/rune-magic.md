# Rune Magic

Unlike other armies, models in a Dwarfen Mountain Holds army cannot purchase any of the magic items listed in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook. Instead, Dwarfs may be equipped with runic items. These are effectively magic items tailored to your own requirements by purchasing and combining the runes given on the following sections.
