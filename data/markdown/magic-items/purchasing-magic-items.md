# Purchasing Magic Items

If a model can purchase magic items, this will be listed among the options given in its rules, along with a limit to how many points can be spent on magic items for that model. Usually, only characters can purchase magic items, though some unit champions or standard bearers have the option to purchase magic items as well.

Any magic items purchased should be noted down and their points added to that of the model that carries them on your muster list
