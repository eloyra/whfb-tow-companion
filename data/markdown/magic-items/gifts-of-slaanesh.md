# Gifts of Slaanesh

The following Daemonic Gifts can only be purchased by Daemons of Slaanesh:

[Infernal Enrapturess](../magic-item/infernal-enrapturess.md) (Ability) 50 points

*Weaving a discordant melody upon an accursed harp, the Daemon enraptures those with the mage-sight.*

Daemonic Heralds whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Whilst within this character's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), friendly [Daemons of Slaanesh](../special-rules/daemon-of-slaanesh.md) that are also Wizards (not including this model) may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) they make. Whilst within this character's Command range, enemy Wizards suffer a -1 modifier to any Casting roll they make.

[Allure of Slaanesh](../magic-item/allure-of-slaanesh.md) (Ability) 35 points

*The Daemon is possessed of a peculiarly captivating and disarming visage.*

Enemy units must make a Leadership test before making any rolls To Hit against this model during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). If this test is failed, only rolls of a natural 6 will hit.

[Siren Song](../magic-item/siren-song.md) (Ability) 30 points

*The Daemon sings an alluring song, drawing its enemies to their doom.*

Single use. At the start of your opponent's [Declare Charges & Charge Reactions](../the-movement-phase/declare-charges-and-charge-reactions.md) sub-phase, nominate a single enemy unit that is able to declare a charge against this model (or its unit). That enemy unit must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) [Leadership Tests](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, the unit may act as normal. However, if this test is failed, it must declare a charge against this model (or its unit).

[Soporific Musk\*](../magic-item/soporific-musk.md) (Ability) 30 points

*The Daemon exudes a heady and cloying musk that dulls the wits of its enemies.*

Enemy models engaged in combat with this model cannot use the [Strike First](../special-rules/strike-first.md) special rule. Enemy models that do not have the Strike First special rule become subject to the [Strike Last](../special-rules/strike-last.md) special rule instead.

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)

[Enrapturing Gaze\*](../magic-item/enrapturing-gaze.md) (Ability) 20 points

*The beguiling gaze of this Daemon holds the attention of all foolish enough to meet it.*

Enemy models engaged in combat with this model cannot use the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) special rule of their [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md).
