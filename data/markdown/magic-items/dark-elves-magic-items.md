# Dark Elves Magic Items

These magic items are unique to Dark Elves armies. These can be purchased by models within a Dark Elves army in exactly the same way as Common magic items, as described in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook.

## Magic Weapons

[Executioner's Axe](../magic-item/executioners-axe.md) (Magic Weapon) 70 points

*A single blow from this huge, black-bladed weapon can cut any opponent in half.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *When making a roll To Wound for a hit caused with the Executioner's Axe, a roll of 2+ is always a success, regardless of the target's Toughness.*

[Sword of Ruin](../magic-item/sword-of-ruin.md) (Magic Weapon) 65 points

*The wickedly sharp edge of the Sword of Ruin can cleave through armour as if it were air.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | \* | - |

***Notes:***  *No*  [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) *,*  [Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md)  *or*  [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md)  *saves are permitted against wounds caused by the Sword of Ruin.*

[Lifetaker](../magic-item/lifetaker.md) (Magic Weapon) 35 points

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

***Notes:***  *High Beastmasters only. Any enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Whip of Agony suffers a -1 modifier to its Toughness characteristic (to a minimum of 1) for the remainder of the game.*

## Magic Armour

[Shield of Ghrond](../magic-item/shield-of-ghrond.md) (Magic Armour) 40 points

*The Daemon-faced Shield of Ghrond consumes the strength of the enemy's attacks*

The Shield of Ghrond is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, all attacks directed against its bearer suffer a -1 modifier to their Strength characteristic (to a minimum of 1).

[Blood Armour](../magic-item/blood-armour.md) (Magic Armour) 30 points

*When anointed with the blood of the foe, this armour becomes ever more endurable.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The Blood Armour is a suit of armour that gives its wearer an [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of 5+. For each unsaved wound the wearer inflicts, this armour value is improved by 1, to a maximum of 2+.

## Talismans

[Pendant of Khaeleth](../magic-item/pendant-of-khaeleth.md) (Talisman) 40 points

*The protection offered by this amulet grows to match the power of an enemy's attack.*

The Pendant of Khaeleth gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack with a Strength of 4 or lower, and a 4+ Ward save against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack with a Strength of 5 or higher.

[Pearl of Infinite Bleakness](../magic-item/pearl-of-infinite-bleakness.md) (Talisman) 15 points

*Swirled with red and black veins, this magical pearl emits a soul-numbing aura.*

The bearer of the Pearl of Infinite Bleakness and any unit they have joined gains the [Immune to Psychology](../special-rules/immune-to-psychology.md) special rule.

## Magic Standards

[Banner of Nagarythe](../magic-item/banner-of-nagarythe.md) (Magic Standard) 65 points

*The personal banner of the Dread-King proclaims his reign over the Elven Kingdoms.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Nagarythe gains the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rule. In addition, when calculating its combat result, the unit may claim an additional bonus of +1 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) point.

[Standard of Slaughter](../magic-item/standard-of-slaughter.md) (Magic Standard) 40 points

*Anointed with the blood of an Ulthuan Elf, this banner exudes a sense of bitter determination.*

When calculating its combat result during a turn in which it charged, a unit carrying the Standard of Slaughter may claim an additional bonus of +D3 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) points.

[Banner of Har Ganeth](../magic-item/banner-of-har-ganeth.md) (Magic Standard) 25 points

*Under the influence of this ancient banner, warriors strike with deadly precision.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Har Ganeth improves the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of its combat weapons by 1.

[Cold-Blooded Banner](../magic-item/cold-blooded-banner.md) (Magic Standard) 20 points

*Those that march beneath this Cold One blood-soaked banner display a steely discipline.*

Single use. A unit carrying the Cold-blooded Banner may use it when making any test against its Leadership characteristic, including a [Break test](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md). When it does, it may roll an extra D6 and discard the highest result.

## Enchanted Items

[Black Dragon Egg](../magic-item/black-dragon-egg.md) (Enchanted Item) 35 points

*When a Black Dragon egg is eaten, the properties of the Dragon are temporarily passed on.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, the bearer of a Black Dragon Egg can consume it. Until the end of that turn, the model has a Toughness characteristic of 6 (which cannot be improved further) and gains [noxious breath](../weapons-of-war/noxious-breath.md).

[Hydra's Tooth](../magic-item/hydras-tooth.md) (Enchanted Item) 30 points

*Fangs are taken from the maw of a slain Hydra and crafted into deadly throwing weapons.*

A Hydra's Tooth is a missile weapon with the following profile:

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 9" | S | -3 | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon can target a specific model within the target unit, such as a champion or a character.*

[The Guiding Eye](../magic-item/the-guiding-eye.md) (Enchanted Item) 25 points

*Set in black iron, this oval ruby grants mystical sight to the wearer.*

Single use. The bearer of the Guiding Eye and any unit they have joined may re-roll any failed rolls To Hit made during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase).

## Arcane Items

[Black Staff](../magic-item/black-staff.md) (Arcane Item) 55 points

*A Black Staff is the talisman of the High Mistresses of the Convent of Sorceresses.*

The bearer of the Black Staff may use it when attempting to cast a spell. If they do so, roll an extra D6 when making the [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) and discard the lowest result. However, if a double 1 is rolled on any two of the dice rolled, the spell is [miscast](../magic/miscast.md).

[Tome of Furion](../magic-item/tome-of-furion.md) (Arcane Item) 15 points

*Furion of Clar Karond inscribed his knowledge onto sheets of flayed skin.*

The bearer of the Tome of Furion knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md).

[Focus Familiar\*](../magic-item/focus-familiar.md) (Arcane Item) 10 points

*The Wizard channels their spells through the eyes of an arcane homunculus.*

Single use. The owner of a Focus Familiar may use it when they attempt to cast a spell. Place a marker (such as a Familiar model) completely within 12" of the owner. The range and all effects of the spell are measured from this marker, rather than from the owner. If the spell requires a line of sight, this is determined from the marker (which has a 360° vision arc).

[\*Extremely Common](extremely-common-magic-items.md)
