# Infamous Origins

*From dishonoured knights to proud hunters, to stoic infantry commanders, those that rise to pwoer andinfamy in the Border Princes, however fleetingly bring with them a wild and eclectic mix of martial skills from the homes they left behind.*

To represent this, any Renegade Lords or Outcast Wizards within an army made using the Renegade Crowns Army of Infamy muster list may have a single Infamous Origin, chosen from those detailed on this page. Each Infamous Origin gives a character certain bonuses in the form of improved characteristics and additional special rules. In some cases, having a character with a particular Infamous Origin may grant additional benefits to other units in the same army.

[The Might of Miragliano](../magic-item/the-might-of-miragliano.md) (Ability) 25 points

*Tilea is famous for its mercenary companies led by skilled commanders of great courage and repute.*

A model with the Might of Miragliano Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic.
- Gains the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) and [Tilean Stoicism](../special-rules/tilean-stoicism.md) special rules.

[The Renegade Knight](../magic-item/the-renegade-knight.md) (Ability) 25 points

*High born and haughty, renegade knights are often disdainful of lesser warriors.*

A model with the Renegade Knight Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Weapon Skill and Strength characteristics.
- May purchase magic items from the [Kingdom of Bretonnia magic item](../warhammer-armies/kingdom-of-bretonnia-army-list.md) lists in *Forces of Fantasy* and *Arcane Journal - Kingdom of Bretonnia*.
- Gains the [Noble Disdain](../special-rules/noble-disdain.md) and [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rules.

[The Noble Outlaw](../magic-item/the-noble-outlaw.md) (Ability) 20 points

*The Border Princes are filled with ruthless outlaws and cutthroats that claim to be of noble birth.*

A model with the Noble Outlaw Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Ballistic Skill and Leadership characteristics.
- Gains the ["Hold the Line"](../special-rules/hold-the-line.md) special rule.
- May purchase magic items from the [Empire of Man magic item lists](empire-of-man-magic-items.md) in *Forces of Fantasy* and *Arcane Journal - Empire of Man*.
- May take one of the following:

  - [Handgun](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md) (+6 points)
  - [Repeater handgun](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md) (+8 points)
  - [Repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) (+8 points)
  - [Shortbow](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+2 points)
  - [Longbow](../weapons-of-war/longbow.md) (+4 points)
- May take one of the following special rules:

  - [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) (+10 points)
  - [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) (+10 points)

[The Wandering Diestro](../magic-item/the-wandering-diestro.md) (Ability) 20 points

*Many a skilled duellist of noble birth has been driven from their home due to intrigue and treachery.*

A model with the Wandering Diestro Infamous Origin:

- Has a +2 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic.
- Gains the [Feint & Dodge](../special-rules/feint-and-dodge.md) special rule.
- May not take any weapons with the [Strike Last](../special-rules/strike-last.md) special rule.
- May take an [additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points)
