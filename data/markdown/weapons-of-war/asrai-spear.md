# Asrai Spear

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *A model wielding an Asrai spear cannot make a*  [supporting attack](../the-combat-phase/supporting-attacks.md)  *during a turn in which it charged. During a turn in which it was charged in its front arc, a model wielding an Asrai spear gains a +1 modifier to its Initiative against the charging unit(s).*
