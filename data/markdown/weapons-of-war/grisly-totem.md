# Grisly Totem

*Adorned with gruesome trophies, Kralmaw's staff evokes a sense of dread in those unfortunate enough to lay eyes upon it.*

The Grisly Totem is a [Braystaff](braystaff.md). In addition, all enemy units within 6" of Kralmaw suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2).
