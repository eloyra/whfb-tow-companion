# Dreadquake Mortar

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-72" | 3(6) | -(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a*  [stone thrower](../war-machines/stone-throwers.md) *, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 5" blast template and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *. The*  [Multiple Wounds (D6)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*
