# Cathayan Lance

*Cathayan cavalry use long, heavy spears similar to lances, but light enough that the weapons of multiple ranks can be brought to bear in the press of melee.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+1 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *Models whose troop type is*  [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) *,*  [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md)  *or*  [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)  *only. A Cathayan lance's Strength and Armour Piercing modifiers apply only against enemy models the wielder charged that turn.*
