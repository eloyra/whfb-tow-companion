# Plague Censer

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *A plague censer's Strength modifier applies only during the first round of combat. In addition, during the*  [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) *, a model armed with a plague censer may re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 1.*
