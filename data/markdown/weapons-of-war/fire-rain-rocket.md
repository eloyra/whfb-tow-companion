# Fire Rain Rocket

Each turn, a Fire Rain Rocket Battery can fire one of the two different types of rocket described below.

**Bastion Rockets:**

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-48" | 4(5) | -1(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a stone thrower, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 3" blast template and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *. The*  [Multiple Wounds (D6)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*

**Rocket Battery:**

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-48" | 3(4) | -1(-1) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a stone thrower, using the Bombardment special rule, a 5" blast template and the Black Powder Misfire table.*
