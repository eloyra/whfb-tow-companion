# Screaming Skull Catapult

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-60" | 4(8) | -1(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a*  [stone thrower](../war-machines/stone-throwers.md) *, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 3" blast template and the*  [Stone Thrower Misfire table](../war-machines/stone-thrower-misfire-table.md) *. The*  [Multiple Wounds (D3+1)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*
