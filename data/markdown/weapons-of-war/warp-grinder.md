# Warp Grinder

*A Warp Grinder is a powerful tunnelling device, capable of rapidly boring through the earth and leaving a wide tunnel in its wake, through which the Skaven scurry to ambush their enemies.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | 5 | -3 | - |

***Notes:***  *If a Weapon Team is equipped with a Warp Grinder, both it and its parent unit gain the*  [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md)  *special rule.*
