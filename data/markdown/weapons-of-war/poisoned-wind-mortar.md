# Poisoned Wind Mortar

*This muzzle-loaded weapon lobs the infamous Poisoned Wind globes of Clan Skryre a much greater distance than the feeble arms of any mere Skaven could.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6-24" | 2(4) | - | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a*  [stone thrower](../war-machines/stone-throwers.md) *, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 3" blast template and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *.*
