# Harvester Cannon

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12" | 4 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a cannon firing*  [grapeshot](../war-machines/grapeshot.md) *, using the*  [Needs More Nails](../war-machines/needs-more-nails.md)  *special rule. If a 'Misfire' is rolled on the Artillery dice, this model loses a single Wound (instead of rolling on a Misfire table).*
