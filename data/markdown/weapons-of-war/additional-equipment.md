# Additional Equipment

Many models carry equipment (or have the option to carry equipment) that improves upon their armour value. The two most common types – the humble shield and barding for a trusty mount – are detailed below.

*Note that, regardless of the equipment carried, a model can never have an armour value better than 2+. Any bonuses that would improve a model's armour further have no effect.*
