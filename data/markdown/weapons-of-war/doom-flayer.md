# Doom-Flayer

*A Doom-flayer is a motorised ball of whirling blades which, when propelled at speed into the ranks of the enemy, is capable of wreaking horrific damage upon even the most heavily armoured warriors.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | 4 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  [Impact Hits](../special-rules/impact-hits.md)  *caused by this weapon have an*  [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md)  *characteristic of -2.*
