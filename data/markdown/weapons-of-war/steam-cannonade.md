# Steam Cannonade

*Powered by the channelled pressure of the Iron Daemon's furnace, a steam cannonade is a twin barrelled organ gun used to blast a lethal storm of red-hot shrapnel and curse-laden shot into the ranks of the enemy.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18" | 5 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *When shooting with a steam cannonade, follow the normal*  [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase)  *sequence. This weapon does not have a 360° line of sight, it can only shoot into the Iron Daemon's front arc.*
