# Ratling Gun

*A relatively modern invention, the multi-barrelled Ratling Gun spews a tremendous amount of lead shot, capable of quickly reducing enemy ranks to a bloody pulp and a red mist.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18" | 4 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like an organ gun, using the*  [Multi-Barrelled](../war-machines/multi-barrelled.md)  *special rule and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *.*
