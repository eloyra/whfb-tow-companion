# Warpfire Thrower

*A Warpfire Thrower unleashes a hellish torrent of powdered warpstone and highly flammable chemicals that can turn an entire formation into a twitching pile of steaming goo.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6" | 4 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a fire thrower, using the*  [Column of Fire](../war-machines/column-of-fire.md)  *special rule and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *. Any unit that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from this weapon must make a*  [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md)  *as if it had taken heavy casualties.*
