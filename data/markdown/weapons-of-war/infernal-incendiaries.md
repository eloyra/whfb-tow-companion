# Infernal Incendiaries

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-48" | 3(3) | -(-) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a*  [stone thrower](../war-machines/stone-throwers.md) *, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 5" blast template and the*  [Black Powder Misfire table](../war-machines/black-powder-misfire-table.md) *. Any unit that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from this weapon must make a*  [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md)  *as if it had taken heavy casualties.*
