# Doomfire

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-60" | 5(10) | -2(-5) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a stone thrower, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 3" blast template and the*  [Hellcannon Misfire table](../special-rules/hellcannon-misfire-table.md) *. Any unit that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from this weapon must make a Panic test as if it had taken heavy casualties.*
