# Plagueclaw Catapult

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12-72" | 2(4) | -3 | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a*  [stone thrower](../war-machines/stone-throwers.md) *, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule, a 5" blast template and the*  [Stone Thrower Misfire table](../war-machines/stone-thrower-misfire-table.md) *. When making a roll To Wound for this weapon, a roll of a natural 6 is always a success, even if the target's Toughness is so high that the weapon would not normally be able to wound it.*
