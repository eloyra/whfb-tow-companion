# Warpfire Blast

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6"-24" | 4(8) | -1(-3) | - |

***Notes:***  *This weapon shoots like a stone thrower, using the*  [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md)  *special rule and a 3" blast template. If a 'Misfire' is rolled on the Artillery dice, this model loses a single Wound (instead of rolling on a Misfire table). The*  [Multiple Wounds (D3+1)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md)  *special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.*
