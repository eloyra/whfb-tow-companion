| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |

***Notes:***  *A Cannon of Khorne has two profiles, representing its two modes of firing. When firing solid shot, this weapon shoots like a*  [cannon](../war-machines/cannon.md) *, using the*  [Cannon Fire](../war-machines/cannon-fire.md)  *special rule. If a 'Misfire' is rolled on the Artillery dice during step 2, this model loses a single Wound (instead of rolling on a Misfire table).*
