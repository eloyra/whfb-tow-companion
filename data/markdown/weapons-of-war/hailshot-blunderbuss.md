# Hailshot Blunderbuss

*A Hailshot blunderbuss is a broad-barrelled weapon that fires a concentrated but short-ranged burst of shot. When fired in massed ranks, these weapons produce a deadly storm, capable of obliterating enemy formations.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12" | 3 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *A model armed with a Hailshot blunderbuss suffers no negative modifiers for firing at long range, for using the*  [Multiple Shots (D3)](../special-rules/multiple-shots.md)  *special rule, or whilst making a*  [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md)  *charge reaction. In addition, if 20 or more models belonging to the same unit shoot at the same target with Hailshot blunderbusses, they may re-roll any rolls of a natural 1 when rolling To Wound.*
