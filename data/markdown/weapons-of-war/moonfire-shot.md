# Moonfire Shot

Glowing eerily with pale light, the touch of these arrows brings searing agony. An [Asrai longbow](asrai-longbow.md) with Moonfire Shot has the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) and [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rules.
