***Notes:***  *When making a roll To Wound for this weapon, a roll of a natural 6 is always a success, even if the target's Toughness is so high that the weapon would not normally be able to wound it. In addition, no*  [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md)  *is permitted against wounds caused by this weapon (* [Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md)  *and*  [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md)  *saves can be attempted as normal).*
