# Lore of Troll Magic

In addition to the [Lores of Magic](https://tow.whfb.app/the-lores-of-magic) detailed in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook, some Wizards, especially those with an affinity for Trolls, may know spells from the Lore of Troll Magic. As usual, if it is stated that a Wizard knows spells from one of a number of given Lores of Magic, you must choose one of these Lores when writing your muster list.

[Big Smartz (Signature Spell)](../spell/big-smartz.md)

*Gesturing lazily with a crooked staff, the caster enhances and sharpens the minds of those nearby, giving them a clearer look upon the world.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this spell is in play, friendly units within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of this model may re-roll any failed [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) tests. Additionally, if a friendly unit within the Command range of this model when this spell is cast failed its Stupidity test during the [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase of the same turn, it may immediately make this test again.

[1. Acidic Bile](../spell/acidic-bile.md)

*Spitting a guttural incantation, the caster launches a ball of sickly green and yellow Troll vomit toward the enemy, scorching flesh and dissolving armour.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target enemy unit. Once placed, the template will scatter D3+1". Any enemy model whose base lies underneath the template's final position risks being hit and suffering a Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[2. Troll Brainz](../spell/troll-brainz.md)

*Under the beguiling influence of this crude magic, even the most experienced warriors can quickly find themselves hopelessly confused and at a loss for what to do.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this spell is in play, the target enemy unit becomes subject to the [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) special rule and reduces their Leadership characteristic by 1.

[3. Ravenous Recourse](../spell/ravenous-recourse.md)

*Through arcane means or by simply being reminded how hungry they are, the assembled Trolls race forward with a sudden burst of vigour.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until the end of this turn, all friendly units that have the [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) special rule and are within 12" of the caster gain a +2 modifier to their Movement characteristic.

[4. Foetid Whirlpool](../spell/foetid-whirlpool.md)

*A deluge of foul swamp water and unmentionable waste is spun into a swirling tornado and unleashed onto the battlefield.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is within 18" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as [difficult terrain](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md). The template scatters D6" during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any enemy unit that moves through the template, or that the template moves over, suffers D3+3 Strength 4 hits, each with an AP of -3.

[5. Torrent of Filth](../spell/torrent-of-filth.md)

*With an almighty belch, the caster heaves a stream of burning bile across their foes as the battlelines clash.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | Combat |

Place a flame template so that the narrow end touches the caster's base edge and the broad end is over a unit they are engaged in combat with. Any model (friend or foe) whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.

[6. Rapid Regeneration](../spell/rapid-regeneration.md)

*Drawing on the regenerative properties of theTrolls, the caster channels that enhanced healing towards those in harm's way.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until the end of your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target friendly unit gains the [Flammable](../special-rules/flammable.md) and [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rules.
