# Lore of the Wilds

In addition to the [Lores of Magic](https://tow.whfb.app/lores-of-magic) detailed in the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook, some Wizards, especially those who dwell within the borders of the Old World's ancient forests, may know spells from the Lore of the Wilds Lore of Magic. As usual, if it is stated that a Wizard knows spells from one of a number of given Lores of Magic, you must choose one of these Lores when writing your muster list.

[Swirling Mists (Signature Spell)](../spell/swirling-mists.md)

*The beguiling mist that has turned about many travellers can be called upon to shroud the forces of Athel Loren from prying eyes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | Self |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, any enemy model that directs a shooting attack at the caster, or any unit they have joined, must re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 6.

[1. Fury of Athel Loren](../spell/fury-of-athel-loren.md)

*The caster summons forth writhing vines and briars from the ground to slash and strangle those who seek to harm Athel Loren and its inhabitants.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

The target enemy unit suffers D3+1 Strength 6 hits, each with an AP of -2 and the [Multiple Wounds (2)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule.

[2. Ariel's Blessing](../spell/ariels-blessing.md)

*Channelling the power of the Mage Queen herself, the caster surrounds their allies in a healing golden light.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 10" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target friendly unit gains the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.

[3. Spiteful Torrent](../spell/spiteful-torrent.md)

*Swarms of Spites and forest insects erupt from the casters' staff, racing across the battlefield as a writhing mass that bites and stings all within reach.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 15" |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is within 15" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as [difficult terrain](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md). The template scatters D6" during every [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase. Any enemy unit that moves through the template, or that the template moves over, suffers 3D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.

[4. Durthu's Wrath](../spell/durthus-wrath.md)

*Having witnessed untold destruction within his homeland, Durthu's rage echoes through Athel Loren and can be drawn upon by those who act in harmony with the forest.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | Combat |

Each model in the [fighting rank](../the-combat-phase/the-fighting-rank.md) of a unit that the caster is engaged with must immediately make an Initiative test. Each model that fails this test suffers D3 Strength 4 hits, with no armour save permitted ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).

[5. Hidden Pathways](../spell/hidden-pathways.md)

*The forests of Athel Loren can be compelled to hurry the passage of those who act with its best interests at heart.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target friendly unit gains the [Reserve Move](../special-rules/reserve-move.md) special rule and may perform a [Quick Turn](../unusual-formations/quick-turn.md), even if it would not normally be able to do so.

[6. Sapping Blight](../spell/sapping-blight.md)

*As roots draw water from the earth, so too can the mages of Athel Loren sap the lifeforce from their foes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Hex](../magic/hex.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | 12" |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, the target enemy unit suffers a -D3 modifier to its Strength characteristic (to a minimum of 1). This spell may target an enemy unit engaged in combat. If this spell is cast, the effects of any other Hex previously cast on the target unit immediately expire.
