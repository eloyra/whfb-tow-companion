# Spell Categories (Lores)

See also: [Spell Categories](../magic/spell-categories.md)

Spells fall into six categories. These determine during which phase of the game they can be cast. These are:

[Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md): Spells that empower the caster's allies, protecting them with sorcerous wards and magically enhancing their fighting prowess.

[Hex](../magic/hex.md): The opposite of Enchantment spells, spells that weaken the enemy in some way, perhaps slowing them down or sapping their strength.

[Conveyance](../magic/conveyance.md): These spells enable Wizards to enhance movement or transport units from place to place.

[Magic Missiles](../magic/magic-missiles.md): These are sorcerous projectiles that the Wizard hurls at the foe. Magic Missiles can only target enemy units.

[Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md): These are roiling globes of magical energy that travel across the battlefield wreaking all kinds of havoc.

[Assailment](../magic/assailment.md): These spells are deadly attacks that strike the foe without warning.
