# Wailing Dirge

*The chilling howl of a restless spirit is an omen of death and despair, capable of crippling even the most resilient prey.*

During the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) of its turn, unless it marched during the preceding [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase), a model with this special rule may make a 'Wailing Dirge' attack. A Wailing Dirge attack may target any enemy unit that is within 8" of this model (including units that are engaged in combat) and that this model can draw a line of sight to, or that this model is engaged in combat with.

The target must make a Leadership test with a -2 modifier to its Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2). If this test is failed, the target suffers a number of wounds equal to the amount by which it failed the test, with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).

*Note that a Wailing Dirge attack can target an enemy character, regardless of the usual rules for targeting*  [Lone characters](../characters/lone-characters.md) *.*
