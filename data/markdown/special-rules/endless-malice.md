# Endless Malice

*A Preyton will rend and tear at the corpses of its victims with such malice as to fill the hearts of onlookers with cold dread.*

If this model wins a round of combat and chooses to restrain and reform (and passes its [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) to do so), enemy units within 6" of it must make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) as if a nearby friend had been destroyed.
