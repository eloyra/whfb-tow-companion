# The Questing Vow

*"...I set down my lance, symbol of duty. I spurn my home and those whom I love, taking up instead the tools of my Quest. I give my body, heart and soul to the Lady whom I seek..."*

A model with this Chivalrous Vow has the [Stubborn](stubborn.md) special rule and can re-roll any failed [Fear](fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror test](terror.md). In addition, a model with this Chivalrous Vow does not have to make a Panic test when a friendly unit with the [Peasantry](peasantry.md) special rule is destroyed whilst within 6" of it, or when it is fled through by a friendly unit with the Peasantry special rule. However, a model with this Chivalrous Vow cannot be equipped with a lance (be it magical or mundane).

A unit with this Chivalrous Vow cannot be joined by a character that has the [Knight's Vow](the-knights-vow.md) or the Peasantry special rule. A character with this Chivalrous Vow cannot join a unit with the Peasantry special rule.
