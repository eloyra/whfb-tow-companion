# Cult of the Bloodied Hound

*Renowned hunters and bloodthirsty warriors that believe the gods favour the bold, members of the Tribe of the Hound value combat above all other endeavours.*

Models belonging to this Chaotic Cult gain a +1 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic.
