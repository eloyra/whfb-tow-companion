# Nuln State Troops

*The City-state of Nuln boasts a large standing army, well-equipped with halberds and handguns.*

Regiments of [Nuln State Troops](../unit/nuln-state-troops.md) and [Nuln Veteran State Troops](../unit/nuln-veteran-state-troops.md) are made as follows, using the profiles for [State Troops](../unit/state-troops.md) and [Veteran State Troops](../unit/veteran-state-troops.md) in the [Empire of Man](../warhammer-armies/empire-of-man-army-list.md) army list, accompanied by detachments of [State Missile Troops](../unit/state-missile-troops.md):

- A regiment of Nuln State Troops consists of a regimental unit of State Troops, accompanied by 1-2 detachments of [State Missile Troops](../unit/nuln-state-missile-troops.md) and/or [Swordsmen](../unit/nuln-swordsmen.md).
- A regiment of Nuln Veteran State Troops consists of a regimental unit of Veteran State Troops, accompanied by 1-2 detachments of State Missile Troops and/or Swordsmen.
- Each unit of State Troops or Veteran State Troops must be equipped with [halberds](../weapons-of-war/halberd.md).
- Each detachment of State Missile Troops must be equipped with [handguns](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md).
- A detachment of Swordsmen consists of State Troops equipped with hand weapons and shields.
- Unlike regular detachments, detachments in a regiment of Nuln State Troops may upgrade one model to a Sergeant (champion) for free.
