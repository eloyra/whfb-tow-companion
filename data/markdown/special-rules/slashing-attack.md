# Slashing Attack

*Screamers' slashing blades tear through the foe as they dive close and pass quickly by.*

A unit with this special rule may perform a 'Slashing Attack' against a single enemy unit that is not engaged in combat. To do so, this unit must move (by flying) over the unit it wishes to attack during the [Remaining Moves](../the-movement-phase/remaining-moves.md) sub-phase. Once this unit's movement is complete, the enemy unit suffers D3 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -, for each model in this unit that moved over it.
