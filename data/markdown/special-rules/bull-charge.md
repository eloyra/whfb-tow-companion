# Bull Charge

*The sheer bulk of an Ogre Tyrant or Bruiser is such that the enemy is often crushed by the force of their charge alone.*

[Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) caused by this model (but not its mount) have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -1.
