# Razor Tusks

*Tuskgors and Razorgors bristle with viciously sharp tusks and spines which, when charged into the enemy, can wreak utter devastation.*

During a turn in which it charged, the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of this model's tusks ([hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)) is improved by 1.

*Note that this special rule only applies to attacks made by a the model, not to a chariot or its crew (should it have one).*
