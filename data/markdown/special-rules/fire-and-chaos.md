# Fire & Chaos

*Thanks to their consumption of vast amounts of warpstone and natural affinity to flame, Warpfire Dragons have an innate resistance to any magic or fire that might be used to bring them down.*

Warpfire Dragons have a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has either the [Magical Attacks](magical-attacks.md) or [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
