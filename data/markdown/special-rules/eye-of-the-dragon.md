# Eye of the Dragon

*From the vantage point of a Sky Lantern's armoured gondola, gunners signal the crews of earthbound war machines, guiding their aim.*

Friendly models whose weapons shoot using the [Bombardment](../war-machines/bombardment.md) special rule can shoot using this model's line of sight rather than their own.
