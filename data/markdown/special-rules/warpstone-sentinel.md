# Warpstone Sentinel

*Formed from clay infused with warpstone dust, Warpstone Sentinels shimmer with a dull light that hurts the eyes. Those that linger in their presence quickly sicken, succumbing to the taint of chaos.*

A Warpstone Sentinel has the [Magical Attacks](magical-attacks.md) special rule. In addition, whilst in base contact with one or more Warpstone Sentinels, enemy units suffer a - 1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic (to a minimum of 1).
