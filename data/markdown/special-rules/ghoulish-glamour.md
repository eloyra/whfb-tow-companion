# Ghoulish Glamour

*Mortals quail in awestruck disbelief at the sight of a Vampire coven lounging upon the decayed luxury of a Coven Throne.*

Enemy units must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) before making any rolls To Hit against this model during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). If this test is failed, only rolls of a natural 6 will hit.
