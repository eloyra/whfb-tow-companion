# Martial Prowess

*Elven warriors spend many hours mastering the art of war, wielding their weapons with a skill and grace absent from the ranks of their enemies.*

A unit with this special rule can make [supporting attacks](../the-combat-phase/supporting-attacks.md) to its flank or rear, as well as to its front.
