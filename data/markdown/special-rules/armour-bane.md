# Armour Bane (X)

*Some weapons are particularly well-suited to piercing armour, though they often require great skill.*

If a model with this special rule rolls a natural 6 when making a roll To Wound, the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of its weapon is improved by the amount shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as 'X').

For example, if a natural 6 is rolled when rolling To Wound with a weapon that has an AP of ' - ' and the Armour Bane (1) special rule, its AP counts as being -1 when making an [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll against that wound.
