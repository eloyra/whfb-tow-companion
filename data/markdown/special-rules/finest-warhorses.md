# Finest Warhorses

*The Warhorses of Bretonnia are bred to bear the weight of a fully armoured knight.*

When a unit with this special rule makes a [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md), [Flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) or [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, it may re-roll any dice that roll a natural 1, before discarding any dice that are required to be discarded.
