# Ark of Sotek

A Bastiladon equipped with an Ark of Sotek gains the following special rules: [Slithering Serpents](slithering-serpents.md) & [Spawn of Sotek](spawn-of-sotek.md)

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of its turn, every enemy unit within D6" of this model suffers 2D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -.

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of its turn, roll a D6. On a roll of 4+, a single Jungle Swarm within 6" of this model regains D3 lost Wounds.
