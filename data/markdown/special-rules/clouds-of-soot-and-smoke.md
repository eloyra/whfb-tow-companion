# Clouds of Soot & Smoke

*Imperial guns produce prodigious clouds of smoke, concealing Engineers from enemy fire.*

Unless it is mounted, this model is considered to be behind [full cover](../the-shooting-phase/target-behind-full-cover.md) whilst it is within 3" of a friendly unit whose troop type is [war machine](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md).
