# Blood Frenzy

*Carnosaurs are huge, aggressive creatures that become almost impossible to control when they taste the blood of the enemy.*

When this model (but not its rider) inflicts an unsaved wound upon an enemy model, it becomes subject to the [Frenzy](frenzy.md) special rule. A model with this special rule may become Frenzied in this way even if it has lost Frenzy earlier in the game.

*Note that this model's rider does not gain a +1 modifier to their Attacks characteristic.*
