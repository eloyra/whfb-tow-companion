# Soporific Musk

*Fiends of Slaanesh exude a pervasive and oily musk that, when inhaled, numbs the mind and slows the limbs of all who venture too close.*

Enemy models engaged in combat with a model with this special rule cannot use the [Strike First](strike-first.md) special rule. Enemy models that do not have the Strike First special rule become subject to the [Strike Last](strike-last.md) special rule instead.
