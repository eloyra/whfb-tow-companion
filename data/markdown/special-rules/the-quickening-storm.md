# The Quickening Storm

*Bathed in invigorating lightning, Dragon Ogres are warded against the effects of hostile magic.*

A model with this special rule has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) or a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md) spell. In addition, if a model with this special rule, or the unit it belongs to, suffers one or more hits from [Storm Call](storm-call.md), it becomes 'Quickened'. A Quickened model has a +1 modifier to both its Initiative and Attacks characteristics. This Quickening lasts until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase.
