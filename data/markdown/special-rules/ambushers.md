# Ambushers

*Stealthy warriors specialise in outflanking the foe, appearing from an unexpected quarter.*

A unit with this special rule may be held in reserve rather than be deployed at the start of the game. From the beginning of round two onwards, roll a D6 during each of your [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phases for each unit of Ambushers in your army that is held in reserve. On a roll of 1-3, the unit is delayed until your next turn at least. On a roll of 4+, the unit arrives, entering the battle as [reinforcements](../movement-in-detail/reinforcements.md) during the [Compulsory Moves](../the-movement-phase/compulsory-moves.md) sub-phase. The unit may be placed on any edge of the battlefield, chosen by its controlling player, but cannot be placed within 8" of an enemy model. If any Ambushers are still held in reserve by the start of round five, they arrive automatically.
