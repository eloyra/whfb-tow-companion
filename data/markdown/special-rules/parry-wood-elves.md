# Parry (Wood Elves)

*Thanks to hours spent training with unmatched intensity, the protectors of Talsyn's sacred sites are able to effortlessly turn aside their opponents' blows.*

When fighting with a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and shield, or [Asrai spear](../weapons-of-war/asrai-spear.md) and [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md), this unit improves its [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1.
