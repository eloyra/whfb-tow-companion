# Will of the Dragons

*For the people of Grand Cathay loyalty to their Dragon masters is paramount. They know, as long as they are true to their will, the Empire of Grand Cathay will never falter before her enemies.*

A unit with this special rule may re-roll a failed [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) when a friendly unit is destroyed whilst within 6" of it, or when it is fled through by a friendly unit.
