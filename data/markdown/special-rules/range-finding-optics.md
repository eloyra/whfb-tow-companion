# Range Finding Optics

*Thanks to an eccentric collection of glass lenses, Burlok is able to accurately direct Dwarfen firepower even at extreme ranges.*

Unless Burlok is fleeing or engaged in combat, once per turn, during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase), a friendly unit of Quarrellers, Thunderers or a Dwarf war machine that is within his [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) can either re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1, or re-roll a single Artillery dice. In addition, Burlok and any unit he has joined do not suffer the usual -1 [To Hit modifier](../the-shooting-phase/to-hit-modifiers.md) when shooting at Long Range.
