# Veteran

*Veteran warriors have seen and done it all, and it takes a lot to unsettle them.*

If the majority of the models in a unit have this special rule, the unit may re-roll any failed [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md).

*Note that a*  [Break test](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md)  *is not a Leadership test.*
