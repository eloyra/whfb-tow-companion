# Ethereal

*Lacking physical bodies, some beings are immune to mundane weapons.*

Ethereal creatures treat all terrain as [open ground](../battlefield-terrain/open-ground.md) for the purposes of movement. They cannot end their movement inside [impassable terrain](../battlefield-terrain/impassable-terrain.md), though they can pass through it. In addition, Ethereal creatures can only be wounded by [Magical attacks](magical-attacks.md). Characters that are not Ethereal cannot join units that are, and vice versa.
