# First to the Fray

*In battle, Doomseekers rush ahead of the Dwarfen lines, eagerly seeking out worthy foes.*

This model increases its maximum possible charge range by 3" and, when it makes a [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) roll, may apply a +D3 modifier to the result.
