# Banished Lords

*Though once-noble knights and lords find themselves banished from the lands of Bretonnia, many seek to find an end to their exile and return to the Lady's light.*

Barons and Paladins within a [Bretonnian Exiles](../warhammer-armies/bretonnian-exiles.md) army cannot have the [Grail Vow](the-grail-vow.md). However, the following characters and units may replace the [Knight's Vow](the-knights-vow.md) with the [Exile's Vow](the-exiles-vow.md) for free:

- Baron or Paladin
- Knights of the Realm on Foot
- Knights Errant
- Mounted Knights of the Realm
- Pegasus Knights
