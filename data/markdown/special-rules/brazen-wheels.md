# Brazen Wheels

*The brazen bulk of a Skull Cannon grinds inexorably across the battlefield, its churning wheels, driven by its daemonic engine, crushing all before it.*

[Stomp Attacks](stomp-attacks.md) made by a Skull Cannon of Khorne have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -3. However, this rule cannot be used against models whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) – they are simply too large to be caught beneath a Skull Cannon's wheels.
