# Mounted Host

*In the frigid wastelands at the top of the world, cohorts of mounted warriors thunder across the frozen earth in constant search of worthy adversaries.*

All characters within a [Heralds of Darkness](../warhammer-armies/heralds-of-darkness.md) army must be mounted or have the [Fly (X)](fly.md) special rule.
