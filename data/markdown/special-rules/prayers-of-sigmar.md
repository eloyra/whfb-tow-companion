# Prayers of Sigmar

*The solemn hymns and chanted prayers of Sigmar's Warrior Priests do more than simply strengthen the faith of believers; through them, Sigmar's power is made manifest.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this character may attempt to invoke Sigmar's blessings by chanting a single Prayer from the list below and making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, the Prayer takes immediate effect:

- **Hammer of Sigmar:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, this model, its mount and a single friendly unit that is within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) when this Prayer is chanted\* may re-roll any rolls To Hit or To Wound of a natural 1 made during the Combat phase.
- **Shield of Faith:** Until your next Start of Turn sub-phase, this model and a single friendly unit that is within its Command range when this Prayer is chanted\* has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase).
- **Soulfire:** A single enemy unit this model is engaged in combat with immediately suffers D6 Strength 3 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2. These hits have the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) and [Magical Attacks](magical-attacks.md) special rules.

*\*Note that if more than one friendly unit is within this model's Command range when this Prayer is chanted, you must tell your opponent which unit is affected.*

*Note also that the effects of these Prayers on friendly units are not cumulative. In other words, a friendly unit cannot be affected by the same Prayer more than once during the same turn.*
