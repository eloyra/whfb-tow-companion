# Bound Spirits

*Snatched from Morr's dark realm by the magic of a Necromancer, the tortured shades of the dead are bound to the will of the one that summoned them.*

Unlike other swarms, this unit is not subject to the [Undisciplined](../troop-types-in-detail/undisciplined.md) rule. In other words, this unit can use the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule of the army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) and the ["Hold your Ground"](../characters/hold-your-ground.md) rule of a [Battle Standard](../characters/the-battle-standard.md).
