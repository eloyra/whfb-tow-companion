# Mark of Chaos Undivided

*Many warriors and Beastmen worship the Gods of Chaos as a pantheon, favouring no one deity above the others.*

Models with the Mark of Chaos Undivided can re-roll any failed [Fear](fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror test](terror.md).
