# Swiftstride

*Mounted warriors, warbeasts and chariots, amongst others, are swift and deadly, crossing the battlefields of the Old World with unexpected speed.*

A unit which consists entirely of models with this special rule increases its [maximum possible charge range](../the-movement-phase/maximum-possible-charge-range.md) by 3" and, before making a [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md), [Flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) or [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, may choose to apply a +D6 modifier to the result.
