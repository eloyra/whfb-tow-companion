# Warriors' Duel

*The Marauder tribes respect any warrior brave enough to face their enemy in single combat.*

Instead of rolling off to determine which player takes the first turn, you may challenge your opponent to a Warrior's Duel. If your opponent accepts, both players choose a single unit champion with a Wounds characteristic of 1 whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) from within their army to fight in a Warrior's Duel.

The chosen combatants then fight as if engaged in a challenge, fighting as many rounds of combat as necessary to determine a victor. Once a victor has been determined, that model's controlling player takes the first turn. If both champions are slain, the players must roll off as normal.

If the challenge is declined, the [Wolves of the Sea](../warhammer-armies/wolves-of-the-sea.md) army counts as having won the roll-off to determine which player takes the first turn.

*Note that, should a Bretonnian army accept the challenge, it counts as having prayed for the*  [Blessings of the Lady](blessings-of-the-lady.md) *. Should a Bretonnian army decline the challenge, it cannot pray for the Blessing of the Lady and any units that would usually begin the game with it, such as models with the*  [Grail Vow](the-grail-vow.md) *, automatically lose it.*
