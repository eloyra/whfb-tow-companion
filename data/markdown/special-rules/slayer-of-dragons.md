# Slayer of Dragons

*Those Slayers that hunt the mightiest of wyrms must learn quickly how best to slay such creatures in battle, lest their eventual doom be considered unworthy.*

When this character makes a roll To Wound, a roll of 4+ is always a success, regardless of the target's Toughness. In addition, each unsaved wound inflicted by this character against an enemy model whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) has the [Multiple Wounds (D3)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
