# Reserve Move

*Warriors that excel at hit and run warfare advance quickly, unleashing a deadly volley before withdrawing.*

Unless it charged, marched or fled during the [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase), a unit in which the majority of the models have this special rule may make a Reserve move at the end of the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) of its turn, after all shooting has been resolved. A unit making a Reserve move moves as described in the [Basic Movement](../movement-in-detail/basic-movement.md) rules. It may [manoeuvre](../movement-in-detail/manoeuvres.md) normally, but cannot [march](../movement-in-detail/marching.md).
