# Requires Two Hands

*Many weapons are unwieldy, requiring a firm two-handed grip in order to use effectively.*

A model cannot use a shield alongside a weapon with this special rule during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) (a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) can still be used against wounds caused by shooting or magic during other phases of the game).
