# Gifts of Khaine

*Blessed by Khaine, a Death Hag and her minions are a fearful sight to behold.*

As described above, a Death Hag may take one of the following gifts of Khaine:

Enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic whilst within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of one or more Death Hags that have this special rule and that are not fleeing.

This character has the [Extra Attacks (+D3)](extra-attacks.md) special rule.

This character, their mount and any unit they have joined cannot lose the [Frenzy](frenzy.md) special rule.
