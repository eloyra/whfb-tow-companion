# Extra Attacks (+X)

*Through fury, extra limbs, or being armed to the teeth, this warrior can strike more blows.*

A model with this special rule has a modifier to its Attacks characteristic, as shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as '+X'). If this modifier is determined by the roll of a dice, roll when the model's combat is chosen during any [Choose & Fight Combat](../the-combat-phase/choose-and-fight-combat.md) sub-phase.
