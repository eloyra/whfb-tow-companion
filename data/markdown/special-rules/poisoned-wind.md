# Poisoned Wind

*Poisoned Wind is one of the most infamous weapons of the Skaven, who first deployed it during bitter tunnel fighting against the Dwarfs of the Worlds Edge Mountains.*

When a model using a weapon with this special rule makes a roll To Wound, a roll of 6+ is always a success, even if the target's Toughness is so high that the weapon would not normally be able to wound it. Finally, no [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) is permitted against wounds caused by a weapon with this special rule ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).

*Note that models wielding weapons that have this special rule are immune to this special rule.*
