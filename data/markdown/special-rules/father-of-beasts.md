# Father of Beasts

*Ghorros Warhoof is well-known to the Centigor herds of the Old World, and his misshapen kin are always eager to prove their worth in his presence.*

Whilst within Ghorros Warhoof's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), friendly [Centigor Herds](../unit/centigor-herd.md) may re-roll the D6 when rolling on the [Drunken](drunken.md) table.
