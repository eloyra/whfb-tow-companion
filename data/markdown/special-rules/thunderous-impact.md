# Thunderous Impact

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, any unit (friend or foe) that was within 2D6" of the strike point when a Cathayan grand cannon shoots suffers a -1 modifier to its Movement characteristic and cannot use the [Swiftstride](swiftstride.md) special rule.
