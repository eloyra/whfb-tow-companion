# Hunting Packs

*Though traditionally employed as skirmishers, some Wolf Rider mobs amass in larger numbers, allowing their wolves to get up close and personal with their prey.*

Any Goblin Wolf Rider Mob within a [Nomadic Waaagh!](../warhammer-armies/nomadic-waaagh.md) army may exchange the [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) and [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) special rules with the [Close Order](close-order.md) and [Horde](horde.md) special rules.
