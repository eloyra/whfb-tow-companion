# Caged Fury

*The Daemon bound within the Hellcannon constantly tests its bonds, attempting to break free.*

During the [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase of each of your turns, make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) for this model. If this test is failed, roll immediately on the [Hellcannon Misfire table](hellcannon-misfire-table.md).
