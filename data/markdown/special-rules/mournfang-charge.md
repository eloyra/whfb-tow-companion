# Mournfang Charge

*Driven by the momentum of the charge, the monstrous tusks of a Mournfang pierce through armour with ease to gore the enemy deeply.*

[Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) caused by a model with this special rule have the [Armour Bane (1)](armour-bane.md) special rule and an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -1.
