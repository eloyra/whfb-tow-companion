# Blessings of Khaine

*As the Cauldron of Blood fills, the baleful spirit of Khaine surrounds his devoted daughters.*

Even if engaged in combat, this model can cast the following [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 2:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 9+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, this model and every friendly Death Hag, unit of Witch Elves or unit of Sisters of Slaughter that is within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) gains one of the following:

- **Fury of Khaine** (the [Furious Charge](furious-charge.md) special rule)
- **Strength of Khaine** (the [Cleaving Blow](cleaving-blow.md) special rule)
- **Bloodshield of Khaine** (a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered)
