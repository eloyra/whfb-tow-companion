# The Grail Vow

*"...That which is sacrosanct, I shall preserve. That which is sublime, I will protect. That which threatens, I will destroy, for my holy wrath doth know no bounds..."*

A model with this Chivalrous Vow has the [Immune to Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md), [Magical Attacks](magical-attacks.md) and [Stubborn](stubborn.md) special rules. In addition, models with this Chivalrous Vow always benefit from the [Blessings of the Lady](blessings-of-the-lady.md) special rule and do not have to pray at the start of the game. However, a model with this Chivalrous Vow cannot refuse a challenge.

A unit with this Chivalrous Vow can only be joined by a character that also has this Chivalrous Vow or by a Handmaiden of the Lady. A character with this Vow cannot join a unit with the [Peasantry](peasantry.md) special rule.
