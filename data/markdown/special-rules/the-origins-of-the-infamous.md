# The Origins of the Infamous

*Those that rise to power in the Border Princes are invariably outcasts from their homelands. Many such once-noble warriors will travel in the company of loyal retainers and bodyguards, whilst others will gather about them cadres of loyal companions or desperate cutthroats.*

Characters within a [Renegade Crowns](../warhammer-armies/renegade-crowns.md) Army of Infamy can purchase an [Infamous Origin](../magic-items/infamous-origins.md). Based on these, some units in your army may gain additional benefits, as detailed below:

- [The Mercenary Hosts](the-mercenary-hosts.md)
- [The Outcast Knights](the-outcast-knights.md)
- [The Outlaw & Renegade](the-outlaw-and-renegade.md)
- [The Wandering Duelists](the-wandering-duelists.md)
