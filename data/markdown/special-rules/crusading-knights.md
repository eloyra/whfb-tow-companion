# Crusading Knights

*When chivalrous knights gather in large numbers to embark upon a sacred crusade or errantry war, they become gripped by a religious fervour, swearing fresh vows to the Lady of the Lake.*

Within an [Errantry Crusade](../warhammer-armies/errantry-crusade.md) army, the following characters and units gain the [Crusader's Zeal](crusaders-zeal.md) special rule. In addition, any of the following characters or units that have the [Knight's Vow](the-knights-vow.md) may replace it with the [Crusader's Vow](the-crusaders-vow.md) for free:

- [Baron](../unit/baron.md) or [Paladin](../unit/paladin.md)
- [Knights of the Realm on Foot](../unit/knights-of-the-realm-on-foot.md)
- [Battle Pilgrims](../unit/battle-pilgrims.md)
- [Knights Errant](../unit/knights-errant.md)
- [Mounted Knights of the Realm](../unit/mounted-knights-of-the-realm.md)
- [Questing Knights](../unit/questing-knights.md)
- [Grail Knights](../unit/grail-knights.md)
- [Pegasus Knights](../unit/pegasus-knights.md)
- [Mounted Yeomen](../unit/mounted-yeomen.md)
