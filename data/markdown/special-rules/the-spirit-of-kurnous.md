# The Spirit of Kurnous

*The restless nature and devastating power of the lord of the hunt flows through the Asrai as they race off in search of their quarry.*

0-1 unit of [Glade Riders](../unit/glade-riders.md), [Warhawk Riders](../unit/warhawk-riders.md) or [Wood Elf Beast Packs](../unit/wood-elf-beast-pack.md) per 1,000 points may have the [Furious Charge](furious-charge.md)\* and [Impetuous](impetuous.md) special rules at the cost of + 10 points per unit.

*\*Note this does not apply to the model's mount (should it have one).*
