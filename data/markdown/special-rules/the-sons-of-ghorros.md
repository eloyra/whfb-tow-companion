# The Sons of Ghorros

*Almost every herd of beasts contains a few dozen of Ghorros get. These brutes fight all the harder in their sire's presence to earn his acknowledgement.*

0-1 [Centigor herd](../unit/centigor-herd.md) in the same muster list as Ghorros may be upgraded to Sons of Ghorros for +2 points per model. Sons of Ghorros have a + 1 modifier to their Weapon Skill and Leadership characteristics (to a maximum of 10) and gain the [Armour Bane (1)](armour-bane.md) special rule.
