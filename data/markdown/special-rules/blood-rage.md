# Blood Rage

*In the tumult of battle, Beastmen are easily overcome by their primal urges, becoming frenzied with a bloodlust that can only be slaked with an excess of slaughter.*

If, when testing to see if it becomes subject to [Primal Fury](primal-fury.md), this unit passes its [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) with any roll of a natural double, it will also become [Frenzied](frenzy.md). A unit with this special rule may become Frenzied in this way even if it has lost Frenzy earlier in the game.
