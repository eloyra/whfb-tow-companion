# Runes of Protection

*Ironbreakers venture into the dark beneath the world protected from the terrors it hides by rune encrusted suits of gromril armour.*

Models with this special rule have a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
