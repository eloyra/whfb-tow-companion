# Stampede

*When Bull Centaurs charge, the ground itself trembles and the enemy is crushed beneath their brazen hooves.*

[Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) caused by a model with this special rule have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2.
