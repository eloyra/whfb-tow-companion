# Covenant of Power

*A nimbus of sorcerous energy swirls around a Casket of Souls' infernal form. So great is this power that the incantations of nearby Liche Priests become infused with a portion of the casket's energy.*

Whilst within 12" of a Casket of Souls, friendly Liche Priests may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) they make. Additionally, any model (friend or foe) that casts a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) whilst within 12" of a Casket of Souls may apply a +1 modifier to the Casting roll.

*Note, however, that this bonus does not apply to any Bound spells cast by a Casket of Souls.*
