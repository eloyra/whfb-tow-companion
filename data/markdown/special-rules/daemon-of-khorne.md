# Daemon of Khorne

*The ranks of Khorne's daemonic hosts are filled with ferocious and frenzied fiends, fuelled by a constant hunger to spill blood and claim skulls.*

Daemons of Khorne have the [Hatred (Daemons of Slaanesh)](hatred.md) special rule. In addition, during a turn in which it made a charge move, a Daemon of Khorne (but not its mount) gains a +1 modifier to its Strength characteristic.
