# The Language of the Priests

*Many were the monarchs of ancient Nehekhara that endeavoured to learn the secrets of the Mortuary Priests, but few were those that became proficient.*

The [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) of a [Nehekharan Royal Host](../warhammer-armies/nehekharan-royal-host.md) must be a Tomb King or a Tomb Prince. For +35 points, this character may have the Arise! special rule and be a Level 1 Wizard. If so, this character knows spells from the Lore of [Necromancy](../the-lores-of-magic/necromancy.md) and must also be chosen to be the army's [Hierophant](the-hierophant.md), even if it does not have the highest [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md) in your army. A Wizard with this special rule may wear armour without penalty.
