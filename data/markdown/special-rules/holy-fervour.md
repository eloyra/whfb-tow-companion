# Holy Fervour

*The presence of the War Altar of Sigmar fills those that march beside it with courage and a grim resolve to overcome their fears in defence of the Empire.*

Unless this unit is fleeing, friendly units within 6" of it automatically pass any [Fear](fear.md) tests they are required to make and can re-roll any failed [Panic tests](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md).
