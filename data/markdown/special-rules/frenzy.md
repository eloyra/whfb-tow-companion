# Frenzy

*For warriors gripped by a fighting frenzy, all rational thought is consumed by a hunger for violence.*

During a turn in which it made a charge move, or during the turn after it made a follow up move, a Frenzied model has a +1 modifier to its Attacks characteristic. This modifier does not apply to the model’s mount (in the case of a [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) model), to the beasts that draw it (in the case of a [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md)), or to its rider (in the case of a [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md)).

In addition:

- If the majority of the models in a unit are Frenzied, the unit automatically passes any [Fear](fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror tests](terror.md) it is required to make.
- If a unit that includes one or more Frenzied models is able to declare a charge during the [Declare Charges & Charge Reactions](../the-movement-phase/declare-charges-and-charge-reactions.md) sub-phase of its turn, it must do so.
- If the majority of the models in a unit are Frenzied, it cannot choose to [Flee](../the-movement-phase/flee-charge-reaction.md) as a charge reaction, nor can it ever choose to make a [Restraint test](../the-combat-phase/restrain-and-reform.md).

## Losing Frenzy

Unlike other special rules, Frenzy can be lost during a game. Any model that loses a round of combat will immediately lose this special rule.
