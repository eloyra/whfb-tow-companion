# Defensive Stance

*Jade Warriors are trained to defend the walls and streets of their great cities.*

Unless it charged during the preceding [Movement phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-movement-phase), or counts as having charged this turn, a unit with this special rule may re-roll any [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) rolls of a natural 1 made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
