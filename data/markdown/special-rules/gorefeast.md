# Gorefeast

*The blood and skulls of thefallen are consumed by a Blood Throne, fed into its infernal workings to bring the daemonic engine fresh vigour.*

For each Wound an enemy unit loses as a result of an [Impact Hit](impact-hits.md) caused by this model, roll a D6. For each roll of 6, this model immediately recovers a single lost Wound.
