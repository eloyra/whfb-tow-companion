# Run with the Pack

*Having long hunted with their packs, Elven Beast Keepers keep pace with their charges whilst instilling them with discipline.*

Whilst one or more Beast Keeper remains within this unit, the unit is not subject to the [Undisciplined](../troop-types-in-detail/undisciplined.md) rule and [war beasts](../troop-types-in-detail/war-beasts.md) within this unit do not [fear](fear.md) models with the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule. In addition, a unit with this special rule may use the Movement characteristic of the majority of the models in the unit.
