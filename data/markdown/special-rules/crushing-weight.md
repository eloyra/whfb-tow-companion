# Crushing Weight

*Enemies unfortunate enough to fall beneath the tremendous weight of a War Wagon's metal-bound wheels are crushed into the dirt.*

[Stomp Attacks](stomp-attacks.md) made by a War Wagon have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of - 1. In addition, and unlike other [chariots](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md), this model treats [low linear obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md) as [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md) rather than as [impassable terrain](../battlefield-terrain/impassable-terrain.md).
