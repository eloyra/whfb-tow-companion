# The Shadow Grows

*As the influence of the Dark Gods spreads, their mortal champions seek to swell their forces with like-minded warriors willing to pledge themselves to one of the infernal pantheon.*

For each character your army includes that has a [Mark of Chaos](marks-of-chaos.md) other than the [Mark of Chaos Undivided](mark-of-chaos-undivided.md), it must also include a non-character unit with the same Mark of Chaos, and vice versa. For example, if your army includes two characters with the [Mark of Khorne](mark-of-khorne.md), it must also include two non-character units with the Mark of Khorne.
