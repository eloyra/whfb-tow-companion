# Spurting Bile Blood

*When harmed, a Jabberslythe's boiling, acidic blood sprays its enemies, burning through their armour and into their flesh.*

For each Wound this model loses during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the attacking enemy unit suffers a Strength 4 hit, with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.
