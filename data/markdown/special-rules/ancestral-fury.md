# Ancestral Fury

*When a royal clan marches to war, the venerable warriors amongst the throng inspire the younger generations with glorious tales of heroes of old.*

During a turn in which a unit in a Royal Clan army makes a [charge move](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) of 3" or more, or made a [follow up](../the-combat-phase/follow-up.md) move, it gains a +1 modifier to its Strength characteristic.
