# Dark Runes

*Infernal runes crawl across the skin of a Doomfire Warlock, blazing with an unholy fire when death draws near.*

This unit has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
