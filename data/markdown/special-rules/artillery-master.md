# Artillery Master

*Engineers are quick to advise artillery crews how best to aim and fire a war machine.*

Unless this character is fleeing or engaged in combat, once per turn, during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase), a friendly unit of Quarrellers, unit of Thunderers or Dwarf war machine that is within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) can either re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1, or re-roll a single Artillery dice.
