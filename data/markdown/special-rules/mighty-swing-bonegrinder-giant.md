# Mighty Swing (Bonegrinder Giant)

The Bonegrinder Giant swings its club at its enemies. The target unit suffers D6+1 hits from the Bonegrinder Giant's club. For this attack, the Bonegrinder Giant's club has a Strength characteristic of S+1 and an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
