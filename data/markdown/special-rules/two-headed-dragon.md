# Two-headed Dragon

*The twin maws of Chaos Dragons breathe death upon their foes; one exhaling dark flame whilst the other emits corrosive gas.*

A Chaos Dragon/Galruch may use either [Dark Fire of Chaos](../weapons-of-war/dark-fire-of-chaos.md) or [Fumes of Contagion](../weapons-of-war/fumes-of-contagion.md) during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) of its turn. It cannot use both during the same turn.
