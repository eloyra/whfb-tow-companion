# Obsidian Sentinel

*An air of chill hangs about the Obsidian Sentinels, stilling the Winds of Magic and blinding the Mage-sight of those Wizards who would seek to harm the Celestial Dragon's realm.*

An Obsidian Sentinel has the [Magic Resistance (-2)](magic-resistance.md) special rule. In addition, any enemy Wizard that wishes to cast a spell whilst within 12" of one or more Obsidian Sentinels must first make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the Wizard's [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) suffers a -3 modifier. If this test is passed, the Wizard's Casting roll suffers a -1 modifier.
