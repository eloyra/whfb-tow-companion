# Riches & Heirlooms

*The Royal Clans of the Dwarfs are amongst the wealthiest denizens of the Old World, and such extravagant wealth means they march to war well-equipped with the finest armour and weapons.*

Character models in this army may spend an additional 25 points on [runes](../magic-items/runic-items.md). In addition, units that are able to purchase [Standard runes](../magic-items/standard-runes.md) may spend an additional 25 points on Standard runes (with the exception of Royal Clan Warriors, who already have this bonus included on their profile).
