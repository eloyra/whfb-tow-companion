# Fast Cavalry

*A highly trained unit of cavalry is able to perform complex manoeuvres, even at a full gallop.*

If all of the models (including characters) within a unit arrayed in an [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) formation have this special rule, the unit may perform its [Quick Turn](../unusual-formations/quick-turn.md) even if it marched.
