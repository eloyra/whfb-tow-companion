# "Stand Back Chief"

*Hunkering down behind their war machines, an Engineer is well-protected from enemy fire.*

A character with this special rule cannot be targeted by enemy shooting or by enemy spells whilst it is within 3" of a friendly unit whose troop type is [war machine](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md).
