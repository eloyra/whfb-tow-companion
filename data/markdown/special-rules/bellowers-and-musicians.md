# Bellowers & Musicians

Rather than using instruments, most Ogre [musicians](../command-groups/musicians.md) belong to a special caste known as Bellowers. As their name implies, a Bellower's instrument is their voice and lungs, with which they can make an incredible noise, even if only another Ogre would find it musical.
