# Toad Rage

*Ripperdactyls are driven into a feeding frenzy by the scent of their favourite food – the Lustrian blot toad. Should one of these ugly amphibians seek refuge amongst the ranks of the enemy, flocks of ravenous Ripperdactyls will quickly descend upon them.*

Before the game starts, but after [Scouts](scouts.md) have been deployed, you may place one Lustrian blot toad marker for each unit of Ripperdactyl Riders in your army (counting only units, not including characters mounted on Ripperdactyls). A single blot toad marker can be placed on any enemy unit on the battlefield, this marker remains throughout the battle.

When engaged in combat with an enemy unit with a blot toad marker, Ripperdactyls (but not their riders) gain the [Extra Attacks (+1)](extra-attacks.md) special rule, and may re-roll any roll To Hit of a natural 1.
