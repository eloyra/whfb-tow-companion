# Enhanced Regeneration

*With so many Trolls in one place, their regenerative properties seem to be in overdrive, slowed only by scorching flame, potent magic and legendary weapons.*

Models with the [Regeneration (X+)](regeneration.md) special rule within a Troll Horde army may re-roll any failed Regeneration saves against Wounds caused by non-magical attacks.
