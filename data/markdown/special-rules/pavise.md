# Pavise

*A pavise is a large, heavy shield, anchored to the ground by iron spikes.*

A pavise gives its wielder a 4+ [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) against attacks made by enemy models that lie within the wielder's front arc. However, a pavise offers no protection to its wielder against attacks made by enemy models that lie within the wielder's flank or rear arcs.
