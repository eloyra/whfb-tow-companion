# Ponderous

*Many weapons are too unwieldy to be used with any accuracy by a warrior on the move.*

A weapon with this special rule suffers a [To Hit modifier](../the-shooting-phase/to-hit-modifiers.md) of -2 for [Moving and Shooting](../the-shooting-phase/moving-and-shooting.md), rather than the usual -1.
