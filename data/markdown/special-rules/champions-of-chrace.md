# Champions of Chrace

*Even amongst the warriors of Chrace, the Lion Guard are renowned fighters, eager to best their foes in single combat.*

Any model in a unit of Lion Guard can accept [challenges](../characters/challenges.md) in the same manner as a character. In addition, once per turn a character that has joined a unit of Lion Guard may re-roll their ["Look Out, Sir!"](../characters/look-out-sir-characters.md) roll.
