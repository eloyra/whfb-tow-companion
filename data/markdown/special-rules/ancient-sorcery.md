# Ancient Sorcery

*The most ancient and powerful of Beastmen Shamans dwell amongst the wild herds, wielding a dark and primal magic that many have forgotten even existed.*

In addition to the Lores of Magic they may normally know spells from, Beastmen Shamans within a [Wild Herd](../warhammer-armies/wild-herd.md) army may know spells from the [Lore of Primal Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-primal-magic.md).
