# Scarab Prince

*Apophas' spirit resides in a swarm of scarabs which, should his spirit ever be banished back to the Underworld, will drown those who brought about the prince's end.*

Should Prince Apophas lose his last Wound, before his model is removed from play, all enemy units within 2D6" of him suffer 2D6 Strength 2 hits with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.
