# From the Storm Clouds

*The ships of Ulthuan's fleet are protected at all times by Lothern Skycutters, sailing unseen in the clouds above and ever ready to descend upon an unsuspecting enemy.*

- 0-1 [Lothern Skycutter](../unit/lothern-skycutter.md) per 1,000 points in a [Sea Guard Garrison](../warhammer-armies/sea-guard-garrison.md) Army of Infamy, including Skycutters taken as character mounts, may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule for +10 points per model.
