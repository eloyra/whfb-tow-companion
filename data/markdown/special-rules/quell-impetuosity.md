# Quell Impetuosity

*Where most Orcs and Goblins are impetuous, Black Orcs are disciplined warriors. This discipline is usually extended to those around them through threats and intimidation.*

Whilst within 6" of a unit with this special rule, a friendly [Impetuous](impetuous.md) unit may re-roll a failed Leadership test when testing to determine if it must declare a charge or act as normal.
