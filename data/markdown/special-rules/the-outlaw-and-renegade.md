# The Outlaw & Renegade

*In the lawless reaches of the Old World, bandits and highwaymen stand ever ready to fall upon the unwary.*

For each character in your army that has the Noble Outlaw Infamous Origin, 0-1 unit of [Hireling Outriders](../unit/hireling-outriders.md) may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule for +1 point per model.
