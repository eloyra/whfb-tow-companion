# Deflect Shots

*It is said that, as the Swordmasters advance into battle, their blades blur and weave faster than the eyes of the enemy can see, knocking aside arrows in mid-flight.*

A model with this special rule has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical shooting attack.
