# Praising the Gods

Once per game, during the Command sub-phase of its turn, a unit belonging to a Chaotic Cult may implore its patron for their blessing by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, until its next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all [Chaotic Cult](chaotic-cults.md) models within the unit gain the following benefit. If this test is failed, the unit's implorations go unheeded:

- [Cult of the Bloodied Hound](cult-of-the-bloodied-hound.md)
- [Cult of the Carrion Crow](cult-of-the-carrion-crow.md)
- [Cult of the Slithering Serpent](cult-of-the-slithering-serpent.md)
- [Cult of the Fell Raptor](cult-of-the-fell-raptor.md)
