# Guardians

*Temple Guard protect the Mage-Priests with their tough, scaled bodies and interlocking shields.*

Should a friendly Slann Mage-Priest model that is within 3" of this unit suffer a hit during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase), roll a D6. On a roll of 2+, you may choose to transfer that hit and all of its effects onto this unit. In addition, any model in a unit of Temple Guard that is within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of a Slann Mage-Priest can issue and accept challenges in the same manner as a character.
