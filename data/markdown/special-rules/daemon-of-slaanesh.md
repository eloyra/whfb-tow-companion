# Daemon of Slaanesh

*Shrouded in hypnotic glamour, the Daemons of Slaanesh surge across the battlefield on lithe limbs, capering from foe to foe, claw-hands slicing through flesh and armour.*

Daemons of Slaanesh have the [Hatred (Daemons of Khorne)](hatred.md) special rule. In addition, Daemons of Slaanesh increase their maximum possible charge range by 1" and have a +1 modifier to the result of any [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) or [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll they make.
