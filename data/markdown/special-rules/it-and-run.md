# 'It & Run

*With a barked command from their leader, Kiknik's riders fall back, ready to charge off in pursuit of new prey.*

Should they win a round of combat, Kiknik and any Goblin Wolf Rider Mob he has joined may choose to [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md) rather than making a follow up or pursuit move.
