# Grand Strategist

*Magistrates are adept at outwitting their enemies, both across the negotiating table and on the battlefield.*

Unless this character is fleeing, all friendly units within their [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) range, except your [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md), can use this character's Leadership characteristic instead of their own. In addition, once per turn a friendly unit that wins a round of combat whilst within this character's Command range may choose to [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md) rather than making a follow up or pursuit move.
