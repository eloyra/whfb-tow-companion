# Worst Among Equals

*Surly and quarrelsome by nature, Slayers are seldom willing to heed the commands of any individual.*

If your army includes [Ungrim Ironfist](../unit/ungrim-ironfist.md), he must be your [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md). If not, you must nominate a [Daemon Slayer](../unit/daemon-slayer.md) or [Dragon Slayer](../unit/dragon-slayer.md) to act as your General, even though the [Loner](loner.md) special rule would normally prevent this. However, to reflect that Slayers make poor leaders, a Dragon Slayer or Daemon Slayer that is the General of a [Slayer Host](../warhammer-armies/slayer-host.md) does not gain the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) special rule.

*Note that, if the General of a Slayer Host Army of Infamy bears the*  [Rune of the Dishonoured](../magic-item/rune-of-the-dishonoured.md)  *runic tattoo, the enemy player can win the bonus*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md)  *for slaying them as normal.*
