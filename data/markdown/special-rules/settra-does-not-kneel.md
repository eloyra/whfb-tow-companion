# Settra Does Not Kneel!

*Settra views all people as his subjects and all lands as his to conquer; anyone that would challenge his rule is swiftly and brutally punished.*

Settra must always accept a [challenge](../characters/challenges.md) unless Nekaph, Emissary of Settra is engaged in the same combat. In which case, Nekaph must accept the challenge on Settra's behalf.
