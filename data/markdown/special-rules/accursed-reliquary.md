# Accursed Reliquary

*Powerful necromantic magic clings to the unhallowed remains enshrined within the heart of a Mortis Engine, energising nearby Undead.*

Whilst within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), friendly units with the [Necromantic Undead](necromantic-undead.md) special rule gain a +1 modifier to their Movement and Initiative characteristics.
