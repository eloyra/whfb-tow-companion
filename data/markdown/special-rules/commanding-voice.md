# Commanding Voice

*Ishaya has spent years barking orders over crashing waves, hurricane winds and the clamour of battle.*

Ishayas [Command Range](../characters/command-range.md) is increased by 3"; in other words, Ishaya Vess has a Command Range of 12". If she is your army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md), she has a Command Range of 15".
