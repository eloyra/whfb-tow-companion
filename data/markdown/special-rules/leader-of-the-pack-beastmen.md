# Leader of the Pack (Beastmen)

*Ungor Ravagers follow their charges to battle, goading them towards the enemy with jagged spears.*

Models with this special rule can be taken as part of a unit of [Primal Warhounds](../unit/primal-warhounds.md). Models with this special rule must be positioned at the rear of their unit, making up its rear rank(s). Any Primal Warhounds the unit contains must always occupy the front ranks) of the unit, pushing past any models with this special rule to get there if necessary (such as when the unit turns).
