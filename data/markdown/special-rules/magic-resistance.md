# Magic Resistance (-X)

*Some creatures are naturally resistant to magic, whilst others bear charms or fetishes intended to ward off its effects.*

The [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) of any enemy spell (including [Bound spells](../magic/bound-spells.md)) that targets a unit that includes one or more models with this special rule suffers a modifier, as shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as '-X').

*Note that this special rule is not cumulative. If two or more models in a unit have this special rule, use the highest modifier.*
