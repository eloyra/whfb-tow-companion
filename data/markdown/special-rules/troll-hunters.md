# Troll Hunters

*Some Slayers hunt their prey with cunning, sneaking close before descending upon them with fury.*

- 0-1 character per 1,000 points in a [Slayer Host](../warhammer-armies/slayer-host.md) Army of Infamy (not including [Ungrim Ironfist](../unit/ungrim-ironfist.md)) may have the [Scouts](scouts.md) special rule for +10 points per model.
