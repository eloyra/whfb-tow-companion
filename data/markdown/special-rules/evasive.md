# Evasive

*In the face of enemy fire, it is often wise to step back! Some warriors are particularly adept at this manoeuvre.*

Once per turn, when a unit in which the majority of the models have this special rule is declared the target during the enemy [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase), that unit may choose to [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md), fleeing directly away from the enemy unit shooting at it. Once this unit has completed its move, the enemy unit may continue with its shooting as declared.
