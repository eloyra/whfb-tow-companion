# Weapon Team Leadership

Whilst within 3" of its parent unit, a Weapon Team gains a positive (+) modifier to its Leadership characteristic equal to the parent unit's current [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md), up to a maximum of Leadership 10.
