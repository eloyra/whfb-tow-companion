# Sepulchral Animus

*Drawing from the powerful magics that animate the great undead constructs of Nehekhara, the Priests of the Mortuary Cult can strengthen their spells and incantations.*

Once per turn, before making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md), any Liche Priest within a [Mortuary Cult](../warhammer-armies/mortuary-cult.md) army may draw upon the magical animus of any nearby undead constructs. To do so, nominate a friendly unit that has the [Nehekharan Undead](nehekharan-undead.md) special rule, that is within the Liche Priest's Command range and whose troop type is [swarms](../troop-types-in-detail/swarms.md), [monstrous infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-infantry.md), [monstrous cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md), [monstrous creature](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-creatures.md) or [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md). The nominated unit immediately loses a single Wound. The Liche Priest may then apply a +1 modifier to their Casting roll.

*Note that this is a modifier to the result of a roll – it does not negate a roll of a natural double 1.*
