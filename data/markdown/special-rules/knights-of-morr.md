# Knights of Morr

*The grim visaged Knights of Morr protect the living and guard the gardens of the dead against the predations of evil forces.*

An army of the Knights of Morr may include [Wizard Lords](../unit/wizard-lord.md) and [Master Mages](../unit/master-mage.md) as Character choices. However, your army cannot include any [Lectors of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md), [Priests of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md), [High Priests of Ulric](../unit/high-priest-of-ulric.md) or [Priests of Ulric](../unit/priest-of-ulric.md).
