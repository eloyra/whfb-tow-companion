# Prayers of Ulric

*The ferocious Priests of Ulric beseech the god of battle, wolves and winter to witness the deeds of his brave followers, and to reward their courage and prowess with his blessings.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this character may attempt to invoke Ulric's blessings by chanting a single Prayer from the list below and making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, the Prayer takes immediate effect:

- **Battle Howl:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, when this model and a single friendly unit that is within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) when this Prayer is chanted\* makes a Charge roll, you may apply a +D3 modifier to the result. This Prayer may only affect models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md).
- **Winter's Chill:** Until your next Start of Turn sub-phase, any enemy model that directs its attacks against this model, its mount or any unit it has joined during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) must re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 6.
- **Wrath of Winter:** Until your next Start of Turn sub-phase, this model, its mount and any unit it has joined gains the [Multiple Wounds (2)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule.

*\*Note that, if more than one friendly unit is within this model's Command range when this Prayer is chanted, you must tell your opponent which unit is affected.*

*Note also that the effects of these Prayers on friendly units are not cumulative. In other words, a friendly unit cannot be affected by the same Prayer more than once during the same turn.*
