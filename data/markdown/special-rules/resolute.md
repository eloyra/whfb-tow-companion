# Resolute

*All Dwarfs are grim and determined warriors that abandon a position with great reluctance.*

Models with this special rule suffer a -1 modifier to the result of any [Flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) roll or [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll they make (to a minimum of 1).

*Note that this modifier does not apply to Chaos Dwarf mounted characters.*
