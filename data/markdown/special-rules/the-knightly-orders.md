# The Knightly Orders

*Though they share many traits, the Knightly Orders of the Empire differ wildly in organisation and beliefs.*

The [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) of a [Knightly Order](../warhammer-armies/knightly-order.md) Army of Infamy must be a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md) or a [Chapter Master](../warhammer-armies/knightly-order.md) who must belong to one of the [Knightly Orders](../magic-items/knightly-orders-of-the-empire.md). Every other Chapter Master and every unit of [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md), [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) or [Demigryph Knights](../unit/demigryph-knights.md) must belong to the same Knightly Order as your General. Additional units may be allowed or restricted, depending upon which Knightly Order your army belongs to:

- [Knights Panther](knights-panther.md)
- [Knights of the White Wolf](knights-of-the-white-wolf.md)
- [Knights of the Blazing Sun](knights-of-the-blazing-sun.md)
- [Knights of Morr](knights-of-morr.md)
- [Knights of the Fiery Heart](knights-of-the-fiery-heart.md)
