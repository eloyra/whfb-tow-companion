# Feigned Flight

*Some units are adept at escaping from the foe and regrouping, drawing the enemy into careless charges before vanishing into the distance.*

If this unit chooses to [Flee](../the-movement-phase/flee-charge-reaction.md) (or [Fire & Flee](fire-and-flee.md)) as a charge reaction, it automatically rallies at the end of its move.
