# Scintillating Sorcery

*Spellcasting fills Pink Horrors with joy, and they emit especial merriment as eldritch energy screeches from their upraised hands.*

A unit of Pink Horrors knows one of the [spells](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-daemons.md) that corresponds to their [Daemonic Alignment](daemonic-alignment.md) (chosen by their controlling player before armies are deployed). The unit may cast this spell as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level equal to the unit's current Rank Bonus.
