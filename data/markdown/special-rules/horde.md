# Horde

*Some troops find strength in numbers, gathering in deep formations that crowd together tightly.*

A unit with this special rule may increase the maximum [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) it can claim (as determined by its [troop type](../troop-types-at-a-glance/troop-type-table.md)) by one.
