# First Charge

*The thundering charge of heavily armed and armoured warriors, freshly arrived upon the battlefield and eager for the fray, is devastating to the cowering foe.*

If this unit's first charge of the game is successful (i.e., if the unit makes contact with the charge target), the charge target becomes [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) until the end of the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) of that turn.
