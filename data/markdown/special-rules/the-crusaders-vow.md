# The Crusader's Vow

*"...To journey far beyond the borders of fair Bretonnia, into the lair of my foe. To take the battle to the enemies of the Lady of the Lake, wherever they might linger. This is my sacred vow..."*

The Crusader's Vow is a Chivalrous Vow. A model with this Chivalrous Vow has the [Veteran](veteran.md) special rule. In addition, a model with this Chivalrous Vow does not have to make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) when a friendly unit with the [Peasantry](peasantry.md) special rule is destroyed whilst within 6" of it, or when it is fled through by a friendly unit with the Peasantry special rule.

A unit with this Chivalrous Vow cannot be joined by a character with the Peasantry special rule. A character with this Chivalrous Vow cannot join a unit with the Peasantry special rule.
