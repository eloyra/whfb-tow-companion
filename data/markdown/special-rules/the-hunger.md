# The Hunger

*Consumed by the red thirst, a Strigoi Ghoul King will feed hungrily upon the hot blood of their foes, replenishing their own strength and vitality.*

At the end of any [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) in which this character inflicted one or more unsaved wounds, roll a D6. On a roll of 6, this character recovers a single lost Wound. However, so great is this character's hunger that, whenever they (and any unit they have joined) make a [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, they roll only a single D6 (rather than the usual 2D6).
