# Move Through Cover

*A well-trained or naturally skilled warrior can traverse unhindered through the densest terrain.*

Models with this special rule do not suffer any modifiers to their Movement characteristic for moving through [difficult](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md) or [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). In addition, a model with this special rule may re-roll any rolls of 1 when making Dangerous Terrain tests.
