# Troubadour of Loec

*The deadly dance of a Shadowdancer melds seamlessly with that of the troupe they lead.*

A Shadowdancer that joins a unit of Wardancers is considered to have the [Dances of Loec](dances-of-loec.md) special rule for as long as they remain with the unit.
