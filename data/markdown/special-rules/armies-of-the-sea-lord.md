# Armies of the Sea Lord

*The companies of Sea Guard that defend the Elven colonies are highly trained soldiers. Accordingly, the Sea Lord refuses to allow pampered princes or bookish mages to lead his forces.*

Characters in a [Sea Guard Garrison](../warhammer-armies/sea-guard-garrison.md) Army of Infamy that are able to take an [Elven Honour](../magic-items/elven-honours.md) may only choose one of the following:

- [Shadow Stalker](../magic-item/shadow-stalker.md)
- [Pure of Heart](../magic-item/pure-of-heart.md)
- [Sea Guard](../magic-item/sea-guard.md)
