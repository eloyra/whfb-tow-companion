# Forgefire

*In the heat of battle, the weapons of a runesmith's companions glow white-hot with the heat of their forging, cutting through the inferior armour of the enemy with ease.*

If this character joins a unit, that unit will gain the [Armour Bane (2)](armour-bane.md) and [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rules. Should this character leave a unit it has joined for any reason, that unit loses these special rules.
