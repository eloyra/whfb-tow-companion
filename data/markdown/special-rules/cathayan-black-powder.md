# Cathayan Black Powder

*Though the production of Cathayan black powder is a closely guarded secret, they freely share their stockpiles with mercenary units and their allies when marching to war.*

The first time any unit in a [Jade Fleet](../warhammer-armies/jade-fleet.md) army that is armed with a [handgun](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md), a [pistol](../weapons-of-war/pistol.md), a [brace of pistols](../weapons-of-war/brace-of-pistols.md), a [repeater handgun](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md), or a [repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) shoots, the range of that weapon is increased by 3".
