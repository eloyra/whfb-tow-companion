# Extended Formation

*Ranging far and wide above the mountain peaks, units of Scout Gyrocopters adopt an extended formation, the better to scour the landscape for the movements of the enemy.*

Whilst in [Skirmish](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) formation, every model in a unit of Scout Gyrocopters must be within 3" of another model belonging to the same unit, rather than the usual 1".
