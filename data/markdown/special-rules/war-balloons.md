# War Balloons

*As well as tracking the movements of enemy forces, the Sky Lanterns of Grand Cathay can descend upon their foes from any direction to unleash a torrent of Crane gun fire and gunpowder bombs.*

0-1 [Sky Lantern](../unit/sky-lantern.md) per 1,000 points may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule for +15 points. In addition, once per game a Sky Lantern that is held in reserve may re-roll the dice when rolling to determine if it arrives this turn as reinforcements or is delayed.
