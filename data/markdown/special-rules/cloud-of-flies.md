# Cloud of Flies

*Swarms of fat flies surround the Plague Priests of Clan Pestilens, blinding and choking their enemies.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against this character during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.
