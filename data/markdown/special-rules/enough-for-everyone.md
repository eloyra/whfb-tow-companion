# Enough for Everyone

*Ogres enjoy loud and explosive weapons and, when they make gunpowder bombs for themselves, they make sure to bring enough for their friends.*

If an Ogre Loader is equipped with [gunpowder bombs](../weapons-of-war/gunpowder-bombs.md), the crew of the war machine that Ogre Loader has joined is also equipped with gunpowder bombs.
