# Veteran Outriders

*Nuln employs many veteran Outriders, accustomed to firing their weapons from the back of a galloping horse, to guard its borders.*

Missile weapons wielded by [Outriders](../unit/outriders.md) included in a [City-state of Nuln](../warhammer-armies/city-state-of-nuln.md) army are not subject to the [Ponderous](ponderous.md) special rule.
