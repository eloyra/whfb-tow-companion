# Peasant's Duty

*It is the role of a Sergeant-at-Arms to keep discipline amongst the ragged ranks of commoners. Should they fail and cause their liege embarrassment upon the field, the penalties can be most severe.*

This character and any unit they have joined may choose to [Give Ground](../movement-in-detail/give-ground.md) rather than [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md), and does not have to make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) when a friendly unit of [Levies](levies.md) [Breaks and flees](../the-combat-phase/loser-breaks-and-flees.md) from combat whilst within 6" of it. Additionally, unless this character is fleeing, any friendly unit that is within their [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) and has the Levies special rule may re-roll a failed Panic test.
