# Aquatic

*Many of Lustria's denizens have an affinity for water, moving with equal ease through swamp and river as they do over land.*

Models with this special rule do not suffer any modifiers to their Movement characteristic when moving through any [difficult](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md) or [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md) feature which has been designated a 'water feature'. This might include shallow streams or fords, swampy ground, fast flowing rivers, ponds or lakes, and players should agree prior to the game if any terrain is a water feature.
