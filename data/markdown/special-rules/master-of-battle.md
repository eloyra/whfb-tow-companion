# Master of Battle

*The masters of the Knightly Order are bold and ferocious warriors, fearless in the face of even the most terrifying enemies.*

If this model joins a unit of Empire Knights, Inner Circle Knights or Demigryph Knights, that unit will gain the [Immune To Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md) special rule. Should this model leave a unit it has joined for any reason, that unit loses this special rule.
