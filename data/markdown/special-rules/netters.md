# Netters

*In dark underground tunnels, Night Goblins throw nets to entangle their prey.*

When this unit's combat is chosen during Step 1.1 of any [Choose & Fight Combat](../the-combat-phase/choose-and-fight-combat.md) subphase, it may attempt to 'entangle' one enemy unit it is engaged with. Roll a D6. On a roll of 1, the unit has entangled itself. On a roll of 2+, the enemy unit is entangled. Until the end of the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), an entangled unit suffers a -1 modifier to its Strength characteristic (to a minimum of 1).

*Note that this modifier is not cumulative.*
