# Oi! Dis Way!

*To put it kindly, Trolls are remarkably unencumbered by intellect. However, when someone with an authoritative voice gives them simple instructions, they are incredibly effective shock troops, capable of tearing a man limb from limb.*

Unless the character is fleeing, friendly Troll Mobs within a [Troll Horde](../warhammer-armies/troll-horde.md) army that are within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of an Orc Shaman, Goblin Shaman or Troll Hag can use the Leadership characteristic of that character instead of their own.
