# Maddening Aura

*So ghastly and unnatural is a Jabberslythe, that a mere glance can drive all sanity and reason from the minds of those that behold it. Wherever such a beast passes, brave souls fall shrieking and babbling in its wake.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of its turn, any enemy unit that is within 8" of this model, including units that are fleeing or that are engaged in combat, must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the unit suffers D3 Strength 3 hits, with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).
