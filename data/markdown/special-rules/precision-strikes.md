# Precision Strikes

*Ishaya has taught her warriors how to find the weakest points in their foes defences, able to find their mark no matter how heavily armoured their enemies might be.*

A [Lothern Sea Guard](../unit/lothern-sea-guard.md) unit joined by Ishaya Vess improves the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of its weapons by 1.
