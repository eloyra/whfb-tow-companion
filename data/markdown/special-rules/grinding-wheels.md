# Grinding Wheels

*The armoured bulk of this massive construction weighs many tonnes, and any enemies unfortunate enough to fall beneath its metal-bound wheels are crushed to a paste.*

[Stomp Attacks](stomp-attacks.md) made by a this model have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2. However, this rule cannot be used against models whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) – they are simply too large to be caught beneath the wheels.

In addition, and unlike other chariots, this model treats [low linear obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md) as open terrain rather than as [impassable terrain](../battlefield-terrain/impassable-terrain.md).
