# Blazing Body

*This creature's flesh burns with the intensity of a furnace, wreathing its form in clouds of black smoke and burning embers.*

At the start of every [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), any model (friend or foe) that is in base contact with this model and that does not also have this special rule suffers a single Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -. This hit has the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule.

*Note that any Wounds lost due to these hits do not count towards the combat result.*
