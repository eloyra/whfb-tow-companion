# Chracian Pride

*The Elves of Chrace are rugged warriors, unaccustomed to taking orders from outsiders. Accordingly, the armies of Chrace seldom give command to princes from foreign lands.*

Characters in a [Chracian Warhost](../warhammer-armies/the-chracian-warhost.md) Army of Infamy that are able to take an [Elven Honour](../magic-items/elven-honours.md) may only choose one of the following:

- [Shadow Stalker](../magic-item/shadow-stalker.md)
- [Chracian Hunter](../magic-item/chracian-hunter.md)
- [Pure of Heart](../magic-item/pure-of-heart.md)

*Note that Chracian Chieftains are considered to have the Chracian Hunter Elven Honour for the purposes of joining units of White Lions of Chrace.*
