# Sorcerous Miasma

*Sorcerous black clouds curl about the Lammasu, protecting it from spells and befouling the magical weapons of its enemies, preventing them from striking the beast to full effect.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), any enemy model that uses a magic weapon to attack a character mounted on a Lammasu counts as being armed with a simple [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) instead.
