# Dance of Death

*Sisters of Slaughter weave through the enemy's defences, plunging deep into their ranks.*

If this unit makes a successful charge move (i.e., if the unit makes contact with the charge target), the charge target suffers a -1 modifier to its Maximum [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) until the end of the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) of that turn.
