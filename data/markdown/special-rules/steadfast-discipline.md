# Steadfast Discipline

*Only the most disciplined were chosen to serve in the ranks of a Royal Host, maintaining a near-constant rate of bow fire whilst advancing towards the enemy.*

A unit with this special rule can [Volley Fire](volley-fire.md) during a turn in which it moved, or whilst performing a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction.
