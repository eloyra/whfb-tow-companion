# Jade Sentinel

*Living statues crafted from gleaming, jade megaliths by the Wizards of the Celestial Court, Jade Sentinels become conduits for sorcery, able to use spells bound to the magical stone of their bodies.*

A Jade Sentinel knows a single spell (chosen by their controlling player before armies are deployed from either the [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md) or [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md) Lore of Magic. It may cast this spell as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) with a Power Level of 3.
