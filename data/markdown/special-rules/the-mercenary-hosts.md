# The Mercenary Hosts

*Tilean mercenary regiments are renowned for the deep formations they adopt in battle, allowing rank upon rank of brave soldiers to overwhelm their enemies with their weight of numbers.*

For each character in your army that has the Might of Miragliano Infamous Origin, 0-1 unit of [Sellsword Infantry](../unit/sellsword-infantry.md) or [Veteran Sellsword Infantry](../unit/veteran-sellswords.md) may change its troop type to [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) and may have the [Phalanx](phalanx.md) special rule for +10 points per unit.
