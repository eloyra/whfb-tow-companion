# Flammable

*Some creatures are especially vulnerable to fire. Once flame has been set to their flesh, it will burn out of control.*

A model with this special rule cannot make a [Regeneration](regeneration.md) save against a wound caused by a [Flaming attack](flaming-attacks.md).
