# Curse of the Necropolis

*A powerful curse hangs over the rulers of Nehekhara. Those that would dare strike down these ancient lords find their blood turned to sand and their bodies engulfed in swarms of desert locusts.*

If a model with this special rule loses its last Wound to an enemy attack, the unit that made the attack must immediately make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the enemy unit suffers D3 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -.
