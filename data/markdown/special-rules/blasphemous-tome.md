# Blasphemous Tome

*Rumoured to have been penned by Nagash himself, the Winds of Magic become nigh uncontrollable in the presence of a Blasphemous Tome.*

All Wizards (friend or foe) may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) whilst within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md). However, if any Wizard [miscasts](../magic/miscast.md) whilst within this model's Command range, they must roll twice on the [Miscast table](../magic/miscast-table.md) and apply both results.
