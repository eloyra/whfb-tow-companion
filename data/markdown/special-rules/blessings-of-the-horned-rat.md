# Blessings of the Horned Rat

*The Horned Rat protects its favoured followers, warding them against the weapons of their enemies.*

This model has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
