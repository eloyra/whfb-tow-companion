# Manoeuvring a Lance

A unit in [Lance](lance-formation.md) formation may not turn or redress the ranks, but may wheel, move backwards, move sideways and reform as normal. When a unit in Lance formation wheels, its movement is measured from its second rank. All other movement is measured from the model at the front of the formation.
