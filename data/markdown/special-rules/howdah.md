# Howdah

*A howdah is an armoured platform built atop a mighty behemoth. From here, a crew of warriors rain missiles upon the enemy.*

To represent its howdah and crew, a behemoth with this special rule has a split profile and follows both the [Split Profile (Chariots)](../troop-types-in-detail/split-profile-chariots.md) and [Firing Platform](../troop-types-in-detail/firing-platform.md). In all other respects, this model is a [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md).
