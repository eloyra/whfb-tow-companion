# Cult of the Fell Raptor

*Many amongst the Tribe of the Raptor with strange visions. When battle is joined, their dark patron grants them the ability to foresee their enemy's actions.*

Models belonging to this Chaotic Cult gain a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
