# Grudgelore

*So deep is Thorgrim's knowledge of grudgelore that he can readily find words to stoke the fires of wrath within his kin and unleash their rage upon any foe.*

Once Thorgrim has set the Grudgestone, both he and any unit he has joined replaces the [Hatred (Orcs & Goblins)](hatred.md) special rule with the Hatred (all enemies) special rule.
