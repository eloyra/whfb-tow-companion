# Expeditionary Marksmen

*After countless hours of diligent training with their handguns, the marksmen in an expeditionary force are able to lay down a devastating fusillade as they advance towards the enemy.*

- 0-1 unit of Thunderers per 1,000 points may be upgraded to Expeditionary Marksmen for +1 point per model. Expeditionary Marksmen do not suffer any negative To Hit modifiers for [Moving and Shooting](../the-shooting-phase/moving-and-shooting.md) Shooting. In addition, any Dwarf Engineer may be upgraded to an Expeditionary Marksman for +10 points.
