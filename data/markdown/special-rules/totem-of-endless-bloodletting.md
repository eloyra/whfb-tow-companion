# Totem of Endless Bloodletting

*A Blood Throne is a physical manifestation of theBlood God's favour. In its presence, Daemons of Khorne are driven into a raging bloodlust.*

Friendly Daemons of Khorne that are within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) and that made a [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) move this turn may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1 made during the Combat phase.
