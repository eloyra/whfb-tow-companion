# King's Guard

*Since the time of Caledor the First, the fierce warriors of Chrace have been the closest bodyguards of Elven kings and princes.*

Any model in a unit of White Lions of Chrace that has been joined by your army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) can issue and accept [challenges](../characters/challenges.md) in the same manner as a character. Should your General leave the unit for any reason, the unit loses this ability.
