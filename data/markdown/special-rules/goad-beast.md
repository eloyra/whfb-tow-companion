# Goad Beast

*The Beastmasters of Clar Karond excel at goading their charges to violence, none more so than the High Beastmasters.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this character may goad a single friendly model whose troop type is [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md) and that is within their [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) (including their own mount). Until the end of this turn, that model gains a +D3 modifier to its Attacks characteristic (to a maximum of 10).
