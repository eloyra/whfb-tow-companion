# Abyssal Cloak

*The crushing darkness of the deepest ocean surrounds the Merwyrm in battle.*

Any enemy model that targets this model during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers a -2 To Hit modifier for firing at long range, rather than the usual -1.
