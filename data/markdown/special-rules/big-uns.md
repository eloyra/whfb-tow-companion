# Big 'Uns

*The biggest, strongest and fightiest Orcs band together into mobs accurately, if unimaginatively, called Big 'Uns.*

When engaged in combat, a model with this special rule has a +1 modifier to its Strength characteristic, and gains the [Armour Bane (1)](armour-bane.md) special rule.
