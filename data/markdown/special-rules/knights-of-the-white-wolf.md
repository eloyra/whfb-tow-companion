# Knights of the White Wolf

*Devotees of Ulric, the Knights of the White Wolf are ferocious warriors who fight to defend the lands of Man.*

An army of the Knights of the White Wolf may include any number of units of [Teutogen Guard](../unit/teutogen-guard.md) as Special choices, and 0-1 unit of [Free Company Militia](../unit/free-company-militia.md) as a Core choice per 1,000 points. However, your army cannot include any [Lectors of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md), [Priests of Sigmar](../unit/priest-of-sigmar.md) or [Witch Hunters](../unit/witch-hunter.md).
