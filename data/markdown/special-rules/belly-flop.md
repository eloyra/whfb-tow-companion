# Belly Flop

The Giant crashes down bodily upon the enemy. Place a small (3") blast [template](../general-principles/templates.md) so that its central hole is directly over the centre of the target unit. Any model (friend or foe, but not including this model) whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single hit, using the Strength characteristic of this model, with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
