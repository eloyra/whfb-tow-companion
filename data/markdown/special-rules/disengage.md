# Disengage

*In the face of overwhelming aggression a Sky Lantern can easily disengage, climbing to the heavens before attacking once more.*

Should this model lose a round of combat and [Give Ground](../movement-in-detail/give-ground.md), it may choose to [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md) instead. Enemy units can follow up as if this model had given ground, moving 2" directly towards it, but cannot pursue it. In addition, should it win a round of combat and choose to restrain and reform, this model may choose to Fall Back in Good Order rather than remaining where it is.
