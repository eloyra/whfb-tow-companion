# Spectral Hounds

*Whilst in his corporeal form, Orion is accompanied by a pair of ethereal hunting hounds, manifestations of Kurnous' will.*

Hounds of Orion can only be wounded by [Magical attacks](magical-attacks.md). In addition, whilst one or more Hounds of Orion remains, Orion himself cannot be targeted by enemy shooting.
