# Large Target

*Monstrous creatures tower above the battlefield, visible to all for leagues around.*

Large Targets do not benefit from [partial](../the-shooting-phase/target-behind-partial-cover.md) or [full cover](../the-shooting-phase/target-behind-full-cover.md). In addition, a unit can draw a [line of sight](../model-and-unit-facing/line-of-sight.md) to a Large Target over or through another unit, and vice versa, provided that unit is not also a Large Target. Finally, a unit that shoots at a Large Target can shoot with one additional rank. For example, a unit armed with crossbows can shoot with its first two ranks when shooting at a Large Target, or with its first three if also standing on a hill.
