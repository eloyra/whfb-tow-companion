# Unity & Harmony

*The soldiers of Grand Cathay are renowned for their devotion to the protection of their homeland, but will fight and die beside their allies to ensure their towns and villages are kept free from danger.*

Any unit of [Jade Warriors](../unit/jade-warriors.md) or [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md) within 3" of a friendly [Mercenary](mercenaries.md) unit may re-roll To Hit rolls of 1 during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
