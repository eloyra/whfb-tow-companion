# Warriors of the Field

*The peasants of Grand Cathay work the land in service of the Celestial Dragon. Through such labour they become accustomed to traversing difficult terrain, learning to do so with the greatest of ease.*

Units of [Peasant Levy](../unit/peasant-levy.md) do not become [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) by [difficult](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md) or [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). In addition, 0-1 unit of Peasant Levy per 1,000 points may have the [Move Through Cover](move-through-cover.md) special rule for +20 points.
