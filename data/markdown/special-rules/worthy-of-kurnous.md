# Worthy of Kurnous

*The Wild Hunt ever searches for large or dangerous prey, trophies worthy of Kurnous himself.*

After deployment but before the first turn begins, nominate a single enemy character, or a single enemy model whose troop type is monster. This model has been marked by the Wild Hunt as a prized trophy. All models within a friendly [Orion's Wild Hunt](../warhammer-armies/orions-wild-hunt.md) Army may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1 made in the [Shooting](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) or [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) against the nominated unit.

In addition, you win a bonus of 100 [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) if the nominated model is slain, has fled the battlefield or is fleeing when the game ends.
