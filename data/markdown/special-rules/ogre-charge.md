# Ogre Charge

*Ogres barge into combat using their great lumbering mass as a weapon, harnessing their tremendous momentum to crush anything they collide with.*

The [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of any [Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) caused by a model with this special rule (but not its mount) is improved by the current [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) of its unit (or, in the case of characters, the current Rank Bonus of any unit they have joined).
