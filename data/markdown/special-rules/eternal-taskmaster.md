# Eternal Taskmaster

*Necrotects were the taskmasters of ancient Nehekhara. Under their stern gaze, armies of workers laboured relentlessly in the service of mighty kings.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this character may attempt to drive a unit they have joined to greater efforts by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase this character and any unit they have joined gains the [Extra Attacks (+1)](extra-attacks.md) and [Hatred (all enemies)](hatred.md) special rules.
