# The Outcast Knights

*Though their honour may be besmirched, outcast knights retain the appearance of chivalry upon the battlefield.*

For each character in your army that has the Renegade Knight Infamous Origin, 0-1 unit of [Freeblade Knights](../unit/freeblade-knights.md) or [Veteran Freeblade Knights](../unit/veteran-freeblades.md) may have the [Lance Formation](lance-formation.md) and the [Noble Disdain](noble-disdain.md) special rules for +2 points per model.
