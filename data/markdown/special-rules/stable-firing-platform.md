# Stable Firing Platform

*Atop the slow moving bulk of a War Wagon, the crew can shoot in all directions, taking careful aim behind its heavily armoured flanks before each shot.*

To reflect the stability of a War Wagon, any missile weapons carried by the crew can be used in the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) even if the War Wagon [marched](../movement-in-detail/marching.md) this turn.
