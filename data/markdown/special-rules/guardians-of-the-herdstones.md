# Guardians of the Herdstones

*Those who vigilantly watch over the sacred sites of the Chaos gods are blessed with powerful boons.*

- [Doombulls](../unit/doombull.md) and [Gorebulls](../unit/gorebull.md) in a [Minotaur Blood Herd](../warhammer-armies/minotaur-blood-herd.md) may:

  - Take [Chaos Mutations](../magic-items/chaos-mutations.md) and/or [Gifts of Chaos](../magic-items/gifts-of-chaos.md) up to a total of 50 points

*Note that this option replaces the option to take Chaos Mutations as shown in Ravening Hordes.*
