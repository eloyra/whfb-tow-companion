# Strategic Mastery

*Hans has honed his craft on countless battlefields across the Old Word against all manner of foes, making him amongst the Empire's foremost commanders.*

In scenarios where players roll off to determine who takes the first turn, you may apply a +1 or -1 modifier to the result of your roll.
