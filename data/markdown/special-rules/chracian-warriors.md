# Chracian Warriors

*White Lions form an insular kindred bound together by the arduous rights they have completed. The only outsiders welcomed into their number are the nobles they are sworn to protect.*

A unit with this special rule may only be joined by your army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md), or by a character with the [Chracian Hunter](../magic-item/chracian-hunter.md) Elven Honour.
