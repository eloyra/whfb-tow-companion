# Discipline of the Dragon

*When the defenders of Grand Cathay march to war, vast regiments of soldiers work in harmony with the wonders of the Celestial Dragon's artificers and engineers. Thus do Sky Lanterns command regiments of peasant warriors from afar, guiding them forward with wondrous precision.*

Once per game, during the [Command sub-phase](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) of your turn, unless it is engaged in combat, each [Sky Lantern](../unit/sky-lantern.md) in your army may attempt to enact one of the following 'Grand Strategies' by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all friendly Cathayan units that are currently within 12" of that Sky Lantern are affected by the chosen Grand Strategy:

- **Defiance of the Dragon:** Whilst this Grand Strategy is in effect, affected units gain the [Stubborn](stubborn.md) special rule. If an affected unit is already Stubborn, it becomes [Unbreakable](unbreakable.md) instead.
- **Strength of the Everlasting Empire:** Whilst this Grand Strategy is in effect, affected units whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) cannot be wounded by a roll To Wound of 2, regardless of the attacker's Strength.
- **Fury of the Falling Blade:** Whilst this Grand Strategy is in effect, affected units whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) gain a +1 modifier to their Movement characteristics and may re-roll their Charge, Flee or Pursuit rolls.
