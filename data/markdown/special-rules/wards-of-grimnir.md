# Wards of Grimnir

*Penned by the keepers of Grimnir's shrine, these potent wards protect the members of his cult from the weapons of the enemy.*

- Any unit in a [Slayer Host](../warhammer-armies/slayer-host.md) Army of Infamy that includes a standard bearer may purchase Wards of Grimnir for +1 point per model. A unit with Wards of Grimnir has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
