# Pride of the Fleet

*Fighting alongside a ship's company, the Sea Guard form the backbone of the Sea Lord's armies.*

Units of Sea Guard in a [Sea Guard Garrison](../warhammer-armies/sea-guard-garrison.md) Army of Infamy gain the [Regimental Unit](regimental-unit.md) special rule. In addition:

- 0-1 unit of [Sea Guard](../unit/lothern-sea-guard.md) per 1,000 points in a Sea Guard Garrison Army of Infamy may have the [Drilled](drilled.md) special rule for free.
