# Blood Greed

*At times, the blood-fuelled frenzy of the Beastmen is so great that they fall upon their foes in a butcherous rage, feeding upon the flesh of the fallen in the heat of battle.*

Whilst [Frenzied](frenzy.md), this model has a +2 modifier to its Attacks characteristic (rather than the usual +1). However, such is this model's desperate need to feed upon flesh that it rolls only a single D6 when making a [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll (rather than the usual 2D6).
