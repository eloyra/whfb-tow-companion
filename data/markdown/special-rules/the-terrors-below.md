# The Terrors Below

*From beneath the shifting and treacherous desert sands, tunnelling undead creatures strike at their enemies, dragging them to a grisly fate before bursting from the surface and into the fray.*

Any Tomb Scorpion or unit of Necroserpents may have this special rule for +2 points per model. When a unit with this special rule is placed on the battlefield as a result of using the [From Beneath the Sands](from-beneath-the-sands.md) special rule, it may nominate a single enemy unit that is within 8" of it and whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) or [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md). The nominated enemy unit must immediately make a number of Initiative tests equal to the [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of this unit. For each test that is failed, a single model belonging to the nominated unit is removed from play as a casualty.
