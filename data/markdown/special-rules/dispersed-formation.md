# Dispersed Formation

*With their great wings extended, Pegasus Knights descend upon the foe from on high.*

Whilst in [Skirmish](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) formation, every model in a unit of Pegasus Knights must be within 2" of another model belonging to the same unit, rather than the usual 1".
