# Gun Limbers

*Veteran gun crews can deploy their weapons from horse-drawn limbers with tremendous speed.*

- 0-1 war machine per 1,000 points, chosen from the following list, may be given the [Vanguard](vanguard.md) and [Veteran](veteran.md) special rules:

  - [Great Cannon](../unit/great-cannon.md) (+5 points)
  - [Mortar](../unit/mortar.md) (+5 points)
  - [Helblaster Volley Gun](../unit/helblaster-volley-gun.md) (+15 points)
  - [Helstorm Rocket Battery](../unit/helstorm-rocket-battery.md) (+10 points)
