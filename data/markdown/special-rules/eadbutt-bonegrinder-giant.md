# 'Eadbutt (Bonegrinder Giant)

The Bonegrinder Giant squints angrily at their enemy and 'eadbutts them. The target unit suffers a single hit and suffers D3+1 wounds with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).
