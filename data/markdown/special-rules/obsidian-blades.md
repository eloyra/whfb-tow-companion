# Obsidian Blades

*Lizardmen favour weapons studded with shards of razor sharp volcanic glass that cut through flesh and armour with ease.*

A hand weapon carried by a model with this special rule has an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -1.

*Note that this special rule only applies to a single, non-magical*  [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md)  *and does not apply to a model's mount (should it have one). If the model is using two hand weapons or any other sort of weapon, this special rule ceases to apply.*
