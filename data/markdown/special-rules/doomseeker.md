# Doomseeker

*With each victory they witness, a Doomseeker laments their inability to meet a worthy doom.*

At the end of the battle, a Doomseeker that has been slain is worth no [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md). However, if a Doomseeker is still alive, the enemy player wins a bonus number of Victory Points equal to 100% of its points cost.
