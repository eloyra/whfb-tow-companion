# Immovable Object

Once this model has been placed on the battlefield during deployment it cannot be moved by its crew during the [Remaining Moves](../the-movement-phase/remaining-moves.md) sub-phase.

*Note that the model can still*  [pivot](../movement-in-detail/pivoting.md)  *freely at any time during its turn (the better to face the enemy) and may make a follow up move as normal.*
