# Courage Beyond Compare

*With the Wardens at their head, the Guardians of Talsyn will hold their ground against any foe, even the nightmarish abominations of Chaos.*

If this character joins a unit of Guardians of Talsyn, that unit gains the [Immune to Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md) special rule. Should this character leave the unit for any reason, the unit loses this special rule.
