# Ignores Cover

*Even dense cover offers no safe haven from a skilled marksman wielding a wellcrafted weapon.*

If a model making a shooting attack has this special rule, it ignores any [To Hit modifiers](../the-shooting-phase/to-hit-modifiers.md) caused by [partial](../the-shooting-phase/target-behind-partial-cover.md) or [full cover](../the-shooting-phase/target-behind-full-cover.md).
