# From the Wilds

*Emerging from the wilderness, Slayers are drawn by the sounds of battle and the promise of violence.*

- 0-1 unit of [Slayers](../unit/slayers.md) with a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of 10 or less in a [Slayer Host](../warhammer-armies/slayer-host.md) Army of Infamy may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) and [Skirmishers](../unusual-formations/skirmish-formation.md) special rules for +1 point per model.
