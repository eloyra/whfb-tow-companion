# Stubborn

*Elite troops will often fight on, refusing to flee from the enemy, regardless of casualties.*

The first time this unit is required to make a [Break test](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md) it may choose not to and will automatically [Falling Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md) instead, even if the [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of the winning side is more than twice that of the losing side. A unit that is not Stubborn does not become Stubborn when joined by a character that is. A Stubborn character cannot use this special rule whilst part of a unit that is not Stubborn.
