# Prepared Positions

*Burlok is an expert at organising the hasty construction of defensible barriers to ensure his artillery and missile troops are protected from harm.*

During deployment, you may 'Entrench' a single non-character model whose troop type is [war machine](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md) and a single unit of Quarrellers or Thunderers. An Entrenched unit is considered to be behind partial cover and to be defending a [low linear obstacle](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md). Should an Entrenched unit move for any reason, it is no longer Entrenched.
