# Rallying Cry

*Striking a heroic pose, a bold leader treats their loyal followers to a short but inspiring speech.*

During the [Command sub-phase](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, this character may nominate a single fleeing friendly unit that is within their Command range. The nominated unit immediately makes a [Rally test](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md). If this test is failed, the unit may attempt to rally again as normal during the Rally sub-phase.
