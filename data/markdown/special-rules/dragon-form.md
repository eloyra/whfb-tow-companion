# Dragon Form

In their Dragon form, the Dragons of Grand Cathay are forces of primordial destruction who take the fight directly to their enemies with tooth and claw, unconcerned with issuing commands and dictating the flow of battlelines. To represent this, whilst in their Dragon form a Cathayan Dragon follows the rules for a character whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md). However, whilst in their Dragon form Cathayan Dragons do not have the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule, even if they are your army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md).
