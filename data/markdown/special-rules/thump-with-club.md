# Thump with Club

The Giant grasps its club two-handed and cracks its enemy on the head. Nominate a single model in the fighting rank of an enemy unit the Giant is engaged with to be the target of this attack. For this attack, the Giant's club has a Strength characteristic of S+4, an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -4 and the [Multiple Wounds (D6)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
