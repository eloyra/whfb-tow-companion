# Blessings of Ulric

*Members of the Teutogen Guard are fierce devotees of Ulric and carry wards blessed by his priests.*

A model with this special rule has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
