# Tower Shields

*These large, heavy shields not only offer unrivalled protection, but provide a stable platform upon which to balance the length and weight of a long-barrelled gun.*

A tower shield is a shield that improves its wielder's [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 3 (rather than the usual 1) against attacks made by enemy models that lie within the wielder's front arc. However, a tower shield offers no protection to its wielder against attacks made by enemy models that lie within the wielder's flank or rear arcs.
