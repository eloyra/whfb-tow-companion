# Crushing Bulk

*The erratic movements and crushing weight of a Doomwheel combine to wreak havoc upon the ordered ranks of the enemy.*

Any [Stomp Attacks](stomp-attacks.md) made by a Doom Wheel have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -1. In addition, an enemy unit that suffers [Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) from this model must immediately make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the unit becomes [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) until the end of the current [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
