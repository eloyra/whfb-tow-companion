# Spectral Reapers

*Galloping above the battlefield upon spectral steeds, Hexwraiths lean low in their saddles to reap the souls of the living.*

A unit with this special rule may perform a 'Spectral Reapers' attack against a single enemy unit that is not engaged in combat. To do so, this unit must move (by [flying](fly.md)) over the unit it wishes to attack during the [Remaining Moves](../the-movement-phase/remaining-moves.md) sub-phase. Once this unit's movement is complete, the enemy unit suffers a single Strength 4 hit, with no [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) permitted ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal), and with the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) and [Magical Attacks](magical-attacks.md) special rules, for each model in this unit that moved over it.
