# The Wandering Duelists

*Outcast nobles and skilled duelists from distant lands band together in their exile, forming companies of deadly warriors.*

For each character in your army that has the Wandering Diestro Infamous Origin, 0-1 unit of [Border Princes Brigands](../unit/border-princes-brigands.md) may have a +1 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic for +2 points per model. In addition, once per game, models within this unit may make enemy models that direct their attacks against them during the Combat phase re-roll successful rolls To Hit.
