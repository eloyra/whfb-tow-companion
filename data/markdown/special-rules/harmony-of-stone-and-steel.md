# Harmony of Stone & Steel

*Under the command of Gate Masters and Gate Keepers, Cathayan soldiers serve their Dragon Emperor with unrivalled discipline.*

A unit joined by a character with this special rule may re-roll any failed [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) when attempting to reform after running down a foe, when attempting to redirect a charge, or when making a [Restraint test](../the-combat-phase/restrain-and-reform.md).
