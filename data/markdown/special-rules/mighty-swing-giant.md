# Mighty Swing (Giant)

The Giant swings its club through the enemy ranks. For this attack, the Giant is subject to the [Random Attacks](random-attacks.md) special rule and has an Attacks characteristic of D6+1, and the Giant's club has a Strength characteristic of S+1 and an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
