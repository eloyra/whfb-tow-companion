# Cunning Hunters

*The Wolf Riders who hunt alongside a Nomadic Waaagh! have learned to encircle the enemy before the battle begins and launch their attacks from all sides.*

Within a [Nomadic Waaagh!](../warhammer-armies/nomadic-waaagh.md) army, any number of Goblin Wolf Rider Mobs may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule at the cost of +1 point per model. In addition, Goblin Bosses and Goblin Shamans that are mounted on a Giant Wolf may have the Ambushers special rule at the cost of +10 points per model.
