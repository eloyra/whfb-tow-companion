# Mark of Nurgle

*Those marked by Nurgle are eternally accompanied by thick clouds of flies, blinding, deafening and choking their enemies.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against a model with the Mark of Nurgle during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) must re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 6.
