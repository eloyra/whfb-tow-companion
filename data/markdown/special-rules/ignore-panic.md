# Ignore Panic

*Black Orcs don't expect much in the way of bravery or discipline from their dim-witted kin, so are rarely perturbed by the sight of them legging it.*

This unit does not have to make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) when a friendly unit that does not also have this special rule is destroyed or [Breaks and flees](../the-combat-phase/loser-breaks-and-flees.md) from combat whilst within 6" of it. Nor does this unit have to make a Panic test when it is fled through by a friendly unit that does not have this special rule.
