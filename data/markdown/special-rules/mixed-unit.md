# Mixed Unit

*Made up of both Gors and Ungors, Primal Warherds are a chaotic foe.*

Models within a Primal Warherd have different Weapon Skill and Toughness characteristics. Before rolling To Wound, hits caused by enemy shooting should be divided as evenly as possible between Gors and Ungors. In combat, enemy models must direct their attacks against models in the [fighting rank](../the-combat-phase/the-fighting-rank.md) of this unit.
