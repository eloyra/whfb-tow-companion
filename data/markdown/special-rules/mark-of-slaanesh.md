# Mark of Slaanesh

*Devotees of Slaanesh have experienced sensations beyond comprehension, rendering them insensible to mortal fears.*

Models with the Mark of Slaanesh have +1 Initiative during the first round of any combat. Additionally, if the majority of the models in a unit have the Mark of Slaanesh, the unit automatically passes any [Panic tests](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) it is required to make.
