# Strike Last

*Some warriors are incredibly slow and ponderous by nature, whilst others may be encumbered by massive weapons that slow them down.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), a model with this special rule that is engaged in combat reduces its Initiative characteristic to 1 (before any other modifiers are applied). If a model has both this rule and [Strike First](strike-first.md), the two rules cancel one another out.
