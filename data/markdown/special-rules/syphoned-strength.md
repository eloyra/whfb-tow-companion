# Syphoned Strength

*Ogdruz has learned to empower his magic by drawing on the physical strength of his Trolls.*

Whilst Ogdruz Swampdigga is within 3" of a friendly Troll Mob with a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of 6 or more that is not fleeing, he may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) he makes.
