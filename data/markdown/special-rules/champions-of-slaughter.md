# Champions of Slaughter

*Those who lead the Blood Herds do so from the front, their acts of gratuitous violence fuelling the primal fury of their kin.*

If the [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) of a [Minotaur Blood Herd](../warhammer-armies/minotaur-blood-herd.md) becomes [Frenzied](frenzy.md) as a result of the [Blood Rage](blood-rage.md) special rule, any friendly units within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) will also become Frenzied.
