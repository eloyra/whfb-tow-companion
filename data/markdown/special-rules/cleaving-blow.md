# Cleaving Blow

*A swift and deadly strike can cleave through the thickest of armour.*

If a model with this special rule rolls a natural 6 when making a roll To Wound for an attack made in combat, it has struck a 'Cleaving Blow'. Enemy models whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md), [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md), [light cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/light-cavalry.md), [heavy cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-cavalry.md) or [war beasts](../troop-types-in-detail/war-beasts.md) are not permitted an [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) save against a Cleaving Blow ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).

*Note that if an attack wounds automatically, this special rule cannot be used.*
