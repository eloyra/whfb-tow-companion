# Mighty Constitution

*The White Lions claim their captain is the mightiest Elf in all of Ulthuan, and certainly his strength and vitality is legendary.*

During a turn in which he made a charge move of 3" or more, Korhil gains a +1 modifier to his Strength characteristic. In addition, Korhil is immune to the [Poisoned Attacks](poisoned-attacks.md) special rule. If he is hit by an attack with this special rule, the attacker must roll To Wound as normal.
