# Protectors of the Ancient Wood

*The warriors of Talsyn are responsible for the protection of Athel Loren's most significant and important locations.*

Whilst it controls a special feature, a unit within a [Host of Talsyn](../warhammer-armies/host-of-talsyn.md) army may re-roll any rolls To Hit and To Wound of a natural 1 during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
