# Bugman's Cart

*A pint of Bugman's XXXXXX and a rousing beer hall song is enough to inspire any Dwarf to raise an axe and take the fight to the enemy.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, any Dwarf unit that is not fleeing or engaged in combat and that is within 6" of a friendly Bugman's Cart (that is itself not fleeing or engaged) gains a +1 modifier to its Movement characteristic until the end of the turn.

*Note that this modifier is not cumulative.*
