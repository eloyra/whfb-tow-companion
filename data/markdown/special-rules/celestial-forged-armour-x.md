# Celestial Forged Armour (X+)

*Created in the armouries of Wei-jin, Celestial Forged Armour is made from the meteoric ore of Kunlan.*

A model with this special rule has a [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. The armour value of this Ward save is shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as 'X+'). In addition, a Wizard with this special rule may wear armour without penalty.
