# Immune to Psychology

*There are warriors so brave, or perhaps so jaded by the dangers of the world, that they heed no peril.*

If the majority of the models in a unit are Immune to Psychology, the unit automatically passes any [Fear](fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror tests](terror.md) it is required to make. However, if the majority of the models in a unit have this special rule, the unit cannot choose to [Flee](../the-movement-phase/flee-charge-reaction.md) as a charge reaction.

*Note that this special rule does not make a unit immune to any test made against Leadership not stated here.*
