# The Wyrm Slayer

*The exploits of the Wyrm Slayer are not only famous within the lands of Bretonnia, but also amongst its enemies.*

Should Sir Cecil Gastonne kill an enemy model whose troop type is [monstrous infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-infantry.md), [monstrous cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-cavalry.md), [monstrous creature](../troop-types-in-detail/monstrous-creatures.md) or [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md) during any [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), he gains the [Terror](terror.md) special rule for the remainder of the game.
