# Vortex of Souls

*When a Casket of Souls is opened, blinding light is unleashed upon the battlefield as the countless bound souls entombed within it scream into the air seeking freedom from their suffering and confinement. These spirits soar forth, bound by the incantations of the Keeper to do their will.*

A Casket of Souls can cast the following [Bound spells](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 2: [Light of Death](../spell/light-of-death.md) & [Light of Protection](../spell/light-of-protection.md)

[Light of Death (Bound Spell)](../spell/light-of-death.md)

*The unleashed spirits plunge through the bodies of the Tomb Kings' enemies, their hapless victims suffering unbearable agony as their life essence is utterly drained.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 7+ |
| **Range** | 36" |

The target enemy unit must immediately make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, it suffers D3 Strength 3 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1. If, however, this test is failed, it suffers D6+3 Strength 6 hits, each with the [Multiple Wounds (2)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule and with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).

[Light of Protection (Bound Spell)](../spell/light-of-protection.md)

*The freed souls cluster about the skeletal legions of the Tomb Kings, blinding their enemies and turning aside their hostile blows.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Enchantment](../magic/enchantment.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 8+ |
| **Range** | [Self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) |

[Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md). Whilst this [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) is in play, this model and any friendly unit within 18" of this model gains a 6+ Ward save against any wounds suffered. In addition, whilst this Bound spell is in play, enemy units suffer a -1 modifier to any rolls To Hit made against this model and any friendly unit within 18" of this model.

However, if this model casts the Light of Death Bound spell, this Bound spell will immediately expire.
