# "Grind Them Down!"

*Only the finest charioteers served in the Royal Hosts of Nehekhara, and none granted such an honour would dare to disappoint their master in battle.*

Whilst within the [General's](../characters/the-general-characters.md) [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), friendly models whose troop type is [Chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md) may re-roll the dice when rolling to determine the number of [Impact Hits](impact-hits.md) they cause.
