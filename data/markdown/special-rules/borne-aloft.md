# Borne Aloft

*Carried upon the broad shoulders of their most trusted retainers, a Dwarf lord towers above the serried ranks of their army.*

A model with Shieldbearers consists of not one, but four models – the character and three loyal retainers – occupying a single base and acting together as a single entity. To represent this, a model with Shieldbearers has a split profile and follows the [Split Profile (Cavalry)](../troop-types-in-detail/split-profile-cavalry.md) rule. In all other respects, this model is [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md).
