# Warp-spawned

*Creatures of the supernatural feed upon magic to manifest as physical beings and, as such, are vulnerable to magical attacks.*

A model with this special rule cannot make a [Regeneration](regeneration.md) save against a wound caused by a [Magical attack](magical-attacks.md). In addition, characters that are not Warp-spawned cannot join units that are, and vice versa.
