# Cold Blooded

*To other races, the cold blooded Lizardmen seem incapable of emotion.*

When required to make a [Fear](fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror test](terror.md), models with this special rule may roll an extra D6 and discard the highest result.
