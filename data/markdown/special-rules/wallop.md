# Wallop

The Bonegrinder Giant grasps its club two-handed and cracks its enemy on the head. The Bonegrinder Giant makes a single attack against the target unit. For this attack, the Bonegrinder Giant's club has a Strength characteristic of S+3, an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -4 and the [Multiple Wounds (2D3)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
