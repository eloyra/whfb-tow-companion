# Talismanic Tattoos

*Some Asrai decorate themselves with swirling tattoos, the mystical meanings of which protect the wearer from harm.*

Talismanic Tattoos give their wearer a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
