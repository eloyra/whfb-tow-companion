# Mercenaries

See also: [Mercenaries (Warhammer Armies)](../warhammer-armies/mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md)

*Mercenary bands roam the Warhammer world, looking for employment in the armies of foreign lands.*

Often, an army can include certain units drawn from another army list as [mercenaries](../warhammer-armies/mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md). Any such units included in your army gain this special rule. Mercenaries cannot use the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule of the army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) nor the ["Hold your Ground"](../characters/hold-your-ground.md) rule of a [Battle Standard](../characters/the-battle-standard.md). Mercenaries cannot be joined by characters drawn from another army list.
