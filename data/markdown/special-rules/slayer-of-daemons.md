# Slayer of Daemons

*In their quest to meet a mighty doom, some Slayers seek out daemonic creatures. Those that fail to find a foe able to best them in combat invariably become skilled in the grim work of banishing such fiends.*

When this character makes a roll To Wound, a roll of 4+ is always a success, regardless of the target's Toughness. In addition, each unsaved wound inflicted by this character against an enemy model with the [Warp-spawned](warp-spawned.md) special rule, or whose troop type is [behemoth](../troop-types-in-detail/behemoths.md), has the [Multiple Wounds (D3)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
