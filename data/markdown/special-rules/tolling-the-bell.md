# Tolling The Bell

*The tolling of a Screaming Bell declares the Horned Rat's supremacy and fills the foe with dread.*

A Screaming Bell may be rung during the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of your turn. Roll on the table below to determine the effect this has:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **2D6** | **Result** |
| **2** | **Magical Backlash:** Every unit (friend or foe) that is within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) suffers D3 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2. |
| **3-4** | **Dissonant Peal:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all enemy units within 18" of this model suffer a -1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2). |
| **5-6** | **Wall of Unholy Sound:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all friendly Skaven units within 18" of this model gain the [Fear](fear.md) special rule. |
| **7** | **Resonant Power:** Until the end of this turn, all friendly Skaven Wizards within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) they make. |
| **8-9** | **Emboldening Clamour:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all friendly Skaven units within 18" of this model gain the [Immune to Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md) special rule. |
| **10-11** | **Rapturous Ringing:** Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all friendly Skaven units within 18" of this model gain the [Extra Attacks (+1)](extra-attacks.md) special rule. |
| **12** | **Sonorous Knell:** Any enemy unit that is within 18" of this model, including units that are fleeing or that are engaged in combat, must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is failed, the unit suffers D6 Strength 4 hits, with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal). |
