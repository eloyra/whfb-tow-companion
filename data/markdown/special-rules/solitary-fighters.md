# Solitary Fighters

*Mounted atop chariots liberated from weaker Orcs, many Black Orcs discover a new love for crumpin' the enemy whilst careening around the battlefield!*

Black Orc Bosses within a [Nomadic Waaagh!](../warhammer-armies/nomadic-waaagh.md) army are not subject to the [Da Boyz](da-boyz.md) or [Quell Impetuosity](quell-impetuosity.md) special rules.
