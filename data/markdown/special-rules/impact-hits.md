# Impact Hits (X)

*The impact of a charge can itself cause severe casualties amongst the foe.*

The number of Impact Hits caused varies from model to model, and will be shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as 'X'). Often, this is determined by the roll of a dice.

## Resolving Impact Hits

Impact Hits can only be made by a charging model that moved 3" or more and that is in base contact with the enemy. Impact Hits are resolved against the charged unit when the combat is chosen during Step 1.1 of the [Choose Combat & Fight](../the-combat-phase/choose-and-fight-combat.md) sub-phase, before issuing challenges. They hit automatically and use the unmodified Strength of the model making them.
