# Mastery of the Storm Winds

*Through long centuries of study and practice of the elemental winds of Yang and Yin, Miao Ying's mastery of the storm has become sublime.*

Miao Ying may discard up to two of her randomly generated spells (rather than the usual one). When she does so, she may replace them with spells chosen from the [Lore of Yang](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yang.md), the [Lore of Yin](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-yin.md), the signature spell of her chosen [Lore of Magic](https://tow.whfb.app/the-lores-of-magic), or with the following spell: [The Storm Dragon's Fury](../spell/the-storm-dragons-fury.md)

[The Storm Dragon's Fury (Signature Spell)](../spell/the-storm-dragons-fury.md)

*With a quick gesture, Miao Ying summons forth lightning from the skies to strike at her foes.*

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Type** | [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md) |
| **Casting Value** | 10+ |
| **Range** | 18" |

The target enemy unit suffers 2D3 Strength 5 hits, each with an AP of -3. These hits have the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule. Once these hits have been resolved, the target enemy unit must [Give Ground](../movement-in-detail/give-ground.md).
