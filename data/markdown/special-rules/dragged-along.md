# Dragged Along

*Great war engines may be dragged to battle by hordes of infantry.*

A model with this special rule that begins its movement within 1" of a friendly unit whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md), that is not fleeing and that contains ten or more models, may replace its Movement characteristic with that of the unit.
