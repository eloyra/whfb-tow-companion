# Nehekharan Undead

*The legions of the Tomb Kings are no longer beings of flesh and blood, but sun-bleached bones animated by ritualistic magic that stride slowly but inexorably towards their enemies.*

Models with this special rule are 'Undead'. Undead models cannot march (unless they have the [Fly (X)](fly.md) special rule and choose to move by flying). In addition, all Undead models have the following universal special rules:

- [Fear](fear.md)
- [Immune To Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md)
- [Unbreakable](unbreakable.md)
- [Unstable](unstable.md)

A character with this special rule cannot join a unit without this special rule, and vice versa.
