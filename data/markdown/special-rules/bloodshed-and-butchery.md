# Bloodshed & Butchery

*A Minotaur's need for bloodshed reaches a crescendo when they gather in their mighty herds. So much so that their desire to rend flesh and shatter bone becomes contagious, spurring other Beastmen on towards battle.*

All [Doombulls](../unit/doombull.md), [Gorebulls](../unit/gorebull.md), [Minotaur Herds](../unit/minotaur-herd.md) and [Gor Herds](../unit/gor-herd.md) within a [Minotaur Blood Herd](../warhammer-armies/minotaur-blood-herd.md) army gain the [Impetuous](impetuous.md) special rule.
