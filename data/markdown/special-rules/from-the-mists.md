# From the Mists

*The Sea Guard are veterans of ship-to-shore warfare and are expert in using the sea mists to confound their enemies.*

After deployment, but before deploying units with the [Scouts](scouts.md) special rule or moving units with the [Vanguard](vanguard.md) special rule, you may immediately remove a single deployed unit belonging to your army from the battlefield and replace it anywhere within your deployment zone.
