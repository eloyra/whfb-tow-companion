# Dwarf Crafted

*Dwarfs craft missile weapons with a skill and precision of both form and function that is not seen in the weapons of other races.*

Models with this special rule do not suffer the usual -1 To Hit modifier when making a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction.
