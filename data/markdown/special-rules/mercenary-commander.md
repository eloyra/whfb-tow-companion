# Mercenary Commander

*Hans has fought alongside and earned the respect of mercenary companies across the Old World, any of whom would answer his call to battle.*

If Hans is the army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md), friendly units with the [Mercenaries](mercenaries.md) special rule may benefit from his [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule and may be joined by him. In addition, friendly Mercenary units are not subject to the [Misbehaving Mercenaries](../warhammer-armies/misbehaving-mercenaries.md) rules.
