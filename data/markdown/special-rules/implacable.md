# Implacable

*Once given orders, a Cathayan Sentinel will not falter in its duty, relentlessly carrying out its last commands until the task is complete.*

Once per game, during a turn in which it was charged, this model may choose not to [Give Ground](../movement-in-detail/give-ground.md) should it lose a round of combat. In addition, once per game this model may re-roll its [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md) roll.
