# Skulls of the Foe

Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, any enemy unit that was within 2D6" of the central hole of the blast template after scattering suffers a -1 modifier to its Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2).
