# Hekarti's Blessing

*Dark Elf Sorceresses make devotions to Hekarti, the Hydra Queen and Elven goddess of conjurations.*

Once per game, a model with this special rule may re-roll a single failed [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md).
