# Wake of Fire

*When a Flamespyre Phoenix is enraged, its plumage explodes into a magical flame, causing the creature to leave a trail of angry fire in its wake.*

This model may perform a 'Wake of Fire' attack against a single enemy unit that is not engaged in combat. To do so, this model must move (by flying) over the unit it wishes to attack during the Remaining Moves sub-phase. Once this model's movement is complete, the enemy unit suffers D6 Strength 4 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1 and with the [Flaming Attacks](flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
