# Strike First

*Some warriors are gifted with supernatural speed and reactions, whilst others bear weapons enchanted to move like quicksilver through the air.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), a model with this special rule that is engaged in combat improves its Initiative characteristic to 10 (before any other modifiers are applied). If a model has both this rule and [Strike Last](strike-last.md), the two rules cancel one another out.
