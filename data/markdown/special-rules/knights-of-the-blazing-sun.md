# Knights of the Blazing Sun

*Templars of Myrmidia, the Knights of the Blazing Sun are sophisticated warriors, famed for their mastery of war.*

An army of the Knights of the Blazing Sun may include 0-2 [Master Mages](../unit/master-mage.md) as Character choices per 1,000 points, and 0-1 [Great Cannon](../unit/great-cannon.md) or [Mortar](../unit/mortar.md) as a Special choice per 1,000 points. However, your army cannot include any [Lectors of Sigmar](../unit/lector-of-sigmar.md) or [High Priests of Ulric](../unit/high-priest-of-ulric.md).
