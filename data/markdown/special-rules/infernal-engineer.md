# Infernal Engineer

*Under the scornful gaze of their masters, the gun crews of Zharr-Naggrund are amongst the finest artillerists in all the known world.*

Unless this model is fleeing or engaged in combat, once per turn a friendly war machine that is within its [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) may re-roll one Scatter dice or one Artillery dice.
