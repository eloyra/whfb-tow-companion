# Warriors of the Wind

*Peasant regiments are more familiar with the landscape than the soldiers of the cities, enabling them to advance upon the enemy, swiftly and unseen.*

0-1 unit of [Peasant Levy](../unit/peasant-levy.md) per 1,000 points may have the [Reserve Move](reserve-move.md) and [Scouts](scouts.md) special rules for +2 points per model.
