# Beguiling Aura

*Unicorns shimmer with magical power, a gleaming aura that also bears an enfolding glamour that bewitches and beguiles any who come near.*

Enemy models must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) before making any rolls To Hit against this model during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). If this test is failed, only rolls of a natural 6 will hit.
