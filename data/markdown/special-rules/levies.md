# Levies

*Many regiments are made up of unwilling fighters, pressed into service.*

Models with this special rule cannot use the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule of the army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) nor the ["Hold your Ground"](../characters/hold-your-ground.md) rule of a [Battle Standard](../characters/the-battle-standard.md). However, little is expected from Levies in battle. Therefore, units that do not have this special rule are not required to make a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) when a friendly unit of Levies [Breaks](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md) and [flees](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) from combat.
