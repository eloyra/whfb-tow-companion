# Quick Shot

*Weapons designed for speed can be brought to bear in less than a heartbeat.*

A weapon with this special rule does not suffer the usual -1 [To Hit modifier](../the-shooting-phase/to-hit-modifiers.md) for [Moving and Shooting](../the-shooting-phase/moving-and-shooting.md). In addition, a unit equipped with weapons with this special rule can use them to make a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction regardless of how close the charging unit is.
