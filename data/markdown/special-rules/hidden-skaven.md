# Hidden (Skaven)

*Clan Eshin Master Assassins hide amongst the teeming hordes of Skaven infantry, ready to strike at the unsuspecting enemy.*

Master Assassins are not placed on the battlefield at the start of the game. Instead, they are 'hidden' within a friendly Skaven unit whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) and that has a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of ten or more. Make a note of which unit each Master Assassin is hiding within. A hidden Master Assassin may be revealed during any [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase or at the start of any [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). Position the revealed Master Assassin as you would a character that has joined the unit.

If a unit in which a Master Assassin is hiding is destroyed or flees the battlefield before the Master Assassin is revealed, the Master Assassin is removed as a casualty. A Master Assassin cannot be your army [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md).
