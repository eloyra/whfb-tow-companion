# Herald of Despair

*The tidings of woe uttered by Nekaph before battle are enough to break the spirits of even the most stout hearted folks.*

Any enemy unit that is in base contact with Nekaph or a unit he has joined must roll an extra D6 when making a [Fear](fear.md) or [Terror](terror.md) test, and discard the lowest result.
