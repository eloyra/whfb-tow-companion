# All Sneaky Like

*Kiknik's packmates are excellent hunters that encircle the enemy before launching their attacks.*

0-1 Goblin Wolf Rider Mobs in the same muster list as Kiknik may have the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule for free. In addition, you may apply a +1 or -1 modifier to the result when rolling to determine if a Goblin Wolf Rider Mob with the Ambushers special rule that is currently held in reserve arrives this turn as reinforcements or is delayed.
