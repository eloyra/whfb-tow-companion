# Knights Panther

*The Knights Panther work tirelessly to unify the divided lands of the Empire and rid the Old World of evil doers.*

An army of the Knights Panther may include 0-1 unit of [Empire Knights](../unit/empire-knights.md) or [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the [Order of the White Wolf](../magic-item/order-of-the-white-wolf.md) as a Mercenary choice. This unit is not subject to the [Misbehaving Mercenaries](../warhammer-armies/misbehaving-mercenaries.md) rules.
