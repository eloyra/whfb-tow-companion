# Commander of Legions

*Though not as fully versed in the art of Necromancy as the Liche Priests, Settra's will alone is enough for the dead to rise at his command.*

Settra gains the [Arise!](arise.md) special rule and, unlike other models with this special rule, may use it even when engaged in combat.
