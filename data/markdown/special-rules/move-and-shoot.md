# Move & Shoot

*Weapons that are easy to use when moving at speed may lack power or range, but make up for it with their versatility.*

A weapon with this special rule can be used in the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) even if the model equipped with it marched this turn.
