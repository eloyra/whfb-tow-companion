# Hit & Run

*Scout Gyrocopters appear from nowhere, descending quickly from the skies or emerging unexpectedly from behind dense terrain, to strike at the enemy before vanishing again.*

Should it win a round of combat, a unit with this special rule may choose to [Fall Back in Good Order](../movement-in-detail/fall-back-in-good-order.md) rather than making a follow up or pursuit move.
