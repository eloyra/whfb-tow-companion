# Stone Skeleton

*A Stonehorn is akin to a living fossil, its massive skeleton having hardened to rock.*

This model is less vulnerable to the [Multiple Wounds (X)](multiple-wounds.md) special rule. If it suffers an unsaved wound from an attack with this special rule, reduce the number of Wounds lost by 1, to a minimum of 1.
