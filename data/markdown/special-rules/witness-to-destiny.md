# Witness to Destiny

*Cursed with the knowledge of their own demise, the Phoenix Guard are able to effortlessly avoid the mundane weapons of the enemy with a disturbing ease.*

This unit has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
