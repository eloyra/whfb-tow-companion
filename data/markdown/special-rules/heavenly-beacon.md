# Heavenly Beacon

*Brightly coloured and illuminated from within, Sky Lanterns act as both a beacon and a rallying point to the forces of the Celestial Dragon.*

Unless this model is fleeing, friendly units within 12" of it may re-roll any failed [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Rally test](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md). In addition, whilst this model is on the battlefield, unless it is fleeing, you may apply a +1 or -1 modifier to the result when rolling to determine if a friendly unit with the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule that is currently held in reserve arrives this turn as reinforcements or is delayed. Finally, a [Lord Magistrate](../unit/lord-magistrate.md) or [Strategist](../unit/strategist.md) mounted on a Sky Lantern has a [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of 12".
