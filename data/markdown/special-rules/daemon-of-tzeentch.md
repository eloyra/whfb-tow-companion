# Daemon of Tzeentch

*Created from the raw stuff of magic by the Changer of Ways, the Daemons of Tzeentch blur with ever-changing colours and crawling warpflame.*

Daemons of Tzeentch have the [Hatred (Daemons of Nurgle)](hatred.md) special rule. In addition, a Daemon of Tzeentch that is also a Wizard may apply a +1 modifier to any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) they make.

*Note that this is a modifier to the result of a roll – it does not negate a roll of a natural double 1.*
