# Human Form

In their Human form, the Dragons of Grand Cathay are wise and beloved leaders that walk amongst their people and fight alongside them in battle. Whilst in their Human form, a Cathayan Dragon follows the rules for a character whose troop type is [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md).
