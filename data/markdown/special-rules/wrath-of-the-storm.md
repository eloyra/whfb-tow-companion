# Wrath of the Storm

*The Storm Dragon is known for her unrelenting fervour in the defence of Grand Cathay against the marauding tribes of the north, a trait she passes on to those who fight beneath her banners.*

All units of [Jade Warriors](../unit/jade-warriors.md) and [Jade Lancers](../unit/jade-lancers.md) included in an army that is led by Miao Ying gain the [Hatred (Warriors of Chaos & Daemonic models)](hatred.md) special rule.
