# Butcher's Cauldron

*Some Emissaries of the Great Maw drag behind them huge cauldrons, used to make offerings to their ravenous god.*

This character replaces the [Impact Hits (2)](impact-hits.md) special rule with the Impact Hits (D3+1) special rule. In addition, during the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, a character with a Butcher's Cauldron may attempt to make an offering to the Great Maw by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase this character and any unit they have joined gains the [Regeneration (5+)](regeneration.md) special rule. However, if this test is failed, this character immediately loses a single Wound.
