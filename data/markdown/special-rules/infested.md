# Infested

*Legend has it that, when slain, Terrorgheists explode into great clouds of multitudinous, swarming bats.*

When this model loses its last Wound, every unit in base contact with it (friend or foe) suffers D6 Strength 2 hits, each with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1.
