# Slimy Shanks

*The thick coating of swamp slime covering the Troll Hag's body makes landing a blow upon her exceedingly difficult.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against this model during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.
