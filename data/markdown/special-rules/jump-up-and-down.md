# Jump Up & Down

The Giant jumps around, kicking and flattening the enemy. For this attack, the Giant does not use its club. Instead, the target unit suffers D6+1 hits, each using the Strength characteristic of this model, with no [armour saves](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) permitted ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).
