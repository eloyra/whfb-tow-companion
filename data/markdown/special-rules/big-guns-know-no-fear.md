# Big Guns Know No Fear

*Even the most fearsome of foes is quickly diminished in the presence of Nuln's untiring artillery.*

Empire Engineers in a [City-state of Nuln](../warhammer-armies/city-state-of-nuln.md) army (and any unit they have joined) gain the [Immune to Psychology](immune-to-psychology.md) special rule whilst there is a friendly war machine within their [Command range](../characters/command-range.md).
