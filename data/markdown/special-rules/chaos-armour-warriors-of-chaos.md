# Chaos Armour (Warriors of Chaos)

*Chaos Armour is the living gift of the gods. As champions progress towards glory, their armour becomes ever more heavy and baroque as new plates are added, each new piece obscuring more of their humanity.*

A model with this special rule has a [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. The [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of this Ward save is shown in brackets after the name of this special rule (shown here as 'X+'). In addition, a Wizard with this special rule may wear armour without penalty.
