# Move or Shoot

*Artillery weapons sacrifice a speedy reload and manoeuvrability for range and power, making them impossible to fire on the move.*

A weapon with this special rule cannot be used in the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) if the model equipped with it moved for any reason during this turn (including [rallying](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md) and [reforming](../movement-in-detail/reform.md)).
