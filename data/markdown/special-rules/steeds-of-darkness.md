# Steeds of Darkness

*The mutated steeds of the Knights of Chaos are swift and powerful war-mounts, standing as a warped mockery of the thoroughbreds of fair Bretonnia.*

When a unit in this army whose troop type is [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) or [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md) makes a [Charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md), [Flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) or [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, it may re-roll any dice that roll a natural 1, before discarding any dice that are required to be discarded.
