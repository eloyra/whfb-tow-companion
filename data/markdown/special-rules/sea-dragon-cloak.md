# Sea Dragon Cloak

*The pelt of a Sea Dragon is worn by Dark Elf pirates and corsairs, both as a mark of their rank and as armour against the missiles of the foe.*

A model with this special rule improves its [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+) against non-magical shooting attacks.
