# Accomplished Archers

*Under the watchful eye of a veteran commander, regiments of Sea Guard train with their bows, perfecting the art of firing at the foe even as they evade attacks.*

Unless mounted on a [Lothern Skycutter](../unit/lothern-skycutter.md), a Sea Guard Garrison Commander has the [Evasive](evasive.md) and [Fire & Flee](fire-and-flee.md) special rules. In addition, any unit of [Lothern Sea Guard](../unit/lothern-sea-guard.md) joined by this character gains the Evasive and Fire & Flee special rules for as long as the character remains with the unit.
