# Primeval Roar

*The wailing cry of a Troglodon stirs the savagery within all Lizardmen and proves they have the Old Ones' favour.*

Once per game, during the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this model may attempt to unleash the primeval savagery of the Lizardmen by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using its own Leadership). If this test is passed, until the end of that turn all friendly Lizardmen units within 7" of this model gain the [Furious Charge](furious-charge.md) special rule.
