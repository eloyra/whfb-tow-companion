# Volley Fire

*Bows and other weapons can loose their projectiles in a high-arcing volley. Even warriors who cannot see the foe can shoot in their general direction.*

When a unit with this special rule makes a shooting attack, half of the models in each rank other than the front rank (or front two ranks if the unit is on a hill), rounding up, can shoot (in addition to the usual models that shoot from the front rank, or front two ranks if the unit is on a hill). A unit cannot Volley Fire if it has moved for any reason during this turn (including [reforming](../movement-in-detail/reform.md)), or when making a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction.

*Note that models in rear ranks use the line of sight of the model at the front of their file.*
