# Hidden Trails

*The Elves of Chrace are skilled woodsmen and trackers all, able to traverse hidden trails through hostile terrain and descend upon their enemies unexpectedly.*

0-1 unit of [Elven Spearmen](../unit/elven-spearmen.md) or [Elven Archers](../unit/elven-archers.md) per 1,000 points within a [Chracian Warhost](../warhammer-armies/the-chracian-warhost.md) Army of Infamy may have the [Move Through Cover](move-through-cover.md) special rule for +1 point per model.

In addition, once per game, after rolling to determine if a unit with the [Ambushers](ambushers.md) special rule that is currently held in reserve as reinforcements arrives, you may apply a +1 or - 1 modifier to the result.
