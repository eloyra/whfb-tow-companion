# Cavalry Support

Whilst a rider may be able to reach above a comrade to strike at the foe, their mount cannot. Should a [cavalry](cavalry.md) model be able to make a [supporting attack](../the-combat-phase/supporting-attacks.md), only the rider can attack, not the mount.
