# Thunderstomp

Colossal feet and gigantic hooves do damage beyond imagining when they kick and stomp upon the enemy. Any [Stomp Attacks](../special-rules/stomp-attacks.md) made by a behemoth have an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2. However, this rule cannot be used against another monster ([monstrous creatures](monstrous-creatures.md) or [behemoth](behemoths.md)) – they are simply too big to be stomped on effectively.
