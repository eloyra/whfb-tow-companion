# Characters (Troop Types)

Some models will have the word 'Character' in brackets after their troop type, indicating that the model is a character. This section does not cover characters in detail. Instead, and because characters are such a powerful and important part of *Warhammer: the Old World*, they have a [section](https://tow.whfb.app/characters) all to themselves.
