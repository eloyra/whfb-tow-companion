# Press of Battle

In the heat of battle, orderly ranks become a swirling melee. Except during a turn in which it charged, the [fighting rank](../the-combat-phase/the-fighting-rank.md) of a unit that has this rule and is arrayed in combat order is not one, but two ranks deep. In other words, models that are directly behind and in base contact with models in the units actual fighting rank are also considered to be within its fighting rank.
