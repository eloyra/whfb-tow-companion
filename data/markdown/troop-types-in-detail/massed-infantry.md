# Massed Infantry

Numbers alone can sometimes overcome the foe. When determining combat results, if one side has a higher [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) than the other and includes one or more units with this rule, that side may claim a bonus of +1 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) point.
