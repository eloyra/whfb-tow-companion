# Parry

With sword and shield, warriors attempt to deflect enemy attacks. Whilst engaged in close combat, a model with this rule that is equipped with and chooses to use a [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) and [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) improves its [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1, to a maximum of 3+.
