# Chariots

A chariot is usually a wheeled war vehicle drawn into battle by beasts of some kind and crewed by heavily armed and armoured warriors. This category can also include large objects, such as mobile altars, that are pushed along or carried. Chariots are split into two sub-categories:

- [Light Chariots](light-chariots.md)
- [Heavy Chariots](heavy-chariots.md)
