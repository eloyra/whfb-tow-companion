# Cavalry

The term cavalry refers to any riders mounted on war beasts – commonly warhorses or similar creatures. It can also include war beasts on their own as packs of animals which will often function in a manner more similar to cavalry than infantry. Cavalry is split into four sub-categories:

- [Light Cavalry](light-cavalry.md)
- [Heavy Cavalry](heavy-cavalry.md)
- [Monstrous Cavalry](monstrous-cavalry.md)
- [War Beasts](war-beasts.md)
