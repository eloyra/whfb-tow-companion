# War Machines

War machines such as cannon and bolt throwers are powerful weapons on the field of battle, able to pulverise whole regiments, breach stone walls or even slay fearsome monsters with a single, well-placed shot. The most commonly seen types of war machine are dealt with as weapons in greater detail in their own [section](https://tow.whfb.app/war-machines).
