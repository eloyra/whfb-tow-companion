# Undisciplined

A unit with this rule cannot use the [Inspiring Presence](../characters/inspiring-presence.md) rule of the army's [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md) nor the ["Hold your Ground"](../characters/hold-your-ground.md) rule of a [Battle Standard](../characters/the-battle-standard.md).
