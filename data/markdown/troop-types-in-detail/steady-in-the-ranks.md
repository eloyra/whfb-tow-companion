# Steady in the Ranks

Due to their solid stature, heavy armour and often elite nature, [heavy infantry](heavy-infantry.md) are rarely concerned by inferior numbers. To represent this, a unit of heavy infantry arrayed in either [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) or [Open Order](../unusual-formations/open-order-formation.md) formation does not become [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) when engaged in its flank or rear unless the enemy unit has a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of ten or more.
