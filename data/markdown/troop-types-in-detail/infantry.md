# Infantry

Infantry includes all units of foot troops and forms the backbone of most armies. Infantry is split into four sub-categories, some of which are subject to their own rules:

- [Regular Infantry](regular-infantry.md)
- [Heavy Infantry](heavy-infantry.md)
- [Monstrous Infantry](monstrous-infantry.md)
- [Swarms](swarms.md)
