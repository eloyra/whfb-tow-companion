# Muster Your Forces!

To play a game of Warhammer: the Old World, each player will need an army to command, so the first thing to do is assemble your forces. You can simply use all the models in your collection, but most players use points values and army lists to ensure their forces are evenly matched for a closely fought battle. This system is explained in the [Warhammer Armies](https://tow.whfb.app/warhammer-armies).
