# Bound Spells

Some models have 'Bound' spells, which may be contained within a magic item or given by a special rule, and which they can cast in the usual manner. If a 'Power Level' is given, this is added to the result of the [Casting roll](casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) to give the casting result. If no Power Level is given, nothing is added – the result of the Casting roll is itself the casting result. When casting a Bound spell, there is no risk of a [miscast](miscast.md) or chance of a [perfect invocation](perfect-invocations.md).

You may attempt to dispel a Bound spell as usual. Magic items that dispel or destroy spells work as normal against Bound spells. When dispelling a Bound spell, there is no risk of being [outclassed in the art](outclassed-in-the-art.md).

A model can only cast a single Bound spell per phase. Possessing a Bound spell does not make a model a [Wizard](wizards.md).
