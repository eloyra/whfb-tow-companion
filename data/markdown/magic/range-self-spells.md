# Range 'Self' Spells

Spells with a range of 'Self' do not require a target, instead being focused upon the casting Wizard. Some such spells have an 'aura' of effect, measured from the caster. As mentioned previously, a Wizard can cast a range 'self' spell whilst engaged in combat.
