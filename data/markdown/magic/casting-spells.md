# Casting Spells

When a spell can be cast depends upon its type:

- [Enchantment](enchantment.md) and [Hex](hex.md) spells can be cast during the Conjuration sub-phase of your Strategy phase.
- [Conveyance](conveyance.md) spells can be cast at any point during the Remaining Moves sub-phase of your Movement phase.
- [Magic Missiles](magic-missiles.md) and [Magical Vortex](magical-vortex.md) spells can be cast when a Wizard is chosen during your Shooting phase.
- [Assailment](assailment.md) spells can be cast when a Wizard fights during any Combat phase.

A Wizard can attempt to cast a number of spells equal to their Level of Wizardry per turn, but may only attempt to cast each spell once per turn. Unless stated otherwise, Wizards that are engaged in combat can only cast Assailment or range [self](range-self-spells.md) spells.

To cast a spell, a Wizard declares which spell they wish to cast and its target (if required).
