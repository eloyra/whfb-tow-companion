# Defended Low Linear Obstacles (Combat)

See also: [Defended Low Linear Obstacles](../battlefield-terrain/defended-low-linear-obstacles.md)

A unit behind a [low linear obstacle](../battlefield-terrain/low-linear-obstacles.md) can defend it by moving its front rank into base contact with the obstacle. Enemies can charge the defenders as normal but do not have to physically cross the obstacle. Instead, the front rank of a charging unit moves into base contact with the opposite side of the obstacle. Unless it has the [Fly (X)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule, a unit that charges an enemy behind a defended obstacle makes a [disordered charge](disordered-charges.md).
