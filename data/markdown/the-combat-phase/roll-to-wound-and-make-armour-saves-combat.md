# Roll to Wound & Make Armour Saves (Combat)

As described in the [Shooting section](../the-shooting-phase/roll-to-wound-and-make-armour-saves-shooting.md), for each successful roll To Hit, a hit has been caused. Unfortunately, causing a hit is not always enough to cause a wound.
