# Incomplete Ranks

If a unit is fighting to its flank or rear, or has been joined by a character that does not fit neatly within its [ranks](../characters/positioning-characters.md), its fighting rank might be incomplete. In such cases, warriors from the middle of the unit would press forward to complete the rank. To represent this, a fighting rank is always assumed to contain the same number of models as the largest rank or file behind it.
