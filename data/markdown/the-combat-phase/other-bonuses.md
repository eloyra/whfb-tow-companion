# Other Bonuses

Many special rules confer additional bonus combat result points. For example, a [Close Order](../special-rules/close-order.md) formation that is in [Combat Order](../forming-units/combat-order-close-order.md) may claim a bonus of +1 combat result point. Any special rules that confer such bonuses will detail the criteria that must be met and how many bonus combat result points can be claimed.
