# 1" Apart (Combat)

If a unit that Breaks or Falls Back in Good Order is still in base contact with one or more enemy units after moving, the players should nudge the units apart, by the smallest amount possible, to maintain the 1" rule. Both players should agree upon how best to do this and ensure neither gains any unfair advantage.
