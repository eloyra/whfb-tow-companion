# Restrain & Reform

Most units may attempt to 'restrain and reform', rather than making a follow up or pursuit move, by making a 'Restraint' test. To make a Restraint test, test against the unit's Leadership characteristic. If this test is failed, the unit must either [follow up](follow-up.md) or [pursue](pursuit.md). If this test is passed, the unit remains where it is and may make a free [reform](../movement-in-detail/reform.md).
