# Loser Breaks & Flees

The losing unit Breaks, immediately turning tail and [fleeing](../movement-in-detail/flee.md). A unit that Breaks and flees from combat flees directly away from the winning unit with the highest Unit Strength. If two enemy units have the same Unit Strength, randomly determine which the unit flees from.
