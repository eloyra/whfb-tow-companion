# Rank Bonus

Depending upon its formation, and if it is wide enough, a unit can claim a Rank Bonus of +1 for each extra rank behind the first, up to the maximum determined by its troop type. How many models a rank must contain to claim a Rank Bonus, as well as the maximum Rank Bonus a unit can claim, is determined by its [troop type](../troop-types-at-a-glance/troop-type-table.md).

*Note that this bonus can be claimed for an incomplete rear rank, as long as there are sufficient models in it. However, a Disrupted unit cannot claim a Rank Bonus.*
