# Rallied Units

A unit that passes a [Rally test](rally-fleeing-units.md) regains its composure and returns to the fight. Upon rallying, a unit may immediately perform a free [reform](../movement-in-detail/reform.md). The unit cannot charge during this turn and counts as having moved for the purposes of shooting, but can otherwise act as normal.
