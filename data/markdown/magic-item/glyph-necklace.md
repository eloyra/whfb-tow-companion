# Glyph Necklace

Talisman 45 points

*This ancient token of protection wards off attacks, causing enemies to fumble their blows.*

The bearer of the Glyph Necklace has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and gains the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.
