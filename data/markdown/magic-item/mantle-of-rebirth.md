# Mantle of Rebirth

Magic Armour 40 points

*This enchanted armour fills the wearer with the vitality of spring, instilling them with power and quickly healing their wound.*

The Mantle of Rebirth is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.
