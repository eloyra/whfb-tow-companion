# Rune of Preservation

Magic Armour 25 points

*The Rune of Preservation was first struck to counter the decapitating blows of Elven swordmasters.*

A model wearing armour inscribed with a Rune of Preservation is immune to both the [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) and [Multiple Wounds (X)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rules. If the wearer suffers an unsaved wound from an attack with either of these special rules, they lose a single Wound.
