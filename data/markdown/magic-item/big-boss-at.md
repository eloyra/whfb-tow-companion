# Big Boss 'At

Enchanted Item 50 points

*Orcs and Goblins are easily impressed, and this ornate helmet is particularly impressive. What's more, the Boss insists that wearing it makes them braver and cleverer, which must be true.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, a character wearing the Big Boss 'At that is not engaged in combat may treat their comrades to some inspiring words by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase this character and any unit they have joined gains the [Unbreakable](../special-rules/unbreakable.md) special rule.
