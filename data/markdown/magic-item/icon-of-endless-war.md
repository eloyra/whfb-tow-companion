# Icon of Endless War

Magic Standard 30 points

*This brass standard constantly drips blood, the smell of which drives all of Khorne's followers mad with battle-lust.*

When a unit carrying the Banner of Rage declares a [charge](../movement-in-detail/the-charge-move.md), it may re-roll its Charge roll.
