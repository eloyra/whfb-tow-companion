# Shadow Magnet

Talisman 40 points

*Warpstone-contaminated veins of ore can absorb light and cast an aura of deep, shadowy darkness.*

Any enemy model that targets the bearer of the Shadow Magnet or any unit they have joined during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.
