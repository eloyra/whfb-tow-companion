# Sloppity Bilepiper

Ability 35 points

*The infectious tunes of a Bilepiper fill their companions with joyous murrain.*

Daemonic Heralds whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Friendly units with the [Daemons of Nurgle](../special-rules/daemon-of-nurgle.md) special rule that begin their movement within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) have a +1 modifier to their Movement characteristic.
