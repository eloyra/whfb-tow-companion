# Monster Hunter's Tapestry

Magic Standard 25 points

*Those who have earned the right to fight under this standard have battled some of the Old World's most dangerous beasts and lived to tell the tale.*

Enemy units may not make [Stomp Attacks](../special-rules/stomp-attacks.md) against a unit carrying the Monster Hunter's Tapestry.
