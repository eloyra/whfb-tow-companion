# Dawnstone

Talisman 35 points

*The magic trapped within this semi-precious stone binds together shattered armour plates.*

The bearer of the Dawnstone may re-roll any [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll of a natural 1.
