# Rune of Stone\*

Magic Armour 5 points

*A simple but brittle rune, the Rune of Stone is the first rune taught to apprentice runesmiths.*

Single use. For each Rune of Stone inscribed upon their armour, a model may re-roll a single failed [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll.
