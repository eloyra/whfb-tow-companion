# Horn of the Great Hunt

Enchanted Item 35 points

*The dissonant blare of this twisted horn summons Beastmen to the blood grounds to hunt and slaughter the weak.*

Once per turn, after rolling to determine if a unit with the [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) special rule that is currently held in reserve as reinforcements arrives, and if the bearer of the Horn of the Great Hunt is on the battlefield and is not fleeing, you may apply a +1 or -1 modifier to the result.
