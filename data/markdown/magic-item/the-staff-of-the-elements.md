# The Staff of the Elements

Arcane Item

*This gnarled wooden staff acts as a conduit for the Winds of Magic, allowing Lady Élisse to better manifest the power of the elements.*

If you wish, Lady Élisse may discard two of her randomly generated spells (rather than the usual one) and instead select two signature spells from the Lore of [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md) and/or the [Lore of the Lady](../the-lores-of-magic/lore-of-the-lady.md).
