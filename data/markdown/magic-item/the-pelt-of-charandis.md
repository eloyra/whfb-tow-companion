# The Pelt of Charandis

Enchanted Item

*In recognition of Korhil's bravery and service, the pelt of Charandis was fashioned into a magnificent, enchanted cloak for him to wear.*

The wearer of the pelt of Charandis improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 whilst engaged in combat, and by 2 (to a maximum of 2+) against non-magical shooting attacks. In addition, the wearer gains the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.
