# Alchemist's Mask

Enchanted Item 35 points

*Created in the House of Secrets of Shang Yang, this sorcerous mask gifts the wearer with the alchemical secrets of the Iron Dragon.*

The wearer of the Alchemist's Mask can cast the [Plague of Rust](../spell/plague-of-rust.md) spell from the Lore of [Elementalism](../the-lores-of-magic/elementalism.md) (see page 327 of the Warhammer: the Old World rulebook) as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1.
