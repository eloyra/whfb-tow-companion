# Battle Banner

Magic Standard 60 points

*This intricate banner, festooned with gemstones, bears detailed scenes of Ulthuan's victories.*

When calculating its combat result, a unit carrying the Battle Banner may claim an additional bonus of +D3 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) points.
