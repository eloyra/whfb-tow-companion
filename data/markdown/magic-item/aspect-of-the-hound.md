# Aspect of the Hound

Ability 20 points

*Fighting as a pack, those in the kindred of the hound work together to bring down larger prey.*

A character who adopts the Aspect of the Hound, and any unit they have joined, may re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 1 during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
