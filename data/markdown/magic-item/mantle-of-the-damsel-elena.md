# Mantle of the Damsel Elena

Talisman 25 points

*Legend has it that Elena continued fighting the Goblin hordes even as the venom of a thousand spider bites coursed through her veins.*

The bearer is immune to the [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) special rule. If the bearer is wounded by an attack with this special rule, the attacker must roll To Wound as normal.
