# Effigy of Mork

Talisman 35 points

*Be it a string of lucky escapes, arcane enchantment, or perhaps the divine intervention of the cunning (yet brutal) Mork, this small trinket seems to keep the wearer out of danger.*

Any model that directs its attacks against the bearer of the Effigy of Mork during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.
