# Rune of Banishment

Magic Weapon 25 points

*First crafted in the days of the Ancestor Gods, the touch of this potent rune is anathema to the creatures of Chaos.*

Enemy models with the [Warp-spawned](../special-rules/warp-spawned.md) special rule cannot make any [Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against hits caused by a weapon inscribed with the Rune of Banishment.
