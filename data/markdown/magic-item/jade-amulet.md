# Jade Amulet\*

Talisman 25 points

*Potent jade talismans are given in thanks for services performed on behalf of the Cult of Sigmar.*

The bearer is immune to the [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) special rule. If the bearer is struck a Killing Blow, they are permitted [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves as normal. If the wound is unsaved, they lose a single Wound.
