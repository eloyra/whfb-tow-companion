# Da Spider Banner

Magic Standard 35 points

*Created by the Forest Goblins of the Rootgrubba tribe, this unsettling banner depicts their fearsome arachnid god, whom the tribe reveres alongside Gork and Mork.*

A unit carrying Da Spider Banner gains the [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) special rule. If the unit already has the Poisoned Attacks special rule, then their attacks will wound automatically on a roll of a natural 5 or 6 To Hit, rather than the usual 6.
