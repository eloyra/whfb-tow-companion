# Icon of Morr

Magic Standard 25 points

*The spine-chilling influence of the god of death can be felt most keenly around this macabre totem.*

A unit carrying the Icon of Morr gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule. If they already have the Fear special rule, they instead gain the [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rule.
