# The Banner of Xen Wun

Magic Standard 15 points

*Enemies often falter before this ashen banner, touched by the spirits of Xen Wun.*

Enemy units that wish to declare a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) or [Fire & Flee](../special-rules/fire-and-flee.md) charge reaction against a unit carrying the Banner of Xen Wun must first make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md). If this test is passed, they may declare a Stand & Shoot or Fire & Flee charge reaction. If this test is failed, they must [Hold](../the-movement-phase/hold.md) instead.
