# The Vortex Shard

Arcane Item 50 points

*Hewn from a shattered Waystone, the Vortex Shard has the power to becalm the Winds of Magic.*

Single use. A Wizard may use this item instead of making a dispel attempt. If they do so, the spell is automatically dispelled with no [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) required. In addition, all [Remains in Play](../magic/remains-in-play.md) spells currently in play are dispelled, including spells cast by friendly Wizards.
