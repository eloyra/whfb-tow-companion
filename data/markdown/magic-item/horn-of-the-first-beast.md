# Horn of the First Beast

Enchanted Item 30 points

*This immeasurably ancient, fossilised horn is said to be taken from the skull of the First Beast. When it is sounded, Beastmen find the savage fury of their race swelling within their hearts.*

Unless the bearer of the Horn of the First Beast is fleeing, any friendly unit that is within 18" of this model may use its Leadership characteristic when testing for [Primal Fury](../special-rules/primal-fury.md).
