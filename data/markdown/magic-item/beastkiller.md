# Beastkiller

Ability 5 points

*As a sign of their prowess as a hunter of monsters, a Beastkiller will adorn their body with trophy horns and tusks.*

Once per turn, this character may re-roll a single failed roll To Wound made against an enemy model with the [Large Target](../special-rules/large-target.md) special rule.
