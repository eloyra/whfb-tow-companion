# Ring of Fury\*

Enchanted Item 25 points

*The Elves of Ulthuan are known for their skill at crafting magical rings, and black iron Rings of Fury are popular amongst Elven warriors.*

The wielder of the Ring of Fury can cast the [Hammerhand](../spell/hammerhand.md) spell from the Lore of [Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md) as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1.
