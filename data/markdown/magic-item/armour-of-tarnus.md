# Armour of Tarnus

Magic Armour 35 points

*This cunning armour was crafted to the specifications of the warrior-Wizard von Tarnus.*

The Armour of Tarnus is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md) which may be purchased and worn by an Imperial Wizard, even if they do not have the option to be equipped with armour, a shield or barding, without penalty. In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
