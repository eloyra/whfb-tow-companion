# Bull Standard

Magic Standard 20 points

*The Bull Standard lends the strength of a charging Rhinox to the Ogres that march beneath it.*

A unit carrying the Bull Standard may re-roll any failed rolls To Wound when resolving [Impact Hits](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) .
