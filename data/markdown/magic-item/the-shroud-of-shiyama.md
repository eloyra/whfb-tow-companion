# The Shroud of Shiyama

Magic Standard 25 points

*The spectral presence of Shiyama, the Spirit Dragon, lingers about those that fight beneath this silken standard crafted in her memory.*

A unit carrying the Shroud of Shiyama gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule. If they already have the Fear special rule, they instead gain the [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) special rule.
