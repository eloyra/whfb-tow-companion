# Ironhide Talisman

Talisman 30 points

*It is said that the one who bears this powerful heirloom can endure even the most telling blows.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. Any enemy model that makes a successful roll To Hit against the bearer of the Ironhide Talisman during the [Shooting](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) phase or [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) must re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 6.

*Note that this magic item does not work against attacks that hit automatically.*
