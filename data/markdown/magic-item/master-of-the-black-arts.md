# Master of the Black Arts

Ability 30 points

*The Winds of Magic bend easily to the will of this Vampire, so matchless is their magical prowess.*

This Vampiric Power may only be taken by a Vampire Count that is a Level 2 Wizard, or by a Vampire Thrall that is a Level 1 Wizard. This Vampire increases their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md) by 1.
