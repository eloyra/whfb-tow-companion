# Rune of the Reckless

Ability 35 points

*Slayers that bear this tattoo fight with a burning and uncontrollable fury.*

A model with this runic tattoo gains the Frenzy special rule and, during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), has a +1 modifier to their rolls To Hit. However, enemy models that target this model during the Combat phase also have a +1 modifier to their rolls To Hit.
