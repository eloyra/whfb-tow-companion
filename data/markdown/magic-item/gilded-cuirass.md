# Gilded Cuirass

Magic Armour 60 points

*Inscribed with the words of the Grail Vow, the blessings of the Lady shine upon the wearer.*

The Gilded Cuirass is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.
