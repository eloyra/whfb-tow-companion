# Halfling Cookbook

Arcane Item 30 points

*The culinary secrets contained within the Halfling Cookbook are a source of great power.*

The bearer of the Halfling Cookbook increases their [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), Dispel range and the range of a single spell chosen by their controlling player (not including range [self](../magic/range-self-spells.md) spells) by 3".
