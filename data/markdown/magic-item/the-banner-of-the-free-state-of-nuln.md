# The Banner of the Free State of Nuln

Magic Standard 20 points

*The City-state of Nuin has no stake in the War of the Three Emperors, instead taking pride in the prosperity of their great city.*

The Banner of the Free State of Nuln may only be taken by a model in a [City-state of Nuln](../warhammer-armies/city-state-of-nuln.md) Army of Infamy. A unit carrying the Banner of the Free State of Nuln gains the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rule.
