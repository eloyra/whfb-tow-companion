# Crown of Jade

Talisman 20 points

*Hostile magic recoils from the wearer of this gleaming crown of office.*

The Crown of Jade gives its wearer a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md), or an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell.
