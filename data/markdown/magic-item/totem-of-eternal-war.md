# Totem of Eternal War

Magic Standard 45 points

*The power of the æther flows from this accursed icon and into the Daemons that march beneath it.*

[Daemonic Locus](../special-rules/daemonic-locus.md) only. Friendly [Daemonic](../special-rules/daemonic.md) units reduce the number of wounds suffered due to the [Daemonic Instability](../special-rules/daemonic-instability.md) special rule by an additional D3 whilst within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of this model.
