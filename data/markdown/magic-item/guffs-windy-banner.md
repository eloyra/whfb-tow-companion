# Guff's Windy Banner

Magic Standard 20 points

*The banner of Guff's Garrison eternally flutters in an inexplicable breeze. Those that fight in its shadow display remarkable intestinal fortitude.*

A unit carrying the Guff's Windy Banner may re-roll any failed [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md).
