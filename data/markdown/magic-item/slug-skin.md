# Slug-skin

Ability 50 points

*This grotesque gift of the Chaos gods causes the recipient's skin to constantly ooze with a vile, foul smelling slime.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against this model or any unit it has joined during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.
