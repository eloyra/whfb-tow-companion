# Shield of the Gorgon

Magic Armour 40 points

*This burnished shield harnesses the petrifying power of the monstrous gorgons that inhabit the deserts of Araby.*

The Shield of the Gorgon is a shield that may only be taken by a model in a [Knightly Order](../warhammer-armies/knightly-order.md) Army of Infamy. In addition, whilst in base contact with the bearer, enemy models suffer a -1 modifier to their Attacks characteristic (to a minimum of 1).
