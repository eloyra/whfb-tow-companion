# Rune of Confusion

Magic Standard 35 points

*Under the shadow of this rune, the enthusiasm of charging enemies quickly wanes.*

Any enemy unit that charges the front arc of a unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Confusion makes a [disordered charge](../the-combat-phase/disordered-charges.md).
