# Flying Horror

Ability 35 points

*The Vampire is able to take to the skies with great, membranous wings, or perhaps can even metamorphose into a monstrous bat.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. This Vampire gains the [Fly (10)](../special-rules/fly.md) rule, but cannot join a unit.
