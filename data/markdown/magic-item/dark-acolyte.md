# Dark Acolyte

Ability 30 points

*Some Vampires have an affinity for necromantic magic beyond that of their peers, which only increases with age and practice.*

This Vampire gains the [Invocation of Nehek](../special-rules/invocation-of-nehek.md) special rule. For the purposes of using this special rule, this model counts as a Level 1 Wizard.
