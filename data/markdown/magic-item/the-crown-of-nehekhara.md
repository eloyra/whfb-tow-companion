# The Crown of Nehekhara

Enchanted Item

*Having incorporated the crowns of conquered rivals into his own, Settra's regal headdress allows him to instil his undying will into all those nearby.*

Settra's [My Will Be Done](../special-rules/my-will-be-done.md) special rule affects all friendly units with the [Nehekharan Undead](../special-rules/nehekharan-undead.md) special rule within 6" of him, not just a unit he has joined.
