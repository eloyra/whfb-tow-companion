# Idol of Mork

Arcane Item 30 points

*On the eve of battle, Orc and Goblin Shamans often craft small idols of Mork, hoping that the most cunning (but brutal) of deities will watch over them.*

The bearer of the Idol of Mork increases their Dispel range by 3". Additionally, once per turn, when attempting a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md), the bearer of the Idol of Mork may re-roll the [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md).
