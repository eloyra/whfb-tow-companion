# Diabolic Splendour

Ability 35 points

*The air around the Champion seethes with chaotic energy, obscuring them from the arrows of the enemy.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. Any enemy model that targets this character or any unit they have joined during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.
