# Sun Standard of Chotec

Magic Standard 40 points

*Beams of reflected sunlight blind all who look too long upon the Sun Standard of Chotec.*

Enemy units cannot declare a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction against a unit carrying the Sun Standard of Chotec. In addition, any enemy model that targets a unit carrying this banner during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.
