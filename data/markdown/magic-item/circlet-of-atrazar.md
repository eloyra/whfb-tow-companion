# Circlet of Atrazar

Talisman 55 points

*This gem-studded circlet radiates an aura that protects its wearer from harm.*

The wearer of the Circlet of Atrazar has +1 Wound on their profile. In addition, if the bearer's troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md), they have a +1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic.
