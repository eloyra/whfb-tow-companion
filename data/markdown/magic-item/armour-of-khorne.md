# Armour of Khorne

Ability 40 points

*Clad in rune-laden armour, the Daemon's hide is all but immune to the blows of mere mortals.*

The Armour of Khorne is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
