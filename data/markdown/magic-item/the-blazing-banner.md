# The Blazing Banner

Magic Standard 25 points

*An eternal flame flickers about the weapons of those that march beneath the Blazing Banner.*

A unit carrying the Blazing Banner gains the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
