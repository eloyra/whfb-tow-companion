# Scrolls of Wei-jin

Arcane Item 10 points

*Those fortunate enough to study the Elemental Winds in the heart of the Celestial City learn many arcane secrets. Such scholars carry with them many scrolls, heavy with knowledge.*

The bearer of the Scrolls of Wei-jin knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md). However, they can only cast a number of spells equal to their Level of Wizardry per turn.
