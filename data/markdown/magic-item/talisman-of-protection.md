# Talisman of Protection

Talisman 30 points

*A powerful magical aura surrounds this humble talisman, protecting its wearer from harm.*

The Talisman of Protection gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
