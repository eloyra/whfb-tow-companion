# Sigil of the Mage Queen

Arcane Item 25 points

*Upon the eve of battle, Ariel, beloved Queen of Athel Loren, infuses arcane vessels with a measure of her power. These tokens she gifts to her most favoured mages.*

Single Use. The bearer of the Sigil of the Mage Queen may use it before making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md). If they do, they may apply a +2 modifier to that Casting roll.
