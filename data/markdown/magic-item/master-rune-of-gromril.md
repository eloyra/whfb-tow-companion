# Master Rune of Gromril

Magic Armour 45 points

*In its purest form, Gromril holds runes better than any other substance in all the world.*

A model wearing armour inscribed with the Master Rune of Gromril has an [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) of 2+, which cannot be improved in any way.
