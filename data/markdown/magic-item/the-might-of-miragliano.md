# The Might of Miragliano

Ability 25 points

*Tilea is famous for its mercenary companies led by skilled commanders of great courage and repute.*

A model with the Might of Miragliano Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic.
- Gains the [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) and [Tilean Stoicism](../special-rules/tilean-stoicism.md) special rules.
