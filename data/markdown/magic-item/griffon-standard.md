# Griffon Standard

Magic Standard 50 points

*A unit flying the Griffon Standard resolutely stands its ground, and few foes can break them.*

When determining your combat result, a unit carrying the Griffon Standard can claim a [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md) of +2 for each extra rank behind the first, rather than the usual +1.
