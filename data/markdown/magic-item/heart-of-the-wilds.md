# Heart of the Wilds

Arcane Item 40 points

*Gifted to Damsel Elliea long ago by the Wood Elves of Athel Loren, this thrumming, mossy stone flows with magical power, drawing upon the strength of nature to fuel its wielder's arcane efforts.*

The bearer of the Heart of the Wilds may apply a +1 modifier to any of their [Casting rolls](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) whilst within any 'natural' terrain feature. For the purposes of this rule, a 'natural' terrain feature includes any woods, or any [difficult](../battlefield-terrain/difficult-terrain.md) or [dangerous terrain](../battlefield-terrain/dangerous-terrain.md). It does not include any constructions, such as walls or buildings. Before the game starts, the players should agree upon which terrain features are natural and which are not.
