# Learned Feng Shi Bo\*

Arcane Item 15 points

*Some Wizards create Feng Shi Bo to aid them with their scholarly studies, tasking the spirits with remembering obscure lore.*

The owner of a Learned Feng Shi Bo knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md).

*Note that this does not increase the Wizard's Level.*
