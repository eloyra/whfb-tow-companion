# Staff of Baduumm

Arcane Item 55 points

*Once carried by the famous Orc Shaman Baduumm, this charred wooden staff still contains large reserves of the raw Waaagh! power that evaporated its namesake.*

The bearer of the Staff of Baduumm applies a +D3 modifier to the result of any [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) they make. However, if they roll a natural double 1 or double 6 when making a Casting roll, centre a 5" blast template over the bearer. Every model (friend or foe) whose base lies underneath the template risks being hit and suffering a single Strength 6 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -1. The staff is then destroyed and cannot be used again.

*Note that this item's effects are in addition to the effects of rolling a natural double 1 or double 6 when making a Casting roll.*
