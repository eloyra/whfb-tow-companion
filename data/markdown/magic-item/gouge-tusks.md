# Gouge-tusks

Ability 30 points

*This Beastman's lower incisors have grown into sharp or excessively large tusks with which they gouge savage wounds into the flesh of their enemies.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of any weapon used by this model (but not its mount) is improved by 1.
