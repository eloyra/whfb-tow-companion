# The Wandering Diestro

Ability 20 points

*Many a skilled duellist of noble birth has been driven from their home due to intrigue and treachery.*

A model with the Wandering Diestro Infamous Origin:

- Has a +2 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic.
- Gains the [Feint & Dodge](../special-rules/feint-and-dodge.md) special rule.
- May not take any weapons with the [Strike Last](../special-rules/strike-last.md) special rule.
- May take an [additional hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/two-hand-weapons-additional-hand-weapon.md) (+3 points)
