# Luckstone\*

Talisman 15 points

*Can luck be trapped within a dull stone pendant? Possibly.*

Single use. The bearer of a Luckstone can re-roll a single failed [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll.
