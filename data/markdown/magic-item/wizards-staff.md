# Wizard's Staff\*

Arcane Item 20 points

*Throughout the Old World, Wizards carry ensorcelled staffs through which they can channel and direct their powers.*

0-1 per Wizard. The bearer of the Wizard's Staff gains a +1 modifier to their [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) when attempting to cast an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell or a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md).
