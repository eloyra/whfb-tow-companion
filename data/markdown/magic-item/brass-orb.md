# Brass Orb

Enchanted Item 50 points

*This fist-sized orb of whirling cogs is capable of opening a crack into the twisting Realm of Chaos.*

Single use. Instead of attacking normally during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), the bearer of the Brass Orb can toss it at a specific model within a unit they are engaged in combat with, such as a champion or a character. Make a single roll To Hit. If successful, the target model must make an Initiative test. If this test is failed, the target model is removed from play as a casualty.
