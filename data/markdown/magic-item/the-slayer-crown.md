# The Slayer Crown

Enchanted Item

*This majestic helmet has been worn by every Slayer King since the time of King Baragor.*

The wearer of the Slayer Crown improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 2. In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
