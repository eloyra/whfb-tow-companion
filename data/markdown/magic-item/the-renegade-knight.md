# The Renegade Knight

Ability 25 points

*High born and haughty, renegade knights are often disdainful of lesser warriors.*

A model with the Renegade Knight Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Weapon Skill and Strength characteristics.
- May purchase magic items from the [Kingdom of Bretonnia magic item](../warhammer-armies/kingdom-of-bretonnia-army-list.md) lists in *Forces of Fantasy* and *Arcane Journal - Kingdom of Bretonnia*.
- Gains the [Noble Disdain](../special-rules/noble-disdain.md) and [Veteran](../special-rules/veteran.md) special rules.
