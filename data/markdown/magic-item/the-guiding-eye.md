# The Guiding Eye

Enchanted Item 25 points

*Set in black iron, this oval ruby grants mystical sight to the wearer.*

Single use. The bearer of the Guiding Eye and any unit they have joined may re-roll any failed rolls To Hit made during the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase).
