# The Griffon Helm

Magic Armour

*Crafted for him after a crushing victory against the Orcs of the Bloody Tusk tribe on the borders of Reikland, the Griffon Helm has shielded Hans from many a mortal blow.*

The Griffon Helm gives Hans a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wound suffered and makes him immune to the [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) special rule. If Hans would be struck by a Killing Blow, he is permitted an [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) save as normal. If the wound is unsaved, he loses a single Wound.
