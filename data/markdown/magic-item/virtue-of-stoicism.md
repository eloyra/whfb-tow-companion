# Virtue of Stoicism

Ability 55 points

*Awe inspiring are the tales that tell of grim Lambard, standing alone against evil hordes to save his dukedom.*

A model with this Virtue (and its unit) may re-roll the 2D6 when making a [Break test](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md).
