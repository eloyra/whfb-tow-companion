# Obsidian Lodestone\*

Talisman 20 points

*An air of chill hangs about obsidian, stilling the Winds of Magic in its presence.*

A model may purchase up to three Obsidian Lodestones. A model that bears a single Obsidian Lodestone has [Magic Resistance (-1)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md), a model that bears two has Magic Resistance (-2), and a model that bears three has Magic Resistance (-3).

[\*Extremely Common](../magic-items/extremely-common-magic-items.md)
