# Blood of the Shadowgave\*

Arcane Item 35 points

*When shattered upon the ground, clay jars of Morghur's blood boil and spit, birthing mewling beastlings that consume raw magic from the very air, before fading back into the cursed soil.*

Single use. A Wizard may use this item instead of making a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md) attempt. If they do so, the spell is automatically dispelled with no [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) required.

*Note that a*  [perfect invocation](../magic/perfect-invocations.md)  *cannot be dispelled.*
