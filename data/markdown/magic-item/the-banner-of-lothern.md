# The Banner of Lothern

Magic Standard 55 points

*The Banner of Lothern is borne aloft by those that exemplify the martial spirit of the Sea Guard.*

If a unit carrying the Banner of Lothern is equipped with [thrusting spears](../weapons-of-war/thrusting-spear.md), half of the models in the third rank or file i.e., the rank or file directly behind the one normally able to make supporting attacks), rounding up, can make supporting attacks.
