# Anointed Armour

Magic Armour 45 points

*Whilst the wearer's faith in the Lady of the Lake holds fast, so too shall the wards of protection placed upon this armour.*

Models with the [Grail Vow](../special-rules/the-grail-vow.md) whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The Anointed Armour is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, as long as the wearer has the [Blessing of the Lady](../special-rules/blessings-of-the-lady.md), they are immune to the [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) and [Multiple Wounds (X)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rules. If the wearer suffers an unsaved wound from an attack with either of these special rules, they lose a single Wound. Should the wearer ever lose the Blessing of the Lady, this effect is lost.
