# Flying Carpet

Enchanted Item 40 points

*Woven from the finest silks in a far-off land, this splendid rug does more than warm the feet!*

Models whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) or [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) only. The bearer of the Flying Carpet gains the [Fly (8)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules. However, the bearer cannot join a unit.
