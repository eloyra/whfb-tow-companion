# Warden of Saphery

Ability 10 points

*When Ulthuan marches to war, the Swordmasters of Hoeth are led by the Wardens of Saphery, exemplars of the martial arts who have studied warfare and personal combat for decades.*

A character with the Warden of Saphery Elven Honour:

- Cannot be mounted.
- May be equipped with a [sword of Hoeth](../weapons-of-war/sword-of-hoeth.md) for no additional points. If so, they may not take any additional, non-magical weapons.
- Gains the [Deflect Shots](../special-rules/deflect-shots.md), [Ithilmar Armour](../special-rules/ithilmar-armour-ithilmar-barding.md) and [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) special rules.
