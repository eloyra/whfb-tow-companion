# Aspect of the Bear

Ability 15 points

*Elves are usually lithe and slight of build, but those who adopt the aspect of the bear are strong and muscular warriors.*

[Infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. A character who adopts the Aspect of the Bear increases their Strength and Toughness characteristics by 1.
