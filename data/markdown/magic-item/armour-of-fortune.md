# Armour of Fortune

Magic Armour 45 points

*Engraved with runes of luck and providence, the Armour of Fortune turns aside fatal blows harmlessly.*

The Armour of Fortune is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and is immune to the Killing Blow special rule. If the wearer is struck a [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md), they are permitted [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves as normal. If the wound is unsaved, they lose a single Wound.
