# Standard of Seeping Decay

Magic Standard 35 points

*A pervasive aura of pestilence surrounds this moth-eaten banner, sapping the health of all around it.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase), a unit carrying the Standard of Seeping Decay may re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 1.
