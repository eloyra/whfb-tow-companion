# Opal Amulet\*

Talisman 20 points

*Potent yet fragile, an Opal Amulet offers powerful protection, but can easily be broken.*

Single use. An Opal Amulet gives its bearer a 2+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against a single wound.
