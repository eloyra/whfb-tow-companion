# Totem of Wrath

Magic Standard 50 points

*Cruel runes etched into the surface of this icon tremble with power, causing those around it to succumb to an animalistic ferocity.*

During a turn in which it charged, models within a unit carrying the Totem of Wrath improve the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of their weapons by 1, and may re-roll any rolls To Wound of a natural 1.
