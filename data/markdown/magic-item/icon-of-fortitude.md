# Icon of Fortitude

Talisman 35 points

*Carefully woven with protective enchantments, this exquisite brooch is said to shield its bearer from the deadliest blows.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The bearer is immune to the [Multiple Wounds (X)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule. If the bearer is wounded by an attack with this special rule, they suffer a single wound.
