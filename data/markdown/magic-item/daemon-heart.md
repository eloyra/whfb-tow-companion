# Daemon Heart

Arcane Item 30 points

*Pulled still beating from the chest of a shadow-skinned Daemon, this gruesome artefact must constantly consume magic lest it fade to dust.*

When attempting a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md), the bearer of the Daemon Heart counts their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md) as being one higher than that of the Wizard whose spell they are dispelling or, in the case of Bound Spells, one higher than that spell's Power Level.

*Note that this applies even if it would mean the bearer counts their*  *Level of Wizardry*  *as being lower than it actually is.*
