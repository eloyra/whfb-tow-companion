# Banner of Confidence

Magic Standard 20 points

*This silken standard fills the hearts of those that fight beneath it with confidence.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Confidence does not suffer the usual -1 To Hit modifier when making a [Stand & Shoot](../the-movement-phase/stand-and-shoot.md) charge reaction.
