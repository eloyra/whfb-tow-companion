# The Accursed Armour

Magic Armour 30 points

*Some strange flaw renders the wearer of this armour resilient beyond measure, yet robs them of dexterity.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The Accursed Armour is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a +1 modifier to their Toughness characteristic, but suffers a -1 modifier to their Weapon Skill and Initiative characteristics (to a minimum of 1).
