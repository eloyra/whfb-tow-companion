# The Golden Shield

Magic Armour 30 points

*A highly polished shield inlaid with swirling patterns, the Golden Shield dazzles the foe.*

The Golden Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, any enemy model that directs its attacks against its bearer during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) must re-roll any rolls To Hit of a natural 6.
