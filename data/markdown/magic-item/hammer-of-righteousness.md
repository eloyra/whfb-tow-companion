# Hammer of Righteousness

Magic Weapon 50 points

*This mighty hammer not only crushes flesh and bone but shatters evil with each swing.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *Models hit by the Hammer of Righteousness must make a*  [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md)  *for each hit suffered. If the test is failed, the hit wounds automatically and no*  [Armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md)  *is allowed (* [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md)  *and*  [Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md)  *may be taken as normal). If the test is passed, rolls To Wound and Armour saves are made as normal.*
