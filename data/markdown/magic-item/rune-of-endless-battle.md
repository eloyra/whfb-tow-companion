# Rune of Endless Battle

Ability 40 points

*Those who bear this tattoo struggle to contain their rage once battle begins.*

During the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) of any turn in which a model with this runic tattoo charged, every attack it makes that causes an unsaved wound allows it to immediately make one additional attack.

*Note that any additional attacks that cause unsaved wounds and any unsaved wounds caused by the*  [Impact Hits (X)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md)  *special rule do not benefit from this special rule.*
