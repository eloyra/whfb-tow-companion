# Spellshield\*

Magic Armour 25 points

*The Winds of Magic lose their power when in the presence of the brass-bound Spellshield.*

The Spellshield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md), or an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell.

[\*Extremely Common](../magic-items/extremely-common-magic-items.md)
