# Twin-Tailed Wand

Arcane Item 40 points

*Though its origin is unknown, this impractical wand allows its bearer to unleash a torrent of magical force.*

Once per turn, the bearer of the Twin-tailed Wand may attempt to cast one of their spells a second time. However, should they [miscast](../magic/miscast.md) a spell, the bearer is overwhelmed by the Winds of Magic and, instead of rolling on the [Miscast table](../magic/miscast-table.md), suffers D3 wounds with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) can be attempted as normal).
