# The Conqueror's Blade

Magic Weapon 55 points

*Many a king and queen, warlord and emperor foolish enough to resist the rule of the Lord of Nehekhara have found their heads parted from their bodies by this wicked weapon.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *Whilst engaged in a challenge, the bearer of the Conqueror's Blade strikes a*  [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md)  *if they roll a natural 5 or 6 when making a To Wound roll, rather than the usual 6. In addition, should the wielder slay the enemy*  [General](../characters/the-general-characters.md)  *in a challenge, you win a bonus of 100*  [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md)  *at the end of the game.*
