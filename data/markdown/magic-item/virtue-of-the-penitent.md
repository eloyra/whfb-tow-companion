# Virtue of the Penitent

Ability 50 points

*A penitent hermit-knight, Duke Corduin set aside his vows of solitude when summoned to fight for his king.*

Duke, Baron or Paladin only. A character with this Virtue gains the [Unbreakable](../special-rules/unbreakable.md) special rule. However, this character cannot join a friendly unit.
