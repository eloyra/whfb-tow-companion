# Necklace of Blessed Teef

Enchanted Item 50 points

*Plucked from the jaws of enemies and fashioned into crude jewellery, the teef that hang from this necklace have been blessed by Mork (or possibly Gork) to protect the wearer as they thunder into battle.*

The bearer of the Necklace of Blessed Teef may re-roll any [Armour Save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) roll, [Ward Save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) roll or [Regeneration Save](../special-rules/regeneration.md) roll of a natural 1.
