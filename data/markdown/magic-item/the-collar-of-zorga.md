# The Collar of Zorga

Talisman 20 points

*Zorga was a respected War Boar wrangler. When his magical collar was stolen, he became a snack for his porcine pets.*

Any enemy 'beast of burden' that directs its attacks against the wearer of the Collar of Zorga or their unit during the Combat phase suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit. For the purposes of this rule, a beast of burden is the mount of any model whose troop type is [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md), the beast(s) that draw any model whose troop type is [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md), and the mount of any mounted character whose troop type is [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md).
