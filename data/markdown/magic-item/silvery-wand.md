# Silvery Wand

Arcane Item 15 points

*This slender wand is covered with many intricately carved lines of winding runes in an ancient script.*

The bearer of the Silvery Wand knows one more spell (chosen in the usual way) than is normal for their [Level of Wizardry](../magic/levels-of-wizardry.md).

*Note that this does not increase the Wizard's Level.*
