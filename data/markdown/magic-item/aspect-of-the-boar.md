# Aspect of the Boar

Ability 10 points

*The kindred of the boar are aggressive fighters who charge into the foe with reckless abandon.*

A character who adopts the Aspect of the Boar improves the [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristics of its weapon(s) by 1 during a turn in which it charged and gains the [Impact Hits (1)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) special rule.
