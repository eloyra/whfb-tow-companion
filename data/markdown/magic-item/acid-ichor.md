# Acid Ichor

Ability 15 points

*The Champion's blood is a boiling, acidic ichor. Those who would harm them find themselves splashed with hissing, burning liquid.*

For every Wound this character loses during a challenge, their enemy suffers a Strength 4 hit, with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
