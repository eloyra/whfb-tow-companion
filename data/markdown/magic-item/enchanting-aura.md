# Enchanting Aura

Ability 35 points

*An enchanting aura hangs about the Champion, ensnaring the mind and slowing the limbs of enemies that venture too close.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. Enemy units engaged in combat with this character cannot use the [Strike First](../special-rules/strike-first.md) special rule. Enemy models that do not have the Strike First special rule become subject to the [Strike Last](../special-rules/strike-last.md) special rule instead.
