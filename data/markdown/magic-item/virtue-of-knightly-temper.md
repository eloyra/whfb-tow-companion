# Virtue of Knightly Temper

Ability 70 points

*With a heart of stone and resolute temper Beren stood unbowed, each sweep of blade spilling the blood of many.*

During a turn in which it charged, a model with this Virtue gains the [Extra Attacks (+D3)](../special-rules/extra-attacks.md) and [Hatred (all enemies)](../special-rules/hatred.md) special rules.
