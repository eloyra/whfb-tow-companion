# Crusader's Tapestry

Magic Standard 40 points

*Those that fight beneath this silken banner are inspired to ever greater acts of heroism, hoping that maybe their own exploits will be woven into its rich history.*

The Crusader's Tapestry may only be taken by a model in an [Errantry Crusade](../warhammer-armies/errantry-crusade.md) Army of Infamy. A unit carrying the Crusader's Tapestry gains the [Frenzy](../special-rules/frenzy.md) special rule.
