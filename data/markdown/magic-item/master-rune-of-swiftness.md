# Master Rune of Swiftness

Magic Weapon 25 points

*This rune was first struck by Thurgrom the Hermit, the last runesmith to study Elven crafts.*

The wielder of a weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Swiftness gains the [Strike First](../special-rules/strike-first.md) special rule.
