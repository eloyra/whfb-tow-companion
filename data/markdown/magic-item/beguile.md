# Beguile

Ability 40 points

*The Vampire has an entrancing gaze that mesmerises their foes, rendering them all but incapable of causing the Vampire harm.*

Enemy units must make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) before making any rolls To Hit against this Vampire during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase). If this test is failed, only rolls of a natural 6 will hit.
