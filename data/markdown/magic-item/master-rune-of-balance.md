# Master Rune of Balance

Talisman 35 points

*Forged in the glowing embers of a burning book of spells, this rune hungers after the Winds of Magic.*

Anvil of Doom and Dwarf Runesmiths only. Once per turn, the bearer of the Master Rune of Balance may use it when attempting a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md). If they do so, roll an extra D6 when making the [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md) and discard the lowest result.
