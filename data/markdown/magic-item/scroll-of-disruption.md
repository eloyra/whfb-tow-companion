# Scroll of Disruption

Arcane Item 30 points

*When spoken aloud, the words upon this ancient scroll cause the Winds of Magic to bluster and the spells of the enemy to unravel.*

Once per turn, the bearer of the Scroll of Disruption may re-roll a [Dispel roll](../magic/dispel-roll-and-dispel-result.md).

*\*Note all dice are re-rolled, including any bonus or discard dice granted by special rules or magic items.*
