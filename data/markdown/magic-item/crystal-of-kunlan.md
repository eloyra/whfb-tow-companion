# Crystal of Kunlan

Talisman 35 points

*The crystals that grow in the peaks of Kunlan unleash sorcerous energy when struck.*

The Crystal of Kunlan gives its bearer a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. In addition, if a natural 6 is rolled when making the Ward save, every enemy model in base contact with the bearer suffers a Strength 3 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -. These attacks have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
