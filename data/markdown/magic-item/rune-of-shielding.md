# Rune of Shielding\*

Magic Armour 15 points

*In ages past, entire regiments would march to war bearing shields struck with the Runes of Shielding.*

A model wearing armour inscribed with a single Rune of Shielding has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered. For each additional Rune of Shielding, this Ward save is improved by 1.
