# Warpstone Armour\*

Magic Armour 25 points

*Cunningly wrought of warpstone-infused iron, this heavy suit of armour protects without stifling the Winds of Magic.*

The Warpstone Armour is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md) which may be purchased and worn by a Wizard, even if they do not have the option to be equipped with armour, a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md) or [barding](../weapons-of-war/barding.md), without penalty.
