# Cautious Shield

Magic Armour 20 points

*This large, magical shield emphasises an already prominent trait of Skaven nature.*

The Cautious Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer may choose to wield it with two hands when their combat is chosen during Step 1.1 of any [Choose & Fight Combat](../the-combat-phase/choose-and-fight-combat.md) sub-phase. If they do, until the end of this [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) it improves its [armour save](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) by 3, but their Attacks characteristic is reduced to 0.
