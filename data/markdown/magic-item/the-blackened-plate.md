# The Blackened Plate

Magic Armour 25 points

*This battered suit of plate mail is blackened with the soot of burning palaces and temples.*

The Blackened Plate is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 3+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rule.
