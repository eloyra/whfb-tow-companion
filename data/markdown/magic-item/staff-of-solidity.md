# Staff of Solidity

Arcane Item 15 points

*The Staff of Solidity absorbs and dissipates malign energy, protecting its wielder from harm.*

Single use. Once per game, when the bearer of the Staff of Solidity is required to roll on the [Miscast table](../magic/miscast-table.md), they may choose not to.
