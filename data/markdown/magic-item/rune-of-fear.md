# Rune of Fear

Magic Standard 30 points

*Those that carry this banner to battle seem to tower over the enemy, looming like mythical giants.*

A unit carrying a standard inscribed with the Rune of Fear gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule.
