# Blade of Antarhak

Magic Weapon 45 points

*Those cut by the Blade of Antarhak age by decades in the blink of an eye, their vitality stolen and bestowed upon the wielder of this fell blade.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+1 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *The Blade of Antarhak may only be taken by models in a*  [Nehekharan Royal Host](../warhammer-armies/nehekharan-royal-host.md)  *Army of Infamy. For each Wound an enemy unit loses as a result of an attack made with the Blade of Antarhak, its wielder immediately recovers a single lost Wound.*
