# Imperial Banner

Magic Standard 60 points

*This fine silken standard is a proud reminder of a time when the Empire was unified as one.*

All friendly units within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of the model carrying the Imperial Banner roll 3D6 when making a [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) or [Terror test](../special-rules/terror.md) and discard the highest result.
