# The Noble Outlaw

Ability 20 points

*The Border Princes are filled with ruthless outlaws and cutthroats that claim to be of noble birth.*

A model with the Noble Outlaw Infamous Origin:

- Has a +1 modifier to their Ballistic Skill and Leadership characteristics.
- Gains the ["Hold the Line"](../special-rules/hold-the-line.md) special rule.
- May purchase magic items from the [Empire of Man magic item lists](../magic-items/empire-of-man-magic-items.md) in *Forces of Fantasy* and *Arcane Journal - Empire of Man*.
- May take one of the following:

  - [Handgun](../weapons-of-war/handgun.md) (+6 points)
  - [Repeater handgun](../weapons-of-war/repeater-handgun.md) (+8 points)
  - [Repeater pistol](../weapons-of-war/repeater-pistol.md) (+8 points)
  - [Shortbow](../weapons-of-war/shortbow.md) (+2 points)
  - [Longbow](../weapons-of-war/longbow.md) (+4 points)
- May take one of the following special rules:

  - [Ambushers](../special-rules/ambushers.md) (+10 points)
  - [Scouts](../special-rules/scouts.md) (+10 points)
