# Jaguar Standard

Magic Standard 20 points

*Those in the shadow of this jungle beast hunt down their enemies with relentless ferocity.*

When a unit carrying the Jaguar Standard makes a [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll, it may roll an extra D6 and discard the lowest result.
