# Enchanted Shield\*

Magic Armour 10 points

*A finely crafted and sturdy shield that protects its bearer from harm time and time again.*

The Enchanted Shield is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, its bearer has a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.
