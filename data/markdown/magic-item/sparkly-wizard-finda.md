# Sparkly Wizard Finda

Talisman 45 points

The bearer of the Sparkly Wizard Finda has the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) and [Hatred (enemy Wizards)](../special-rules/hatred.md) special rules.
