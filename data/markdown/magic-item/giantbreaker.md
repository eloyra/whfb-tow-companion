# Giantbreaker

Ability 20 points

*Any Ogre that has defeated a Giant in hand-to-hand combat can be, justifiably, supremely confident in their abilities.*

This character (but not their mount) has a +1 modifier to their Strength characteristic. However, this character cannot refuse a [challenge](../characters/challenges.md) and neither they, nor any unit they have joined, can [Flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) as a charge reaction.
