# Aspect of the Cat

Ability 10 points

*Hunting from the shadows, the kindred of the cat are experts at hunting down prized game.*

A character who adopts the Aspect of the Cat gains the [Armour Bane (1)](../special-rules/armour-bane.md) special rule and may re-roll any failed rolls To Hit during a [challenge](../characters/challenges.md).
