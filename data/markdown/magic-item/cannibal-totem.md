# Cannibal Totem

Magic Standard 30 points

*The Cannibal Totem protects those who devour the strong in honour of the Great Maw.*

A unit carrying the Cannibal Totem gains the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule, but rolls only a single D6 when making a [Pursuit](../the-combat-phase/pursuit.md) roll (rather than the usual 2D6).
