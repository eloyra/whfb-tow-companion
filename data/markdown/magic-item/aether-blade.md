# Æther Blade

Ability 55 points

*The Daemon's weapon flickers in and out of reality, slipping through armour and into the soft flesh beneath.*

No [armour saves](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) are permitted against wounds caused by this Daemon's [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md) ([Ward](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves can be attempted as normal).
