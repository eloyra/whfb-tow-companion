# Ring of Jet

Enchanted Item 30 points

*Carved from purest jet quarried from the banks of the Dragon River, the Ring of Jet is infused with the power of Shiyama, allowing its wielder to summon ancestral spirits.*

The wielder of the Ring of Jet can cast the [Unquiet Spirits](../spell/unquiet-spirits.md) spell from the Lore of [Necromancy](../the-lores-of-magic/necromancy.md) as a [Bound Spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), with a Power Level of 1.
