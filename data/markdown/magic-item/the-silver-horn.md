# The Silver Horn

Enchanted Item 15 points

*The triumphant call of this elegant hunting horn lends speed to the hooves of even the weariest steed.*

Characters with the [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rule only. The bearer of the Silver Horn (and any unit they have joined) may re-roll the D6 when using the Swiftstride special rule.
