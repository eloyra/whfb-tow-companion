# Blight-Tipped Arrows\*

Enchanted Item 15 points

*Coated in virulent toxins harvested from the depths of the Wild Wood, blight-tipped arrows need only nick the flesh to bring about a slow and painful death.*

Single use. Models with [Asrai longbow](../weapons-of-war/asrai-longbow.md) only. A Blight-Tipped Arrow can be fired as normal from the bearer's Asrai longbow. If an enemy model suffers an unsaved wound from a Blight-Tipped Arrow, at the beginning of each subsequent [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase that model must make a Toughness test. If the test is failed, the model immediately suffers a single wound with no [armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md) or [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves permitted ([Ward saves](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) may be attempted as normal).
