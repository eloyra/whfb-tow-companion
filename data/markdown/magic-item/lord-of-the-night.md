# Lord of the Night

Ability 15 points

*The Vampire howls into the cold night, their cry answered by the baying of countless dark creatures.*

During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, this Vampire may attempt to resurrect fallen creatures of the night by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own Leadership). If this test is passed, a single friendly unit of Fell Bats, Bat Swarms or Dire Wolves that is within 12" of this model recovers D6 lost Wounds.
