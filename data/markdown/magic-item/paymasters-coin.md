# Paymaster's Coin\*

Talisman 25 points

*This heavy golden coin may not be enchanted, but its weight in its owner's pocket certainly inspires heroism.*

Single use. The bearer of a Paymaster's Coin can re-roll any failed rolls To Hit made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase).
