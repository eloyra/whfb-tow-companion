# Banner of Arcane Protection

Magic Standard 70 points

*The heavy fabric of this banner is interwoven with powerful protective charms.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Arcane Protection gains the [Magic Resistance (-3)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule. In addition, friendly units within 6" of the model carrying this standard gain the Magic Resistance (-1) special rule.
