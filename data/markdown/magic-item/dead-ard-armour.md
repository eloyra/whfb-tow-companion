# Dead 'Ard Armour

Magic Armour 35 points

*This battle-scarred armour is made up of thick armour plates capable of turning aside the most telling of blows.*

Black Orc Bosses and Orc Bosses whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md), [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) or [chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/chariots.md) only. The Dead 'Ard Armour is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, its wearer improves their Toughness characteristic by 1.
