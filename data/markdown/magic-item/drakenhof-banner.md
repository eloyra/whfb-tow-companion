# Drakenhof Banner

Magic Standard 50 points

*The infamous Count Vlad von Carstein had thrall Necromancers enchant his household standard to sustain his bodyguard.*

If a unit carrying the Drakenhof Banner has the [Regeneration (X+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule, it improves the armour value of its Regeneration save by 1.
