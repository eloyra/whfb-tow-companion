# Sun & Moon Blades

Magic Weapon 20 points

*According to legend, the blade of the Sun brings enlightenment, whilst the blade of the Moon brings only death.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *If the wielder of the Sun & Moon Blades is struck a*  [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) *, roll a D6. On a roll of 4+, the Killing Blow is parried and the Killing Blow is discarded with no further effect.*
