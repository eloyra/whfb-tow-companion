# Blade of Silvered Steel

Magic Weapon 30 points

*Those who wield this blessed weapon will hunt the dead and the damned with unmatched zeal.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+1 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *The Blade of Silvered Steel may only be taken by a model in a*  [Knightly Order](../warhammer-armies/knightly-order.md)  *Army of Infamy. Undead models cannot make*  [Armour](../the-combat-phase/make-armour-saves-combat.md)  *or*  [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md)  *saves against a wound caused by this weapon.*
