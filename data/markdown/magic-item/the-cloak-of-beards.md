# The Cloak of Beards

Enchanted Item 30 points

*An aura of fear and anger hangs about this grisly trophy of the War of the Beard, filling allies and enemies alike with unease.*

The wearer of the Cloak of Beards causes [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md). However, so unnerving is the cloak that other models cannot use the wearer's Leadership.
