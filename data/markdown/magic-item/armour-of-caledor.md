# Armour of Caledor

Magic Armour 35 points

*Every piece of the Armour of Caledor has been taken from the recovered fragments of now-lost armour that was once worn by a mighty Caledorian hero.*

The Armour of Caledor is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
