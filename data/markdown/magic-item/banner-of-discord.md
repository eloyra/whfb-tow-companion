# Banner of Discord

Magic Standard 60 points

*The air around this warped banner shimmers with the explosive unbinding of enemy spells.*

A unit carrying the Banner of Discord gains the [Magic Resistance (-3)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule. In addition, friendly units within 6" of the model carrying this standard gain the Magic Resistance (-1) special rule.
