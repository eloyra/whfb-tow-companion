# Virtue of Purity

Ability 30 points

*Chaste and untainted by base emotions, Duke Rademund was the Lady's most virtuous champion.*

A model with this Virtue always benefits from the [Blessings of the Lady](../special-rules/blessings-of-the-lady.md) special rule and always has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md), even if this model's army did not pray at the start of the game.
