# Crusader's Lance

Magic Weapon 60 points

*In the hands of young Bretonnian heroes throughout the kingdom's many errantry wars, this keen lance has struck down scores of Orcs, Goblins and Beastmen.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *The Crusader's Lance may only be taken by a model in an*  [Errantry Crusade](../warhammer-armies/errantry-crusade.md)  *Army of Infamy whose troop type is*  [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md)  *or*  [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md)  *only. The Crusader's Lance can only be used during a turn in which the wielder charged. In subsequent turns (or if the wielder did not charge), the model must use its hand weapon instead.*
