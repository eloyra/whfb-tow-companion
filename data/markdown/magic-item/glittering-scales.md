# Glittering Scales

Magic Armour 35 points

*Each and every scale of this armoured surcoat is crafted from shards of precious metal.*

The Glittering Scales is a suit of [light armour](../weapons-of-war/light-armour.md). In addition, once per turn, you may make your opponent re-roll a single roll To Hit made against the wearer.
