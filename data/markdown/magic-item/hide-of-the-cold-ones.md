# Hide of the Cold Ones

Magic Armour 20 points

*The hide of a Cold One exudes a toxic slime that dulls the wearer's mind.*

May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Hide of the Cold Ones improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). However, its wearer is also subject to the [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md) special rule.
