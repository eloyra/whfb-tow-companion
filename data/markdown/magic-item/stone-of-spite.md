# Stone of Spite

Enchanted Item 45 points

*Contained within this glowing stone is the soul of an Ungor, slain in a hideous ritual. When crushed underhoof, a wave of bitterness and spite is released, banishing the Winds of Magic.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, this character may use the Stone of Spite. Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, all Wizards (friend or foe) suffer a -2 modifier to their Casting rolls.
