# Golden Lion

Enchanted Item 25 points

*Jade Lions are revered in Cathay for their bravery, and golden amulets carved into their likeness inspire great courage.*

The bearer of the Golden Lion and their unit may re-roll any failed [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md), [Panic](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md), [Rally](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md) or [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) test.
