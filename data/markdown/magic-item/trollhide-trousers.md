# Trollhide Trousers\*

Magic Armour 40 points

*Foul smelling and uncomfortable, Trollhide Trousers possess remarkable regenerative properties.*

May be worn with other armour. The wearer of the Trollhide Trousers improves their [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1 (to a maximum of 2+). In addition, their wearer has the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) special rule.
