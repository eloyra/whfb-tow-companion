# Master Rune of Smiting

Magic Weapon 75 points

*The secret of this rune was preserved in the Rundrokikron, an ancient tome kept in the treasure troves of Karak Kadrin.*

A weapon inscribed with the Master Rune of Smiting gains the [Multiple Wounds (D6)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
