# Da Boss' Trophy Rack

Enchanted Item

*Grisly trinkets swing from Kiknik's trophy rack, striking fear into his enemies' hearts.*

During a turn in which he charged, Kiknik and any Goblin Wolf Rider Mob he has joined cause [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) and receive a bonus of +1 [combat result](../the-combat-phase/calculate-combat-result.md) point.
