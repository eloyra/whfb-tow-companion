# Trollhide Shawl

Talisman

*Though it stinks, this bedraggled garment of rotted Trollhide helps keep Ogdruz safe from harm.*

Ogdruz Swampdigga improves his [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 1. In addition, he has the [Regeneration (5+)](../special-rules/regeneration.md) and [Flammable](../special-rules/flammable.md) special rules.
