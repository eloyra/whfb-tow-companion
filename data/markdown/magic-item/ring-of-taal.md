# Ring of Taal

Enchanted Item 20 points

*By evoking the name of Taal, the primordial god of the wilderness, the wearer shields themselves in nature's grasp.*

Single use. The bearer of the Ring of Taal may cast the [Oaken Shield](../spell/oaken-shield.md) spell from the [Lore of Battle Magic](../the-lores-of-magic/battle-magic.md) as a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md) with a Power Level of 3.
