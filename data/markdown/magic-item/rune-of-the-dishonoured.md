# Rune of the Dishonoured

Ability 50 points

*This dreadful mark prolongs the shame of the one who bears it.*

Daemon Slayers and Dragon Slayers only. Once a model with this runic tattoo is reduced to their last Wound, they gain a 3+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any further wounds suffered. However, at the end of the battle, a model with this runic tattoo that has been slain is worth no Victory Points. If it is still alive, the enemy player wins a bonus number of [Victory Points](../warhammer-battles/victory-points-warhammer-battles.md) equal to 100% of its points cost.
