# Armour of Stars

Magic Armour 40 points

*Decorated with glittering gems set in deepest blue lacquer, the Armour of Stars is a wonder to behold.*

Models whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) or [cavalry](../troop-types-in-detail/cavalry.md) only. The Armour of Stars is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, the wearer is immune to the [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md) special rule. If the wearer is struck a Killing Blow, they are permitted armour and [Regeneration](../special-rules/regeneration.md) saves as normal. If the wound is unsaved, they lose a single Wound.
