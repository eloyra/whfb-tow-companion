# Gut Maw

Magic Armour 45 points

*The wearer of this polished gut-plate is reinvigorated by the life force of their defeated enemies.*

The Gut Maw is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, for each unsaved wound the wearer causes during a [Challenge](../characters/challenges.md), they recover a single lost Wound.
