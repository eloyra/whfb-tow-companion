# Aura of Quetzl

Talisman 40 points

*Blessed by Quetzl, this string of beaded shells, feathers and bones exudes a powerful protective aura.*

Any enemy model that directs its attacks against the bearer of the Aura of Quetzl during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit.
