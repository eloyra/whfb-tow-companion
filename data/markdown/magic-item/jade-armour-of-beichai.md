# Jade Armour of Beichai

Magic Armour 40 points

*Crafted in secret in the city of Beichai, this jade armour glows from within with unwholesome power.*

The Jade Armour of Beichai is a suit of [full plate armour](../weapons-of-war/full-plate-armour.md). In addition, the wearer of the Jade Armour has a 5+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered and gains the [Magic Resistance (-2)](../special-rules/magic-resistance.md) special rule.
