# Slayer's Hourglass

Talisman 10 points

*As the sand tumbles through the hourglass, the decapitating strikes and snapping jaws of monstrous beasts become sluggish and slow.*

Enemy models whose troop type is [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md) suffer a -1 modifier to their Weapon Skill characteristic whilst in base contact with the bearer of the Slayer's Hourglass.
