# Arch-Lightning Rod

Enchanted Item 40 points

*Crackling lightning fills the heavens with peril, grounding the creatures of the sky.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, if they are not engaged in combat, the bearer of the Arch-Lightning Rod may attempt to use it by making a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) (using their own, unmodified Leadership). If this test is passed, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase enemy units cannot use the [Fly (X)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule.
