# Glittering Wotnots

Arcane Item 40 points

*Supposedly made from the shards of a shattered magic mirror, these gaudy gems hung from a Shaman's staff will sometimes reflect hostile magic back upon its caster.*

If the bearer (or their unit) is the target of an enemy spell, they may use the Glittering Wotnots instead of making a [Wizardly dispel](../magic/wizardly-dispel.md) attempt. Roll a D6. On a roll of 1-3, the Glittering Wotnots have no effect and the spell is cast as normal. On a roll of 4+, the spell is reflected back upon the caster and they (and their unit) become the target of the spell. The caster may make a Wizardly dispel attempt.
