# 'Eadbuttin' 'At\*

Enchanted Item 15 points

*'Eadbuttin' contests are popular in Orc culture. Especially cunning (or brutal) participants employ magical means to improve their odds.*

0-1 per model. An 'Eadbuttin' 'At gives its wearer the [Impact Hits (1)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) special rule. This Impact Hit has an [Armour Piercing](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) characteristic of -2.
