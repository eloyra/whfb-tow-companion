# The Armour of the Warbird

Magic Armour 45 points

*The wearer of the Armour of the Warbird takes to the skies to strike at their enemies from above.*

Models whose troop type is [regular infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/regular-infantry.md) or [heavy infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/heavy-infantry.md) only. The Armour of the Warbird is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, the wearer gains the [Counter Charge](../special-rules/counter-charge.md), [Fly (9)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules. However, the bearer cannot join a unit.
