# Cloak of Feathers

Enchanted Item 40 points

*When the wearer of this finely woven cloak extends their arms, the feathers rise up like powerful wings, lifting them into the air.*

Skink Heroes whose troop type is [infantry](../troop-types-in-detail/infantry.md) only. The wearer of the Cloak of Feathers gains the [Fly (10)](../special-rules/fly.md) and [Swiftstride](../special-rules/swiftstride.md) special rules.
