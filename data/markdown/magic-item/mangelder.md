# Mangelder

Magic Weapon 40 points

*The Mangelder saps the strength of its victims' minds as well as of their bodies, and those few who survive wounds inflicted by the weapon rarely fight again.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+1 | -1 | - |

***Notes:***  *The wielder of the Mangelder causes*  [Terror](../special-rules/terror.md) *. Any enemy unit that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Mangelder suffers a -1 modifier to its Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2) until the end of the turn.*
