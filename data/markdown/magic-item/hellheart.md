# Hellheart

Arcane Item 20 points

*Biting into the heart of a Chaos Spawn, the Ogre creates a vortex of magical anarchy.*

Single use. During the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn, the bearer of the Hellheart may consume it. When they do so, they immediately lose a single Wound. However, until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, if any enemy Wizard rolls any natural double when making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md), the spell is [miscast](../magic/miscast.md).
