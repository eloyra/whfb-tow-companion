# Grut's Sickle

Arcane Item 40 points

*Swinging the sickle of Grut the Bloodthirsty, a Butcher fuels their magic with the blood of their kin.*

After making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md), the bearer of Grut's Sickle may use it to roll an additional D3 and add its result to their Casting roll. However, a single friendly model within 3" of the bearer immediately loses a single Wound.

*Note that the result of this additional D3 cannot cause a*  [miscast](../magic/miscast.md)  *or a*  [perfect invocation](../magic/perfect-invocations.md) *.*
