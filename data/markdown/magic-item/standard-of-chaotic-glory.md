# Standard of Chaotic Glory

Magic Standard 30 points

*This banner flickers in and out of existence, creating a tear in reality through which the power of Chaos flows.*

[Daemonic Locus](../special-rules/daemonic-locus.md) only. Friendly [Daemonic](../special-rules/daemonic.md) units gain a +1 modifier to their Leadership characteristic whilst within this character's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) (to a maximum of 10).
