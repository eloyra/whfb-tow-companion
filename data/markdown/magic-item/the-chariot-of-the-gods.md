# The Chariot of the Gods

Unique

*This mighty chariot is blessed by all of the gods and goddesses of Nehekhara, causing its wheels to blaze with divine flame.*

[Impact hits](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) caused by the Chariot of the Gods have the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) and [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rules. In addition, when fielded within a [Nehekharan Royal Host](../warhammer-armies/nehekharan-royal-host.md) army, this model may join a unit of Tomb Guard Chariots as if its troop type were [light chariot](../troop-types-in-detail/light-chariots.md).
