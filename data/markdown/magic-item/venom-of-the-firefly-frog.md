# Venom of the Firefly Frog

Enchanted Item 15 points

*Extracted from the rare and deadly firefly frog, even a tiny amount of this poison can prove fatal.*

All attacks made during the [Combat phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-combat-phase) by the bearer of the Venom of the Firefly Frog have the [Poisoned Attacks](../special-rules/poisoned-attacks.md) and [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) special rules.

*Note that this only applies to non-magical weapons and does not apply to a model's mount (should it have one).*
