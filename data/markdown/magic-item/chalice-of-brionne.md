# Chalice of Brionne

Enchanted Item

*This gilded relic draws power away from those who would harm the servants of the Lady.*

Whilst within this model's [Command range](../characters/command-range.md), enemy characters that attempt to use any special rule or magic item that requires them to make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md) ([Rallying Cry](../special-rules/rallying-cry.md), for example) suffer a -2 modifier to their Leadership characteristic (to a minimum of 2).
