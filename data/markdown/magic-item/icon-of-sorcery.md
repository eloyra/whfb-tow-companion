# Icon of Sorcery\*

Magic Standard 35 points

*The runes upon this banner swirl and distort as they magnify the sorcerous might of the Horrors that caper beneath it.*

Pink Horrors only. When a unit carrying the Icon of Sorcery casts a [Bound spell](../magic/bound-spells.md), it does so with a power level equal to twice its [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md).

[\*Extremely Common](../magic-items/extremely-common-magic-items.md)
