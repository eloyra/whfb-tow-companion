# Banner of the Dragon's Wrath

Magic Standard 45 points

*Those that march to war beneath the Banner of the Dragon's Wrath descend upon the foe with the fury of Li Dao, the Fire Dragon.*

A unit carrying the Banner of the Dragon's Wrath gains the [Flaming Attacks](../special-rules/flaming-attacks.md) and [Impact Hits (1)](../special-rules/impact-hits.md) special rules.
