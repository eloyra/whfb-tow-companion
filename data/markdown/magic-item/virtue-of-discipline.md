# Virtue of Discipline

Ability 5 points

*Famed for his discipline, Marcus took to the field of battle undaunted by his enemies.*

A model with this Virtue (and its unit) can march whilst within 8" of an enemy unit without first having to make a [Leadership test](../model-profiles/leadership-tests.md).
