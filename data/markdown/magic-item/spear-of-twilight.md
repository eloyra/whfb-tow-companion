# Spear of Twilight

Magic Weapon 65 points

*When this spear strikes, the fury and rage of the wielder is channelled into the body of the foe with appalling and destructive force.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -2 | - |
