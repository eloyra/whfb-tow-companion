# The Blessed Blade of Ptra

Magic Weapon

*Blessed by the sun god, the blade of Ptra's white-hot edge is infused with the heat of the desert sun. It burns so brightly with divine fire that it blinds those who dare stand before the mighty Settra.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S | -3 | - |

***Notes:***  *Any enemy model that suffers one or more unsaved wounds from the Blessed Blade of Ptra suffers a -1 modifier to its rolls To Hit, both when shooting and in combat, for the remainder of the game.*
