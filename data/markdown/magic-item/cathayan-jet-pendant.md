# Cathayan Jet Pendant\*

Talisman 10 points

*Finely crafted Cathayan jet pendants are believed to protect against all forms of hostile magic.*

A Cathayan Jet Pendant gives its bearer a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), a [Magical Vortex](../magic/magical-vortex.md), or an [Assailment](../magic/assailment.md) spell.

[\*Extremely Common](../magic-items/extremely-common-magic-items.md)
