# Fistful of Laurels\*

Enchanted Item 15 points

*Symbols of leadership snatched from the corpses of enemy commanders impress Ogres only briefly.*

Single use. The bearer of a Fistful of Laurels and their unit may re-roll the 2D6 when making a [Break test](../the-combat-phase/break-test.md).

[\*Extremely Common](../magic-items/extremely-common-magic-items.md)
