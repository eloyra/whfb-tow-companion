# Shield of the Mirror Pool

Magic Armour 40 points

*Hostile enemy magic is absorbed by this ancient shield, and reflected back upon the caster.*

The Shield of the Mirror Pool is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, each time the bearer loses one or more Wounds to a [Magic Missile](../magic/magic-missiles.md), the caster suffers a single Strength 5 hit with an [AP](../the-shooting-phase/armour-piercing.md) of -2.
