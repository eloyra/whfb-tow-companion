# Dwarf Hide Banner

Magic Standard 40 points

*Made from the tanned hides and shorn beards of vanquished Dwarfs, this banner fills the Skaven with the courage to face their ancient enemies.*

A unit carrying the Dwarf Hide Banner gains the [Hatred (Dwarfs)](../special-rules/hatred.md) and [Stubborn](../special-rules/stubborn.md) special rules.
