# Maw Shard

Enchanted Item 50 points

*Some have travelled to the broken lands around the Great Maw and brought back strange objects - among them the Maw Shard, an extra-worldly artefact.*

If an enemy Wizard rolls any natural double when making a [Casting roll](../magic/casting-roll-casting-result-and-casting-value.md) whilst within 18" of the bearer of the Maw Shard, the spell is [miscast](../magic/miscast.md), regardless of the casting result.
