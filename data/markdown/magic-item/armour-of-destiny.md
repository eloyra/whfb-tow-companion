# Armour of Destiny

Magic Armour 70 points

*The potent Armour of Destiny protects its wearer from all but the most grievous of harm.*

The Armour of Destiny is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered.
