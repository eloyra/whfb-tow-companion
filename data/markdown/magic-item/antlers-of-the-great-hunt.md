# Antlers of the Great Hunt

Enchanted Item 25 points

*The knight's helm is adorned with a splendid set of antlers, the quarry of a great hunt slain by the wearer. The antlers denote exceptional prowess as a horseman and hunter.*

If the wearer of the Antlers of the Great Hunt is mounted on a Barded Warhorse or a Warhorse, they and any unit they join gains the [Move Through Cover](../special-rules/move-through-cover.md) special rule.
