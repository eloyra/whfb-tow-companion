# Order of the Knights of Morr

Ability +15 points per Character / +3 points per model

*Clad in jet-black armour, these dour warriors strive to protect the living from the ever-present threat of the undead.*

Models belonging to the Order of the Knights of Morr:

- Gain the [Hatred](../special-rules/hatred.md) (Undead models) special rule.
- Have a 6+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against any wounds suffered that were caused by a non-magical enemy attack.

In addition, a [Grand Master](../unit/grand-master.md), [Chapter Master](../unit/chapter-master.md) or unit of [Inner Circle Knights](../unit/inner-circle-knights.md) belonging to the Order of the Knights of Morr:

- Gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) and [Magical Attacks](../special-rules/magical-attacks.md) special rules.
