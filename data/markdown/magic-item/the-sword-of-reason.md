# The Sword of Reason

Magic Weapon 65 points

*This great blade is sometimes wielded by Yuan Bo when his duties require him to enforce the Celestial Dragon's will upon the unruly.*

| Range | Strength | Armour Piercing | Special Rules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Combat | S+2 | -2 | - |

***Notes:***  *The wielder of the Sword of Reason strikes a*  [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md)  *if they roll a natural 5 or 6 when making a roll To Wound, rather than the usual 6.*
