# Storm Banner

Magic Standard 65 points

*This ancient banner possesses the power to wrack the sky with storms and tear the heavens apart with its fury.*

Single use. A unit carrying the Storm Banner may activate it during the [Command](../the-strategy-phase/command.md) sub-phase of their turn. Until your next [Start of Turn](../the-strategy-phase/start-of-turn.md) sub-phase, enemy units cannot use the [Fly (X)](../special-rules/fly.md) special rule and all enemy shooting suffers an additional -1 To Hit modifier.
