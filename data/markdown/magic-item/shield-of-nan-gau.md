# Shield of Nan-Gau

Magic Armour 20 points

*Carved from a section of the Great Bastion, the Shield of Nan-Gau is a massive stone and iron shield.*

The Shield of Nan-Gau is a [shield](../weapons-of-war/shield.md). In addition, during a turn in which its bearer was charged, the Shield of Nan-Gau improves its bearer's [armour value](../weapons-of-war/armour-value.md) by 2 (to a maximum of 2+).
