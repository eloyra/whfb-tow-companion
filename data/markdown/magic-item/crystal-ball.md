# Crystal Ball

Arcane Item 20 points

*Wizards who serve at court often rely upon reading portents and divining the future in order to best advise their masters.*

Once per turn, you may make your opponent re-roll a single roll To Hit or To Wound made against a friendly character model that is within the [Command range](../characters/command-range.md) of the bearer of the Crystal Ball.
