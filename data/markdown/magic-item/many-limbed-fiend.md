# Many-limbed Fiend

Ability 20 points

*Some Beastman grow additional arms, allowing them to unleash even more carnage upon the battlefield.*

This model (but not its mount) has a +1 modifier to its Attacks characteristic. However, this bonus attack must be made using an ordinary [hand weapon](../weapons-of-war/hand-weapon.md).
