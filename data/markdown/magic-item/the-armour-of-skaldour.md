# The Armour of Skaldour

Magic Armour

*Etched deeply with powerful runes, the Armour of Skaldour has turned countless blades and protected Thorgrim through many bloody battles.*

The Armour of Skaldour is a suit of [heavy armour](../weapons-of-war/heavy-armour.md). In addition, its wearer has a 4+ [Ward save](../the-shooting-phase/ward-saves.md) against a [Killing Blow](../special-rules/killing-blow.md), or against any wounds suffered that were caused by an attack that has the [Multiple Wounds (X)](../special-rules/multiple-wounds.md) special rule.
