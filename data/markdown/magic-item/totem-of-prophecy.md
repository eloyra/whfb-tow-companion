# Totem of Prophecy

Magic Standard 30 points

*Those who look upon this totem witness the truth and learn of their insignificance in the scheme of the Old Ones.*

A unit carrying the Totem of Prophecy gains the [Fear](../special-rules/fear.md) special rule.
