# Woodland Boundaries

A woodland terrain feature must have a clearly defined edge. This allows individual trees to be removed and replaced to facilitate the movement of models.

Regardless of the position of any individual trees within the terrain feature, any model or unit counts as being behind partial cover as long as over half of the model, or over half of the models within the unit, is within the woods (i.e., within the terrain feature’s clearly defined edge).
