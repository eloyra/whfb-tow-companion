# Low & High Linear Obstacles

This category includes walls, hedges, fences; even ditches and furrows. A [low linear obstacle](low-linear-obstacles.md) is defined as being less than 2" high. Anything that is 2" high or more is a [high linear obstacle](high-linear-obstacles.md).
