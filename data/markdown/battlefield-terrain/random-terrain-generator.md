# Random Terrain Generator

These are tables that can be used to randomly generate terrain for your battlefield, thus removing any personal bias that may creep in when choosing terrain.

## The Size of Things

Each terrain feature generated by rolling on a terrain generator should measure between 2" and 8" at its widest point. Should a feature be rolled a second time, you may either place a second feature of the same type and size, or you may replace the first feature with a larger version, measuring between 8" and 12" at its widest point.

In the case of [linear terrain features](linear-terrain-features.md), should more be generated, each additional feature is added to the first, increasing its length.

Similarly, in the case of [buildings](buildings.md) should more be generated, you may either place additional buildings, each measuring up to 6" along its longest side, or you may replace an existing feature with a larger building, measuring between 6" and 12" along its longest side.
