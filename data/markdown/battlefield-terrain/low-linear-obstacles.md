# Low Linear Obstacles

For the purposes of movement, low linear obstacles are treated as [difficult terrain](difficult-terrain.md). Should a unit engaged in combat ever find itself straddling a low linear obstacle, it becomes [Disrupted](../forming-units/disrupted-units.md) and cannot claim a [Rank Bonus](../the-combat-phase/rank-bonus.md).
