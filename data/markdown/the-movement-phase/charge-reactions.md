# Charge Reactions

Once the active player has declared all of their charges, the inactive player declares a 'charge reaction' for each of the charge targets. There are three charge reactions available to the inactive player: [Hold](hold.md), [Stand & Shoot](stand-and-shoot.md) and [Flee](flee-charge-reaction.md).
