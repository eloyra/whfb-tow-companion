# Fleeing Units (Movement)

Units that failed to [Rally](../the-strategy-phase/rally-fleeing-units.md) during the [Strategy phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-strategy-phase) will continue to flee during the Compulsory Moves sub-phase. Fleeing units must be moved at the beginning of this subphase, before moving any other units that are obliged to make a compulsory move.

Moving a fleeing unit is often a complicated procedure. For this reason, [fleeing](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) itself is covered in greater detail after the [basics of movement](../movement-in-detail/basic-movement.md) and [manoeuvre](../movement-in-detail/manoeuvres.md) have been explained in more detail.
