# Hold

The unit opts to stand its ground and receive the charge. This is the usual response for units that do not have missile weapons or those units that favour their chances in the fight ahead.

Fleeing units cannot Hold. Units already engaged in combat when charged must Hold. Any unit that forgets to declare a charge reaction will Hold.
