# Flee (Charge Reaction)

Any unit that is not already engaged in combat may Flee as a charge reaction. Units already fleeing must Flee when charged.

When a unit chooses to Flee from a charge, it flees directly away from the charging unit:

- [Pivot](../movement-in-detail/pivoting.md) the unit about its centre so that it is facing directly away from the centre of the charging enemy unit.
- After pivoting, the unit makes an immediate [flee](../movement-in-detail/flee.md) move.

Should a fleeing unit not run far enough, it may be [run down](../movement-in-detail/running-down-the-foe.md) and destroyed by the charging unit.
