# Wizard's Bane

To fulfil this objective, you must successfully dispel three of your opponent's spells in a single turn, without being [Outclassed in the Art](../magic/outclassed-in-the-art.md) during the same turn.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 75 VP.
