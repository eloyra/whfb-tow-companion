# Overwhelming Firepower

To fulfil this objective, you must destroy an enemy unit in the [Shooting phase](https://tow.whfb.app/the-shooting-phase) that began that phase with a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of 10 or more.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 75 VP.
