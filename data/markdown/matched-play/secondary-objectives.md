# Secondary Objectives

*Whilst the spectacle of two rival armies drawing battlelines across an open field in preparation for a grim and bloody fight to the death is unmistakably captivating, battles in the Old World are seldom such simple matters. Armies fight for control of land, to protect sacred sites, to seize vital resources or to burn and scatter an enemy's victuals.*
