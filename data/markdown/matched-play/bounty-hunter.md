# Bounty Hunter

To fulfil this objective, you must kill the most expensive unit in your opponent's army. Units that have fled off the battlefield, or that are fleeing at the end of the battle, count towards fulfilling this objective.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 75 VP.
