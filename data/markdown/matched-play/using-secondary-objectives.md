# Using Secondary Objectives

During the course of a matched play event, the organiser may choose to apply one or more Secondary objectives to any scenario. Each scenario includes recommended Secondary objectives, chosen to work best with that scenario, but organisers are free to use any of the Secondary objectives listed on the following pages in any scenario.
