# Cut Off Their Retreat

To fulfil this objective, you must have one of your units, with a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) of 15 or more (not counting units that are fleeing or that have succumbed to Stupidity), within 6" of the longest edge of the battlefield within your opponent's deployment zone at the end of the battle.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 75 VP.
