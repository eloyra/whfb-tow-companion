# Army Composition

*The army composition rules given in the Warhammer: the Old World rulebook give players great freedom to create their ideal army. However, when writing muster lists for matched play events, players may prefer tighter restrictions. The organisers of a matched play event should specify which of the following sets of army composition rules their event will use.*
