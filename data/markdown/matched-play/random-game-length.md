# Random Game Length

If the Random Game Length game end condition is being used, then players will roll to determine if the game suddenly ends, or continues for another round. Starting at the end of the fifth round, roll a D6 at the end of each round and add the round number to the result.

If the result is 10 or more, the battle ends immediately. If the total is less than 10, the battle continues for at least one more round.
