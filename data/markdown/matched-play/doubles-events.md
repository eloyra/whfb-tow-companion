# Doubles Events

*Doubles events are ever-popular and enjoyable. Joining forces with a friend, using your collective gaming experience to seek victory, and combining your collections to create a single army are just some of the reasons why Doubles events are well-attended.*

The following sections introduce some additional rules for anyone wishing to run a Doubles event, or simply for playing Doubles games with and against regular opponents.
