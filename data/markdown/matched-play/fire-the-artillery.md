# "Fire the Artillery!"

To fulfil this objective, you must cause an enemy unit to fail a [Panic test](../the-psychology-of-war/panic-tests.md) and flee as a result of being shot at by a unit whose unit type is [war machine](../troop-types-in-detail/war-machines.md).

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 100 VP.
