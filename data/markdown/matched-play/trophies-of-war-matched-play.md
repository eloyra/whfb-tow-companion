# Trophies of War (Matched Play)

You win a bonus of 50 Victory Points for every enemy standard claimed as a [trophy](../warhammer-battles/trophies-of-war-warhammer-battles.md). Additionally, if the enemy Battle Standard Bearer is slain, has fled off the battlefield or is fleeing when the game ends, you win another bonus of 50 Victory Points.
