# Beast Hunter

To fulfil this objective, you must kill all units in your opponent's army whose troop type is [monster](../troop-types-in-detail/monsters.md). Any enemy units whose troop type is monster that have fled the battlefield, or that are fleeing at the end of the battle, count towards fulfilling this objective.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 100 VP.
