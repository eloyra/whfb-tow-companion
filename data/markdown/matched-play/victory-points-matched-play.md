# Victory Points (Matched Play)

There are a number of ways in which players can win Victory Points (VP) during the course of a matched play game, all of which require players to fulfil certain objectives. These fall into two distinct categories - Common objectives, which are explained below, and [Secondary objectives](secondary-objectives.md).
