# Captured Colours

You fulfil this objective by capturing enemy standards as [Trophies of War](trophies-of-war-matched-play.md).

If you capture:

- Two enemy standards, you win a bonus of 50 VP.
- Three enemy standards, you win a bonus of 100 VP.
- Four or more enemy standards, you win a bonus of 150 VP.
