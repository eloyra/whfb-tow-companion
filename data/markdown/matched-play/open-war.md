# Open War

Used in conjunction with Grand Army and Army of Infamy composition lists, the [army composition rules](../warhammer-armies/army-lists-warhammer-armies.md) of the *Warhammer: the Old World* rulebook give players the most freedom of choice when writing their muster lists. In matched play, this is referred to as 'Open War' army composition rules.

*Note that, under these rules,*  [mercenaries](../warhammer-armies/mercenaries-warhammer-armies.md)  *and*  [allied contingents](../warhammer-armies/allied-contingents.md)  *are always permitted.*
