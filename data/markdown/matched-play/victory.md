# Victory!

*Battles in the Old World are won or lost by whichever General is able to outmanoeuvre their opponents and lay waste to their enemy with little sign of mercy. As such, in most matched play games of Warhammer: the Old World the victor is determined by how many Victory Points each player is able to win.*
