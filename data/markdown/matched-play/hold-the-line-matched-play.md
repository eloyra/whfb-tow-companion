# Hold the Line (Matched Play)

To fulfil this objective, you must have two of your units, each with a [Unit Strength](../troop-types-at-a-glance/unit-strength.md) 10 or more (not counting units that are fleeing or that have succumbed to [Stupidity](../special-rules/stupidity.md)), within your opponent's deployment zone at the end of the battle.

If you fulfil this objective, you win a bonus of 75 VP.
