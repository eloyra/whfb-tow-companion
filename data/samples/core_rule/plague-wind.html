Plague Wind
URL Copied!

  • Lore of Daemons
  • Daemons of Chaos
  • Last update: 2024 October 12
  • Daemons of Chaos, p. 34
Signature Spell

A debilitating cloud of noxious air forms, swirling around the battlefield and infecting all it touches.

Type

Magical Vortex

Casting Value

7+

Range

12"

Daemons of Nurgle only. Remains in Play. Place a small (3") blast template so that its central hole is within 12" of the caster. Whilst in play, the template is treated as dangerous terrain. The template moves D6" in a random direction during every Start of Turn sub-phase. Any unit (friend or foe, but not including Daemons of Nurgle) the moving template touches or moves over suffers a -1 modifier to its Toughness characteristic for the remainder of the turn.
